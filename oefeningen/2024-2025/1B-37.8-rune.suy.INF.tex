Bereken in $\mathbb{C}$ de vierkantswortels van $7+24i$\\

$ (x+yi)^2 = 7+24i $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{ \\
\begin{array}{l} 
    x^2 - y^2 = 7 \\
    2xy = 24 \\
\end{array} \\
\right . $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{ \\
\begin{array}{l} 
    x^2 - y^2 = 7 \\
    x(-y) = -12 \\
\end{array} \\
\right . $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{ 
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = 7 \\
    x^2(-y)^2 = -144
\end{array}
\right . $\\

$\Rightarrow$ $x^2$ en $y^2$ zijn de oplossingen van de vierkantsvergelijking $z^2 - 7z - 144 = 0$\\

$ D = (-7) - 4\cdot1\cdot(-144) = 49 + 400+160+16 = 625 = 25^2$\\

$ z_1 = \dfrac{7-25}{2} = -9 \Rightarrow y^2 = 9 \Leftrightarrow y = \pm 3$\\

$ z_1 = \dfrac{7+25}{2} = 16 \Rightarrow x^2 = 16 \Leftrightarrow y = \pm 4$\\

$$ \text{Antwoord: } 4+3i \text{ en } -4-3i$$\\
