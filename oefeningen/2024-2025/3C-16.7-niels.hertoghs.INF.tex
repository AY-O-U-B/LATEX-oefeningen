$\lim\limits_{x \to 1} \dfrac{x - 1}{9x^2 - 5x - 4} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$f(x) = x - 1 \Rightarrow f'(x) = 1$ \\

$g(x) = 9x^2 - 5x - 4 \Rightarrow g'(x) = 18x - 5$ \\

$\lim\limits_{x \to 1} \dfrac{x - 1}{9x^2 - 5x - 4} = \lim\limits_{x \to 1} \dfrac{f'(x)}{g'(x)} = \lim\limits_{x \to 1} \dfrac{1}{18x - 5} = \dfrac{1}{18 - 5} = \dfrac{1}{13}$ \\
