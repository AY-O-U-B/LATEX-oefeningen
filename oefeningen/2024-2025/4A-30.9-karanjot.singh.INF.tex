$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{x^2 + 5}} dx$ \\

Stel $u = x^2 + 5$ \\

$\Rightarrow du = 2x dx$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{2} du = x dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{u}} du$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int u^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} du$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{u^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{\tfrac{1}{2}} + C$ \\

$= u^{\tfrac{1}{2}} + C$ \\

$= \sqrt{x^2 + 5} + C$ \\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{x^2 + 5}} dx = \sqrt{x^2 + 5} + C$ \\
