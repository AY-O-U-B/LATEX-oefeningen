$\displaystyle \int_0^{1-} \operatorname{Bgsin} x  dx$ \\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle \int \operatorname{Bgsin} x  dx$ \\

$\left\{
   \begin{array}{l}
       u = \operatorname{Bgsin} x \\
       dv = dx
   \end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
   \begin{array}{l}
       du = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} dx \\
       v = x
   \end{array}
\right.$ \\

$= x \operatorname{Bgsin} x - \displaystyle \int \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} dx$ \\

$= x \operatorname{Bgsin} x + \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{d(1 - x^2)}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}$ \\

$= x \operatorname{Bgsin} x + \sqrt{1 - x^2} + C$ \\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle \int_0^{1-} \operatorname{Bgsin} x  dx = \left[ x \operatorname{Bgsin} x + \sqrt{1 - x^2} \right]_0^1$ \\

$= \operatorname{Bgsin}(1) - 1 = \dfrac{\pi}{2} - 1$ \\
