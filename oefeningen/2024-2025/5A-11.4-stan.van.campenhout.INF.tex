$ \displaystyle\int\limits_0^1\left(x^2+3x+2\right)e^x dx $\\

O.I. $ = \displaystyle\int\left(x^2+3x+2\right) d\left(e^x\right) $\\

$ = \left(x^2+3x+2\right)e^x - \displaystyle\int\left(2x+3\right)d\left(e^x\right) $\\

$ = \left(x^2+3x+2\right)e^x - \left(2x+3\right)e^x + \displaystyle\int 2e^xdx $\\

$ = \left(x^2+3x+2-2x-3+2\right)e^x + c = \left(x^2+x+1\right)e^x + c $\\

$ \Rightarrow $ B.I. $ = -\left[\left(x^2+x+1\right)e^x\right]_{-1}^0 =-\left(1-\dfrac{1}{e}\right) = \dfrac{1-e}{e} $\\
