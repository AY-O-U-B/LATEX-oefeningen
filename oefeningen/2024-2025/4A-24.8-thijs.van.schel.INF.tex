$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{\sqrt{1+\ln(x)}}{x}dx$ \\

$t = 1+\ln(x)$, $dt = \dfrac{1}{x}dx$\\

$\displaystyle\int t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}dt$\\

$= \dfrac{t^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{\dfrac{3}{2}}+c$\\

$= \dfrac{2t^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{3}+c$\\

$= \dfrac{2t\sqrt{t}}{3}+c$\\

$= \dfrac{2(1+\ln(x))\sqrt{1+\ln(x)}}{3}$\\
