$ I= \displaystyle\int e^x \sin x  dx $ \\

Stel
$
\left\{
  \begin{array}{l}
    u = e^x \\
    dv = \sin x  dx
  \end{array}
\right.
\quad \Rightarrow \quad
\left\{
  \begin{array}{l}
    du = e^x  dx \\
    v = -\cos x
  \end{array}
\right.
$ \\


$ \Rightarrow  I= -e^x \cos x + \displaystyle\int e^x \cos x  dx $ \\

Stel 
$
\left\{
  \begin{array}{l}
    u = e^x \\
    dv = \cos x  dx
  \end{array}
\right.
\quad \Rightarrow \quad
\left\{
  \begin{array}{l}
    du = e^x  dx \\
    v = \sin x
  \end{array}
\right.
$ \\

$ \Rightarrow I=-e^x \cos x + e^x \sin x - \displaystyle\int e^x \sin x  dx $ \\

$ \Rightarrow 2I = -e^x \cos x + e^x \sin x + C $ \\

$\Rightarrow I = \dfrac{e^x (\sin x - \cos x)}{2} + C $ \\
