$f(x) = \dfrac{1}{x^2}$\\

Berekent men de afgeleide functie als volgt:\\

$f(x+h) = \dfrac{1}{(x+h)^2} $\\

$\Rightarrow f(x+h)-f(x) = \dfrac{1}{(x+h)^2} - \dfrac{1}{x^2} = \dfrac{-2xh-h^2}{(x+h)^2x^2}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h} = \dfrac{-2xh-h^2}{(x+h)^2x^2h} = \dfrac{-2x-h}{(x+h)^2x^2}$\\

$\Rightarrow f'(x)=\lim\limits_{h\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h} = \lim\limits_{h\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{-2x-h}{(x+h)^2x^2} = \dfrac{-2}{x^3}$ \\

Nu met $nx^{n-1}$\\

$f(x) = \dfrac{1}{x^2} = x^{-2}$\\

$f'(x) = -2x^{-3} = \dfrac{-2}{x^3}$\\
