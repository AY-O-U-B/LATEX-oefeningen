$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{\Bgtan x}{1+x^2}dx$\\

$u = \Bgtan x$

$du = \dfrac{dx}{1+x^2}$

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{u}{1+x^2}(1+x^2)du$\\

$= \displaystyle\int udu$\\

$= \dfrac{u^2}{2}$

$= \dfrac{\Bgtan^2x}{2} + C$
