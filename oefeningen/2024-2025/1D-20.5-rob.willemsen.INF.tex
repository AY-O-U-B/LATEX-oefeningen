$z^4 + 4 = z^4 + 4z^2 + 4 - 4z^2$ \\

$z^4 + 4 = (z^2 + 2)^2 - (2z)^2$ \\

$z^4 + 4 = (z^2 - 2z + 2)(z^2 + 2z + 2)$ \\

$z^2 - 2z + 2 = 0 \Rightarrow z = \dfrac{2 + 2i}{2} = 1 + i$ of $z = \dfrac{2 - 2i}{2} = 1 - i$ \\

$z^2 + 2z + 2 = 0 \Rightarrow z = \dfrac{-2 + 2i}{2} = -1 + i$ of $z = \dfrac{-2 - 2i}{2} = -1 - i$ \\

\textbf{Antwoord:} De nulpunten in $\mathbb{C}$ zijn $1+i$, $1-i$, $-1+i$ en $-1-i$. De ontbinding over $\mathbb{R}[z]$ is $A(z) = (z^2 - 2z + 2)(z^2 + 2z + 2)$. \\
