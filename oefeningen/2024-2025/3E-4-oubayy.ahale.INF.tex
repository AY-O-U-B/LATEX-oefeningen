$ V(t) = 5000 \left( 1 - \dfrac{t}{40} \right)^2 $\\

$ V'(t) = 5000 \cdot \dfrac{d}{dt} \left[ \left( 1 - \dfrac{t}{40} \right)^2 \right] $\\

$ = 5000 \cdot 2 \left( 1 - \dfrac{t}{40} \right) \cdot \dfrac{d}{dt} \left( 1 - \dfrac{t}{40} \right) $\\

$ = 10000 \left( 1 - \dfrac{t}{40} \right) \cdot \left( -\dfrac{1}{40} \right) $\\

$ = -250 \left( 1 - \dfrac{t}{40} \right) $\\

\medskip

Stel $ Q(t) = 5000 - V(t) $\\

$ Q'(t) = - \left[ -250 \left( 1 - \dfrac{t}{40} \right) \right] $\\

$ = 250 \left( 1 - \dfrac{t}{40} \right) $\\

\medskip

4.1. Na 5 minuten:\\

$ Q'(5) = 250 \left( 1 - \dfrac{5}{40} \right) = 250 \left( 1 - 0.125 \right) = 218.75 $ liter per minuut\\

\medskip

4.2. Na 10 minuten:\\

$ Q'(10) = 250 \left( 1 - \dfrac{10}{40} \right) = 250 \left( 1 - 0.25 \right) = 187.5 $ liter per minuut\\

\medskip

4.3. Na 20 minuten:\\

$ Q'(20) = 250 \left( 1 - \dfrac{20}{40} \right) = 250 \left( 1 - 0.5 \right) = 125 $ liter per minuut\\
