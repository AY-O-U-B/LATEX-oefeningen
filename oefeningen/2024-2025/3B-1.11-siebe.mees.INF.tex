$ f(x) = x \Bgsin x $ \\

$ f'(x) = x' \cdot \Bgsin x + x \cdot \dfrac{d}{dx} \left[ \Bgsin x \right] $ \\

$ = 1 \cdot \Bgsin x + x \cdot \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} $ \\

$ = \Bgsin x + \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$ \\

$ f''(x) = \dfrac{d}{dx} \left[ \Bgsin x  \right] + \dfrac{d}{dx} \left[ x \cdot (1-x^2) ^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} \right]$ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} + x' \cdot (1-x^2)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} + x \cdot \dfrac{d}{dx} \left[(1-x^2)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} \right] $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} + 1 \cdot (1-x^2)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} + x \cdot \dfrac{x}{(1-x^2)^{\tfrac{3}{2}}} $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} + \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} + \dfrac{x^2}{(1-x^2)^{\tfrac{3}{2}}} $\\

$ = \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} + \dfrac{x^2}{(1-x^2)^{\tfrac{3}{2}}} $\\
