$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{ln(1+2\sin x)}{\sin x}$\\

$\overset{H}{=}  \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{2 \cos x}{1+2 \sin x}}{\cos x} $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{2 \cos x}{\cos x (1+2\sin x)} $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{2}{1+2\sin x} $\\

$ = \dfrac{2}{1+2\cdot 0} = 2 $\\
