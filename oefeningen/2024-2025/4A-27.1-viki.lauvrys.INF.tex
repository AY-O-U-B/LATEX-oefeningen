$\displaystyle\int \left( x^2 +1 \right)^5 x dx $ \\

Stel $ u = x2 +1 \Rightarrow du = 2x  dx $ \\

$ \Rightarrow \displaystyle\int u^5 \dfrac{du}{2} $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int u^5 du $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \dfrac{u^6}{6} + C $ \\

$ = \dfrac{u^6}{12} + C $ \\

$ = \dfrac{(x^2 +1)^6}{12} + C $ \\
