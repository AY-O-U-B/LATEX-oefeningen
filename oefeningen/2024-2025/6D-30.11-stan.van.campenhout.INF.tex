$ \displaystyle\sum\limits_{n = 2}^\infty\left(\dfrac{\left|\sin 2^n\right|}{n^3}+\dfrac{5\ln n}{n^3}\right) $\\

$ = \displaystyle\sum\limits_{n = 2}^\infty\dfrac{\left|\sin 2^n\right|}{n^3} + 5\displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^\infty\dfrac{\ln n}{n^3} $\\

$ \displaystyle\sum\limits_{n = 2}^\infty\dfrac{1}{n^3} $ majorant convergent over $  \displaystyle\sum\limits_{n = 2}^\infty\dfrac{\left|\sin 2^n\right|}{n^3} $\\

$ \displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^\infty\dfrac{\ln n}{n^3} \Rightarrow $ convergent, al in een eerdere oefeningen bewezen \\

$ \Rightarrow $ 5 $\times$ convergent = convergent \\

$ \Rightarrow $ convergent + convergent = convergent \\
