$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{1-3x}$\\

Stel $t = 1-3x\Rightarrow dt = -3dx$\\

$= -\dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{t}dt$\\

$= -\dfrac{1}{3} \ln|t|$ + C\\

$= -\dfrac{1}{3} \ln|1-3x|$ + C\\
