$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{1+\sin x}$\\

$\tan \dfrac{x}{2} = t \Rightarrow x = 2\operatorname{Bgtan}t \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{2dt}{1+t^2}, \sin x = \dfrac{2t}{1+t^2}$\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\dfrac{2}{1+t^2}}{1+\dfrac{2t}{1+t^2}}dt = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\dfrac{2}{1+t^2}}{\dfrac{(1+t^2)+2t}{1+t^2}}dt$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2}{(1+t)^2}d(1+t) = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2}{u^2}du = -\dfrac{2}{u}+c = -\dfrac{2}{1+t}+c$\\

$= -\dfrac{2}{1+\tan\dfrac{x}{2}} + c$
