$\displaystyle\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{4}} \tan^2x dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{4}} \left(\dfrac{1}{\cos^2x} - 1\right) dx = \left[\tan x - x\right]_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{4}} $\\

$= 1 - \dfrac{\pi}{4}$\\
