$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{4x^2+12x+18} = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{(2x=3)^2 + 9} $\\

Stel $ 2x+3 = t \Rightarrow 2dx = dt \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{dt}{2}$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t^2+9} = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t^2 + 3^2}$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} \operatorname{Bgtan}\left(\dfrac{t}{3}\right) + C = \dfrac{1}{6} \operatorname{Bgtan} \left( \dfrac{2x+3}{3}\right) + C$\\
