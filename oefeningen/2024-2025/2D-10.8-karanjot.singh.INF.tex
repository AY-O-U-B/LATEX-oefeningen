	$\llim_{x \to 0} \dfrac{x^3 + 3x^2 - 4x}{x^2 - x} = \dfrac{0}{0}$\\
	
	$  = \llim_{x \to 0} \dfrac{x(x^2 + 3x - 4)}{x(x - 1)}$\\
	
	$  = \llim_{x \to 0} \dfrac{x(x + 4)(x - 1)}{x(x - 1)} $\\
	
	$ = \llim_{x \to 0} (x + 4)$\\
	
	$ = 4$\\
