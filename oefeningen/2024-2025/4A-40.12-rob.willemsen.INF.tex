$\dint \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{2+3x-x^2}}$ \\

$=\dint \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{-(x^2-3x-2)}}$ \\

$=\dint \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{-((x-\frac{3}{2})^2-\frac{17}{4})}}$ \\

$=\dint \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{\dfrac{17}{4}- \left(x-\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^2}}$ \\

$=\Bgsin\left(\dfrac{x-\dfrac{3}{2}}{\dfrac{\sqrt{17}}{2}}\right) + C$ \\

$=\Bgsin\left(\dfrac{2x-3}{\sqrt{17}}\right) + C$ \\
