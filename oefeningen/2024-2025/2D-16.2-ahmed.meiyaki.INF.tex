$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\cos x}{x - \dfrac{\pi}{2}} = \dfrac{0}{0} $\\

$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} - x\right)}{x - \dfrac{\pi}{2}} $\\

$=\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} - x\right)}{-\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} - x\right)} = -1 $\\
