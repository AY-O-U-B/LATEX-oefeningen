$ = \ddg(\operatorname{Bgsin}{(g)}) \ddx(2x^2+1)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-g^2}}2\cdot 2x$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-(2x^2+1)^2}}2*2x$\\

$= \dfrac{2x}{{\mid x \mid \sqrt{-x^2-1}}} \Rightarrow \operatorname{dom}f' = \emptyset$\\
