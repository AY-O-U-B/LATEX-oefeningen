$\log_{x}\sqrt{8} - \log_{x}4^{\tfrac{1}{3}} +\dfrac{5}{6} = 0$\\

$\Leftrightarrow\log_{x}\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{8}}{4^{\tfrac{1}{3}}}\right) + \dfrac{5}{6} = 0$\\

$\Leftrightarrow\log_{x}(2^{\tfrac{5}{6}}) + \dfrac{5}{6} = 0$\\

$\Leftrightarrow\log_{x}(2^{\tfrac{5}{6}}) = \dfrac{-5}{6}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow x^{\tfrac{-5}{6}} = 2^{\tfrac{5}{6}}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow x = 2^{\left({\tfrac{5}{6}}\right)^{\tfrac{-6}{5}}}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow x = \dfrac{1}{2}$\\
