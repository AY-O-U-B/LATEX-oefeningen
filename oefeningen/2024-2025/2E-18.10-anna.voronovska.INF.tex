$4 + \log_x \sqrt{2} - \log_x 4\sqrt{32} = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 4 + \log_x \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4\sqrt{32}} = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 4 + \log_x \dfrac{2^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{2^2 \sqrt{2^5}} = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 4 + \log_x \left( \dfrac{2^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{2^{\tfrac{9}{2}}} \right) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 4 + \log_x (2^{-\tfrac{8}{2}}) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 4 + \log_x 2^{-4} = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_x 2^{-4} = -4$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x^{-4} = 2^{-4}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x = 2$
