$ I =  \intgrl \dfrac{5^x}{5^x +2} dx$\\

Stel: $t = 5^x \adR dt = 5^x \ln 5dx \asa 5^x dx = \dfrac{dt}{\ln 5} $\\

$= \intgrl\dfrac{1}{5^x +2} \cdot \Lh 5^x dx \Rh = \intgrl\dfrac{1}{t} \dfrac{dt}{\ln 5}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\ln 5} \ln|t| +c = \dfrac{\ln |5^x +2|}{\ln 5} + c$\\
