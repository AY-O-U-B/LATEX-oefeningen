$ f'(x) =  \dfrac{1 + x}{1 - x} \cdot \dfrac{-\left(1 + x\right) - \left(1 - x\right)}{\left(1 + x\right)^2} $\\

$ = \dfrac{-2}{\left(1 - x\right)\left(1 + x\right)} = -\dfrac{2}{1 - x^2} $\\
