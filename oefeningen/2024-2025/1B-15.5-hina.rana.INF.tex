$\dfrac{5-1}{2i-1} + \dfrac{9}{2i+1}$\\

$ = \dfrac{(5-i)(2i+1)+9(2i-1)}{(2i-1)(2i+1)}$\\

$ = \dfrac{(5-i)(2i+1) + 18i - 9}{(2i)^2 - 1}$\\

$ = \dfrac{10i+5-2i^2 -i +18i -9}{(2i)^2 -1}$\\

$ = \dfrac{-2i^2 +27i -4}{4i^2 -1}$\\

$ = \dfrac{2+27i-4}{-4-1}$\\

$ = \dfrac{27i-2}{-5}$\\

$ = \dfrac{-2}{-5}-\dfrac{27}{5}i$\\

$ = \dfrac{2}{5} - \dfrac{27}{5}i$\\
