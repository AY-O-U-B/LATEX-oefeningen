$ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{8(x^3 - 1)}{9x^4+10x^2+1}dx$\\

Ontbinding noemer:\\

Stel $y = x^2 \Rightarrow 9y^2 + 10y + 1 = 0$. \\

$D = 10^2 - 4 \cdot 9 \cdot 1 = 100 - 36 = 64$. \\

$y = \dfrac{-10 \pm 8}{18} \Rightarrow y_1 = -1$ en $y_2 = -\dfrac{1}{9}$. \\

Dus de noemer is: $9(x^2+1) \left(x^2+\dfrac{1}{9}\right)$\\

$ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{8(x^3 - 1)}{9x^4+10x^2+1}dx = \dfrac{8}{9}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{x^3-1}{\left(x^2+1\right)\left(x^2+\dfrac{1}{9}\right)}dx $\\

$ \Rightarrow \dfrac{Ax + B}{x^2+1} + \dfrac{Cx + D}{x^2 + \dfrac{1}{9}} $\\

$ A\left(i\right) + B = \dfrac{i^3 - 1}{-1 + \dfrac{1}{9}} = \dfrac{-i - 1}{-\dfrac{8}{9}} = \dfrac{9}{8} + \dfrac{9}{8}i $\\

$ \Rightarrow A = \dfrac{9}{8}$ en $B = \dfrac{9}{8} $\\

$ C\left(\dfrac{1}{3}i\right) + D = \dfrac{\left(\dfrac{1}{3}i\right)^3 - 1}{-\dfrac{1}{9}+1} = \dfrac{-\dfrac{1}{27}i - 1}{\dfrac{8}{9}} = -\dfrac{9}{8} - \dfrac{1}{24}i $\\

$ \Rightarrow C = -\dfrac{1}{8}$ en $D = -\dfrac{9}{8} $\\

$ = \dfrac{8}{9} \left( \displaystyle\int\dfrac{\dfrac{9}{8}x + \dfrac{9}{8}}{x^2+1}dx 
+ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{-\dfrac{1}{8}x 
- \dfrac{9}{8}}{x^2+\dfrac{1}{9}}dx \right)$\\ 

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{x+1}{x^2+1}dx - \dfrac{1}{9}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{x+9}{x^2+\dfrac{1}{9}}dx $\\

$ =  \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x^2+1}dx
+ \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x^2+1}d\left(x^2+1\right)
-\dfrac{1}{18}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x^2+\dfrac{1}{9}}d\left(x^2+\dfrac{1}{9}\right) 
- \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x^2+\dfrac{1}{9}}dx $\\

$ = \Bgtan\left(x\right)
+ \dfrac{1}{2}\ln|x^2+1|
-\dfrac{1}{18}\ln\left|x^2+\dfrac{1}{9}\right|
-3\Bgtan\left(3x\right) + C $\\
