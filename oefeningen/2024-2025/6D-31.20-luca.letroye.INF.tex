$ \displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \dfrac{1}{n\ln\left(1+\dfrac{1}{n}\right)} = \displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \dfrac{1}{\ln\left(1+\dfrac{1}{n}\right)^n}$\\

$\Rightarrow $ negatief criterium voor divergente reeksen\\

$\lim\limits_{n\rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{1}{n\ln\left(1+\dfrac{1}{n}\right)} = 1 \Rightarrow$ divergent\\
