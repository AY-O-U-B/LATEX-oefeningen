$ y^2 = x^4+x^5                     $\\

$\Leftrightarrow y = \sqrt{x^4+x^5} $\\

$\Leftrightarrow y = x^2\sqrt{x+1}  $\\

$\dint_{-1}^{0} x^2\sqrt{x+1} dx    $\\

$t = x+1 \Leftrightarrow x = t-1    $\\

$x=-1\;\Rightarrow\;t=0$, $x=0\;\Rightarrow\;t=1             $\\

$= \dint_{0}^{1} (t-1)^2 \cdot \sqrt{t} dt                   $\\

$= \dint_{0}^{1} \left(t^2 - 2t + 1\right) \cdot \sqrt{t} dt $\\

$=\dint_{0}^{1} \left(t^{\tfrac{5}{2}}- 2t^{\tfrac{3}{2}} + \sqrt{t}\right) dt $\\

$=\dint_{0}^{1} t^{\tfrac{5}{2}} dt - \dint_{0}^{1} 2t^{\tfrac{3}{2}} dt + \dint_{0}^{1} \sqrt{t} dt $\\

$=\left[\dfrac{2t^{\tfrac{7}{2}}}{7}\right]^{1}_{0} - \left[\dfrac{4t^{\tfrac{5}{2}}}{5}\right]^{1}_{0} + \left[\dfrac{2t^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{3}\right]^{1}_{0} $\\

$= \dfrac{16}{105} $\\

Voor de volledige oppervlakte vermenigvuldigen we met 2\\

$= \dfrac{32}{105} $\\
