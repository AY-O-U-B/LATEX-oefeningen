$f(x) = \dfrac{4x + 5}{6x + 7}$ \\

Quotiëntregel: $\left(\dfrac{u}{v}\right)' = \dfrac{u'v - uv'}{v^2}$ \\

Stel $u = 4x + 5$ en $v = 6x + 7$ \\

$u' = \dfrac{d}{dx}[4x + 5] = 4$ \\

$v' = \dfrac{d}{dx}[6x + 7] = 6$ \\

$\dfrac{df}{dx} = \dfrac{(6x + 7) \cdot \dfrac{d}{dx}[4x + 5] - (4x + 5) \cdot \dfrac{d}{dx}[6x + 7]}{(6x + 7)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{(6x + 7) \cdot 4 - (4x + 5) \cdot 6}{(6x + 7)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{4(6x + 7) - 6(4x + 5)}{(6x + 7)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{24x + 28 - 24x - 30}{(6x + 7)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{28 - 30}{(6x + 7)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{-2}{(6x + 7)^2}$ \\
