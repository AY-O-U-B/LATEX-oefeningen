Schrijf $ \operatorname{cis}(-135\degree) $ in de vorm $ a + bi $.\\

$ r=1 $\\

$ \theta = -135\degree $\\

\medskip

$ x = r\cos(\theta) = \cos(-135 \degree) = \dfrac{-\sqrt{2}}{2}$\\

$ y = r\sin(\theta) = \sin(-135 \degree) = \dfrac{-\sqrt{2}}{2}$\\

\medskip

$$ \text{Antwoord: } \dfrac{-\sqrt{2}}{2} - \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}i $$\\
