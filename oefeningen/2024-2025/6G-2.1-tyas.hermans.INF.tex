$f(x) = \left\{
    \begin{array}{lrr}
        0 & als & x\in[-\pi, 0[ \\
        4 & als & x\in[0, \pi[  \\
        7 & als & x\in\{-\pi, 0, \pi\}
    \end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow f(1) \ne f(-1) \Rightarrow$ niet even\\

$\Rightarrow f(1) \ne -f(1) \Rightarrow$ niet oneven\\

$\Rightarrow f(x) = \dfrac{a_0}{2} + \displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(a_n \cos(nx)+b_n \sin(nx))$\\

$\Rightarrow a_0 = \dfrac{1}{\pi} \left(\dint_{-pi}^{0}0dx + \dint_{0}^{\pi}4dx\right) = \dfrac{1}{\pi}\left(\dint_{0}^{\pi}4dx\right) = 4$\\

$\Rightarrow a_n = \dfrac{1}{\pi}\left(\dint_{0}^{\pi}4\cos(nx)dx\right) = \dfrac{4}{\pi}\dint \cos(nx)dx$\\

$= \dfrac{4}{\pi}\left[\dfrac{\sin(nx)}{n}\right]_{0}^{\pi} = \dfrac{4}{\pi} \cdot 0 = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow b_n = \dfrac{1}{\pi} \left(\dint_{0}^{\pi}4\sin(nx) dx\right) = \dfrac{4}{\pi}\dint_{0}^{\pi}\sin(nx)dx$\\

$= \dfrac{4}{\pi}\left[-\dfrac{\cos(nx)}{n}\right]_{0}^{\pi} = \dfrac{4}{\pi}\left[-\dfrac{\cos(n\pi)-cos(0)}{n}\right] = \dfrac{4}{\pi}\left[-\dfrac{(-1)^n - 1}{n}\right]$\\

$\Rightarrow n$ even $\Rightarrow b_n = \dfrac{4}{\pi}\left(-\dfrac{0}{n}\right) = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow n$ oneven $\Rightarrow b_n = \dfrac{4}{\pi}\left(\dfrac{2}{n}\right) = \dfrac{8}{n\pi}$\\

$\Rightarrow f(x) = 2 + \displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left(-4\left(\dfrac{(-1)^n - 1}{n\pi}\right)\sin(nx)\right)$\\

alleen over oneven getallen:\\

$= 2 + \dfrac{8}{\pi}\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\dfrac{\sin (2n + 1) x}{2n + 1}$\\
