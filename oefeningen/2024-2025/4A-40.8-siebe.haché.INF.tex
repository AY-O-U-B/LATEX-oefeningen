$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{2 - 3x^2}}$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{3\left(\dfrac{2}{3} - x^2\right)}}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{6}}{3}\right)^2 - x^2}}$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \Bgsin \left( \dfrac{x}{\dfrac{\sqrt{6}}{3}} \right) + c$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \Bgsin \left( \dfrac{3x}{\sqrt{6}} \right) + C$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \Bgsin \left( \dfrac{3\sqrt{6} x}{6} \right) + C$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \Bgsin \left( \dfrac{\sqrt{6} x}{2} \right) + C$\\
