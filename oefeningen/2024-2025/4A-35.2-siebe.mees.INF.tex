$\displaystyle\int 3^{4x} dx $\\

$ \overset{4x = u}{=} \displaystyle\int 3^u dx $\\

$ \overset{dx = \tfrac{du}{4}}{=} \displaystyle\int 3^u \cdot \dfrac{du}{4} $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{4} \displaystyle\int 3^u du $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot \dfrac{3^u}{\ln(3)} + C $\\

$ \overset{u = 4x}{=} \dfrac{3^{4x}}{4 \ln(3)} + C $\\
