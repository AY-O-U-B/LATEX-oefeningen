$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{x^4dx}{\sqrt{1-x^{10}}}=\displaystyle\int\dfrac{x^4dx}{\sqrt{1-x^{5 \cdot 2}}}$\\

$u = x^5 \Rightarrow du = 5x^4dx \Rightarrow x^4dx = \dfrac{1}{5}du$\\

$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{x^4dx}{\sqrt{1-x^{10}}} = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-u^2}}\cdot\dfrac{1}{5}du = \dfrac{1}{5}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-u^2}}du$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{5} \operatorname{Bgsin}(u) + c = \dfrac{1}{5} \operatorname{Bgsin}(x^5) + c$\\
