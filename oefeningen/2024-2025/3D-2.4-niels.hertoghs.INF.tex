$f(x) = x^3-3x^2$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow f'(x) = 3x^2 - 6x$ \\

$3x^2 - 6x = 0 \Leftrightarrow 3x(x - 2) = 0$ \\

$x_1 : 3x = 0 \Leftrightarrow x_1 = 0$\\

$x_2 : x - 2 = 0 \Leftrightarrow x_2 = 2$\\

$f(0) = 0$\\

$f(2) = 8 - 12 = -4$\\
