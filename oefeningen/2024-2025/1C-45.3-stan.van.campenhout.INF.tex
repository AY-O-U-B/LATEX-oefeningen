$ z^3 = -i = 0 - 1i $\\

modulus: $r = \sqrt{0^2 + (-1^2)} = 1 $\\

argument: $\tan\theta = \dfrac{-1}{0} \Rightarrow \theta = \dfrac{\pi}{2} $\\

$ z^3 = \operatorname{cis}\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{2} + k \cdot 2\pi\right) \Rightarrow z = \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{-\dfrac{\pi}{2} + k \cdot 2\pi}{3}\right)
\forall k \in \{0, 1, 2\} $\\

$k = 0 \rightarrow z=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{-\dfrac{\pi}{2} + 0 \cdot 2\pi}{3}\right)=\operatorname{cis}\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{6}\right)
=\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2} - \dfrac{1}{2}i $\\

$k = 1 \rightarrow z=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{-\dfrac{\pi}{2} + 1 \cdot 2\pi}{3}\right) = \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{3\pi}{2}}{3}\right)
= \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = i $\\

$k = 2 \rightarrow z=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{-\dfrac{\pi}{2} + 2 \cdot 2\pi}{3}\right)  = \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{7\pi}{2}}{3}\right)
= \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{7\pi}{6}\right) = -\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2} - \dfrac{1}{2}i $\\
