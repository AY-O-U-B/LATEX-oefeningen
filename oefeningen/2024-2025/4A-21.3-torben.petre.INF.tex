Bereken de volgende onbepaalde integraal, eventueel door gebruik van goniometrische en/of hypergoniometrische formules $\displaystyle\int \sin x\sinh xdx$ \\\\

$u = \sin x, du = \cos xdx, v = \cosh x, dv = \sinh xdx, \displaystyle\int udv = uv - \displaystyle\int vdu$\\

$\displaystyle\int \sin x\sinh xdx = \sin x\cosh x - \left(\displaystyle\int \cosh x\cos xdx\right)$ \\

$u = \cos x, du = -\sin xdx, v = \sinh x, dv = \cosh xdx, \displaystyle\int udv = uv - \displaystyle\int vdu$\\

$\displaystyle\int \sin x\sinh xdx = \sin x\cosh x - \left(\cos x\sinh x - \displaystyle\int \sin x\sinh xdx\right)$ \\

$2 \displaystyle\int \sin x\sinh xdx = \sin x\cosh x - \cos x\sinh x + C$ \\

$\displaystyle\int \sin x\sinh xdx = \dfrac{\sin x\cosh x - \cos x\sinh x}{2} + C$ \\
