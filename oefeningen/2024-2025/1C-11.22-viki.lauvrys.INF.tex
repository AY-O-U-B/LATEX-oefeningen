$ z = \dfrac{1}{2} - i \dfrac{\sqrt{-3}}{2} $ \\

modulus: \\

$ |z| = \sqrt{\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^2 + \left(\dfrac{\sqrt{-3}}{2}\right)^2} $ \\

$|z| = \sqrt{1} = 1$\\

    
Argument: \\

$ \arg(z) = \operatorname{Bgtan} \left(\dfrac{-\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}}{\dfrac{1}{2}}\right) $\\
	
$ \arg(z) = \operatorname{Bgtan} \left(-\sqrt{3}\right) $\\
	
$ \arg(z) = -\dfrac{\pi}{3} $\\
	
	
Goniometrische vorm: \\

$ z = |z| \cdot \operatorname{cis}(\arg(z))$\\
	
$ z = \operatorname{cis}\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{3}\right)$\\
