$w = \ln(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)$, met $x = s - t$, $y = s + t$, $z = 2\sqrt{st}$\\

\textbf{Stap 1: Afgeleiden van $w$}\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x} = \dfrac{2x}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y} = \dfrac{2y}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z} = \dfrac{2z}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$\\

\textbf{Stap 2: Afgeleiden van $x$, $y$ en $z$}\\

$x = s - t \Rightarrow \dfrac{\partial x}{\partial s} = 1$ , $\dfrac{\partial x}{\partial t} = -1$\\

$y = s + t \Rightarrow \dfrac{\partial y}{\partial s} = 1$ , $\dfrac{\partial y}{\partial t} = 1$\\

$z = \sqrt{st} \Rightarrow \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial s} = \dfrac{t}{\sqrt{st}}$ , $\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial t} = \dfrac{s}{\sqrt{st}}$ \\


\textbf{Stap 3: Toepassen van de kettingregel voor $\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s}$:}\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s} = \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x} \cdot \dfrac{\partial x}{\partial s} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y} \cdot \dfrac{\partial y}{\partial s} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z} \cdot \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial s}$\\

$= \dfrac{2x}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \cdot 1 + \dfrac{2y}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \cdot 1 + \dfrac{2z}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \cdot \dfrac{t}{\sqrt{st}}$\\

$= \dfrac{2x + 2y + 2z \cdot \dfrac{t}{\sqrt{st}}}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$\\


\textbf{Stap 4: Toepassen van de kettingregel voor $\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t}$:}\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} = \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x} \cdot \dfrac{\partial x}{\partial t} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y} \cdot \dfrac{\partial y}{\partial t} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z} \cdot \dfrac{\partial z}{\partial t}$\\

$= \dfrac{2x}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \cdot (-1) + \dfrac{2y}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \cdot 1 + \dfrac{2z}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \cdot \dfrac{s}{\sqrt{st}}$\\

$= \dfrac{-2x + 2y + 2z \cdot \dfrac{s}{\sqrt{st}}}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$\\


\textbf{Stap 5: Invullen van $x$, $y$ en $z$:}\\

Teller van $\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s}$:\\

$2x+2y+2z \cdot \dfrac{t}{\sqrt{st}} = 2 (s-t) + 2(s+t) + 2 \cdot 2 \sqrt{st} \cdot \dfrac{t}{\sqrt{st}}$ \\

$= 4s + 4t$ \\

Teller van $\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t}$:\\

$-2x+2y+2z \cdot \dfrac{s}{\sqrt{st}} = -2 (s-t) + 2(s+t) + 2 \cdot 2 \sqrt{st} \cdot \dfrac{s}{\sqrt{st}}$ \\

$= 4s + 4t$ \\


Noemer:\\

$x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = (s-t)^2 + (s+t)^2 + (2\sqrt{st})^2$ \\

$= 2s^2 + 4st + 2t^2 = 2 (s+t)^2$ \\

\textbf{Stap 6: Einduitkomst:}\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s} = \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} = \dfrac{4s + 4t}{2 (s+t)^2} =  \dfrac{2(s + t)}{(s+t)^2} = \dfrac{2}{s + t}$\\
