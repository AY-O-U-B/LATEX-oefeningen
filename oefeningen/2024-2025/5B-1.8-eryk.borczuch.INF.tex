$\displaystyle\int_{1}^{+\infty} \dfrac{1}{x} dx  $\\

$= \lim\limits_{a \to +\infty} \displaystyle\int_{1}^{a} \dfrac{1}{x} dx$\\

$= \lim\limits_{a \to +\infty} [\ln |x|]_{1}^{a}$\\

$= \ln|+\infty| - ln|1| $\\

$= +\infty$\\
