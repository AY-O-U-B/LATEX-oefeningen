$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{5 dx}{(4x^2 + 1)^2} $ \\

Stel  
$
\left\{
   \begin{array}{l}
       t = 2x \\
       dt = 2 dx
   \end{array}
\right.
$ \\

$ \Rightarrow \dfrac{5}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{(t^2 + 1)^2} $ \\

$ = \dfrac{5}{2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} \left( \operatorname{Bgtan} (t) + \dfrac{t^2}{t^2 + 1} \right) + C $ \\

$ = \dfrac{5}{4} \operatorname{Bgtan} (2x) + \dfrac{10x}{4((2x)^2 + 1)} + C $ \\
