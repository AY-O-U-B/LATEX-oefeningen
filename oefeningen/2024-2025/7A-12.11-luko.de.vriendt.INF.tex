$f(x,y,z) = e^x + yz \hspace{1cm} \vec{a}(1,1,1) \hspace{1cm} \vec{h}(1,-1,1)$ \\

$Df(x,y,z) =
\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
	e^x & z & y
\end{array}
\right)$ \\

$Df(1,1,1)=
\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
	e & 1 & 1
\end{array}
\right)$ \\

$\Rightarrow Df(\vec{a},\vec{h}) = Df(\vec{a})\vec{h} =
\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
	e & 1 & 1
\end{array}
\right)
\left(
\begin{array}{c}
	1 \\
    -1 \\
    1
\end{array}
\right) = e \cancel{- 1} \cancel{+ 1} = e$ \\
