$y^2 = x^2 - x^4$ \\

$\Rightarrow 4 \displaystyle\int_{0}^{1} \sqrt{x^2-x^4}dx = 4 \displaystyle\int_{0}^{1} x \sqrt{1-x^2}dx$ \\

$u = 1-x^2$ \\

$du = -2xdx$ \\

$= 4 \displaystyle\int_{0}^{1}\dfrac{-1}{2} \sqrt{u}du = 2 \displaystyle\int_{0}^{1} x \sqrt{u}du$ \\

$= 2 \left[ \dfrac{2u^{3/2}}{3} \right]_{0}^{1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{4 \cdot 1}{3} - \dfrac{4 \cdot 0}{3}$ \\

$= \dfrac{4}{3}$ \\
