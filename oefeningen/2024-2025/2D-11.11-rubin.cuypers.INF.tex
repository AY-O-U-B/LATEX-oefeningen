$\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow8}\left(\dfrac{2-\sqrt[3]{x}}{x-8}\right)=\dfrac{0}{0}$\\

$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{x\rightarrow8}\left(\dfrac{2-\sqrt[3]{x}}{x-8}\right)= \lim\limits_{x\rightarrow8} -\dfrac{2-\sqrt[3]{x}}{8-x}$\\

$=-\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow8} \dfrac{\cancel{2-\sqrt[3]{x}}}{\cancel{(2-\sqrt[3]{x})}(4+2\sqrt[3]{x}+\sqrt[3]{x}^2)}$\\

$=-\dfrac{1}{12}$\\
