Bereken: $\forall x \ge 0: \Bgsin x + \Bgcos x$\\

$\Bgsin x + \Bgcos x = \Bgsin(\sin \alpha) + \Bgcos(\sin \alpha)$ \\

met $\sin \alpha = x$ en $\alpha \in \left[ \dfrac{-\pi}{2}, \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right]$ \\

$= \alpha + \Bgcos(\sin \alpha) = \alpha + \dfrac{\pi}{2} - \alpha = \dfrac{\pi}{2}$\\

want $\dfrac{\pi}{2} \in \left[0, \dfrac{\pi}{2}\right]$ en $\cos\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}-\alpha\right) = \sin \alpha \Rightarrow \Bgcos(\sin \alpha) = \dfrac{\pi}{2} - \alpha$\\
