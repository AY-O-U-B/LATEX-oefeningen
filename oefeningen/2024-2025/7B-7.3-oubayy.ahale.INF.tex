$Df =\left( \begin{array}{cc}
	y \cos(xy) & x \cos(xy) \\
	y \cosh(xy) & x \cosh(xy)
\end{array}\right)$\\

$\det Df = (y \cos(xy))(x \cosh(xy)) - (x \cos(xy))(y \cosh(xy))$\\

$ = xy\cos(xy)\cosh(xy) - xy\cos(xy)\cosh(xy)$\\

$ = xy\cos(xy)\cosh(xy)(1-1) $\\

$ = 0$\\
