$= \integral\dfrac{x^2 + 1 - 1}{x^2 + 1}dx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{x^2 + 1}{x^2 + 1}dx - \integral\dfrac{1}{x^2 + 1}dx$ \\

$= \integral dx - \integral\dfrac{1}{x^2 + 1}dx$ \\
