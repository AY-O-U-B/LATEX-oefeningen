$\log_3 (x-3) = \dfrac{1}{2 \log_2 3} + \log_{81} (3x-13)^2$  \\

$\Rightarrow \log_3 (x-3) = \dfrac{\log_3 2}{2} + \dfrac{\log_3 (3x-13)^2}{\log_3 81}$  \\

$\Rightarrow \log_3 (x-3) = \dfrac{\log_3 2}{2} + \dfrac{2 \log_3 (3x-13)}{4}$  \\

$\Rightarrow \log_3 (x-3) = \dfrac{\log_3 2}{2} + \dfrac{\log_3 (3x-13)}{2}$  \\

$\Rightarrow \log_3 (x-3) = \dfrac{\log_3 2 + \log_3 (3x-13)}{2}$  \\

$\Rightarrow \log_3 (x-3) = \dfrac{\log_3 \left( 2(3x-13) \right)}{2}$  \\

$\Rightarrow 2 \log_3 (x-3) = \log_3 (6x-26)$  \\

$\Rightarrow \log_3 (x-3)^2 = \log_3 (6x-26)$  \\

$\Rightarrow (x-3)^2 = 6x - 26$  \\

$\Rightarrow x^2 - 6x + 9 = 6x - 26$  \\

$\Rightarrow x^2 - 12x + 35 = 0$  \\

$\Delta = 144 - 4 \cdot 35 = 4$  \\

$x_{1,2} = \dfrac{12 \pm 2}{2}$  \\
