$a\left(2iz - 3 + i\right) + b\left(z + 1 + i\right) = 5$\\


$\Rightarrow 2iaz - 3a + ai + bz + b + bi = 5$\\

$\Rightarrow\left\{
    \begin{array}{lll}
        (2i\,a + b)\,z           & = & 0,\\
        -3a + ai + b + bi        & = & 5
    \end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow\left\{
    \begin{array}{lll}
        b                      & = & -2i\,a,\\
        -a - a\,i              & = & 5
    \end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow\left\{
    \begin{array}{lll}
        a                      & = & -\dfrac{5}{1+i} = -\dfrac{5(1-i)}{2},\\
        b                      & = & -2i\left(-\dfrac{5}{1+i}\right) = 5 + 5i
    \end{array}
\right.$
