$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{4x^2 + 1}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{(2x)^2 + 1}$ \\

Stel $u = 2x \Rightarrow du = 2 dx \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{2} du = dx $\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{du}{u^2 + 1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \operatorname{Bgtan}(u) + C = \dfrac{1}{2} \operatorname{Bgtan}(2x) + C$ \\
