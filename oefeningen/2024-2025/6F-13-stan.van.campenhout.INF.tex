$ f_n : [1, +\infty[ \rightarrow \mathbb{R}  : x \rightarrow \dfrac{x^n}{x^n + 1} $\\

$ \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\dfrac{x^n}{x^n + 1} = 
\left\{
\begin{array}{lll}
1 & \text{als} & x > 1 \\
\dfrac{1}{2} & \text{als} & x = 1
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$ f : [1, +\infty[ \rightarrow \mathbb{R} : x \rightarrow 
\left\{
\begin{array}{lll}
1 & \text{als} & x > 1 \\
\dfrac{1}{2} & \text{als} & x = 1
\end{array}
\right. \text{en } f_n \stackrel{p}{\rightarrow} f $\\

$ \Rightarrow f_n \stackrel{u}{\not\rightarrow} f $ want $f$ is discontinu \\
