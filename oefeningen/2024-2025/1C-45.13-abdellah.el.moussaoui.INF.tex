Trek de derdemachtswortels in $\mathbb{C}$:\\

$z^3=27\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{5\pi}{6}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_k=3\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{5\pi}{6}+2k\pi}{3}\right),k=0,1,2$\\

$\Rightarrow z_k=3\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{5\pi}{18}+\dfrac{2k\pi}{3}\right),k=0,1,2$\\

$\Rightarrow z_0=3\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{5\pi}{18}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_1=3\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{17\pi}{18}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_2=3\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{29\pi}{18}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow \boxed{z_0=3\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{5\pi}{18}\right),z_1=3\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{17\pi}{18}\right),z_2=3\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{29\pi}{18}\right)}$\\
