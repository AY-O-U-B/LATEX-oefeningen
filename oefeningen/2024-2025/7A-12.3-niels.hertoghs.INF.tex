$f(x,y) = x^3 - x^2y + xy^2 - y^3$ in $ a = (1,-1)$ in de richting $h(2,3)$ \\

$Df(a,h) = \lim\limits_{\lambda\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{f((1,-1) + \lambda (2,3)) - f(1,-1)}{\lambda}$ \\

$ = \lim\limits_{\lambda\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{f(2\lambda + 1,3\lambda -1) - f(1,-1)}{\lambda}$ \\

$f(1,-1) = 4$\\

$f(2\lambda + 1,3\lambda -1) = (2\lambda+1)^3 - (2\lambda+1)^2 (3\lambda-1) + (3\lambda-1)^2 (2\lambda+1)-(3\lambda-1)^3$\\

$ = 13\lambda^3 + 28\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 4$\\

$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{\lambda\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-13\lambda^3 + 28\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 4 -4}{\lambda} = \lim\limits_{\lambda\rightarrow 0} (-13\lambda^2 + 28\lambda - 6) = -6$\\
