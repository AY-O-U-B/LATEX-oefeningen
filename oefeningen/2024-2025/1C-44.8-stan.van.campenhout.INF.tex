$z^2 = 1 + \sqrt{3}i $\\

modulus: $r = \sqrt{1^2 + \sqrt{3}^2} = \sqrt{4} = 2 $\\

argument: $\tan\theta = \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{1} \Rightarrow \theta = \operatorname{Bgtan}\sqrt{3} = \dfrac{\pi}{3} $\\

$ z^2 = 2 \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3} + k \cdot 2\pi\right) \Rightarrow z = \sqrt{2} \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{\pi}{3} + k \cdot 2\pi}{2}\right)
\forall k \in \{0 , 1\} $\\

$ k = 0 \rightarrow \sqrt{2} \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{\pi}{3} + 0 \cdot 2\pi}{2}\right) = \sqrt{2} \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{6}\right) 
= \dfrac{\sqrt{6}}{2} + \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}i $\\

$ k = 1 \rightarrow \sqrt{2} \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{\pi}{3} + 1 \cdot 2\pi}{2}\right) = \sqrt{2} \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{7\pi}{6}\right) 
= -\dfrac{\sqrt{6}}{2} - \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}i $\\
