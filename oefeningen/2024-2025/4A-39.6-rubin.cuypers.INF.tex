$ I=\displaystyle\int \dfrac{4x+7}{(x^2+2x+2)^3} dx$\\

$ = 2\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\cancel{2x+2}}{(x^2+2x+2)^3} \dfrac{1}{\cancel{2x+2}} d(x^2+2x+2) + 3\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{(x^2+2x+2)^3} dx$\\ 

$= \dfrac{-\cancel{2}}{(x^2+2x+2)^2 \cancel{2}}+ 3 \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{(x^2+2x+2)^3} dx$\\

$ z=x+1 $ en $ dx=dz$ en $x^2+2x+2=(x+1)^2 +1$\\

$\Rightarrow   \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{(x^2+2x+2)^3} dx = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{(z^2+1)^3} dz$\\

$= M_3(z)=\dfrac{3}{8}  \operatorname{Bgtan} z+ \dfrac{3z}{8(z^2+1)}+\dfrac{z}{4(z^2+1)^2}+C$\\

$\Rightarrow I=\dfrac{-1}{(x^2+2x+2)^2}+3 M_3(x+1) +C$\\

$=\dfrac{-1}{(x^2+2x+2)^2}+\dfrac{9}{8}  \operatorname{Bgtan} (x+1)+ \dfrac{9(x+1)}{8(x^2+2x+1)}+\dfrac{3(x+1)}{4(x^2+2x+1)^2}+C$\\

$=\dfrac{9}{8}  \operatorname{Bgtan} (x+1) + \dfrac{9(x+1)}{8(x^2+2x+2)} +\dfrac{3x-1}{4(x^2+2x+2)^2}+C$\\
