$\integral\dfrac{\ln^3 x}{x}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $t = \ln x \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{1}{x}dx$ \\

$= \integral t^3dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{t^4}{4} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{\ln^4 x}{4} + c$ \\
