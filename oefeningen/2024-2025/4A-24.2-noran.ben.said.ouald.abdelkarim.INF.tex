$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x} \ln^2 x dx = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\ln^2 x}{x} dx $ \\

Stel $t = \ln x \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{1}{x} dx$\\

$ \Rightarrow \displaystyle\int t^2 dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{t^3}{3} + C $ \\

$ = \dfrac{\ln^3 x}{3} + C $ \\
