$\displaystyle\int \left(x^2 + x - 2\right)e^x dx $\\

$=\left(x^2 + x - 2\right)e^x - \displaystyle\int e^x \left(2x + 1\right) dx $\\

$=\left(x^2 + x - 2\right)e^x - \left(e^x \left(2x + 1\right) - \displaystyle\int e^x \cdot 2 dx\right) $\\

$=\left(x^2 + x - 2\right)e^x - \left(2x + 1\right)e^x + 2e^x + C $\\

$= \left(x^2 - x - 1\right)e^x + C $\\
