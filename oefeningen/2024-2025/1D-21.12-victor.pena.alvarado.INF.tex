	$P(z)=z^4 +3z^3 + 6z^2 + 12z + 8$ \\
	
	$P(-1)= 1 - 3 + 6 - 12 + 8 = 0$\\
	
	$\Rightarrow$ \begin{tabular}{l|llll|l}
		  & 1   & 3   & 6   &12  & 8 \\
		-1&     & -1  & -2  & -4 & -8\\  \cline{1-6}
		  & 1   & 2   & 4   &8   &0
	\end{tabular} $\Rightarrow P(z) = (z+1)(z^3 + 2z^2 + 4z + 8)$\\
	
	$P(-2) = (-2+1)(-8  + 8 - 8 + 8) = (-1)(0) = 0$ \\
	
	$\Rightarrow$ \begin{tabular}{l|lll|l}
		  & 1   & 2   & 4   &8   \\
		-2&     & -2& 0  & -8 \\  \cline{1-5}
		  & 1   & 0   & 4   &0  
	\end{tabular} $\Rightarrow P(z) = (z+1)(z+2)(z^2 + 4)$\\
	
	De wortels van $z^2 + 4$ zijn gegeven door: \\
	
	$z = \dfrac{0 \pm i\sqrt{16-0}}{2} = \dfrac{0 \pm 4i}{2} = \pm 2i$\\
	
	
	Dus, $P(z) = (z+1)(z+2)(z+2i)(z-2i)$, met wortels $-2,-1,2i$ en $-2i$ \\
