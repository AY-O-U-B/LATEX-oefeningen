\normalsize

Opgave: $\dint (2x + 20)^48 dx$\\

$\Rightarrow t = 2x + 20$, $t' = 2 \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{dt}{2}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\dint t^{48} dx = \dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{t^{49}}{49} + c$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{(2x + 20)^{49}}{49} + c$\\
