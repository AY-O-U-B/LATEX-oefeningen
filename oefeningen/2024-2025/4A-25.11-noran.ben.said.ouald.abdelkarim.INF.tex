$ \displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{x + 2}{x + 1}\right) dx $ \\

 
$ \Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{x + 1 + 1}{x + 1}\right) dx$\\

$ \Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \left(1 + \dfrac{1}{x + 1}\right) dx$\\

$ \Rightarrow \displaystyle\int 1 dx + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x + 1} dx $ \\

Stel $t = x + 1 \Rightarrow dt = dx$\\

$ = x + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t} $\\


$=x + \ln |x + 1| + C $
