$2i = (x + yi)^2$ \\

$2i = x^2 - y^2 + 2ixy$ \\

$\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
x^2 - y^2 = 0 \\
2xy = 2
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow x^2 = y^2 \Rightarrow (x - y)(x + y) = 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow x = y \quad \text{of} \quad x = -y$ \\

\vspace{0.3em}

\textbf{Geval 1: } $x = y$ \\

$2xy = 2 \Rightarrow 2x^2 = 2 \Rightarrow x^2 = 1 \Rightarrow x = \pm 1$ \\

$\Rightarrow (x, y) = (1, 1)$ of $(-1, -1)$ \\

\vspace{0.3em}

\textbf{Geval 2: } $x = -y$ \\

$2xy = 2 \Rightarrow 2x(-x) = -2x^2 = 2 \Rightarrow x^2 = -1$ ⟶ $\text{geen reële oplossing}$ \\

\vspace{0.3em}

$\Rightarrow \text{Oplossingen: } x + iy = 1 + i \quad \text{en} \quad -1 - i$ \\

\textbf{Dus:} De vierkantswortels van $2i$ zijn $1 + i$ en $-1 - i$. \\
