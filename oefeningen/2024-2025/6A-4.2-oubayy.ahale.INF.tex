Gegeven: $ x_4 + x_6 = 30$ en $x_9 + x_{11} = 50 $\\

$ x_4 = x_1 + 3v$ en $x_6 = x_1 + 5v             $\\

$ x_9 = x_1 + 8v$ en $x_{11} = x_1 + 10v         $\\

$ x_4 + x_6 = 30 \Leftrightarrow x_1 + 3v + x_1 + 5v = 30 $\\

$ \Leftrightarrow 2x_1 + 8v = 30 \Leftrightarrow x_1 = \dfrac{30 - 8v}{2} = 15 - 4v $\\

$ x_9 + x_{11} = 50 \Leftrightarrow x_1 + 8v + x_1 + 10v = 50 $\\

$ \Leftrightarrow 2x_1 + 18v = 50 \Leftrightarrow x_1 = \dfrac{50 - 18v}{2} = 25 - 9v $\\

$ 15 - 4v = 25 - 9v                              $\\

$ \Leftrightarrow 5v = 10 \Leftrightarrow v = 2  $\\

$ \Rightarrow x_1 = 15 - 4 \cdot 2 = 7           $\\

\textbf{Antwoord: } $\boxed{7}                   $\\
