\normalsize

$\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{n+1}{n^3+3n^2} > \displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n^2}$\\

$\llim_{n\rightarrow \infty}\dfrac{\tfrac{n+1}{n^3+3n^2}}{\tfrac{1}{n^2}} = \llim_{n\rightarrow \infty}\dfrac{n^2(n+1)}{n^3+3n^2} = 1$\\

Verhoudingscriterium:
$\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n^2}$ is convergent, dus $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{n+1}{n^3+3n^2}$ is convergent.\\
