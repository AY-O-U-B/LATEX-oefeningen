$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{-3 + 4x - x^2}}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{-(x^2 - 4x + 3)}}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{-(x^2 - 4x + 4 - 1)}}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{-((x - 2)^2 - 1)}}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{1 - (x - 2)^2}}$ \\

Stel $u = x - 2 \Rightarrow du = dx$\\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{du}{\sqrt{1 - u^2}}$ \\

$= \operatorname{Bgsin} u + C = \operatorname{Bgsin}(x - 2) + C$ \\
