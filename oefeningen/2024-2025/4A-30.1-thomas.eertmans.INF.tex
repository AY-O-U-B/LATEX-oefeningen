$\displaystyle\int x \sqrt{x^2+1} \ dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \left(x^2+1\right)^{\tfrac{1}{2}} \ d(x^2+1)$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{2(x^2+1)^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{3} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{(x^2+1)^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{3} + c$
