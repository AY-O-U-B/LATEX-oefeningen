$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{2x^2 + 2x +1}$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x^2 + x + \dfrac{1}{2}}$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\left(x + \dfrac{1}{2}\right)^2 + \dfrac{1}{4}}$\\

Stel $t = x+\dfrac{1}{2}$ dan $dt = dx$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t^2 + \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^2}$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot 2 \operatorname{Bgtan}\left(\dfrac{x+\dfrac{1}{2}}{\dfrac{1}{2}}\right) + c$\\

$ = \operatorname{Bgtan}\left(2x+1\right) + c $\\
