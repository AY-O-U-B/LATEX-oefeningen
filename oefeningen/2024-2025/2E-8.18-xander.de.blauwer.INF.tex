$2\left(3^x + 2^{x-1}\right) = 3(2^x - 3^{x-1})$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 2\cdot 3^x + 2^x = 3 \cdot 2^x - 3^x$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 2\cdot 3^x + 2^x - 3\cdot 2^x + 3^x = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 3^x(2+1) + 2^x(-3+1) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 3^{x+1} \cdot (-2^{x+1}) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 3^{x+1} = 2^{x+1} (\text{delen door: } 2^x)$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \dfrac{3^{x+1}}{2^x} = 2$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 3 \cdot \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^x = 2$ \\
  
$\Leftrightarrow \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^x = \dfrac{2}{3}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x = -1$
