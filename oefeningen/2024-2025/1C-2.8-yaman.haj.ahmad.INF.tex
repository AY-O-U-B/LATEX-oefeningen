    \begin{array}{l}
        r = \sqrt{(-2\sqrt{3})^2 + (2)^2} = 4\\
        \theta = \operatorname{Bgtan}\left(\dfrac{2}{-2\sqrt{3}}\right) = \dfrac{5\pi}{6}
    \end{array}
\right. \rightarrow \left(4, \dfrac{5\pi}{6}\right)$
