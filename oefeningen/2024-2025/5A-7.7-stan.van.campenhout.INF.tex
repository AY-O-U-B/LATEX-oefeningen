= \displaystyle\int\limits_0^1\dfrac{1}{\left(t+\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^2+\dfrac{3}{4}}dt $\\

$ = \left[\dfrac{2}{\sqrt{3}}\operatorname{Bgtan}\left(\dfrac{2t+1}{\sqrt{3}}\right)\right]_0^1 = \dfrac{2\pi}{3\sqrt{3}} - \dfrac{\pi}{3\sqrt{3}} = \dfrac{\pi}{3\sqrt{3}} $\\
