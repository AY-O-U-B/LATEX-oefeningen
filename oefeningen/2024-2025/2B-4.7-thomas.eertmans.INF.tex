$\tan(2\Bgtan x)$ \\

Stel $\theta = \Bgtan x \Rightarrow \tan \theta = x$ \\

$\tan 2\theta = \dfrac{2 \tan \theta}{1-\tan^2\theta}$ \\

$\Rightarrow \tan(2\Bgtan x) = \dfrac{2x}{1-x^2}$
