$\dfrac{1 - 3i}{1 - i} + \dfrac{i}{2 - i} = \dfrac{(1 - 3i)\cdot(2 - i) + (1 - i)\cdot i}{(1 - i)\cdot(2 - i)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2 + i - 6i - 3 + i + 1}{2 - i - 2i - 1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{-6i}{1 - 3i} = \dfrac{-6i \cdot (1 + 3i)}{1^2 + 3^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{18 - 6i}{10} = \dfrac{9 - 3i}{5}$ \\
