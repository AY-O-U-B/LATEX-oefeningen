Los op: $\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2-x}-\sqrt{x+2}}\right)$\\

$\lim\limits_{x \to 0+}\left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2-x}-\sqrt{x+2}}\right) = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2-0^+}-\sqrt{0^++2}}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2^-}-\sqrt{2^+}} = \dfrac{1}{0^-} = -\infty$\\

en:

$\lim\limits_{x \to 0-}\left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2-x}-\sqrt{x+2}}\right) = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2-0^-}-\sqrt{0^-+2}}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2^+}-\sqrt{2^-}} = \dfrac{1}{0^+} = +\infty$\\
