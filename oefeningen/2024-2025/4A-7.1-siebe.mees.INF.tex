$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sqrt{x}-1}{x} dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sqrt{x}}{x} - \dfrac{1}{x} dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{x} - \dfrac{1}{x} dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int  x^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} - \dfrac{1}{x} dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int  x^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} dx - \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x} dx$\\

$ = 2\sqrt{x} - \ln|x| + C $\\
