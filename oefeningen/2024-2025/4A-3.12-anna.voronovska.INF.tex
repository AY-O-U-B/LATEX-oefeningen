$\displaystyle\int 4x \sqrt[3]{3x} dx 
= \displaystyle\int 4x \cdot (3x)^{\tfrac{1}{3}} dx 
= 4 \cdot \sqrt[3]{3} \displaystyle\int x^{\tfrac{4}{3}} dx 
= 4 \cdot \sqrt[3]{3} \cdot \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{7}{3}}}{\dfrac{7}{3}} + c$\\

$= \dfrac{4 \cdot 3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} \cdot 3 \cdot x^{\tfrac{7}{3}}}{7} + c
= \dfrac{4 \cdot 3^{\tfrac{4}{3}} \cdot x^{\tfrac{7}{3}}}{7} + c
= \dfrac{4 \cdot \sqrt[3]{3^4} \cdot \sqrt[3]{x^7}}{7} + c$\\
