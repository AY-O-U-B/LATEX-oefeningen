$\operatorname{Bgcot}2 = \operatorname{Bgcot}3 + \operatorname{Bgcot}7$  \\

Stel $\alpha = \operatorname{Bgcot} 3 \Rightarrow \cot \alpha = 3 $ \\

Stel $\beta = \operatorname{Bgcot}  7 \Rightarrow \cot \alpha = 7 $ \\

$\Rightarrow \operatorname{Bgcot}3 + \operatorname{Bgcot}7 = \operatorname{Bgcot} \left(\dfrac{\cot \alpha\cot \beta - 1}{\cot \beta + \cot \alpha}\right)$ \\

$= \operatorname{Bgcot} \left(\dfrac{3 \cdot 7 - 1}{10}\right)$ \\ 

$= \operatorname{Bgcot}2$ \\
