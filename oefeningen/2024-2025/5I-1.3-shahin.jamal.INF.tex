$y = x^2-\dfrac{1}{8}\ln x \Rightarrow y' = 2x - \dfrac{1}{8x} \Rightarrow (y')^2= 4x^2 - \dfrac{1}{2} + \dfrac{1}{64x^2}$\\
 
$\Rightarrow 1 + (y')^2 = 1 + 4x^2 - \dfrac{1}{2} + \dfrac{1}{64x^2} = \dfrac{256x^4 + 32x^2 + 1}{64x^2}$\\

$\displaystyle\int\limits_2^3 \sqrt{\dfrac{256x^4 + 32x^2 + 1}{64x^2}}dx = \displaystyle\int\limits_2^3 \sqrt{\dfrac{\left(16x^2+1\right)^2}{64x^2}}dx = \displaystyle\int\limits_2^3 \dfrac{16x^2+1}{8x}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\limits_2^3 \dfrac{16}{8}x + \dfrac{1}{8x}dx = \left[x^2+\dfrac{1}{8} \ln \lvert x\rvert\right]_2^3 = 9 + \dfrac{1}{8}\ln \lvert 3 \rvert - \left(4+\dfrac{1}{8}\ln\lvert 2 \rvert\right)$\\

$= 5 + \dfrac{1}{8}\ln \dfrac{3}{2}$\\
