$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{xdx}{\sqrt{7-x^2}}$\\

Stel $t = 7-x^2 \Rightarrow dt = -2x dx \Rightarrow -\dfrac{1}{2} dt = x dx $\\

$= -\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{\sqrt{t}}$\\

$= -\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}$\\

$= -\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot\dfrac{2\sqrt{t}}{1} + C$\\

$= -\sqrt{7-x^2} + C$\\
