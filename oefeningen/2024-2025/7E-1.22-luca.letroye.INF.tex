$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 3x^2+2y-2 $\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = x+y^2+1 \Rightarrow x = -y^2-1 $\\

$\Rightarrow 3(-y^2-1)^2+2y -2 = 0$\\

$ = 3y^4+6y^2+2y+1 = 0$\\

$ = 3y^4+5y^2 +(y+1)^2= 0$\\
