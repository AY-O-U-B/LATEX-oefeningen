$\dint \left(\dfrac{1}{2}x^5 - \dfrac{1}{3}x^4 + 7x^2 - 1\right) dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \dint x^5 dx - \dfrac{1}{3} \dint x^4 dx + 7 \dint x^2 dx - 1 \dint dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{6}x^6 - \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \dfrac{1}{5}x^5 + 7 \cdot \dfrac{1}{3}x^3 - 1 \cdot x + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{12}x^6 - \dfrac{1}{15}x^5 + \dfrac{7}{3}x^3 - x + C$ \\
