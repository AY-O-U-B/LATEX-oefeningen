$\lim\limits_{x \to 2} \left( \dfrac{3x - 2}{2x} \right)^{\tfrac{1}{x-2}}$  \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 2} \left( \dfrac{x-2 + 2x}{2x} \right)^{\tfrac{1}{x-2}}$  \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 2} \left( 1 + \dfrac{x-2}{2x} \right)^{\tfrac{1}{x-2}}$  \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 2} \left( \left( 1 + \dfrac{x-2}{2x} \right)^{\tfrac{2x}{x-2} \cdot \tfrac{x-2}{2x}} \right)^{\tfrac{1}{x-2}}$  \\

$= e^{\lim\limits_{x \to 2} \tfrac{\tfrac{x-2}{x-2}}{2x}}$  \\

$= e^{\lim\limits_{x \to 2} \tfrac{1}{2x}}$  \\

$= e^{\tfrac{1}{4}}$  \\
