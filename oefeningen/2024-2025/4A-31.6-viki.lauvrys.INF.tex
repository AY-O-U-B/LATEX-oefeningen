$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{(x+1) \sqrt{x}} $ \\

Stel $ t = \sqrt{x} \Rightarrow dx = 2t dt $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2t}{(t^2 +1) t} dt $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2}{t^2 +1} dt $ \\

$ = 2 \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t^2 +1} $ \\

$ = 2 \operatorname{Bgtan} ( t ) + C $ \\

$ = 2 \operatorname{Bgtan} \left( \sqrt{x} \right) + C $ \\
