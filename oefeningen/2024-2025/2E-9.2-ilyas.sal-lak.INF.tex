$4^{x^3+x} = 2^{x^3} \cdot 8^{x^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow \left(2^2\right)^{x^3+x} = 2^{x^3} \cdot \left(2^3\right)^{x^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 2^{2(x^3+x)} = 2^{x^3} \cdot 2^{3x^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 2^{2x^3+2x} = 2^{x^3+3x^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 2x^3 + 2x = x^3 + 3x^2$ \\

$\Rightarrow x^3 - 3x^2 + 2x = 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow x\left(x^2 - 3x + 2\right)$ \\

$\Rightarrow x(x-1)(x-2) = 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow x = 0$ of $x = 1$ of $x = 2$
