$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(\left(x - 7\right) \tan\left(\dfrac{-1}{2x + 3}\right)\right) $\\

$=\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(x - 7\right) \cdot \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(\dfrac{\sin\left(\dfrac{-1}{2x + 3}\right)}{\cos\left(\dfrac{-1}{2x + 3}\right)}\right) \cdot \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(\dfrac{\dfrac{-1}{2x + 3}}{\dfrac{-1}{2x + 3}}\right) $\\

$=\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(x - 7\right) \left(\dfrac{-1}{2x + 3}\right) \cdot \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(\dfrac{\sin\left(\dfrac{-1}{2x + 3}\right)}{\dfrac{-1}{2x + 3}}\right) \cdot \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(\dfrac{1}{\cos\left(\dfrac{-1}{2x + 3}\right)}\right) $\\

$=\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \dfrac{-x + 7}{2x + 3} $\\

$=\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \dfrac{-x}{2x} = \dfrac{-1}{2} $\\
