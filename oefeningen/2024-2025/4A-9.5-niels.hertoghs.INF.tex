	$\displaystyle\int \cos^2\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right) dx$\\
	
	$t = \dfrac{x}{2} \rightarrow 2 dt = dx$\\
	
	$= 2 \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1 + \cos(2t)}{2} dt$\\
	
	$= t + \dfrac{\sin(2t)}{2} + C$\\
	
	$= \dfrac{x + \sin(x)}{2} + C$\\
