$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x^2 + 9} dx $ \\

Stel $t = \dfrac{x}{3} \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{1}{3} dx$\\

$ \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{9} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{t^2 + 1} dx $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{t^2 + 1} dx $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3} \text{Bgtan} t + C $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3} \text{Bgtan} \dfrac{x}{3} + C $ \\
