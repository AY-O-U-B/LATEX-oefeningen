$ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{\cos x}{1-\sin x}dx = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{1-\sin x}d\left(\sin x\right)
\stackrel{\sin x = t}{=} \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{1-t}dt $\\

$ = -\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{1-t}d\left(1-t\right) = -\ln|1-t| + c \stackrel{t = \sin x}{=} -\ln|1-\sin x| + c $\\
