$f(x) = (2x-1)^{\tfrac{1}{2}}$ op $\left[\dfrac{1}{2},4\right]$\\

$f'(x) = 3(2x-1)^{\tfrac{1}{2}}$\\

$L = \dint_{\tfrac{1}{2}}^4\sqrt{1+(3(2x-1)^{\tfrac{1}{2}})^2}dx$\\

$ = \dint_{\tfrac{1}{2}}^4(18x-8)^{\tfrac{1}{2}}dx$\\

$t = 18x- 8 \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{dt}{18}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{18}\dint_{\tfrac{1}{2}}^4 t^{\tfrac{1}{2}} dt = \dfrac{1}{18}\left[\dfrac{2t^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{3}\right]_1^{64} = \dfrac{1}{27}\left[t^{\tfrac{3}{2}}\right]_1^{64}$\\

$= \dfrac{511}{27}$\\
