$\tan(\operatorname{Bgcos}x)$\\

Stel $\operatorname{Bgcos} x = y$ \\

$\Rightarrow \cos y = x$ met $y\in \left[0, \pi \right]$\\ 

$\Rightarrow \tan y = \dfrac{\sin y}{\cos y} = \dfrac{\sqrt{1-\cos^2y}}{\cos y} = \dfrac{\sqrt{1-x^2}}{x}$
