$V=\pi \Int_0^1 (\sqrt{x}^2-x^4)dx = \pi\Int_0^1(x-x^4)dx$\\

$= \pi \left[ \left[\dfrac{x^2}{2}\right]_0^1 -  \left[\dfrac{x^5}{5}\right]_0^1\right] = \pi\left( \dfrac{1}{2} - \dfrac{1}{5} \right) = \dfrac{3\pi}{10}$\\
