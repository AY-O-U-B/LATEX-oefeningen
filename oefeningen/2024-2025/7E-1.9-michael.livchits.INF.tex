$f(x,y)= 10+12x-12y-3x^2-2y^2$\\
	
Eerste orde:\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 12-6x \Rightarrow x = 2$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = -12-4y \Rightarrow x = -3$\\

Kritiek punt: $(2,-3)$\\

Hessian matrix:\\

$\Rightarrow D^2f(x,y,z,t) = \left(\begin{array}{cc}
\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} = -6 & \dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y\partial x} = 0\\
\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x\partial y} = 0 & \dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} = -4
\end{array}\right)$\\

$\det H(x,y) = 24$\\

$H(2,-3) = 24 > 0$ en $\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} = -6 < 0\Rightarrow$ bereikt een maximum\\
