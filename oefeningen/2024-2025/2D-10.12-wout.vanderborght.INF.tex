Bereken de volgende limiet: \\

$ \Limit{x \rightarrow -\tfrac{1}{2}} \dfrac{-6x^2-x+1}{-10x^2-x+2} = \cancel{\dfrac{0}{0}} $ \\

Teller ontbinden: \\

\begin{tabular}{c|cc|c}
    & $-6$ & $-1$ & $1$ \\
    $\dfrac{-1}{2}$ & & $3$ & $-1$ \\ \hline
    & $-6$ & $2$ & $0$
\end{tabular} \\ 

Noemer ontbinden: \\

\begin{tabular}{c|cc|c}
    & $-10$ & $-1$ & $2$ \\
    $\dfrac{-1}{2}$ & & $5$ & $-2$ \\ \hline
    & $-10$ & $4$ & $0$
\end{tabular} \\ 

$ \Leftrightarrow \Limit{x \rightarrow -\tfrac{1}{2}} \dfrac{\cancel{(2x+1)} (-6x+2)}{\cancel{(2x+1)} (-10x+4)} = \dfrac{5}{9} $ \\
