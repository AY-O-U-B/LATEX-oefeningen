$\displaystyle\int\left(\dfrac{5x}{2}\right)dx$\\

Stel $\dfrac{5}{2}x = t\Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{2dt}{5}$\\

$= \dfrac{2}{5}\displaystyle\int(\sin t) dt$\\

$= \dfrac{-2}{5}\cos t + C$\\

$= \dfrac{-2}{5}\cos \dfrac{5x}{2} + C$\\
