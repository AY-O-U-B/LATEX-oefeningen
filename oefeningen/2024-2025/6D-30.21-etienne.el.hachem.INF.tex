Opgave: $\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \dfrac{2n+2}{\sqrt{n^3+5}}$ \\

$\dfrac{2}{\sqrt{n}} \Rightarrow$ divergent want macht van n $<$ 1 (criterium 4) \\

$\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{\dfrac{2n+2}{\sqrt{n^3+5}}}{\dfrac{2}{\sqrt{n}}}$ \\

$ = \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{\dfrac{2}{\sqrt{n}}}{\dfrac{2}{\sqrt{n}}} = 1$ \\

criterium 6: \\

verhoudingsgedrags is hetzelfde $\Rightarrow$ Divergent.
