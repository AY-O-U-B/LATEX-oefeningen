$\displaystyle\int e^x\sqrt{1+e^x}dx$\\

$t=1+e^x \left( \Rightarrow dt=e^x dx \right)$\\

$\displaystyle\int \sqrt{t}dt$\\

$=\displaystyle\int t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}dt$\\

$=\dfrac{t^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{\tfrac{3}{2}}+C$\\

$=\dfrac{2}{3}t^{\tfrac{3}{2}}+C$\\

$ 1+e^x=t$\\ 

$=\dfrac{2}{3}(1+e^x)^{\tfrac{3}{2}}+C$\\
