$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{x^2 \sqrt{x - 2}}{\sqrt[3]{x - 2}}  dx = \int x^2 \sqrt[6]{x - 2}  dx$ \\

Stel $u = x - 2 \Rightarrow du = dx$\\

$x^2 = x \cdot x = (u + 2)^2$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int (u + 2)^2 \cdot u^{\tfrac{1}{6}}  du = \int (u^2 + 4u + 4) u^{\tfrac{1}{6}}  du$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int u^{\tfrac{13}{6}}  du + 4 \int u^{\tfrac{7}{6}}  du + 4 \int u^{\tfrac{1}{6}}  du$ \\

$= \dfrac{6}{19} u^{\tfrac{19}{6}} + 4 \cdot \dfrac{6}{13} u^{\tfrac{13}{6}} + 4 \cdot \dfrac{6}{7} u^{\tfrac{7}{6}} + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{6}{19} (x - 2)^{\tfrac{19}{6}} + \dfrac{24}{13} (x - 2)^{\tfrac{13}{6}} + \dfrac{24}{7} (x - 2)^{\tfrac{7}{6}} + C$ \\
