$3^{x+4} \cdot 2^x = 0.0625 = \dfrac{1}{16}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 3^{x+4} \cdot 2^x = \dfrac{1}{2^4} = 2^{-4}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 3^x \cdot 3^4 \cdot 2^x = 2^{-4}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 3^x \cdot 81 \cdot 2^x = 2^{-4}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 81 \cdot (3 \cdot 2)^x = 2^{-4}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 81 \cdot 6^x = 2^{-4}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 6^x = \dfrac{2^{-4}}{81} = \dfrac{1}{16 \cdot 81} = \dfrac{1}{1296}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 6^x = 6^{-4}$ \\

$\Rightarrow x = -4$ \\
