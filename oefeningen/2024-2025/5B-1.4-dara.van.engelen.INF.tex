$ I = \displaystyle\int_1^{+\infty} x^{-\tfrac{2}{3}} dx $\\
	
$\displaystyle\int x^{-\tfrac{2}{3}}dx = \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{1}{3}}}{\dfrac{1}{3}} = 3x^{\tfrac{1}{3}} + C = 3\sqrt[3]{x} + C$\\

	
$ I = \lim\limits_{b\rightarrow +\infty} \left[ 3\sqrt[3]{x} \right]_1^b = \lim\limits_{b\rightarrow +\infty} \left( 3\sqrt[3]{b} -3\right) = +\infty \Rightarrow$ Divergent\\
