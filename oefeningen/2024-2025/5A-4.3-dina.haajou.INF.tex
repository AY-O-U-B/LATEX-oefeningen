$\displaystyle \int_{-2}^{4} x^3 dx$ \\

$F(x) = \dfrac{1}{4}x^4$ \\

$\left[ \dfrac{1}{4}x^4 \right]_{-2}^{4}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot 4^4 - \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot (-2)^4$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot 256 - \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot 16$ \\

$= 64 - 4$ \\

$= 60$ \\

$\displaystyle \int_{-2}^{4} x^3 dx = 60$ \\
