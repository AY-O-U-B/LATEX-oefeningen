\normalsize
$z = -3i$\\

\medskip

\textbf{Modulus:}
\[
r = \sqrt{0^2 + (-3)^2} = \sqrt{9} = 3
\]

\medskip

\noindent \textbf{Argument:}
\[
\tan \alpha = \frac{-3}{0}
\]

Omdat de noemer 0 is, kiezen we:
\[
\alpha = \frac{3\pi}{2}
\]

\medskip

\textbf{Goniometrische vorm:}
\[
z = 3 \operatorname{cis} \frac{3\pi}{2}
\]
