Opgave: $z + |z| = 5(5 + i)$\\

$\Rightarrow z = (x + yi) \Rightarrow (x + yi) + \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} = 5(5 + i)$\\

$\Rightarrow \left(x + \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}\right) + yi = 25 + 5i$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
	\begin{array}{l}
		x + \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} = 25 \\
		y = 5
	\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow x + \sqrt{x^2 + 25} = 25 \Rightarrow x^2 + 25 = (25 - x)^2$\\

$\Rightarrow x^2 + 25 = 25^2 + x^2 - 2 \cdot 25 \cdot x$\\

$\Rightarrow x^2 - x^2 + 50x = 25^2 - 25$\\

$\Rightarrow 50x = 625 - 25 \Rightarrow x = \dfrac{600}{50} = \dfrac{60}{5} = 12$\\

$z = 12 + 5i$\\
