$\displaystyle\int (t-2+3)t^{\tfrac{1}{3}}dt$\\

$=\displaystyle\int (t+1)t^{\tfrac{1}{3}}dt$\\

$=\displaystyle\int \left(t^{\tfrac{4}{3}}+t^{\tfrac{1}{3}}\right)dt$\\

$=\dfrac{t^{\tfrac{7}{3}}}{\tfrac{7}{3}}+\dfrac{t^{\tfrac{4}{3}}}{\tfrac{4}{3}}+C$\\

$=\dfrac{3}{7}t^{\tfrac{7}{3}}+\dfrac{3}{4}t^{\tfrac{4}{3}}+C$\\

$x+2=t$\\

$=\dfrac{3}{7}(x+2)^{\tfrac{7}{3}}+\dfrac{3}{4}(x+2)^{\tfrac{4}{3}}+C$\\
