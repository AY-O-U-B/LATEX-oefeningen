$f(x) = (x^2 - 1)^4$ \\

$f'(x) = 4(x^2 - 1)^3\cdot\dfrac{d}{dx}(x^2 - 1)$ \\

$= 4(x^2 - 1)^3\cdot2x$ \\

$= 8x(x^2 - 1)^3$ \\

$f''(x) = \dfrac{d}{dx}(8x)\cdot(x^2 - 1)^3 + 8x\cdot\dfrac{d}{dx}\left((x^2 - 1)^3\right)$ \\

$= 8(x^2 - 1)^3 + 8x\cdot3(x^2 - 1)^2\cdot2x$ \\

$= 8(x^2 - 1)^3 + 48x^2(x^2 - 1)^2$ \\

$= 8(x^6 - 3x^4 + 3x^2 - 1) + 48x^2(x^4 - 2x^2 + 1)$ \\

$= 56x^6 -120x^4 + 72x^2 - 8$ \\

$f'''(x) = \dfrac{d}{dx}(56x^6) - \dfrac{d}{dx}(120x^4) + \dfrac{d}{dx}(72x^2) - \dfrac{d}{dx}(8)$ \\

$= 336x^5 - 480x^3 + 144x$ \\
