$w = \ln(u+v+z)$ met $u = \cos^2t$, $v = \sin^2t$ en $z = t^2$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial u} = \dfrac{1}{u+v+z}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial v} = \dfrac{1}{u+v+z}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z} = \dfrac{1}{u+v+z}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial u}{\partial t} = -2\cos t\sin t$\\

$\dfrac{\partial v}{\partial t} = 2\sin t\cos t$\\

$\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial t} = 2t$\\

$Dw(u,v,w)=\left(\begin{array}{ccc}
\dfrac{1}{u+v+z} & \dfrac{1}{u+v+z} & \dfrac{1}{u+v+z}
\end{array}\right)$\\

$D(u,v,z)(t)=\left(\begin{array}{c}
-2\cos t\sin t \\
2\sin t\cos t \\
2t
\end{array}\right)$\\

$Dw(u,v,z)\cdot D(u,v,z)(t) = \dfrac{2t}{u+v+z} = \dfrac{2t}{1+t^2}$\\
