$\displaystyle\int\limits_{1}^{2} \dfrac{dx}{x^2} = \int\limits_{1}^{2} x^{-2}dx $ \\

$ = \left[\dfrac{x^{-1}}{-1}\right]_{1}^{2} $ \\

$ = \left[-\dfrac{1}{x}\right]_{1}^{2} $ \\

$ = -\dfrac{1}{2} - (-1) $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} $ \\
