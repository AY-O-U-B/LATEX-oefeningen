Opgave: $\llim_{x \to 0} \dfrac{\sqrt[5]{1 + x} - \sqrt{1 + x}}{2x}$\\

$\Rightarrow y = 1 + x \overset{x \to 0}{\Rightarrow} y \to 1$\\

$\Rightarrow \llim_{y \to 1} \dfrac{y^{\tfrac{1}{5}} - y^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{2(y - 1)}$\\

$\Rightarrow z = y^{\tfrac{1}{10}} \overset{y \to 1}{\Rightarrow}z \to 1$\\

$\Rightarrow \llim_{z \to 1} \dfrac{z^2 - z^5}{2\left(z^{10} - 1\right)} = \llim_{z \to 1} \dfrac{z^2\left(1 - z^3\right)}{2\left(\left(z^5\right)^2 - 1^2\right)} = \llim_{z \to 1} \dfrac{z^2\left(1 - z^3\right)}{2\left(z^5 - 1\right)\left(z^5 + 1\right)}$\\

$1 - z^3 \Rightarrow $\\

\begin{tabular}{c|cccc}
    & $-1$         & $0$  & $0$                         & $1$  \\
$1$ & $\downarrow$ & $-1$ & $-1$                        & $-1$ \\ \hline
    & $-1$         & $-1$ & $\multicolumn{1}{c|}{-1}$ & $0$

$z^5 - 1 \Rightarrow $\\

\begin{tabular}{c|cccccc}
    & $1$          & $0$ & $0$ & $0$ & $0$                      & $-1$ \\
$1$ & $\downarrow$ & $1$ & $1$ & $1$ & $1$                      & $1$ \\ \hline
    & $1$          & $1$ & $1$ & $1$ & $\multicolumn{1}{c|}{1}$ & $0$
\end{tabular}\\

$\Rightarrow \llim_{z \to 1} \dfrac{z^2(z - 1)\left(-z^2 - z - 1\right)}{2(z - 1)\left(z^4 + z^3 + z^2 + z + 1\right)\left(z^5 + 1\right)}$\\

$= \llim_{z \to 1} \dfrac{z^2\left(-z^2 - z - 1\right)}{2\left(z^4 + z^3 + z^2 + z + 1\right)\left(z^5 + 1\right)} = \dfrac{-3}{20}$\\
