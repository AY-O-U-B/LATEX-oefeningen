Opgave: $\dint \dfrac{2dx}{2x - 4}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dint \dfrac{2dx}{2x - 4} = \dint \dfrac{dx}{x - 2}$\\

$\Rightarrow t = x - 2 \Rightarrow dx = dt $\\

$= \dint \dfrac{dt}{t} = \ln |t| + c = \ln |x-2| + c$\\
