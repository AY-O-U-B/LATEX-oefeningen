$z^4 = 16 = 16\cdot\cis(k\cdot2\pi)$ met $k \in \Z$ \\

Dan zijn de vierdemachtswortels:\\

$z_0 = 2 \cdot\cis\left(\dfrac{0\cdot2\pi}{4}\right) = 2$\\

$z_1 = 2 \cdot\cis\left(\dfrac{1\cdot 2\pi}{4}\right) = 2i$\\

$z_2 = 2 \cdot\cis\left(\dfrac{2\cdot 2\pi}{4}\right) = -2$\\

$z_3 = 2 \cdot\cis\left(\dfrac{3\cdot 2\pi}{4}\right) = -2i$\\
