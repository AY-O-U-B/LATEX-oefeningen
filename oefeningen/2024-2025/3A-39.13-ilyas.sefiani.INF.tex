$\dfrac{df}{dx}(\operatorname{Bgsin}{(x^{2x})})$\\

$=\dfrac{(x^{2x})^{'}}{\sqrt{1-x^{4x}}}$\\

$=\dfrac{2x(x^{2x-1})x'+x^{2x}(2x)'\ln(x)}{\sqrt{1-x^{4x}}}$\\

$=\dfrac{2x(x^{2x-1})+2x^{2x}\ln(x)}{\sqrt{1-x^{4x}}}$\\

$=\dfrac{2x^{2x}+2x^{2x}\ln(x)}{\sqrt{1-x^{4x}}}$\\

$=\dfrac{2x^{2x}(1+\ln(x))}{\sqrt{1-x^{4x}}}$\\
