$x_n = \sqrt{n+1} - \sqrt{n}$ \\

$\lim\limits_{n \to \infty} \sqrt{n+1} - \sqrt{n}
= \lim\limits_{\to +\infty} \dfrac{n+1 - n}{\sqrt{n+1} + \sqrt{n}}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\infty} = 0$\\

$\underset{n \to \infty}{\operatorname{adh}} x_n = \{0\}$ \\

$\limsup\limits_{n \to +\infty} x_n = 0 $\\

$\liminf\limits_{n \to +\infty} x_n = 0$ \\
