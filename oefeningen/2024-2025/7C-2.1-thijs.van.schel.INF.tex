1ste partiĂ«le afgeleiden:\\

$\left\{ 
\begin{array}{l} 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x,y) = y^2 \cos(xy^2) \\ 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(x,y) = 2xy \cos(xy^2) 
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

2de partiĂ«le afgeleiden:\\

$\left\{ 
\begin{array}{l} 
\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2}(x,y) = -y^4 \sin(xy^2) \\ 
\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2}(x,y) = 2x \cos(xy^2) - 4x^2y^2 \sin(xy^2) \\
\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y \partial x}(x,y) = 2y \cos(xy^2) - 2xy^3 \sin(xy^2) \\
\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x \partial y}(x,y) = 2y \cos(xy^2) - 2xy^3 \sin(xy^2)
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

$D^2f(x,y) = \left(\begin{array}{c c} -y^4 \sin(xy^2) & 2y \cos(xy^2) - 2xy^3 \sin(xy^2) \\ 2y \cos(xy^2) - 2xy^3 \sin(xy^2) & 2x \cos(xy^2) - 4x^2y^2 \sin(xy^2) \end{array}\right)$ \\
