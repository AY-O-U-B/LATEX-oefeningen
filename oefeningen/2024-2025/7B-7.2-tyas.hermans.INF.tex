$f:\Omega\subseteq\mathbb{R}^2\rightarrow\mathbb{R}^2: (x,y) \rightarrow \left(e^{xy}, \ln\dfrac{x}{y}\right)$\\

$Df(x,y) = \left(
\begin{array}{cc}
ye^{xy} & xe^xy\\
\dfrac{1}{x} & \dfrac{-1}{y}
\end{array}
\right)$\\

$\det( Df(x,y)) = -2e^{xy} \neq 0$\\

$Df^-1(x,y) = \dfrac{1}{-2e^{xy}}\cdot \left(
\begin{array}{cc}
\dfrac{-1}{y}  & -xe^{xy}\\
\dfrac{-1}{x} & ye^{xy}
\end{array}
\right) = \left(
\begin{array}{cc}
\dfrac{1}{2ye^{xy}}  & \dfrac{x}{2}\\
\dfrac{1}{2xe^{xy}} & \dfrac{-y}{2}
\end{array}
\right)$\\
