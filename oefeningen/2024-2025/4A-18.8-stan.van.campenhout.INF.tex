$ \displaystyle\int x \sin^2 x dx = \displaystyle\int x \cdot \dfrac{1 - \cos 2x}{2} dx
= \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int x - x\cos 2x dx $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int x dx - \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int x\cos 2x dx 
= \dfrac{x^2}{4} - \dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int x\cos 2x d\left(2x\right) $\\

$ = \dfrac{x^2}{4} - \dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int x d\left(\sin 2x\right) 
= \dfrac{x^2}{4} - \dfrac{1}{4}x\sin 2x + \dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int \sin2x dx $\\

$ = \dfrac{x^2}{4} - \dfrac{1}{4}x\sin 2x + \dfrac{1}{8}\displaystyle\int \sin2x d\left(2x\right) $\\

$  = \dfrac{x^2}{4} - \dfrac{1}{4}x\sin 2x - \dfrac{1}{8}\cos2x +c $\\
