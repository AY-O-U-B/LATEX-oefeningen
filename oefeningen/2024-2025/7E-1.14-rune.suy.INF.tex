$f(x,y) = x^3 + y^3 + 3xy + 3$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 3x^2 + 3y$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 3y^2 + 3x$\\

$\left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        3x^2 + 3y = 0\\
        3y^2 + 3x = 0
    \end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Leftrightarrow -x^2 = y$\\

$\Leftrightarrow 3x^4+3x=0$\\

$\Leftrightarrow x(x-1)(3x^2 - 3x + 3)$\\

$x = 0 \Rightarrow y=0$\\

of\\

$x=-1 \Rightarrow y=-1$\\

$H(x,y) = \det \left(\begin{array}{cc}
    6x & 3\\
    3 & 6y
\end{array}\right) = 36xy-9$\\

$H(0,0) = -9 < 0$ dus $(0,0)$ is een zadelpunt\\

$H(-1,-1) = 27 > 0$ en $\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2}, \dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} < 0$ dus $(-1,-1)$ is een maximum\\
