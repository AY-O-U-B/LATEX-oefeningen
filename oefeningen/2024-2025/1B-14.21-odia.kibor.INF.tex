$\dfrac{1}{\cos x + i\sin x}$

$= \dfrac{1}{\cos x + i\sin x} \cdot \dfrac{\cos x - i\sin x}{\cos x - i\sin x}$

$= \dfrac{\cos x - i\sin x}{\cos^2 x + \sin^2 x}$

$= \cos x - i\sin x$
