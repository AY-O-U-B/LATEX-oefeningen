$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \dfrac{\partial (e^x)}{\partial x} (\cos y - \sin y) + e^x \left(\dfrac{\partial (\cos y - \sin y)}{\partial x}\right)$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = e^x (\cos y - \sin y)$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \dfrac{\partial (e^x)}{\partial y} (\cos y - \sin y) + e^x \left(\dfrac{\partial (\cos y - \sin y)}{\partial y}\right)$\\

$\Leftrightarrow\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = -e^x (\sin y + \cos y)$\\
