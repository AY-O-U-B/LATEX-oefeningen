$f(x) = \lfloor x \rfloor - 4$ \\

$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0-} (\lfloor x \rfloor - 4) = -1 - 4 = -5$ \\

$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0+} (\lfloor x \rfloor - 4) = 0 - 4 = -4$ \\

Limiet bestaat niet. \\

$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 4-} (\lfloor x \rfloor - 4) = 3 - 4 = -1$ \\

$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 4+} (\lfloor x \rfloor - 4) = 4 - 4 = 0$ \\

Limiet bestaat niet. \\

$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{1}{2}} (\lfloor x \rfloor - 4) = 0 - 4 = -4$ \\

Limiet: $-4$ \\

$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \sqrt{2}} (\lfloor x \rfloor - 4) = 1 - 4 = -3$ \\

Limiet: $-3$ \\

$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow -\tfrac{5}{4}} (\lfloor x \rfloor - 4) = -2 - 4 = -6$ \\

Limiet: $-6$ \\

$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow +\infty} (\lfloor x \rfloor - 4) \geq +\infty - 5 = +\infty $ \\

Limiet: $+\infty$ \\

Want: $x - 5 < \lfloor x \rfloor - 4 \leq x - 4$ \\
