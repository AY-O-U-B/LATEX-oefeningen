$ \displaystyle\int(x^2+1)(x+1)^5 dx $ \\

Stel $u = (x+1) \Rightarrow du = dx $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int((u-1)^2+1)u^5 du $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int(u^2-2u+2)u^5 du$ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int u^7du -2\displaystyle\int u^6du + 2\displaystyle\int u^5du $ \\

$ = \dfrac{u^8}{8} -2\dfrac{u^7}{7} + 2\dfrac{u^6}{6}+ C $ \\

$ = \dfrac{(x+1)^8}{8} -2\dfrac{(x+1)^7}{7} + \dfrac{(x+1)^6}{3}+ C$
