$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\tan(3x+5)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{d(3x+5)}{\tan(3x+5)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\cos(3x+5)}{\sin(3x+5)} d(3x+5)$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{d(\sin(3x+5))}{\sin(3x+5)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \ln |\sin(3x+5)| + c$
