$\dint e^x \sqrt[3]{e^x-1} dx$ \\

$t = e^x-1 \Rightarrow dt = e^x dx$ \\

$= \dint \sqrt[3]{t} dt$ \\

$= \dint t^{\tfrac{1}{3}} dt$ \\

$= \dfrac{3}{4}t^{\tfrac{4}{3}} + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{3}{4}t \cdot t^{\tfrac{1}{3}} + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{3}{4}(e^x-1) \cdot \sqrt[3]{e^x-1} + C$ \\
