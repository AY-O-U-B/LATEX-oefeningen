$w = yz+zx+xy$\\

$x = s^2-t^2 \Rightarrow \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x} = z+y$\\

$y = s^2+t^2 \Rightarrow \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y} = z+x$\\

$z = s^2t^2 \Rightarrow \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z} = y+x$\\

$\dfrac{\partial x}{\partial s} = 2s$\\

$\dfrac{\partial y}{\partial s} = 2s$\\

$\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial s} = 2st^2$\\

$\dfrac{\partial x}{\partial t} = -2t$\\

$\dfrac{\partial y}{\partial t} = 2t$\\

$\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial t} = 2ts^2$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s} = (z+y)(2s)+(z+x)(2s)+(y+x)(2st^2)$\\

$ = (s^2t^2+s^2+t^2)(2s)+(s^2t^2+s^2-t^2)(2s)+(2s^2)(2st^2)$\\

$ = 2s^3t^2+2s^3+2st^2+2s^3t^2+2s^3-2st^2+4s^3t^2$\\

$= 8s^3t^2+4s^3$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} = (s^2t^2+s^2+t^2)(-2t)+(s^2t^2+s^2-t^2)(2t)+(2s^2)(2st^2)$\\

$= -2s^2t^3-2s^2t-2t^3+2s^2t^3+2s^2t-2t^3+4s^4t$\\

$= -4t^3+4s^4t$\\
