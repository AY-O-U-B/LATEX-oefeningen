	\item $f'(x) = \cos x +\cos(2x) + \cos(3x) = \cos x + \cos (3x) + \cos (2x)$ \\

		$\Leftrightarrow f'(x) = 2 \cos\left(\dfrac{4x}{2}\right) \cdot \cos \left(\dfrac{2x}{2} \right) + \cos (2x) $\\
