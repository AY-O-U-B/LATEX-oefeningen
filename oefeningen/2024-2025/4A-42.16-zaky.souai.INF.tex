$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{2x^3+5x^2-1}{x^2+x-2}\,dx$\\

$\displaystyle x^2+x-2=(x+2)(x-1)$\\

$\displaystyle \dfrac{2x^3+5x^2-1}{x^2+x-2}=2x+3+\dfrac{x+5}{x^2+x-2}$\\

$\displaystyle \dfrac{x+5}{x^2+x-2}=\dfrac{x+5}{(x+2)(x-1)}=\dfrac{A}{x+2}+\dfrac{B}{x-1}$\\

$\displaystyle A=\left.\dfrac{x+5}{x-1}\right|_{x=-2}=\dfrac{3}{-3}=-1$\\

$\displaystyle B=\left.\dfrac{x+5}{x+2}\right|_{x=1}=\dfrac{6}{3}=2$\\

$\displaystyle \dfrac{2x^3+5x^2-1}{x^2+x-2}=2x+3-\dfrac{1}{x+2}+\dfrac{2}{x-1}$\\

$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{2x^3+5x^2-1}{x^2+x-2}\,dx=\int (2x+3)\,dx-\int\dfrac{dx}{x+2}+2\int\dfrac{dx}{x-1}$\\
