$= \dfrac{1}{2}\dint x^2 dx - \dfrac{1}{3}\dint x^4 dx + 7\dint x^2 dx - \dint dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{x^6}{6} - \dfrac{1}{3}\dfrac{x^5}{5} + \dfrac{7x^3}{3} - x + c$\\

$= \dfrac{x^6}{12} - \dfrac{x^5}{15} + \dfrac{7x^3}{3} - x + c$\\
