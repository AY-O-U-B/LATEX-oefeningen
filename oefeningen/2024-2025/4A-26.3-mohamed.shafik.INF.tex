Bereken $\displaystyle\int (2x-7)^5 dx$\\

Substitutie: $t = 2x - 7$, $dt = 2dx$, $dx = \dfrac{dt}{2}$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int (2x-7)^5 dx &=& \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int t^5 dt\\
&=& \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{t^6}{6} + c\\
&=& \dfrac{(2x-7)^6}{12} + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
