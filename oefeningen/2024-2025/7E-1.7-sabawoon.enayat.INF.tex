Opgave: Bepaal de kritieke punten en ga na of de functie in die punten een extremum bereiken:\\

$f(x, y) = 2y^2 - x(x - 1)^2$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \dfrac{d}{dx}\left(-x(x - 1)^2\right) = \dfrac{d}{dx}\left(-x^3 + 2x^2 - x\right) = -3x^2 + 4x - 1$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \dfrac{d}{dy}\left(2y^2\right) = 4y$\\

$\Rightarrow \left\{
	\begin{array}{lcr}
		-3x^2 + 4x - 1 & = & 0 \\
		4y             & = & 0
	\end{array}
\right. \Rightarrow \left\{
	\begin{array}{lcr}
		3x^2 - 4x + 1 & = & 0 \\
		y             & = & 0
	\end{array}
\right.$\\

$D = 16 \cdot - 4\cdot 3 = 4 \Rightarrow x_{1,2} = \dfrac{4 \pm 2}{6} \Rightarrow x_1 = \dfrac{1}{3}$ en $x_2 = 1$\\

$\Rightarrow$ Kritieke punten: $\left(\dfrac{1}{3}, 0\right)$ en $(1, 0)$\\

$H(x,y) = \left(
	\begin{array}{cc}
		\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2}         & \dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x\partial y} \\
		\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y\partial x} & \dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2}
	\end{array}
\right) = \left(
	\begin{array}{cc}
		-6x + 4 & 0 \\
		0       & 4
	\end{array}
\right)$\\

$\Delta(x, y) = \operatorname{det} H(x, y) = (-6x + 4) \cdot 4 = -24x + 16$\\

$\Delta\left(\dfrac{1}{3}, 0\right) = -\dfrac{24}{3} + 16 = -8 + 16 = 8 > 0 \Rightarrow$ minimum of maximum\\

$\Rightarrow A\left(\dfrac{1}{3}, 0\right) = -\dfrac{6}{3} + 4 = -2 + 4 = 2 > 0\Rightarrow$ minimum\\

$\Delta(1, 0) = -24 + 16 = -8 < 0 \Rightarrow$ zadelpunt\\
