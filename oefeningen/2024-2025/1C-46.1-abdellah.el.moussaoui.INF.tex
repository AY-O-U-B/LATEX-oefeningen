$z^4=1$\\

$1=\operatorname{cis}(0)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_k=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{0+2k\pi}{4}\right),\ k=0,1,2,3$\\

$\Rightarrow z_0=\operatorname{cis}(0)=1$\\

$\Rightarrow z_1=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)=i$\\

$\Rightarrow z_2=\operatorname{cis}(\pi)=-1$\\

$\Rightarrow z_3=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}\right)=-i$\\

$\Rightarrow \boxed{z_0=1,\ z_1=i,\ z_2=-1,\ z_3=-i}$\\
