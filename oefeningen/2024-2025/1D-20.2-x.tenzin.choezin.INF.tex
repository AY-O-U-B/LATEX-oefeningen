$z^4 + z^2 - 12$\\

Stel $t = z^2 \Rightarrow t^2 + t -12$\\

Nulpunten berekening\\

$D = 1 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot (-12) = 49 \Rightarrow \sqrt{49} = 7$\\

$t_{1} = \dfrac{-1 + 7}{2} = 3$\\

$t_{2} = \dfrac{-1 - 7}{2} = -4$\\

Door $t = z^2 \Leftrightarrow z = \sqrt{t}$\\

$z_{1,2} = \pm \sqrt{3}$\\

$z_{3,4} = \pm 2i$\\ 

\textbf{Ontbinden van de veelterm}\\

$(z^2 + 4) (z + \sqrt{3}) (z - \sqrt{3})$\\
