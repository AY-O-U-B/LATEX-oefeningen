$ \displaystyle\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}\cos^3xdx = \displaystyle\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}\left(1-\sin^2x\right)d\left(\sin x\right) 
\stackrel{t = \sin x}{=} \displaystyle\int\limits_0^1\left(1-t^2\right)dt $\\

$ = \left[t-\dfrac{t^3}{3}\right]_0^1 = \left(1-\dfrac{1}{3}\right) - \left(0\right) = \dfrac{2}{3} $\\
