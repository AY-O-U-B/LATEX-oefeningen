$f(x,y) = \dfrac{4}{\pi} \operatorname{Bgtan}(xy)$ in het punt $a(1,1)$ \\

$f(1,1) = 1$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x, y) = \dfrac{4}{\pi} \cdot \dfrac{1}{1 + (xy)^2} \cdot y$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(1,1) = \dfrac{2}{\pi}$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(x, y) = \dfrac{4}{\pi} \cdot \dfrac{1}{1 + (xy)^2} \cdot x$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(1,1) = \dfrac{2}{\pi}$ \\

$\Rightarrow z = 1 + \dfrac{2}{\pi}(x - 1) + \dfrac{2}{\pi}(y - 1)$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{\pi}(x + y - 2) + 1$ \\
