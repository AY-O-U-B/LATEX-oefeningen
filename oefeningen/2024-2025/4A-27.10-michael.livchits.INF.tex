$ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{2x+1}{x^2+x-5}dx$\\

Stel $u=x^2+x-5$ dan is $\dfrac{du}{2x-1} = dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{u}dx$\\

$ = \ln|u|+c$\\

$ = \ln|x^2+x-5| + c $\\
