$\displaystyle\int e^x (1+e^x)^{-2} dx $\\

$u = 1 + e^x \Rightarrow du = e^x dx$\\

$\Rightarrow\displaystyle\int e^x (1+e^x)^{-2} dx \Rightarrow \int u^{-2}du $\\

$\Rightarrow\displaystyle\int u^{-2}du = \dfrac{u^{-1}}{-1} = -u^{-1} $\\

$\Rightarrow -u^{-1} = \dfrac{1}{u} \Rightarrow -\dfrac{1}{1 + e^x} + C$\\
