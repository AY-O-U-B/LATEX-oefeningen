Bereken de volgende onbepaalde integraal $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x + 4}{x^2 - x +1 dx}$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{2}(2x - 1) + \dfrac{9}{2} dx}{x^2 - x +1}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{2x -1 dx}{x^2 - x + 1} + \dfrac{9}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x^2 - x + 1}$\\

$u = x^2 - x + 1 , du = 2x - 1 dx, v = x - \dfrac{1}{2}, dv = dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{du}{u} + \dfrac{9}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dv}{v^2 + \dfrac{3}{4}}$\\

$t = \dfrac{2v}{\sqrt{3}},	dt = \dfrac{2dv}{\sqrt{3}}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\ln |u| + \dfrac{9}{2} \cdot \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}t}{2}\right)^2 + \dfrac{3}{4}}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\ln |u| + \dfrac{9\sqrt{3}}{4}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{\dfrac{3}{4}(t^2 + 1)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\ln |u| + \dfrac{9\sqrt{3}}{4} \cdot \dfrac{4}{3}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t^2 + 1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\ln |u| + 3\sqrt{3} \Bgtan t + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\ln |x^2 - x +1| + 3\sqrt{3} \Bgtan \left(\dfrac{2x - 1}{\sqrt{3}}\right) + C$ \\
