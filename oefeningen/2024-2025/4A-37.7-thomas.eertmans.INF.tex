$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x^2-2x+10}$ \\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x^2-1+9}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{9}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\dfrac{1}{9}(x-1)^2+1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{9} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\left(\dfrac{1}{3}(x-1)\right)^2+1}$ \\

Stel $u = \dfrac{1}{3}(x-1)\Rightarrow du = \dfrac{dx}{3} \Leftrightarrow dx = 3du $\\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{du}{u^2+1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \Bgtan(u) +c$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \Bgtan\left(\dfrac{1}{3}(x-1)\right) +c$
