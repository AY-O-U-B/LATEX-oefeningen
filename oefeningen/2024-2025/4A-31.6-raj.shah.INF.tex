$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{(x+1)\sqrt{x}}$\\

Stel $\sqrt{x} = t$ dan $dt = \dfrac{dx}{2 \sqrt{x}} \Leftrightarrow dx = 2\sqrt{x} dt$\\ 

$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{2t}{\left(t^2 + 1\right) t} dt = 2 \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{t^2 + 1}dt$\\

$ = 2 \operatorname{Bgtan} t + c$\\

$ = 2 \operatorname{Bgtan} \sqrt{x} + c$\\
