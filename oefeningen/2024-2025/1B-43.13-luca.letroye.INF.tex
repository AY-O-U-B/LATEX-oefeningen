$2(3z^2 - z + 1) + i(z+1) = 0$\\

$ \Rightarrow 6z^2 + (-2+i)z + (2+i) = 0$\\

$D = {(-2+i)}^2 -4 \cdot 6 \cdot (2+i) = 3-4i - 24(2+i) = 3-4i - 48 - 24i = -45-28i  $\\

$\Rightarrow -45-28i = x^2 - y^2 + 2xyi$ \\

$
\left\{
    \begin{array}{lll} 
        x^2 - y^2 &=& -45 \\
        2xy &=& -28
    \end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
    \begin{array}{lll} 
        x^2 - y^2 &=& -45\\
        x^2(-y)^2 &=& -196\\
    \end{array}
\right.
$\\

$\Rightarrow \lambda^2 + 45\lambda - 196 = 0$\\

$ D_\lambda = 45^2 -4 \cdot 1 \cdot (-196) = 2025 + 784 = 2809 = 53^2$\\

$\lambda_1 = \dfrac{-45+53}{2} = 4 = x^2 \Rightarrow x = \pm 2$\\

$\lambda_2 = \dfrac{-45-53}{2} = -49 = -y^2 \Rightarrow y = \pm 7$\\

$\Rightarrow z_1 = \dfrac{2-i+(2-7i)}{12} = \dfrac{4-8i}{12} = \dfrac{1}{3} - \dfrac{2}{3}i$\\

$\Rightarrow z_2 = \dfrac{2-i - (2-7i) }{12} = \dfrac{6i}{12} = \dfrac{1}{2}i$\\
