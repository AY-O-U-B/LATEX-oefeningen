$\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^\infty \dfrac{n+\sqrt{n}}{n^3 +n}$ \\

verhoudingscriterium reeks $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^\infty \dfrac{1}{n^2}$ \\

$\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty} \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{n^2}}{\dfrac{n+\sqrt{n}}{n^3 +n}} = \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty} \dfrac{n^3}{n^3} = 1$ \\

$\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^\infty \dfrac{1}{n^2}$ en $\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^\infty \dfrac{n+\sqrt{n}}{n^3 +n}$ \\

beiden zijn dus convergent.
