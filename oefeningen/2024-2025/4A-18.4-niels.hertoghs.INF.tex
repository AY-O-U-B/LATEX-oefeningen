$\displaystyle\int x^3\cos xdx$\\

$
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x^3 \\
dv = \cos xdx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 3x^2dx \\
v = \sin x
\end{array}
\right.
$\\

$= x^3 \sin x - 3 \displaystyle\int x^2 \sin x dx$\\

\left\{
\begin{array}{l}

dv = \sin xdx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}

\end{array}
\right.
$\\
