$ \forall x > 0: \Bgtan(x)+\Bgtan\!\left( \dfrac{1}{x} \right) $\\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
y=\Bgtan(x)\ \Longleftrightarrow\ \tan(y)=x \text{ en } y \in \left]0, \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right[ \\
z=\Bgtan\!\left( \dfrac{1}{x} \right) \Longleftrightarrow\ \tan(z)= \dfrac{1}{x} \text{ en } z \in \left]0, \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right[ 
\end{array}
\right . $\\

$ \tan z = \dfrac{1}{\tan y} = \cot y = \tan\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}-y \right) \rightarrow z = \dfrac{\pi}{2} - y $\\

$ \Rightarrow \Bgtan(x)+\Bgtan\!\left( \dfrac{1}{x} \right) $\\

$ = y + z $\\

$ = y + \left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} - y \right)$\\

$ = \dfrac{\pi}{2} $\\
