$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{1-4x}{2x^2+5x+2}\,dx$\\

$\displaystyle 2x^2+5x+2=(2x+1)(x+2)$\\

$\displaystyle \dfrac{1-4x}{2x^2+5x+2}=\dfrac{1-4x}{(2x+1)(x+2)}=\dfrac{A}{2x+1}+\dfrac{B}{x+2}$\\

$\displaystyle A=\left.\dfrac{1-4x}{x+2}\right|_{x=-\frac{1}{2}}=\dfrac{3}{\frac{3}{2}}=2$\\

$\displaystyle B=\left.\dfrac{1-4x}{2x+1}\right|_{x=-2}=\dfrac{9}{-3}=-3$\\

$\displaystyle \dfrac{1-4x}{2x^2+5x+2}=\dfrac{2}{2x+1}-\dfrac{3}{x+2}$\\

$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{1-4x}{2x^2+5x+2}\,dx=2\int\dfrac{dx}{2x+1}-3\int\dfrac{dx}{x+2}$\\

$\displaystyle =\ln|2x+1|-3\ln|x+2|+c$
