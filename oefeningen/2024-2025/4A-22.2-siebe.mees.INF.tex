$ \displaystyle\int \Bgsin^2 x dx $\\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u = \Bgsin^2 x \\
dv = dx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 2\Bgsin x \cdot \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} dx \\
v = x
\end{array}
\right . $\\

$ = x\Bgsin^2 x - \displaystyle\int x \cdot \dfrac{2\Bgsin x}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} dx $\\

$ = x\Bgsin^2 x - 2\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x\Bgsin x}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} dx $\\

$ t = \Bgsin x \left( \Rightarrow x = \sin t, dx = \cos t dt, \sqrt{1-x^2} = \sqrt{1-\sin^2 t} = \sqrt{\cos^2 t} = \cos t \right)$\\

$ = x\Bgsin^2 x - 2\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sin t \cdot t}{\cos t}\cos t dt $\\

$ = x\Bgsin^2 x - 2\displaystyle\int t\sin t dt $\\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u = t \\
dv = \sin t dt
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du = dt \\
v = -\cos t
\end{array}
\right . $\\

$ = x\Bgsin^2 x - 2\left(-t\cos t + \displaystyle\int \cos t dt\right) $\\

$ = x\Bgsin^2 x - 2\left(-t\cos t + \sin t\right) $\\

$ = x\Bgsin^2 x + 2t\cos t - 2\sin t $\\

$ \Bgsin x = t $\\

$ = x\Bgsin^2 x + 2\Bgsin x\sqrt{1-x^2} - 2x + C $\\
