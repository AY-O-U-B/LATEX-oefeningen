Herschrijven: $z^4 + 4 = z^4 + 4z^2 + 4 - 4z^2 = \left(z^2 + 2\right)^2 - \left(2z\right)^2$\\
  
$= \left(z^2 - 2z + 2\right)\cdot\left(z^2 + 2z + 2\right)$\\
  
discriminant en nulpunten:\\
  
$\left(z^2 - 2z + 2\right)$\\
  
$\Delta_1 = \left(-2\right)^2 - 4\cdot 1 \cdot 2 = -4$ \\
  
$\Rightarrow z_1 = \dfrac{-\left(-2\right) - \sqrt{-4}}{2\cdot 1} = \dfrac{2 - 2i}{2} = 1 - i$ \\
  
$\Rightarrow z_2 = \dfrac{-\left(-2\right) + \sqrt{-4}}{2\cdot 1} = \dfrac{2 + 2i}{2} = 1 + i$ \\
  
$\left(z^2 + 2z + 2\right)$ \\
  
$\Delta_2 = 2^2 - 4\cdot 1 \cdot 2 = -4$ \\
  
$\Rightarrow z_3 = \dfrac{-2 - \sqrt{-4}}{2\cdot 1} = \dfrac{-2 - 2i}{2} = -1 - i$ \\
  
$\Rightarrow z_4 = \dfrac{-2 + \sqrt{-4}}{2\cdot 1} = \dfrac{-2 + 2i}{2} = -1 + i$ \\
