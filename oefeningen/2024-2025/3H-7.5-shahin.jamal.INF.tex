$r^2(\cos^2\theta - 9\sin\theta) = 9$\\

$\Leftrightarrow r^2\left(\dfrac{x^2}{r^2} - 9\dfrac{y^2}{r^2}\right) = 9$\\

$\Leftrightarrow x^2 - 9y^2 = 9$\\
