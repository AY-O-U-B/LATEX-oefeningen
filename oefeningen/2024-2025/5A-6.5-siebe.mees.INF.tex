$ \displaystyle\int_{0}^{9} e^{3x} dx $\\

$ t = 3x \left(dt = 3dx, dx = \dfrac{dt}{3} \right)$\\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
x = 0 \rightarrow t = 3 \cdot 0 = 0 \\
x = 9 \rightarrow t = 3 \cdot 9 = 27
\end{array}
\right . $\\

$ = \displaystyle\int_{0}^{27} e^t \dfrac{dt}{3} $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3}\displaystyle\int_{0}^{27} e^t dt $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3}\left[ e^t \right]_{0}^{27} $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3}\left( e^{27} - e^0 \right) $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3}\left( e^{27} - 1 \right) $\\
