$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{4x^2-6x+1}{2x^3-x^2}dx = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{4x^2-6x+1}{x^2(2x-1)}dx$\\

$\dfrac{4x^2-6x+1}{x^2(2x-1)} = \dfrac{A}{x^2} + \dfrac{B}{x} + \dfrac{C}{2x-1}$\\

$A = \left . \dfrac{4x^2-6x+1}{2x-1} \right |_{x=0} = -1$\\

$B = \dfrac{4x^2-6x+1}{x^2(2x-1)} + \dfrac{1}{x^2} = \dfrac{4x^2-6x+1+(2x-1)}{x^2(2x-1)}= \dfrac{4x^2-4x}{x^2(2x-1)} = \dfrac{4x-4}{x(2x-1)}$\\

$\Rightarrow B = \left . \dfrac{4x-4}{2x-1} \right |_{x=0} = 4$\\

$C = \left . \dfrac{4x^2-6x+1}{x^2} \right |_{x=\tfrac{1}{2}} = -4$\\

$\Rightarrow - \displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{x^2} + 4\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{x} - 4\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{2x-1}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{x} + 4\ln\lvert x \rvert - 2\ln \lvert 2x-1 \rvert + c$\\
