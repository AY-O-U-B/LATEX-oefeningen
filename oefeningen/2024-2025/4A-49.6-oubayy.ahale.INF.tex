$ I = \dint \dfrac{dx}{2 \sec x + 3 \tan x} $\\

$ = \dint \dfrac{dx}{2 \dfrac{1}{\cos x} + 3 \dfrac{\sin x}{\cos x}} $\\



$ = \dint \dfrac{dx}{\dfrac{2 + 3 \sin x}{\cos x}} $\\



$ = \dint \dfrac{\cos x}{2+3 \sin x} dx $\\

Stel $ t = 2+3 \sin x $\\

$ \Rightarrow dt = 3 \cos x \cdot dx           $\\

$ \Leftrightarrow dx = \dfrac{dt}{3 \cos x}    $\\

$ \Rightarrow I = \dint \dfrac{dt}{3t}         $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \ln{\left|t\right|} + c $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \ln{\left|2+3 \sin x\right|} + c $\\
