$\lim\limits_{x \to 2} (2x-3)^{\tfrac{1}{3x-6}} = \lim\limits_{x \to 2} \left( 1 + (2x-4) \right)^{\tfrac{1}{3(x-2)}}$ \\

Stel $2x-4 = u$, dan: \\

$\lim\limits_{u \to 0} \left( (1 + u)^{\tfrac{1}{3 \cdot \tfrac{u}{2}}} \right) = \lim\limits_{u \to 0} \left( (1 + u)^{\tfrac{1}{u}} \right)^{\tfrac{2}{3}} =$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{u \to 0} e^{\tfrac{2}{3}} = e^{\tfrac{2}{3}}$\\
