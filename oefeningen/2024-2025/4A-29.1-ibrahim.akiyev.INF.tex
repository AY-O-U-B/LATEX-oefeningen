$\displaystyle \int \sqrt{x+6} dx$\\

$u = x + 6 $\\

$\Rightarrow\displaystyle \int \sqrt{x+6} dx = \int \sqrt{u} du$\\

$\Rightarrow\displaystyle \int \sqrt{u} = u^{\tfrac{1}{2}} \Rightarrow \int \sqrt{u^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}du$\\

$\Rightarrow\displaystyle \int u^n du = \dfrac{u^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \Rightarrow \dfrac{u{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{\tfrac{3}{2}} = \dfrac{2}{3} u^{\tfrac{3}{2}}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{2}{3}(x + 6)^{\tfrac{3}{2}} + C $\\
