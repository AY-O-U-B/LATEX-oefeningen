$\displaystyle\int (x^3-x^2)\cos(x)dx$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x^3-x^2 \\
dv = \cos(x)dx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 3x^2-2xdx \\
v = \sin(x)
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$=(x^3-x^2)\sin(x) - \displaystyle\int (3x^2-2x)\sin(x)dx$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u = 3x^2-2x  \\
dv = \sin(x)dx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du = (6x-2)dx \\
v = -\cos(x)
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$=(x^3-x^2)\sin(x)+ (3x^2-2x)\cos(x)- \displaystyle\int (6x-2)\cos(x)dx$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u = 6x-2 \\
dv = \cos(x)dx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 6dx \\
v = \sin(x)
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$=(x^3-x^2)\sin(x)+ (3x^2-2x)\cos(x)-(6x-2)\sin(x)-6\cos(x)+C$\\
