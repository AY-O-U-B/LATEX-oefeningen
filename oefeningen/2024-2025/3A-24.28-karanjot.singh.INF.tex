$f(x) = \tan x - \dfrac{1 - \cos x}{\sin x}$\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{d}{dx} (\tan x) - \dfrac{d}{dx} \left( \dfrac{1 - \cos x}{\sin x} \right)$\\

$\dfrac{d}{dx} (\tan x) = \sec^2 x$\\

$\dfrac{d}{dx} \left( \dfrac{1 - \cos x}{\sin x} \right) = \dfrac{\sin x \cdot \sin x - (1 - \cos x) \cdot \cos x}{\sin^2 x}$\\

$= \dfrac{\sin^2 x - \cos x + \cos^2 x}{\sin^2 x}$\\

$= 1 - \dfrac{\cos x \cdot (1 - \cos x)}{\sin^2 x}$\\

$f'(x) = \sec^2 x - \left(1 - \dfrac{\cos x \cdot (1 - \cos x)}{\sin^2 x}\right)$\\
