$ \displaystyle\int x \sqrt{1 - 4x^2} dx $ \\

Stel $t = 1 -4x^2 \Rightarrow dt = -8x dx  \Rightarrow -\dfrac{1}{8}dt = dx$\\

$ = - \dfrac{1}{8} \displaystyle\int \sqrt{t} dt $\\

$ = - \dfrac{2 \cdot \sqrt{1-4x^2} \cdot (1-4x^2)}{24} + C $\\

$ = - \dfrac{\sqrt{1-4x^2} \cdot (1-4x^2)}{12} + C $\\
