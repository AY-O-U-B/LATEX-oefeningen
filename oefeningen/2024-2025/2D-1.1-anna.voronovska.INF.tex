$\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi - 2}{4}} \sin\left(2x + 1\right) =\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi - 2}{4}} \sin\left(2 \cdot \left(\dfrac{\pi - 2}{4}\right) + 1\right) =\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi - 2}{4}} \sin\left(\dfrac{\pi - 2}{2} + 1\right) =\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi - 2}{4}} \sin\left(\dfrac{\pi - 2}{2} + \dfrac{2}{2}\right) =\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi - 2}{4}} \sin\left(\dfrac{\pi - 2 + 2}{2}\right) =
\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi - 2}{4}} \sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = 1$\\
