$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{x- \dfrac{\pi}{2}}{\cos x} $\\ 

$$ y = x - \dfrac{\pi}{2} $$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{y \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{y}{\cos \left(y + \dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)} $\\ 

$ = \lim\limits_{y \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{y}{- \sin y} = - \lim\limits_{y \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{y}{\sin y} $\\ 

$ = -1 $\\
