$y = x^3$ en $x = 0$ en $y = 1$ \\

Bovengrens: $y = 1$ \\

Ondergrens: $y = x^3$ \\

$S = \dint _0^1 (1 - x^3) dx$ \\

$= \left[x- \dfrac{x^4}{4}\right]_0^1$ \\

$= \left(1 - \dfrac{1}{4}\right) - \left(0 - \dfrac{0^4}{4}\right)$ \\

$= 1 - \dfrac{1}{4} = \dfrac{3}{4}$ \\
