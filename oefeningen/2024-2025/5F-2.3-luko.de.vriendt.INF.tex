$S = \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle \int_{0}^{2\pi}\cancel{1^2}d\theta - \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle \int_{0}^{\pi}\cancelto{\dfrac{1-\cos(2\theta)}{2}}{\sin^2(\theta)}d\theta$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\left[\theta\right]_0^{2\pi} - \dfrac{1}{4}\left[\theta -\dfrac{1}{2}\sin(2\theta)\right]_0^\pi$ \\

$=\dfrac{1}{2}\left(2\pi - 0\right) - \dfrac{1}{4}\left((\pi-0)-(0-0)\right)$ \\

$=\pi - \dfrac{\pi}{4} = \dfrac{3\pi}{4}$ \\
