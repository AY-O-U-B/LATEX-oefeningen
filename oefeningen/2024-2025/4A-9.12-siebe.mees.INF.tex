$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{1-\cos2x} $\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{1-\cos2x} dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{1-(1-2\sin^2(x))} dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{2\sin^2(x)} dx$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{\sin^2(x)} dx$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \csc^2(x) dx$\\

$ = -\dfrac{1}{2} \cot(x) + C $\\

$ = -\dfrac{1}{2\tan(x)}+ C$\\
