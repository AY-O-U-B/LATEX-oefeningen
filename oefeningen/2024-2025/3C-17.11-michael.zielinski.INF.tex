$\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi}{2}} 
      \dfrac{\tan\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right)-1}{\cos x}$\\

$f(x)=\tan\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right)-1$\\

$f'(x)=\dfrac{1}{2}\sec^{2}\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right)$\\

$g(x)=\cos x$\\

$g'(x)=-\sin x$\\

$\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi}{2}} 
      \dfrac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}
      =\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi}{2}} 
        \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{2}\sec^{2}\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right)}{-\sin x}$\\

$\sec^{2}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)=2,\;
 \sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)=1$\\

$\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi}{2}} 
      \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot 2}{-1}=-1$\\
