$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{5dx}{x^2-2x+5}$ \\

$= 5\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx\cdot\dfrac{1}{4}}{(x-1)^2+4}$ \\

$= \dfrac{5}{4} \displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{\dfrac{1}{4}(x-1)^2+1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{5}{4} \displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{\left(\dfrac{1}{2}(x-1)\right)^2+1}$ \\

Stel $u = \dfrac{1}{2}(x-1)\Rightarrow du = \dfrac{dx}{2} \Leftrightarrow dx = 2du $\\

$= \dfrac{5}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{du}{u^2+1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{5}{2} \Bgtan(u) + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{5}{2}\Bgtan\left(\dfrac{1}{2}(x-1)\right) +c$
