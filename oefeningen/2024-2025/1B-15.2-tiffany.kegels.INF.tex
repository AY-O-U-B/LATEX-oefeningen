$\dfrac{1-3i}{1-i} + \dfrac{i}{2-i} = \left( \dfrac{1-3i}{1-i} \cdot \dfrac{1+i}{1+i} \right) + \left(\dfrac{i}{2-i} \cdot \dfrac{2+i}{2+i} \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{4-2i}{2} + \dfrac{2i-1}{5}$\\

$= \dfrac{10-5i}{5} + \dfrac{2i-1}{5}$\\

$= \dfrac{9-3i}{5}$\\
