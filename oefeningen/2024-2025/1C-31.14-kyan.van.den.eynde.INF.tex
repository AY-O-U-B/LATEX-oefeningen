$\left(\dfrac{1-3i}{2-i}\right)^8$\\

\begin{tabular}{r|cc}
	          & $1-3i$           & $2-i$ \\ \hline
r	          & $\sqrt{10}$      & $\sqrt{5}$ \\
$\tan \theta$ & $-3$             & $-\dfrac{1}{2}$ \\
arg. z	      & $\Bgtan (-3)$    & $\Bgtan \left(\dfrac{-1}{2}\right)$ \\
\end{tabular}

$\Rightarrow \left(\dfrac{1-3i}{2-i}\right)^8 = \left(\dfrac{\sqrt{10} \cis \Bgtan(-3)}{\sqrt{5} \cis \Bgtan\left(-\dfrac{1}{2}\right)}\right) = \left(\sqrt{2} \cis \Bgtan \left(\dfrac{-3 + \dfrac{1}{2}}{1+(-3)\left(-\dfrac{1}{2}\right)}\right)\right)^8\\
= \left(\sqrt{2} \cis \left(\Bgtan(-1)\right)\right)^8 = \left(\sqrt{2} \cis \left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4}\right)\right)^8 = 16 \cis \left(\dfrac{24\pi}{4} \right) = 16 \cis (6\pi) = 16$ \\
