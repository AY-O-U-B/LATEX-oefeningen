$f(x)=\Bgtan\left(\dfrac{x}{a}\right)$ met $ a \in \mathbb{R}_0$\\

$\Rightarrow f'(x)=\dfrac{1}{1+\dfrac{x^2}{a^2}} \left(\dfrac{x}{a}\right)'=\dfrac{1}{1+\dfrac{x^2}{a^2}}\dfrac{1}{a}$\\

$=\dfrac{a^2}{a^2+x^2}\dfrac{1}{a}$\\

$=\dfrac{a}{a^2+x^2}$\\
