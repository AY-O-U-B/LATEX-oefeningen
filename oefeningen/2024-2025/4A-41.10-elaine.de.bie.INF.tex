Bepaal de volgende integraal:
$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{x^2 - 2x + 2}} $\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
	x^2 - 2x + 2 &=& \left(x^2 - 2x + 1^2 \right) + 2 - 1 \\
	&=& \left(x - 1 \right)^2 + 1 \\
\end{eqnarray*}

$ \Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{\left(x - 1 \right)^2 + 1}} $\\

$ = \ln \left| \left(x - 1\right) + \sqrt{\left(x - 1\right)^2 + 1} \right| + C $\\
