Bereken de volgende limiet: \\

$ \Limit{x \rightarrow 2} \dfrac{x^3-x^2-8x+12}{3x^3-10x^2+4x+8} = \cancel{\dfrac{0}{0}} $ \\

Teller ontbinden: \\

\begin{tabular}{c|ccc|c}
    & $1$ & $-1$ & $-8$ & $12$ \\
    $2$ & & $2$ & $2$ & $-12$ \\ \hline
    & $1$ & $1$ & $-6$ & $0$
\end{tabular} \\

Noemer ontbinden: \\

\begin{tabular}{c|ccc|c}
    & $3$ & $-10$ & $4$ & $8$ \\
    $2$ & & $6$ & $-8$ & $-8$ \\ \hline
    & $3$ & $-4$ & $-4$ & $0$
\end{tabular} \\

$ \Leftrightarrow \Limit{x \rightarrow 2} \dfrac{\cancel{(x-2)} (x^2+x-6)}{\cancel{(x-2)} (3x^2-4x-4)} = \cancel{\dfrac{0}{0}} $ \\

Teller verder ontbinden: \\

\begin{tabular}{c|cc|c}
    & $1$ & $1$ & $-6$ \\
    $2$ & & $2$ & $6$ \\ \hline
    & $1$ & $3$ & $0$
\end{tabular} \\

Noemer verder ontbinden: \\

\begin{tabular}{c|cc|c}
    & $3$ & $-4$ & $-4$ \\
    $2$ & & $6$ & $4$ \\ \hline
    & $3$ & $2$ & $0$
\end{tabular} \\

$ \Leftrightarrow \Limit{x \rightarrow 2} \dfrac{\cancel{(x-2)} (x+3)}{\cancel{(x-2)} (3x+2)} = \dfrac{5}{8} $ \\
