\begin{eqnarray*}
f(x) &=& (x-2)^3 + 1 \\
f'(x) &=& 3(x-2)^2 \\
f''(x) &=& 6(x-2)
\end{eqnarray*}

Nulpunten: \\

$f(x) = 0 \Leftrightarrow (x-2)^3 + 1 = 0 \Leftrightarrow (x-2)^3 = -1 \Leftrightarrow x = 1$ \\

Extrema: \\

$f'(x) = 0 \Leftrightarrow 3(x-2)^2 = 0 \Leftrightarrow x = 2$ \\

Buigpunten: \\

$f''(x) = 0 \Leftrightarrow 6(x-2) = 0 \Leftrightarrow x = 2$ \\
