$\displaystyle\int x^2\cdot \cosh x \cdot dx$\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    u = x^2 \\
    dv = \cosh x \cdot dx
\end{array} \right.
\Rightarrow\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    du = 2x \cdot dx\\
    v = \sinh x\\
\end{array} \right. $\\

$= x^2 \sinh x - \displaystyle\int 2x \sinh x \cdot dx$\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    u = 2x\\

    dv = \sinh x \cdot dx
\end{array} \right. $
$\Rightarrow$
$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    du = 2 \cdot dx\\
    
    v = \cosh x\\
\end{array} \right. $\\

$= x^2 \sinh x- (2x \cosh x - \displaystyle\int 2 \cosh x \cdot dx)$\\

$= x^2 \sinh x - 2x \cosh x+ 2 \displaystyle\int \cosh x \cdot dx$\\

$= x^2 \sinh x-2x \cosh x + 2 \sinh x + C$\\

$= (x^2 + 2) \sinh x-2x \cosh x + C$\\
