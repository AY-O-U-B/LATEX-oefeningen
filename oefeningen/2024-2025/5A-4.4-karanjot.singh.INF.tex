$\displaystyle\int_{-\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \cos x dx$ \\

Onbepaalde integraal: $\displaystyle\int \cos x dx = \sin x + C$ \\

We gaan terug naar onze bepaalde integraal: \\

$\displaystyle\int_{-\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \cos x dx$ \\

$= \sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) - \sin\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)$ \\

$= 1 - (-1) = 1 + 1 = 2$ \\

Dus, $\displaystyle\int_{-\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \cos x dx = 2$ \\
