$2^x = \sqrt[3]{2}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow 2^x = 2^{\tfrac{1}{3}}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_2 (2^x) = \log_2 2^{\tfrac{1}{3}}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow x = \dfrac{1}{3}$\\
