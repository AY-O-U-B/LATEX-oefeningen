$4 \cdot \log_{x} \sqrt{3} + \log_{x}3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} + \log_{x}\dfrac{1}{9} + \dfrac{1}{3} = 0 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow 2 \cdot \log_{x}3 + \log_{x}3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} + \log_{x}\dfrac{1}{9} = -\dfrac{1}{3} $\\

$\Leftrightarrow 2 \cdot \log_{x}3 + \log_{x}3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} + \log_{x}\dfrac{1}{9} = -\dfrac{1}{3} $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_{x}\dfrac{9}{9} + \log_{x}3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} = -\dfrac{1}{3} $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_{x}3 = -1 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow x = \dfrac{1}{3} $\\
