$I = \displaystyle\int_{1}^{2} x^2  \cosh (x) \cdot dx $\\

$\left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        u = x^2 \\
        dv = \cosh (x) \cdot dx
    \end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        du =  2x \cdot dx \\
        v = \sinh (x)
    \end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow I =  \left[ x^2 \sinh (x) \right]_{1}^{2} - 2 \displaystyle\int_{1}^{2}x  \sinh (x) \cdot dx $\\

$\left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        u = x \\
        dv = \sinh (x) \cdot dx
    \end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        du = dx \\
        v = - \cosh (x)
    \end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow I =  \left[ x^2 \sinh (x) \right]_{1}^{2} - 2 \left [ x \cosh (x) - \sinh (x) \right ]_1^2$\\

$\Rightarrow I =  \left[ x^2 \sinh (x) - 2 x \cosh (x) + 2 \sinh (x) \right ]_1^2$\\

$= 6 \sinh (2) - 4 \cosh (2) - 3 \sinh (1) + 2 \cosh (1)$\\\
