$f(x) = -2x^2 - 5x + 3$\\

$f'(x) = -4x - 5$\\

$f'(x) = 0$\\

Dan: \\

$-4x - 5 = 0$ \\

$-4x = 5 $\\

$x = -\dfrac{5}{4}$\\

$f\left(-\dfrac{5}{4}\right) = -2\cdot \left(-\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^2 - 5\left(-\dfrac{5}{4}\right) + 3 = -2 \cdot \dfrac{25}{16} + \dfrac{25}{4} + 3 =$\\

$= -\dfrac{50}{16} + \dfrac{100}{16} + \dfrac{48}{16} = \dfrac{98}{16} = \dfrac{49}{8}$\\
