$= \dint \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{-x^2 + 2x - 1 + 4}}$\\

$= \dint \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{4 - (x - 1)^2}}$\\

$= \dint \dfrac{d(x - 1)}{\sqrt{4 - (x - 1)^2}}$\\
