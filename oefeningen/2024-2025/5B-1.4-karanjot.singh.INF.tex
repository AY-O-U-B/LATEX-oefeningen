$\displaystyle\int_1^{+\infty} x^{-\tfrac{2}{3}} dx$ \\

$\displaystyle\int x^{-\tfrac{2}{3}} dx = \dfrac{3x^{\tfrac{1}{3}}}{1} + C = 3x^{\tfrac{1}{3}} + C$ \\

$\Rightarrow  \lim\limits_{b \rightarrow +\infty} \left[ 3x^{\tfrac{1}{3}} \right]_1^b$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{b \rightarrow +\infty} \left( 3b^{\tfrac{1}{3}} - 3 \cdot 1^{\tfrac{1}{3}} \right)$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{b \rightarrow +\infty} \left( 3b^{\tfrac{1}{3}} - 3 \right)$ \\

$= +\infty$ \\

$\Rightarrow$ De integraal is divergent. \\
