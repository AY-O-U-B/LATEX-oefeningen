$f(x) = (x^2-1)^4$ \\

$f'(x) = 4(x^2-1)^3 \cdot 2x$ \\

$= 8x(x^2-1)^3$ \\


$f''(x) = 8(x(x^2-1)^3)'$ \\

$= 8(1 \cdot (x^2-1)^3 + x \cdot 3(x^2-1)^2 \cdot 2x)$ \\

$= 8(x^2-1)^3 + 48x^2(x^2-1)^2$ \\

$= 8(x^6 - 3x^4 + 3x^2 - 1) + 48x^2(x^4 - 2x^2 + 1)$ \\

$= 56x^6 -120x^4 + 72x^2 - 8$ \\

$f'''(x) = 336x^5 - 480x^3 + 144x$
