Bepaal de eerste drie termen van de rekenkundige rij als \\

$ x_{5} = 13 $ \\

$ x_{8} + x_{14} = 50 $ \\


$\Leftrightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    x_{5} = 13 \\
	
    x_{8} = x_{5} + 3v = 13 + 3v \\
	
	x_{14} = x_{5} + 9v = 13 + 9v \\
	
	x_{8} + x_{14} = 50 \\
	
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    x_{5} = 13 \\
	
    x_{8} = 13 + 3v \\
	
	x_{14} = 13 + 9v \\
	
	\left( 13 + 3v \right) + \left( 13 + 9v \right) = 50 \\
	
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    x_{5} = 13 \\

\right.$ \\

\right.$ \\
