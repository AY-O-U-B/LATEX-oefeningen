$\displaystyle\int x^2 \cosh x  dx = x^2 \sinh x - 2 \displaystyle\int x \sinh x  dx$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    u = x^2 \\
    dv = \cosh x  dx \\
\end{array}
\right. 
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    du = 2x  dx \\
    v = \sinh x \\
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    u = x \\
    dv = \sinh x  dx \\
\end{array}
\right. 
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    du = dx \\
    v = \cosh x \\
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$= x^2 \sinh x - 2x \cosh x + 2 \displaystyle\int \cosh x  dx$ \\

$= x^2 \sinh x - 2x \cosh x + 2 \sinh x + c$ \\
