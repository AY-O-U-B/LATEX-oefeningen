$ \lim\limits_{(x,y,z)\rightarrow (0,0,0)}\dfrac{x^2 + y^2 - z^2}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} $\\

$ \stackrel{x = 0, y = 0}{=} \lim\limits_{z \rightarrow 0}\dfrac{-z^2}{z^2} = -1 $\\

$ \stackrel{z = 0, y = 0}{=} \lim\limits_{(x)\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{x^2}{x^2} = 1 $\\

$ \Rightarrow $ limiet bestaat niet \\
