$ \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left(\sin(2x)\tan(3x)\right)
= \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left(\dfrac{\sin(2x)\sin(3x)}{\cos(3x)}\right) $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left(\dfrac{\sin(2x)\left(3\sin x - 4\sin^3x\right)}{4\cos^3x - 3\cos x}\right) $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left(\dfrac{2\sin x\cos x\sin x\left(3-4\sin^2x\right)}{\cos x\left(4\cos^2x-3\right)}\right) $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left(\dfrac{2\sin^2x\left(3-4\sin^2x\right)}{4\cos^2x-3}\right) $\\

$ = \dfrac{2\left(3-4\right)}{-3} = \dfrac{2}{3} $\\
