$f'(x) = \dfrac{\left(1-\tan\left(x\right)\right)\left(\sec^2\left(x\right)\right)-\left(1 + \tan\left(x\right)\right)\left(-\sec^2\left(x\right)\right)}{\left(1-\tan\left(x\right)\right)^2}$\\\\

$= \dfrac{\left(1-\tan\left(x\right)\right)\left(1-\tan^2\left(x\right)\right)+\left(1+\tan\left(x\right)\right)\left(1+\tan^2\left(x\right)\right)}{\left(1+\tan\left(x\right)\right)^2}$\\\\

$= \dfrac{\left(1+\tan^2\left(x\right)\right)\left(\left(1-\tan\left(x\right)\right)+\left(1+\tan\left(x\right)\right)\right)}{\left(1+\tan\left(x\right)\right)^2}$\\\\

$= \dfrac{2\left(1+\tan^2\left(x\right)\right)}{\left(1+\tan\left(x\right)\right)^2}$
