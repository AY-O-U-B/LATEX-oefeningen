$\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin 2x+\cos x}{2+\cos 2x-\sin x} = \dfrac{0}{0}$\\

$\lim\limits_{x \to\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{2\cos 2x-\sin x}{-2\sin 2x-\cos x} = \dfrac{-3}{0}$\\

$\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi}{2}+} \dfrac{2\cos 2x-\sin x}{-2\sin 2x-\cos x} = \dfrac{-3}{-0^{-}-0^{-}} = \dfrac{-3}{0^{+}} = -\infty$\\

$\lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi}{2}-} \dfrac{2\cos 2x-\sin x}{-2\sin 2x-\cos x} = \dfrac{-3}{-0^{+}-0^{+}} = \dfrac{-3}{0^{-}} = +\infty$\\
