$\integral\dfrac{\cos x}{1 + \sin^2 x}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $t = \sin x \Rightarrow dt = \cos xdx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{1}{1 + t^2}dt$ \\

$= \operatorname{Bgtan}t + c$ \\

$= \operatorname{Bgtan}(\sin x) + c$ \\
