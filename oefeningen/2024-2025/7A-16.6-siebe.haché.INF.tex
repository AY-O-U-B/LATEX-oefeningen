$f(x,y) = 
\left\{
\begin{array}{ll}
\dfrac{x^3 - y^3}{x^2 + y^2} & \text{als } (x,y) \neq (0,0) \\
0 & \text{als } (x,y) = (0,0)
\end{array}
\right. \\$

continuĂŻteit: \\

$x = r \cos(\theta)$ en $y = r \sin(\theta): \\$

$\lim\limits_{r \to 0} \dfrac{r^3 \cos^3(\theta) - r^3 \sin^3(\theta)}{r^2 \cos^2(\theta) + r^2 \sin^2(\theta)} = \lim\limits_{r \to 0} \dfrac{r^3 (\cos^3(\theta) - \sin^3(\theta))}{r^2} = \lim\limits_{r \to 0} r (\cos^3(\theta) - \sin^3(\theta)) = 0 \\$

$\Rightarrow$ de limiet is gelijk aan $f(0,0) = 0$, dus $f$ is continu in $(0,0)$. \\

partiĂ«le afgeleiden: \\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(0,0) = \lim\limits_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{f(\lambda, 0) - f(0,0)}{\lambda} = \lim\limits_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{\lambda^3 - 0}{\lambda^2 + 0} - 0}{\lambda} = \lim\limits_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{\lambda}{\lambda} = 1 \\$

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(0,0) = \lim\limits_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{f(0, \lambda) - f(0,0)}{\lambda} = \lim\limits_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{0 - \lambda^3}{0 + \lambda^2} - 0}{\lambda} = \lim\limits_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{-\lambda}{\lambda} = -1 \\$

$\Rightarrow$ beide partiĂ«le afgeleiden bestaan in $(0,0)$. \\

afleidbaarheid: \\

stel $\bar h = (h_1, h_2)$ en $h_1 \neq h_2 \neq1$\\

$Df(\bar 0,\bar h) = \lim\limits_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{f(\lambda h_1, \lambda h_2) - f(0,0)}{\lambda} \\$

$= \lim\limits_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{\lambda^3 h_1^3 - \lambda^3 h_2^3}{\lambda^2 h_1^2 + \lambda^2 h_2^2} - 0}{\lambda} = \lim\limits_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{\lambda^3 (h_1^3 - h_2^3)}{\lambda^3 (h_1^2 + h_2^2)} = \dfrac{h_1^3 - h_2^3}{h_1^2 + h_2^2} \\$

$\Rightarrow$ alle richtingsafgeleiden bestaan, dus $f$ is afleidbaar in $(0,0)$. \\

differentieerbaarheid: \\

$Df(\bar 0,\bar h) \neq Df(\bar 0) \cdot \bar h \\$

$\dfrac{h_1^3 - h_2^3}{h_1^2 + h_2^2} \neq h_1 - h_2 \\$

$\Rightarrow f$ is niet differentieerbaar in $(0,0)$. \\
