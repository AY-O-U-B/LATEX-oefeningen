$\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x\right)=\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(2-\dfrac{1}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x\right)+1+\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}2$\\

$\Rightarrow \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x\right)=\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(2-\dfrac{1}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x\right)+\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\dfrac{1}{2}+\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}2$\\

$\Rightarrow \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x\right)=\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(2-\dfrac{1}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^{\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x\right)}=\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^{\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(2-\dfrac{1}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x\right)}$\\


$\Rightarrow \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x = 2-\dfrac{1}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x$\\

Laat $X = \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x$\\

$X + \dfrac{1}{3}X=2$\\

$4X=6$\\

$X=\dfrac{3}{2}$\\

$\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}x=\dfrac{3}{2}$\\

$x=\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^{\tfrac{3}{2}} = \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4}$\\
