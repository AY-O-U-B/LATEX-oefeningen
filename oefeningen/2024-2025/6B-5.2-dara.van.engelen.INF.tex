Bereken $n$ als $x_n = 1$, $s_n = 31$ en $q= 0.5$\\

$s_n = x_1 \cdot \dfrac{1-q^n}{1-q}$\\

$x_n = x_1 \cdot q^{n-1} \Leftrightarrow x_1 = \dfrac{x_n}{q^{n-1}}$\\

$\Rightarrow 31 = \dfrac{1}{0.5^{n-1}} \cdot \dfrac{1-0.5^n}{0.5^{n-1}} \Leftrightarrow \dfrac{31}{2} = \dfrac{1-0.5^n}{0.5^{n-1}}$\\

Stel $a = 0.5^n \Rightarrow 0.5^{n-1} = \dfrac{a}{0.5} = 2a$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{31}{2} = \dfrac{1-a}{2a} \Leftrightarrow 31a = 1-a \Leftrightarrow 32a = 1 \Leftrightarrow a = \dfrac{1}{32}$\\

Dus $\dfrac{1}{32} = 0.5^n \Leftrightarrow \dfrac{1}{32} = \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^n \Leftrightarrow n = 5$
