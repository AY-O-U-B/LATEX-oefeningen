opgave: $\bgcot x + \bgcot \left(-x\right) = \pi$\\

Zij $y = \bgcot x \Rightarrow \cot y = x$ met $y \in \left]0, \pi\right[$\\

Dan is $\cot (\pi - y) = -\cot y = -x$ dus geldt:\\

$\pi - y = \bgcot(-x)$ want $\pi - y \in \left]0, \pi\right[$ en $\cot(\pi - y) = -x$\\

Dus:

\[
\bgcot x + \bgcot(-x) = y + (\pi - y) = \pi
\]
