$r = uvw-u^2-v^2-w^2$ met\\

$u = y+z$, $v = x+z$, $w = x+y$\\

$f:(u,v,w) \rightarrow uvw-u^2-v^2-w^2$ \\

$g:(x,y,z) \rightarrow (x+y,x+z,y+z)$ \\

$r = Df(u,v,w)\cdot Dg(x,y,z)$ \\

$Df(u,v,w) = \left(\begin{array}{ccc}
vw-2u & uw-2v & uv-2w 
\end{array}\right)$ \\

$Dg(x,y,z) = \left(\begin{array}{ccc}
0 & 1 & 1 \\
1 & 0 & 1 \\
1 & 1 & 0
\end{array}\right)$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial r}{\partial x} = uw-2v+uv-2w = (y+z)(x+y) - 2(x+z) + (y+z)(x+z) - 2(x+y)$ \\

$ = 2xy+y^2+2zx+2zy-4x-2z+z^2-2y$\\

$\dfrac{\partial r}{\partial y} = vw-2u+uv-2w = (x+z)(x+y)-2(y+z)+(y+z)(x+z)-2(x+y)$ \\

$ = 2yx+x^2+2zx+2zy-4y-2z-2x+z^2$\\

$\dfrac{\partial r}{\partial z} = vw-2u+uw-2v = (x+z)(x+y)-2(y+z)+(y+z)(x+z)-2(x+z)$ \\

$ = 2xy+x^2+2zx+2zy-4z-2y+y^2-2x$\\

$\Rightarrow \left(\begin{array}{ccc}
2xy+y^2+2zx+2zy-4x-2z+z^2-2y & 2yx+x^2+2zx+2zy-4y-2z-2x+z^2 & 2xy+x^2+2zx+2zy-4z-2y+y^2-2x
\end{array} \right)$\\
