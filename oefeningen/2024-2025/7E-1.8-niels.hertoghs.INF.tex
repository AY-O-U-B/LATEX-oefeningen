$f(x,y) = 2x^2 +y^2 +4x -4y +5$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 4x -4 =0 \Leftrightarrow x=-1$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}= 2y -4= 0 \Leftrightarrow y=2$\\

Te onderzoeken punt: $(-1,2)$\\

$H(x,y) = \det Df(x,y) = \left|\begin{array}{cc}
	4 & 0 \\
	0 & 2 
\end{array}\right| = 8$\\

$H(-1,2)= 8 > 0$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2}$,$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} = 4,2 > 0 \Rightarrow $ minimum in $(-1,2)$\\
