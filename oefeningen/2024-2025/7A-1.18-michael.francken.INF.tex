$\lim\limits_{\left(x,y\right)\rightarrow\left(1,1\right)} \Bgsin\left(\dfrac{xy}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\right)$\\

$=\Bgsin\left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\right)=\Bgsin\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{2}\sqrt{2}}\right)$\\

$=\Bgsin\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\right)=\dfrac{\pi}{4}$\\
