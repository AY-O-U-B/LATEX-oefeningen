$f(x,y) = 3x^2 +12x + 4y^3 - 6y^2 + 5$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 6x + 12 = 0 \Leftrightarrow x = -2 $\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 12y^2 - 12y = 0 \Leftrightarrow y\left(y-1\right) = 0 $\\

In $(-2,0)$ en $(-2,1)$ is het raakvlak horizontaal.
