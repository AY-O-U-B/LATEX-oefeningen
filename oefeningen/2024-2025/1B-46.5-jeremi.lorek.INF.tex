$\dfrac{z}{z-2i} - \dfrac{3iz}{(z+i)(z-2i)} = 2$  \\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{z(z+i) - 3iz}{(z-2i)(z+i)} = 2$  \\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{z^2 + zi - 3iz}{z^2 + zi - 2iz + 2} = 2$  \\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{z^2 - 2iz}{z^2 - iz + 2} = 2$  \\

$\Rightarrow z^2 - 2iz = 2z^2 - iz + 4$  \\

$\Rightarrow -z^2 = 4$ \\

$\Rightarrow z^2 = -4 $ \\

$\Rightarrow z = \pm 2i$  \\

$\Rightarrow z = -2i \quad (\text{want als } z=2i, \text{ dan } \dfrac{2i}{0} \text{ wat niet mag.})$ \\
