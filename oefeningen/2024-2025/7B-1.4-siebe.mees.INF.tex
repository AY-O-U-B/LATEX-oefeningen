$w=\ln(u+v+z)$\\

$u=\cos^2t, v=\sin^2t, z=t^2$\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt} =\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial u}\dfrac{du}{dt} +\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial v}\dfrac{dv}{dt} +\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z}\dfrac{dz}{dt}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial u} =\dfrac{1}{u+v+z}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial v} =\dfrac{1}{u+v+z}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z} =\dfrac{1}{u+v+z}$\\

$\dfrac{du}{dt} =-2\cos t\sin t$\\

$\dfrac{dv}{dt} =2\sin t\cos t$\\

$\dfrac{dz}{dt} =2t$\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt}=\dfrac{1}{u+v+z}(-2\cos t\sin t)+\dfrac{1}{u+v+z}(2\sin t\cos t)+\dfrac{1}{u+v+z}(2t)$\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt}=\dfrac{2t}{u+v+z}$\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt}=\dfrac{2t}{\cos^2t+\sin^2t+t^2}$\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt}=\dfrac{2t}{1+t^2}$\\
