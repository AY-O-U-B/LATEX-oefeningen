$= \dfrac{0}{0} \overset{H}{=} \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0}\dfrac{\ln(1+x) + \dfrac{x}{1+x}}{e^x-1}$\\

$= \dfrac{0}{0} \overset{H}{=} \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0}\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{1+x} + \dfrac{1+x-x}{(1+x)^2}}{e^x}$\\
