$\displaystyle\int (5x+7)^{-3} dx$\\

Stel $t = 5x+7$, dan is $dt = 5 dx \Leftrightarrow dx = \dfrac{1}{5}dt$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{5} \displaystyle\int t^{-3}dt$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{5} \left(\dfrac{t^{-2}}{-2}\right) + c = \dfrac{1}{5} \cdot \left(\dfrac{1}{-2t^2}\right) + c$\\

$ = \dfrac{-1}{10(5x+7)^2} + c $
