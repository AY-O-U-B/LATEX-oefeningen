$w = \sqrt{u^2+v^2+z^2}, u = 3e^t\sin s, v = 3e^t\cos s, z = 4e^t$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s} =\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\dfrac{u}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2+z^2}} & \dfrac{v}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2+z^2}} & \dfrac{z}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2+z^2}}
\end{array}
\right) \cdot \left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
3e^t\cos s\\
-3e^t\sin s\\
0\\
\end{array}
\right)$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s} = \dfrac{0}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2+z^2}} = 0$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} =\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
\dfrac{u}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2+z^2}} & \dfrac{v}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2+z^2}} & \dfrac{z}{\sqrt{u^2+v^2+z^2}}
\end{array}
\right) \cdot \left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
3e^t\sin s\\
3e^t\cos s\\
4e^t\\
\end{array}
\right)$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} = \dfrac{25e^{2t}}{5e^t} = 5e^t$\\
