$\integral\dfrac{2^{x + 2} + 5^{x - 2}}{4^{x - 1}}dx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{2^{x + 2}}{4^{x - 1}}dx + \integral\dfrac{ 5^{x - 2}}{4^{x - 1}}dx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{2^{x + 2}}{2^{2(x - 1)}}dx + \integral\dfrac{ 5^{x}\cdot 5^{-2}}{4^{x}\cdot 4^{-1}}dx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{2^{x + 2}}{2^{2x - 2}}dx + \dfrac{5^{-2}}{4^{-1}}\integral\dfrac{ 5^{x}}{4^{x}}dx$ \\

$= \integral 2^{x+2-(2x-2)} dx +  \dfrac{4}{25}\integral\dfrac{5^x}{4^x}dx$ \\

$= \integral 2^{-x+4} dx + \dfrac{4}{25}\integral\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^xdx$ \\

$= \integral 2^{-x} \cdot 2^4 dx + \dfrac{4}{25}\dfrac{\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x}{\ln \dfrac{5}{4}}$ \\

$= 16 \integral 2^{-x} dx + \dfrac{4\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x}{25\ln \dfrac{5}{4}}$ \\

$= 16 \integral \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^{x} dx + \dfrac{4\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x}{25\ln \dfrac{5}{4}} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{16\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^x}{\ln \dfrac{1}{2}} + \dfrac{4\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x}{25\ln \dfrac{5}{4}} + c$ \\
