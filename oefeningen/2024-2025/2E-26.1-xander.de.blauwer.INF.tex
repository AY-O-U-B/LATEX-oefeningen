$\log_2(\log_x 10) = -\dfrac{1}{2}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_x 10 = 2^{\tfrac{-1}{2}} = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x^{\tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}} = 10$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x = 10^{\sqrt{2}}$ \\
