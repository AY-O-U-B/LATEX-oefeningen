$\dint \dfrac{\sqrt{3x}}{x^2} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{\sqrt{3} \sqrt{x}}{x^2} dx$ \\

$= \dint \sqrt{3} \cdot \dfrac{\sqrt{x}}{x^2} dx$ \\

$= \sqrt{3} \dint x^{\tfrac{1}{2}} x^{-2} dx$ \\

$= \sqrt{3} \dint x^{\tfrac{-3}{2}} dx$ \\

$= \sqrt{3} \cdot \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{-1}{2}}}{\dfrac{-1}{2}} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{1} \cdot \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x}} \cdot \dfrac{-2}{1} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{-2\sqrt{3}}{\sqrt{x}} + c$ \\
