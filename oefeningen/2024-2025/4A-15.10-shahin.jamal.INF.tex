$\displaystyle\int x \sinh x \ dx$\\

$\left\{
   \begin{array}{l}
       u = x\\
       dv = \sinh x dx
   \end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
   \begin{array}{l}
       du = dx \\
       v = \cosh x
   \end{array}
\right.$\\

$= x\cosh x - \displaystyle\int \cosh x \ dx$\\

$= x \cosh x - \sinh x + c$\\
