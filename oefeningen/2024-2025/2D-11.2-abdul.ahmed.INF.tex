$\llim_{x \rightarrow 3} \dfrac{\sqrt{2x + 3} - 3}{x-3}$\\

$= \llim_{x \rightarrow 3} \dfrac{2x + 3 - 9}{(x-3 \left)(\right \sqrt{2x + 3} + 3 )}$\\

$= \llim_{x \rightarrow 3} \dfrac{2(x - 3)}{(x-3 \left)(\right \sqrt{2x + 3} + 3 )}$\\

$= \llim_{x \rightarrow 3} \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{2x + 3} + 3}$\\

$= \dfrac{2}{6}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{3}$\\
