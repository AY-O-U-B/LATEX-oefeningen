$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{4x+7}{2x^2-3x-2}dx = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{4x+7}{(2x+1)(x-2)}dx$\\

$\dfrac{4x+7}{(2x+1)(x-2)} = \dfrac{A}{2x+1} + \dfrac{B}{x-2}$\\

$A = \left . \dfrac{4x+7}{x-2} \right |_{x=-\dfrac{1}{2}} = -2 $\\

$B = \left . \dfrac{4x+7}{2x+1} \right |_{x=2} = 3 $\\

$\dfrac{4x+7}{2x^2-3x-2} = \dfrac{-2}{2x+1} + \dfrac{3}{x-2}$\\

$\Rightarrow -2\displaystyle\int\dfrac{d(2x+1)}{2x+1} + 3\displaystyle\int\dfrac{d(x-2)}{x-2}$\\

$= -\ln\lvert 2x+1 \rvert + 3\ln\lvert x-2 \rvert + c$\\
