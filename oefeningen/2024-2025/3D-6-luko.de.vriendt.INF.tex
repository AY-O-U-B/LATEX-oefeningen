$f(x) = \dfrac{2 - 3x}{x^2 - 3x + 2}$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{-3(x^2 - 3x + 2) - ((2 - 3x)(2x - 3)))}{(x^2 - 3x + 2)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{-3x^2 + 9x - 6 - 4x + 6 + 6x^2 - 9x}{(x^2 - 3x + 2)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{x(3x - 4)}{((x-2)(x-1))^2}$ \\

$f'(x) = 0 \Leftrightarrow \dfrac{x(3x - 4)}{((x-2)(x-1))^2} = 0$ \\
