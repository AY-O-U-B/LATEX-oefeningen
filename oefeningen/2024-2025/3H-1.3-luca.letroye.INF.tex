$x^2+9y^2 = 1$\\

$\Rightarrow r^2\cos^2\theta + 9 r^2\sin^2\theta = 1$\\

$\Rightarrow r^2(\cos^2\theta + 9 \sin^2\theta) = 1$\\

$\Rightarrow r = \pm \sqrt{\dfrac{1}{\cos^2 \theta + 9 \sin^2 \theta}}$\\
