$\Leftrightarrow2^{2x-1} = \dfrac{2^{2x}}{2} \Rightarrow 2^{2x-1} = \dfrac{4n^2}{2} = 2n^2$\\

$\Leftrightarrow 2n^2 -5n +3 = 0$\\

$\Leftrightarrow D = (-5)^2 - 4 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 = 1$\\

$\Leftrightarrow n = \dfrac{5 \pm 1}{4} \Rightarrow n = \dfrac{3}{2} \vee n = 1$\\
