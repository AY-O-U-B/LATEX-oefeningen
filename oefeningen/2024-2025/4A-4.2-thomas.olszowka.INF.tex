$\dint \dfrac{1}{\thirdroot{x}} dx$ \\

$= \dint x^{\tfrac{-1}{3}} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{2}{3}}}{\dfrac{2}{3}} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{\thirdroot{x^2}}{1} \cdot \dfrac{3}{2} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{3\thirdroot{x^2}}{2} + c$ \\
