$\dint\dfrac{5xdx}{(x^2-3)^2}$\\


$u = x^2-3$\\

$du = 2xdx$\\

$xdx=\dfrac{du}{2}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dint\dfrac{5}{(u)^2}\dfrac{du}{2}$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{2}\dint\dfrac{1}{(u)^2}du$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{2}\dint(u)^{-2} du$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{2}\cdot\dfrac{u^{-1}}{-1}+c$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{2}\cdot\dfrac{1}{-u}+c$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{-2u}+c$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{-2(x^2-3)}+c$\\
