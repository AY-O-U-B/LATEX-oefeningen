$\dfrac{1 - 2i}{(1 + 2i)^2}$\\

$= \dfrac{1 - 2i}{-3 + 4i}$\\

$= \dfrac{(1 - 2i)(-3 - 4i)}{(-3 + 4i)(-3 - 4i)}$\\

$= \dfrac{(1 - 2i)(-3 - 4i)}{(-3)^2 + (4)^2}$\\

$= \dfrac{1 \cdot (-3) + 1 \cdot (-4i) - 2i \cdot (-3) + (-2i) \cdot (-4i)}{9 + 16}$\\

$= \dfrac{-3 - 4i + 6i + 8i^2}{25}$\\

$= \dfrac{-3 + 2i - 8}{25}$\\

$= \dfrac{-11 + 2i}{25}$\\

$= -\dfrac{11}{25} + \dfrac{2i}{25}$\\
