$f(x) = \dfrac{g(x)}{h(x)}$\\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{g'(x)h(x) - g(x)h'(x)}{h(x)^2}$\\


$f(x) = \dfrac{x^3 - 1}{x^2 + x}$\\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{(3x^2)(x^2 + x) - (x^3 - 1)(2x + 1)}{(x^2 + x)^2} = \dfrac{(3x^4 + 3x^3) - (2x^4+ x^3 - 2x - 1)}{x^4 + 2x^3 + x^2}$\\

$ = \dfrac{x^4 + 2x^3 + 2x + 1}{x^4 + 2x^3 + x^2}$\\
