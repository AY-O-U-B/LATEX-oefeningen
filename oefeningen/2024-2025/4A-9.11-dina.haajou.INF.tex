$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{1 + \cos(x)}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{2\cos^2\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{\cos^2\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle \int \sec^2\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right) dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot 2 \tan\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right) + c$ \\

$= \tan\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right) + c$ \\
