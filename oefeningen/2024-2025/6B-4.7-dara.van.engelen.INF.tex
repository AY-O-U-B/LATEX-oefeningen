Bereken $v$ als $x_1 = 0.4$ en $s_{10} = 16.5$\\

$s_{10} = \dfrac{0.4+x_{10}}{2} \cdot 10 = 16.5 $\\

$x_{10} = x_1+9v$\\

$\Rightarrow s_{10} = \dfrac{0.4 + 0.4 + 9v}{2} \cdot 10 = 16.5$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \dfrac{0.8 + 9v}{2} = 1.65 \Leftrightarrow 0.8 + 9v = 3.3 \Leftrightarrow 9v = 2.5 \Leftrightarrow 9v = \dfrac{5}{2} \Leftrightarrow v = \dfrac{5}{18}$\\
