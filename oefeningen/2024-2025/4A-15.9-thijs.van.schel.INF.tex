$\dint \dfrac{x^3}{e^{2x}}dx$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x^3 \\
dv = \dfrac{dx}{e^{2x}}
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 3x^2dx \\
v = -\dfrac{1}{2e^{2x}}
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$= \dfrac{-x^3}{2e^{2x}} + \dfrac{3}{2}\dint \dfrac{x^2}{e^{2x}}dx$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x^2 \\
dv = \dfrac{dx}{e^{2x}}
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 2xdx \\
v = -\dfrac{1}{2e^{2x}}
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$= \dfrac{-x^3}{2e^{2x}} + \dfrac{3}{2}\left[\dfrac{-x^2}{2e^{2x}}+\dint \dfrac{x}{e^{2x}}dx\right]$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x \\
dv = \dfrac{dx}{e^{2x}}
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = dx \\
v = -\dfrac{1}{2e^{2x}}
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$= \dfrac{-x^3}{2e^{2x}} + \dfrac{3}{2}\left[\dfrac{-x^2}{2e^{2x}}+\left(\dfrac{-x}{2e^{2x}}+\dfrac{1}{2}\dint \dfrac{1}{e^{2x}}dx\right)\right]$\\

$= \dfrac{-x^3}{2e^{2x}} - \dfrac{3x^2}{4e^{2x}}- \dfrac{3x}{4e^{2x}}-\dfrac{3}{8e^{2x}} + C$
