$\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\dfrac{\sqrt{x+4}-2}{x}$\\

$=\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\dfrac{\left(\sqrt{x+4}-2\right)\left(\sqrt{x+4}+2\right)}{x\left(\sqrt{x+4}+2\right)}$\\

$=\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\dfrac{x}{x\left(\sqrt{x+4}+2\right)}$\\

$=\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\dfrac{1}{\left(\sqrt{x+4}+2\right)}$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{4}+2}$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{4}$\\
