$f(x) = \cosh x = \dfrac{e^x + e^{-x}}{2}$\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{(e^x + e^{-x})' \cdot 2 - 0}{4} = \dfrac{(e^x - e^{-x}) \cdot 2}{4}$\\

$= \dfrac{e^x - e^{-x}}{2} = \sinh x$\\
