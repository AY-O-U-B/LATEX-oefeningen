$Df\left(\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3}, -\dfrac{2\pi}{3}\right), (4, -3)\right) = \limit_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{f\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3} + 4\lambda, -\dfrac{2\pi}{3} - 3\lambda\right) - f\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3},\dfrac{-2\pi}{3}\right)}{\lambda}$ \\

$= \limit_{\lambda \to 0} \dfrac{\sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3} + 4\lambda\right) \cdot \cos\left(\dfrac{-2\pi}{3} - 3\lambda\right) - \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{4}}{\lambda}$ \\

$\overset{H}{=} \limit_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \left(4\cos\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3} + 4\lambda\right)\cos\left(\dfrac{-2\pi}{3} - 3\lambda\right) + \sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3} + 4\lambda\right)\left(-3\sin\left(\dfrac{-2\pi}{3} - 3\lambda\right)\right)\right)$ \\

$= 4\cos\dfrac{\pi}{3}\cos\dfrac{-2\pi}{3} - \sin\dfrac{\pi}{3}3\sin\dfrac{-2\pi}{3}$ \\

$= -1 - \dfrac{9}{4}$ \\

$= -\dfrac{13}{4}$ \\
