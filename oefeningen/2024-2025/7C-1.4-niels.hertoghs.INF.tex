$ f: \mathbb{R}^2 \rightarrow \mathbb{R} : (x, y) \rightarrow x^4y^3$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 4x^3y^3 $ \\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 3y^2x^4 $ \\

$ \Rightarrow
Df(x,y) = \left(
\begin{array}{cc} 
4x^3 y^3 & 3x^4y^2  
\end{array}\right)$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f^2}{\partial x^2} =12x^2y^3$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f^2}{\partial x\partial y}= 12x^3y^2$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial f^2}{\partial y^2} = 6yx^4$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f^2}{\partial y\partial x}= 12x^3y^2$ \\


$ \Rightarrow
D^2f(x,y) = \left( \begin{array}{ccc}
12y^3x^2 & 12y^2x^3 \\
12x^3y^2 & 6x^4y
\end{array} \right)$\\
