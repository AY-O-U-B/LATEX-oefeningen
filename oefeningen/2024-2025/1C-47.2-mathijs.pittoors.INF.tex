\begin{eqnarray*}
z^5 & = & -1 \\
    & = & 1 \cdot \cis \pi\\
    & = & \cis \left( \dfrac{\pi + 2\pi k}{5} \right), \forall k \in \mathbb{N} \cap [0, 4]. \\
\end{eqnarray*}
Dus de geldige wortels zijn: \\

\begin{eqnarray*}
z_0 & = & \cis \dfrac{\pi}{5} \\
z_1 & = & \cis \dfrac{3\pi}{5} \\
z_2 & = & \cis \dfrac{5\pi}{5} = -1 \\
z_3 & = & \cis \dfrac{7\pi}{5} \\
z_4 & = & \cis \dfrac{9\pi}{5}
\end{eqnarray*}
