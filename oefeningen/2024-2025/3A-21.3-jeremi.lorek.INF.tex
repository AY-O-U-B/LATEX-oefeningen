$A(x) = (x - a) Q_1(x)$  \\

$\Leftrightarrow A'(x) = Q_1(x) + (x - a) Q_1'(x)$  \\

$\Rightarrow A'(a) = 0 \Leftrightarrow Q_1(a) + (a - a) Q_1'(a) = 0$  \\

$\Leftrightarrow Q_1(a) = 0$  \\

$\Rightarrow Q_1(x) = (x - a) Q_2(x)$  \\

$\Rightarrow A(x) = (x - a) Q_1(x) = (x - a)^2 Q_2(x)$  \\

$\Rightarrow A(x)$ deelbaar door $(x - a)^2$  \\
