De functie is discontinu in het punt $a = 1, f(a) = 2$\\

pak $\varepsilon < \dfrac{1}{2}$ \quad stel $\delta > 0$\\

stel $x \in \left](1 - \delta)\vee 0, 1\right[$\\

dan $\left| f(x) - 2\right| = 2 - x > 1 > \dfrac{1}{2} \geq \varepsilon$\\

dus $\left| f(x) - 2\right| \geq \varepsilon$ en $\left|x - 1\right| < \delta$\\

dus $f$ is discontinu in $1$\\
