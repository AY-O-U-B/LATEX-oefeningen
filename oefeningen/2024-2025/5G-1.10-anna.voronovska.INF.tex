Bereken het volume $V$ dat men bekomt door de functie $f(x) = \sqrt{1 + \sin^{2} x}$ op het interval $\left[ -\dfrac{\pi}{2}, \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right]$ om de $X$-as te wentelen.\\

$V = \pi \displaystyle\int_{-\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left( \sqrt{1 + \sin^{2} x} \right)^{2} dx$\\

$= \pi \displaystyle\int_{-\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left(1 + \sin^{2} x\right) dx$\\

$= \pi \displaystyle\int_{-\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left( 1 + \dfrac{1 - \cos(2x)}{2} \right) dx$\\

$= \pi \displaystyle\int_{-\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left( \dfrac{3}{2} - \dfrac{\cos(2x)}{2} \right) dx$\\

$= \pi \left[ \dfrac{3}{2}x - \dfrac{\sin(2x)}{4} \right]_{-\dfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}}$\\

$= \pi \left( \left( \dfrac{3\pi}{4} - \dfrac{\sin(\pi)}{4} \right) - \left( -\dfrac{3\pi}{4} - \dfrac{\sin(-\pi)}{4} \right) \right)$\\

$= \pi \left( \dfrac{3\pi}{4} - 0 - \left(-\dfrac{3\pi}{4}\right) + 0 \right)$\\

$= \pi \left( \dfrac{6\pi}{4} \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{3\pi^{2}}{2}$\\
