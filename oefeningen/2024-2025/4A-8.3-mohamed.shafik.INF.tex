Bereken $\displaystyle\int (3 \sin x - 5 \cos x) dx$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int (3 \sin x - 5 \cos x) dx &=& 3 \displaystyle\int \sin x dx - 5 \displaystyle\int \cos x dx\\
&=& 3 \cdot (-\cos x) - 5 \cdot \sin x + c\\
&=& -3 \cos x - 5 \sin x + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
