$f(x,y)=e^{-x}\sin^2y$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = -e^{-x}\sin^2y \\
	\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 2\sin y\cos ye^{-x}
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$H(x,y) = \left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
	e^{-x}\sin^2y & -2\sin y\cos ye^{-x} \\
	-2\sin y\cos ye^{-x} & -2\sin y\cos ye^{-x}
\end{array}
\right)$\\

$\det H(x,k\pi) = 0$\\

$f(x,y) \ge 0$\\

$f(x,k\pi)=0$ met $x \in \R, k \in \N$\\

$\Rightarrow (x,k\pi)$ is een minimum.\\
