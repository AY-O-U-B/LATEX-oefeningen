$f(x) = \sin\left(\dfrac{1}{2}x\right)$ \\

Kettingregel: stel $u = \dfrac{1}{2}x$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow f'(x) = (\sin u)' \cdot u'$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow f'(x) = \cos u \cdot \dfrac{1}{2}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{\cos\left(\dfrac{1}{2}x\right)}{2}$
