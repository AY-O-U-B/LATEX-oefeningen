$T_4\left(f,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)(x)$ voor $f(x) = \sin x$\\

$f'(x) = \cos x$\\

$f''(x) = -\sin x$\\

$f'''(x) = -\cos x$\\

$f''''(x) = \sin x$\\

$T_4\left(f,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)(x) = \displaystyle\sum^{4}_{k=0}\limits\dfrac{f^{(k)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{k!}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^k$\\

$1-\dfrac{1}{2}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^2+\dfrac{1}{24}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^4$\\
