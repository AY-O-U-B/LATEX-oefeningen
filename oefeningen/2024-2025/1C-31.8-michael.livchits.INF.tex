$\left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}+\dfrac{i}{\sqrt{6}}\right)^8$\\

$r = \sqrt{\left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\right)^2+\left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{6}}\right)^2} = \sqrt{\dfrac{2}{3}}$\\

$\sin\theta = \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{6}}}{\sqrt{\dfrac{2}{3}}} = \dfrac{1}{2}$\\

$\cos\theta = \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}}{\sqrt{\dfrac{2}{3}}} = \sqrt{\dfrac{3}{4}}$\\

$\theta = \dfrac{\pi}{6}$\\
