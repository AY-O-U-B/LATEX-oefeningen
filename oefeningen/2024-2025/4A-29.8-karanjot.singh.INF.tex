$\displaystyle\int (3 - x)^{-\tfrac{3}{2}} dx$ \\

Stel $u = 3 - x \Rightarrow du = -dx$ \\

$\Rightarrow dx = -du$ \\

$= \displaystyle\int u^{-\tfrac{3}{2}} (-du)$ \\

$= -\displaystyle\int u^{-\tfrac{3}{2}} du$ \\

$= -\dfrac{u^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}}{-\tfrac{1}{2}} + C$ \\

$= 2u^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} + C$ \\

$= 2\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{u}} + C$ \\

$= 2\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3 - x}} + C$ \\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int (3 - x)^{-\tfrac{3}{2}} dx = 2(3 - x)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} + C$ \\
