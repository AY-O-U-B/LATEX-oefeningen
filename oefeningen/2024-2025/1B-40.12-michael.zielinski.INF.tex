$z^2 = 8z - 17$ \\
  
$z^2 - 8z + 17 = 0$ \\
  
$z = \dfrac{8 \pm \sqrt{(-8)^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 17}}{2 \cdot 1}$ \\
  
$(-8)^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 17 = 64 - 68 = -4$ \\
  
$z = \dfrac{8 \pm \sqrt{-4}}{2} = \dfrac{8 \pm 2i}{2} = 4 \pm i$ \\
  
$z_1 = 4 + i$,\quad $z_2 = 4 - i$ \\
