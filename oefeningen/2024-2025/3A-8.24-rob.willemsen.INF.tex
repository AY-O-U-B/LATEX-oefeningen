$f(x) = \dfrac{-x^5 + 3x^3 - 2x^2 + x + 1}{x^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{-x^5}{x^2} + \dfrac{3x^3}{x^2} - \dfrac{2x^2}{x^2} + \dfrac{x}{x^2} + \dfrac{1}{x^2}$ \\

$= -x^3 + 3x - 2 + x^{-1} + x^{-2}$ \\

$f'(x) = -3x^2 + 3 - x^{-2} - 2x^{-3}$ \\

$= -3x^2 + 3 - \dfrac{1}{x^2} - \dfrac{2}{x^3}$ \\

$= \dfrac{-3x^5 + 3x^3 - x - 2}{x^3}$ \\
