$ f(x) = e^x \cdot \sin x $ \\

$* f'(x) = \dfrac{d}{dx} \left[ e^x \right] \cdot \sin x + e^x \dfrac{d}{dx} \left[ \sin x \right] $ \\

$ = e^x \cdot \sin x + e^x \cdot \cos x $ \\

$ = e^x (\sin x+ \cos x) $ \\

$ * f'(x) = 0 $ \\

$ \Rightarrow e^x (\sin x+ \cos x)  = 0 $ \\

$ \Rightarrow \sin x+ \cos x  = 0 $ \\

$ \Rightarrow \sin x = -\cos x $ \\

$ \Rightarrow \tan x = -1 $ \\

$ \rightarrow x = -\dfrac{\pi}{4} + n\pi, n \in \mathbb{Z} $ \\
