$ \Rightarrow (3 + 2i)^4 - 2(3 + 2i)^2 + (3 + 2i) + 10$\\

$ = (9 + 12i - 4)(9 + 12i - 4) - 2(9 + 12i - 4) + 3 + 2i + 10 $\\

$ = (5 + 12i)^2 -18 -24i + 8 + 3 + 2i + 10 $\\

$ = 25 + 120i - 144 + 3 - 22i = 98i - 116$\\

$\textbf{Resultaat: 98i - 116}$
