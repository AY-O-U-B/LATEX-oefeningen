$\dfrac{d}{dx}( \log (x \cdot \sin x))$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{x \cdot \sin(x)\cdot \ln 10 } \cdot \dfrac{d}{dx}(x\cdot \sin x)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{x \cdot \sin(x)\cdot \ln 10 } \cdot (\sin x + x\cos x)$\\

$= \dfrac{\sin x + x\cos x}{x \cdot \sin(x)\cdot \ln 10 }$\\
