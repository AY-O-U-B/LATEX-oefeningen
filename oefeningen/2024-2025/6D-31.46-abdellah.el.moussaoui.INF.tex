$\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \dfrac{n^n}{n!}$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \left| \dfrac{\dfrac{\left(n+1\right)^{n+1}}{\left(n+1\right)!}}{\dfrac{n^n}{n!}} \right|$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \left| \dfrac{\left(n+1\right)^{n+1}}{\left(n+1\right)!}\cdot\dfrac{n!}{n^n} \right|$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \dfrac{\left(n+1\right)^{n+1}}{\left(n+1\right)n!}\cdot\dfrac{n!}{n^n}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \dfrac{\left(n+1\right)^n}{n^n}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \left(\dfrac{n+1}{n}\right)^n$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \left(1+\dfrac{1}{n}\right)^n$\\

$ = e$\\

Omdat $e>1$, is de reeks divergent volgens het criterium van d'Alembert.\\
