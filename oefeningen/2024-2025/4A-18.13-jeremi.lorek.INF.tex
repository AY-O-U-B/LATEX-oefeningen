$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{x - \sin x}{2 \sin^2 \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{x}{\sin^2 \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)} dx - \dfrac{1}{2} \int \dfrac{\sin x}{\sin^2 \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)} dx$ \\

$
\left\{
   \begin{array}{l}
       u = x \\
       dv = \dfrac{dx}{\sin^2 \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)} \\
   \end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
   \begin{array}{l}
       du = dx \\
       v = -2 \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) \\
   \end{array}
\right.
$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \left( -2x \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) + 2 \displaystyle \int \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) dx \right) - \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{\sin x}{\sin^2 \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)} dx$ \\

$= -x \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) + \displaystyle \int \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) dx - \dfrac{1}{2} \int \dfrac{2 \sin \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) \cos \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)}{\sin^2 \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)} dx$ \\

$= -x \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) + \displaystyle \int \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) dx - \int \dfrac{\cos \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)}{\sin \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)} dx$ \\

$= -x \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) + \displaystyle \int \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) dx - \int \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) dx$ \\

$= -x \cot \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) + C $ \\
