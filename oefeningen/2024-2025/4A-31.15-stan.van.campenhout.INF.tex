$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x}-1} dx \stackrel{x = t^2}{=} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{t^2}-1} d\left(t^2\right) 
= 2\displaystyle\int \dfrac{t}{t - 1}dt =  2\displaystyle\int \dfrac{t - 1 + 1}{t - 1}dt$\\

$ = 2\displaystyle\int dt + 2\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{t - 1}d\left(t - 1\right) 
= 2t + 2\ln|t - 1| + c  \stackrel{t = \sqrt{x}}{=} 2\sqrt{x} + 2\ln|\sqrt{x} - 1| + c $\\
