$T_4(f, 0)(x)$ voor $f(x) = \dfrac{1}{1+x}$ \\

$\left|\begin{array}{ll}
     &  D\left[ \dfrac{1}{1+x} \right] = \dfrac{-1}{(1+x)^2} \\
     \\
     &  D\left[ \dfrac{-1}{(1+x)^2} \right] = \dfrac{2}{(1+x)^3} \\
     \\
     &  D\left[ \dfrac{2}{(1+x)^3} \right] = \dfrac{-6}{(1+x)^4} \\
     \\
     &  D\left[ \dfrac{-6}{(1+x)^4} \right] = \dfrac{24}{(1+x)^5} \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\
\\

$\begin{array}{ll}
    \Rightarrow T_4(f, a)(x) = & \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{1+a}}{0!} - \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{(1+a)^2}}{1!}(x-a) + \dfrac{\dfrac{2}{(1+a)^3}}{2!}(x-a)^2 \\
    & - \dfrac{\dfrac{6}{(1+a)^4}}{3!}(x-a)^3 + \dfrac{\dfrac{24}{(1+a)^5}}{4!}(x-a)^4 \\
\end{array}$ \\

$T_4(f, 0)(x) = 1 - x + x^2 - x^3 + x^4$ \\
