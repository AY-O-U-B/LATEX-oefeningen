$r\cos\left(\theta+\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right) = -2$\\

$\Leftrightarrow r\left(\cos\theta\cos\dfrac{\pi}{4}-\sin\theta\sin\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right) = -2$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}x-\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}y = -2$\\

$\Leftrightarrow y = x + \sqrt{8}$\\
