$ s_9 = 28 \cdot \dfrac{1 - \left(\tfrac{1}{2}\right)^9}{1 - \tfrac{1}{2}} $ \\

$ = 28 \cdot \dfrac{1 - \tfrac{1}{512}}{\tfrac{1}{2}} $ \\

$ = 28 \cdot \dfrac{\tfrac{511}{512}}{\tfrac{1}{2}} $ \\

$ = 28 \cdot \dfrac{511}{256} $ \\

$ = \dfrac{14308}{256} = \dfrac{3577}{64} $ \\
