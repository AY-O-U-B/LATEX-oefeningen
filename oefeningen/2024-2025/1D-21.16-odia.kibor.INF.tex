$z^5 + 7z^4 + 4z + 28$\\

$-7$ is een nulpunt\\

\begin{tabular}{c|cccccc}
		& $1$ & $7$  & $0$ & $0$ & $4$ & $28$\\
$-7$ 	&     & $-7$ & $0$ & $0$ & $0$ & $-28$\\ \hline
		& $1$ & $0$  & $0$ & $0$ & $4$ & $0$\\
\end{tabular}\\

$\Rightarrow (z + 7)(z^4 + 4)$\\

Ontbindt $z^4 + 4$\\

$z^4 + 4 \rightarrow z^4 = -4 \rightarrow z^2 = \pm 2i$\\

Voor $z^2 = 2i$\\

$\Rightarrow (x + yi)^2 = x^2 - y^2 + 2xyi$\\

$\left\{
	\begin{array}{lll}
		x^2 - y^2 & = & 0\\
		2xy & = &  2
	\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left\{
	\begin{array}{lll}
		x^2 - y^2 & = & 0\\
		xy & = & 1\\
        (xy < 0)
	\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left\{
	\begin{array}{lll}
		x^2 - y^2 & = & 0\\
		x^2 (-y^2) & = & -1
	\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\lambda^2 -1 = 0 \Rightarrow \lambda^2 = 1 \Rightarrow \lambda = \pm 1$\\

$x^2 = 1 \Rightarrow x = \pm 1$\\

$-y^2 = -1 \Rightarrow y = \pm 1$\\

Oplossing voor 2i: $1 + i$ of $-1 - i$\\

Voor $z^2 = -2i$\\

$\Rightarrow (x + yi)^2 = x^2 - y^2 + 2xyi$\\

$\left\{
	\begin{array}{lll}
		x^2 - y^2 & = & 0\\
		2xy & = &  -2
	\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left\{
	\begin{array}{lll}
		x^2 - y^2 & = & 0\\
		xy & = & -1\\
        (xy > 0)
	\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left\{
	\begin{array}{lll}
		x^2 - y^2 & = & 0\\
		x^2 (-y^2) & = & -1
	\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\lambda^2 -1 = 0 \Rightarrow \lambda^2 = 1 \Rightarrow \lambda = \pm 1$\\

$x^2 = 1 \Rightarrow x = \pm 1$\\

$-y^2 = -1 \Rightarrow y = \pm 1$\\

Oplossing voor -2i: $1 - i$ of $-1 + i$\\



\textbf{nulpunten:} $-7, (1 + i), (-1 - i), (1 - i), (-1 + i)$
