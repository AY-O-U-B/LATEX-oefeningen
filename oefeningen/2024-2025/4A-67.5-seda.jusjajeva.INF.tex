$\integral\dfrac{x}{x^4 + 2x^2 + 2}dx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{x}{x^4 + 2x^2 + 1 + 1}dx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{x}{(x^2 + 1)^2 + 1}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $t = x^2 + 1 \Rightarrow dt = 2xdx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\integral\dfrac{1}{t^2 + 1}dt$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\operatorname{Bgtan} t + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\operatorname{Bgtan} (x^2 + 1) + c$ \\
