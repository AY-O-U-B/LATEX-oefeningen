    Bereken de volgende onbepaalde integralen door substitutie:\\
	
	$\dint \dfrac{2dx}{(2x + 3)^4} $\\
	
	$ u = 2x + 3 $\\
	
	$du = 2dx \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{du}{2}$\\
	
	$\dint \dfrac{2dx}{(2x + 3)^4} = \dint \dfrac{2 \cdot \tfrac{du}{2}}{u^4} = \dint u^{-4} du$\\
		
	$= \dfrac{u^{-3}}{-3} +c= -\dfrac{1}{3} u^{-3}+c$\\
	
	$= -\dfrac{1}{3(2x + 3)^3} + C$\\
