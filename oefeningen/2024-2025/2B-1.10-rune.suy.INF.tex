Schrijf in functie van $x$: $\cot(\Bgsin x)$\\

$\alpha = \Bgsin x\Leftrightarrow x = \sin \alpha$ en $\alpha \in \left[ \dfrac{-\pi}{2}, \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right]$\\

$\cot^2\alpha + 1 =  \dfrac{1}{\sin^2\alpha}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \cot^2 \alpha = \dfrac{1}{\sin^2\alpha} - 1 = \dfrac{1}{x^2}-1$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \cot \alpha = \sqrt{\dfrac{1}{x^2} - 1} = \dfrac{\sqrt{1-x^2}}{x}$\\
