$(-3, -11, -17, -15, 1, 37, 99, ...)$\\

$\Rightarrow (-8, -6, 2, 16, 36, 62, ...)$\\

$\Rightarrow (2, 8, 14, 20, 26, ...)$\\

$\Rightarrow (6, 6, 6, 6, ...)$\\

$x_n = an^3 + bn^2 + cn + d$\\

$\Rightarrow \left\{
    \begin{array}{lcr}
         a +  b +  c + d & = & -3\\
        8a + 4b + 2c + d & = & -11\\
       27a + 9b + 3c + d & = & -17\\
       64a + 16b + 4c + d & = & -15\\
    \end{array}
\right. \Rightarrow \left\{
    \begin{array}{lcr}
         7a + 3b + c & = & -8\\
        19a + 5b + c & = & -6\\
        37a + 7b + c & = & 2\\
    \end{array}
\right. \Rightarrow \left\{
    \begin{array}{lcr}
        12a + 2b & = & 2\\
        18a + 2b & = & 8\\
    \end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow 6a = 6 \Rightarrow a = 1$\\

$\Rightarrow b = -5 \Rightarrow c = 0 \Rightarrow d = 1$\\

$x_n = n^3 - 5n^2 + 1$\\

$x_8 = 8^3 - 5(8^2) + 1 = 512 - 320 + 1 = 193$\\
