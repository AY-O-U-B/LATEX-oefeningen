$ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{17x}{4x^4 +15x^2 -4}dx = \dfrac{17}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{4x^4 +15x^2 -4}d\left(x^2\right) 
\stackrel{x^2 = t}{=} \dfrac{17}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{4t^2 +15t -4}dt $\\

$ = \dfrac{17}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{\left(4t - 1\right)\left(t+4\right)}dt $\\

$ \rightarrow \dfrac{1}{\left(4t - 1\right)\left(t+4\right)} = \dfrac{A}{4t-1}+\dfrac{B}{t+4} $\\

$ \Rightarrow A = \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{1}{4}+4} = \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{17}{4}} = \dfrac{4}{17} $\\

$ \Rightarrow B = \dfrac{1}{4\cdot-4-1} = -\dfrac{1}{17} $\\

$ = \dfrac{17}{2}\cdot\dfrac{4}{17}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{4t-1}dt - \dfrac{17}{2}\cdot\dfrac{1}{17}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{t+4}dt 
= \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{4t-1}d\left(4t\right)-\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{t+4}dt $\\

$ = \dfrac{\ln|4t-1|}{2} - \dfrac{\ln|t+4|}{2}+c \stackrel{t = x^2}{=} \dfrac{\ln|4x^2-1|}{2} - \dfrac{\ln|x^2+4|}{2}+c $\\
