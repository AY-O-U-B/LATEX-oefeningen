$\dint\limits_1^2 (x^2+4)^{\tfrac{1}{3}} xdx = \dfrac{1}{2}\dint\limits_1^2 (x^2+4)^{\tfrac{1}{3}}d(x^2+4)$\\

$= \left[\dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{(x^2+4)^{\tfrac{4}{3}}}{\dfrac{4}{3}}\right]^2_1=\left[\dfrac{3(x^2+4)^{\tfrac{4}{3}}}{8}\right]^2_1$\\

$= \dfrac{3(2^2+4)^{\tfrac{4}{3}}}{8}-\dfrac{3(1+4)^{\tfrac{4}{3}}}{8}=6-\dfrac{3\cdot5^{\tfrac{4}{3}}}{8}$
