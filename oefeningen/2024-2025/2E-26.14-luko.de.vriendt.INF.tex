BVW: \\

$x+4 > 0 \Rightarrow x > -4$ \\

$x-2 > 0 \Rightarrow x > 2$ \\

$x > 0 $ \\

$\log_3 (x+4) + \log_3 (x-2) = 2\log_3 (x)$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_3 (x+4) + \log_3 (x-2) + \log_3 \left(x^{-2}\right) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_3 \left(\dfrac{(x+4)(x-2)}{x^2}\right) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_3 \left(\dfrac{x^2-2x+4x-8}{x^2}\right) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \dfrac{x^2+2x-8}{x^2} = 1$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x^2+2x-8 = x^2$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 2x-8 = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x = 4$ \\
