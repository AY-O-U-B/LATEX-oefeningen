$f(x,y) = 
\left\{ 
\begin{array}{ll} 
\dfrac{x^2y^2(x+y)}{x^4+y^4} & \text{als } (x,y) \neq (0,0)\\ 
0 & \text{als } (x,y) = (0,0) 
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

Continuïteit:\\

Stel $x = r\cos\theta$ en $y = r \sin \theta$\\

$\dlim_{(x,y)\rightarrow (0,0)} f(x,y) = \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{(r^2 \cos^2 \theta)(r^2 \sin^2 \theta)(r\cos\theta + r\sin\theta)}{r^4 \cos^4 \theta + r^4 \sin^4 \theta}$\\

$= \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{r^5 \cos^2 \theta \sin^2 \theta (\cos\theta + \sin\theta)}{r^4 (\cos^4 \theta + \sin^4 \theta)}$\\

$= \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} r \left( \dfrac{\cos^2 \theta \sin^2 \theta (\cos\theta + \sin\theta)}{\cos^4 \theta + \sin^4 \theta} \right)$\\

$\Rightarrow f$ is continu in $(0,0)$ \\

Partiële afgeleiden:\\

$\left\{ 
\begin{array}{l} 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(0,0) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(\lambda,0) - f(0,0)}{\lambda} = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{0}{\lambda} = 0 \\ 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(0,0) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(0,\lambda) - f(0,0)}{\lambda} = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{0}{\lambda} = 0 
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow f$ is partieel afleidbaar in $(0,0)$\\

Afleidbaarheid:\\

Stel $\overline{h} = (h_1,h_2)$\\

$Df(\overline{0}, \overline{h}) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(\lambda h_1, \lambda h_2) - f(0,0)}{\lambda}$\\

$= \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{(\lambda^2 h_1^2)(\lambda^2 h_2^2)(\lambda h_1 + \lambda h_2)}{\lambda^4 h_1^4 + \lambda^4 h_2^4}}{\lambda}$\\

$= \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\lambda^5 h_1^2 h_2^2 (h_1 + h_2)}{\lambda (\lambda^4 h_1^4 + \lambda^4 h_2^4)}$\\

$= \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\lambda^5 h_1^2 h_2^2 (h_1 + h_2)}{\lambda^5 (h_1^4 + h_2^4)} = \dfrac{h_1^2 h_2^2 (h_1 + h_2)}{h_1^4 + h_2^4}$\\

$\Rightarrow f$ is afleidbaar in $(0,0)$\\

Differentieerbaarheid:\\

Voor differentieerbaarheid in $(0,0)$ moet gelden: $Df(\overline{0}, \overline{h}) = \left(\begin{array}{c c} \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(0,0) & \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(0,0) \end{array}\right) \left(\begin{array}{c} h_1 \\ h_2 \end{array}\right)$\\

$\left(\begin{array}{c c} 0 & 0 \end{array}\right) \left(\begin{array}{c} h_1 \\ h_2 \end{array}\right) = 0$\\

$Df(\overline{0}, \overline{h}) = \dfrac{h_1^2 h_2^2 (h_1 + h_2)}{h_1^4 + h_2^4} \neq 0$\\

$\Rightarrow f$ is niet differentieerbaar in $(0,0)$\\
