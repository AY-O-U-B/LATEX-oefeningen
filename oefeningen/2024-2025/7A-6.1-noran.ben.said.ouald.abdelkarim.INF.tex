$\lim\limits_{(x,y,z) \to (0,0,0)} \dfrac{x + y + z}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

Stel $x = y = z = t$ \\

$\lim\limits_{t \to 0} \dfrac{t + t + t}{t^2 + t^2 + t^2} = \dfrac{3t}{3t^2} = \dfrac{1}{t} = \infty$ \\
