$ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x^2 +3x + 2}dx = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{\left(x+2\right)\left(x+1\right)}dx $\\

$ \rightarrow \dfrac{1}{\left(x+2\right)\left(x+1\right)} = \dfrac{A}{x+2} + \dfrac{B}{x+1} $\\

$ \Rightarrow A = \dfrac{1}{-2+1} = -1 $\\

$ \Rightarrow B = \dfrac{1}{-1+2} = 1 $\\

$ = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x+1}dx - \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x+2}dx = \ln|x+1| - \ln|x+2| +c $\\
