$\integral\dfrac{9x^3 - 21x^2 + 17x}{9x^2 - 12x + 5}dx$ \\

$\integral\dfrac{A(x)}{B(x)}dx = \integral Q(x)dx + \integral\dfrac{R(x)}{B(x)}dx$ \\

$\begin{array}{cccc|cccc}
    9x^3 & -21x^2 & +17x &    & 9x^2 & -12x & +5 \\ 
                              \cline{5-7}
    9x^3 & -12x^2 & +5x  &    &      & x    & -1 \\ 
    \cline{1-3}
         & -9x^2  & +12x &    &      &      &    \\
         & -9x^2  & +12x & -5 &      &      &    \\ 
         \cline{2-4}
         &        &      & 5  &      &      &    \\
\end{array}$ \\

$\Rightarrow \integral\left(x - 1\right)dx + \integral\dfrac{5}{9x^2 - 12x + 5}dx$ \\

$= \integral x dx - \integral dx + 5\integral\dfrac{dx}{9x^2 - 12x + 5}dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2} - x + 5\integral \dfrac{dx}{9x^2 - 12x + 4 + 1}dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2} - x + 5\integral \dfrac{dx}{(3x - 2)^2 + 1}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $t = 3x - 2 \Rightarrow dt = 3dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2} - x + 5\integral \dfrac{1}{t^2 + 1}\cdot\dfrac{1}{3}dt$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2} - x + \dfrac{5}{3}\operatorname{Bgtan}t + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2} - x + \dfrac{5}{3}\operatorname{Bgtan}(3x - 2) + c$ \\
