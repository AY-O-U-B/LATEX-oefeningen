$s_7 $ van $ (-2,4,-8,\ldots)$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
v = 4 - (-2) = 6 \\
= -8 - 4 = -12
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow $ geen rekenkundige rij\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
r = \dfrac{4}{-2} = -2 \\

r = \dfrac{-8}{4} = -2
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow $ meetkundige rij met $ x_1=-2 $ en $ r=-2$\\

$\Rightarrow s_n = x_1 \dfrac{1-q^n}{1-q}$\\

$s_n = -2 \cdot \dfrac{1-(-2)^n}{1-(-2)}$\\

$s_7 = -2 \cdot \dfrac{1-(-2)^7}{1-(-2)}$\\

$= -2 \cdot \dfrac{1-(-128)}{1-(-2)}$\\

$= -2 \cdot \dfrac{129}{3}$\\

$= -86$\\
