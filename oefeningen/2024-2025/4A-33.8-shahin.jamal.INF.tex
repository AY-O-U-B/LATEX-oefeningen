$\displaystyle\int e^x\sqrt[3]{e^x-1}dx$\\

$u = e^x-1 \Rightarrow du = e^x dx$\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \sqrt[3]{u}du = \displaystyle\int u^{\tfrac{1}{3}}du = \dfrac{3}{4}u^{\tfrac{4}{3}} + c$\\

$= \dfrac{3}{4}(e^x-1)^{\tfrac{4}{3}} + c$\\
