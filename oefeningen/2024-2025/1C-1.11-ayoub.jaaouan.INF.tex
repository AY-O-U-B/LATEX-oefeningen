\textbf{De cartesische coordinaten van} $\left(1, \dfrac{3\pi}{2}\right)$

$\left \{\begin{array}{l} x = r\cos\theta \\ y = r\sin\theta \end{array}\right. \Rightarrow \left \{\begin{array}{l} x = \cos\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}\right) \\ y = \sin\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}\right) \end{array}\right.$ \\\\



$\Rightarrow x = \cos\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = 0$\\\\

$\Rightarrow y = \sin\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}\right) = \sin\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = -1$\\\\

\textit{\textbf{Resultaat:}} $\left(0, -1\right)$
