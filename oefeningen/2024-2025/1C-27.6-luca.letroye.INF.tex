$\left(4\cis \dfrac{\pi}{5} \right)^5 : \left(8\cis \dfrac{\pi}{4} \right)^2 $\\

teller: $4^5 \cis \dfrac{\pi}{5} \cdot 5 = 1024 \cis \pi$\\

noemer: $8^2 \cis \dfrac{\pi}{4} \cdot 2 = 64 \cis \dfrac{\pi}{2}$\\

$\Rightarrow  1024 \cis \pi : 64 \cis \dfrac{\pi}{2}$\\

$ = \dfrac{1024}{64} \cis \left(\pi - \dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = 16 \cis \dfrac{\pi}{2}$\\

$z = 16(0+1i) = 16i$
