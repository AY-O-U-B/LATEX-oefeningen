$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \left(\dfrac{\sqrt{2+x}-\sqrt{2-x}}{x}\right)$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \left(\dfrac{(\sqrt{2+x}-\sqrt{2-x})(\sqrt{2+x}+\sqrt{2-x})}{x(\sqrt{2+x}+\sqrt{2-x})}\right)$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \left(\dfrac{\sqrt{2+x}^2-\sqrt{2-x}^2}{x(\sqrt{2+x}+\sqrt{2-x})}\right)$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \left(\dfrac{(2+x)-(2-x)}{x(\sqrt{2+x}+\sqrt{2-x})}\right)$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \left(\dfrac{2x}{x(\sqrt{2+x}+\sqrt{2-x})}\right)$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \left(\dfrac{2}{\sqrt{2+x}+\sqrt{2-x}}\right)$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{2+0}+\sqrt{2-0}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{2}+\sqrt{2}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{2\sqrt{2}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}$ \\
