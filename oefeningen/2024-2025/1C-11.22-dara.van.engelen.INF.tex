$ \dfrac{1}{2} - \dfrac{i\sqrt{3}}{2} $\\

$ |z| = \sqrt{\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^2 + \left( \dfrac{-\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)^2} = \sqrt{\dfrac{1}{4} + \dfrac{3}{4}} = \sqrt{1} = 1$\\

arg(z)  $= \operatorname{Bgtan}\left( \dfrac{-\sqrt{3}}{2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2}\right) = \operatorname{Bgtan}\left( -\sqrt{3}\right) = \dfrac{-\pi}{3}$\\

goniometrische vorm: $ z = \operatorname{cis} \left (\dfrac{-\pi}{3}\right)$
