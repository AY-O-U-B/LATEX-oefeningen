$\dint \fourthroot{5x^3} dx$ \\

$= \fourthroot{5} \dint \fourthroot{x^3} dx$ \\

$= \fourthroot{5} \dint x^{\tfrac{3}{4}} dx$ \\

$= \fourthroot{5} \cdot \dfrac{\fourthroot{x^7}}{\dfrac{7}{4}} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{4\fourthroot{5} x \fourthroot{x^3}}{7} + c$ \\
