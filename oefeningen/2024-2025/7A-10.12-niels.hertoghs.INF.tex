$f(x,y,z) = x^2 + y^3 + z^4$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 2x$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 3y^2$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial z} = 4z^3$\\

$\Rightarrow 
\left(\begin{array}{ccc}
2x & 3y^2 & 4z^3
\end{array}\right)$
