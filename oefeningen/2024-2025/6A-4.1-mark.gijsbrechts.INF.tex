Zoek de eerste term van de rekenkundige rij met \\

$ x_{3} = 27 $ \\

$ x_{9} = 3 $ \\


$ \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    x_{3} = 27 = x_{1} + 2v \\
    x_{9} = 3 =  x_{1} + 8v  
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    x_{1} = 27 - 2v \\
    \left( 27 - 2v \right) + 8v = 3 
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    x_{1} = 27 - 2v \\
    6v = -24 
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    x_{1} = 35 \\
    v = -4 
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$ x_{1} = 35 $ \\
