$ = \left[\dfrac{x}{2}\right]_\pi^{2\pi} + \left[\dfrac{1}{4}\sin 2x\right]_\pi^{2\pi} = \pi - \dfrac{\pi}{2} + 0 - 0 = \dfrac{\pi}{2} $\\
