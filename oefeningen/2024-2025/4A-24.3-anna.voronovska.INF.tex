$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x-1}{x^2} \ln xdx = \displaystyle\int \left( \dfrac{x}{x^2} - \dfrac{1}{x^2} \right) \ln xdx = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\ln x}{x}dx - \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\ln x}{x^2}dx $\\

Eerste integraal:\\

$u = \ln x \Rightarrow du = \dfrac{1}{x}dx$:\\

$ \displaystyle\int udu = \dfrac{u^2}{2} = \dfrac{\ln^2 x}{2}$\\

Tweede integraal:\\

$\displaystyle\int \ln x \cdot x^{-2}dx$\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    u = \ln x \\ 
    dv = x^{-2} \, dx 
\end{array} \right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    du = \dfrac{1}{x} \, dx \\ 
    v = -\dfrac{1}{x} 
\end{array} \right.$\\

$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\ln x}{x^2}dx = \ln x \cdot \left( -\dfrac{1}{x} \right) - \displaystyle\int \left( -\dfrac{1}{x} \right) \cdot \dfrac{1}{x}dx = -\dfrac{\ln x}{x} + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x^2}dx = -\dfrac{\ln x}{x} - \dfrac{1}{x}$\\

Dus:\\

$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x-1}{x^2} \ln xdx = \dfrac{\ln^2 x}{2} - \left( -\dfrac{\ln x}{x} - \dfrac{1}{x} \right) + c = \dfrac{\ln^2 x}{2} + \dfrac{\ln x}{x} + \dfrac{1}{x} + c$
