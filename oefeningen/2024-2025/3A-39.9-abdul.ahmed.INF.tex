$\dfrac{d}{dx} \left((\sin x)^{\cos x}\right)$\\

$=\dfrac{d}{dx} \left(e^ {\cos x \cdot\ln(\sin x)}\right)$\\

$=e^ {\cos x \cdot\ln(\sin x)}\left(-\sin x \cdot \ln (\sin x) +\dfrac{\cos x}{\sin x} \cdot \cos x\right)$\\

$=(\sin x)^{\cos x}\left(\cot x \cdot \cos x -\sin x \cdot \ln (\sin x)\right)$\\
