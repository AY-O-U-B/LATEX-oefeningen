Bereken $\displaystyle\int \cosh^3 x dx$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int \cosh^3 x dx &=& \displaystyle\int \cosh x \cdot \cosh^2 x dx\\
&=& \displaystyle\int \cosh x (1 + \sinh^2 x) dx\\
\end{eqnarray*}

Substitueer $t = \sinh x$, dan is $dt = \cosh x dx$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
&=& \displaystyle\int (1 + t^2) dt\\
&=& t + \dfrac{t^3}{3} + c\\
&=& \sinh x + \dfrac{\sinh^3 x}{3} + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
