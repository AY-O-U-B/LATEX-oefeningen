$f(x,y) = (x^2 - y^2, 2xy)$\\

$Df(x,y) = \left(\begin{array}{c c} \dfrac{\partial}{\partial x}(x^2 - y^2) & \dfrac{\partial}{\partial y}(x^2 - y^2) \\ \dfrac{\partial}{\partial x}(2xy) & \dfrac{\partial}{\partial y}(2xy) \end{array}\right)$\\

$= \left(\begin{array}{c c} 2x & -2y \\ 2y & 2x \end{array}\right)$\\

$\det(Df(x,y)) = (2x)(2x) - (-2y)(2y) = 4x^2 + 4y^2 = 4(x^2 + y^2)$\\

$\Rightarrow \det(Df(x,y)) \neq 0$, $\forall (x,y) \in \mathbb{R}^2 \setminus \{(0,0)\}$\\

$\Rightarrow (Df^{-1})(f(x,y)) = (Df(x,y))^{-1} = \dfrac{1}{4(x^2 + y^2)} \left(\begin{array}{c c} 2x & 2y \\ -2y & 2x \end{array}\right)$\\

$= \left(\begin{array}{c c} \dfrac{2x}{4(x^2 + y^2)} & \dfrac{2y}{4(x^2 + y^2)} \\ \dfrac{-2y}{4(x^2 + y^2)} & \dfrac{2x}{4(x^2 + y^2)} \end{array}\right)$\\
