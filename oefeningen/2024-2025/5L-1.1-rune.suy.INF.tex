$f'(x) = \dfrac{4}{3} \Rightarrow f'(x)^2 = \dfrac{16}{9} \Rightarrow f'(x)^2 + 1 = \dfrac{25}{9}$\\

$S = 2\pi \Int_0^3 \left| \dfrac{4}{3}x \right| \sqrt{\dfrac{25}{9}}dx = \dfrac{20}{9}\pi \left[x^2\right]_0^3 = 20\pi$\\
