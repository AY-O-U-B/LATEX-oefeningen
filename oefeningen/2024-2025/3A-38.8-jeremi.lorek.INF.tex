$f(x) = \log_x 2 = \dfrac{\ln 2}{\ln x}$  \\

$\dfrac{d}{dx} \left( \dfrac{\ln 2}{\ln x} \right) = \ln 2 \cdot \dfrac{d}{dx} \left( \dfrac{1}{\ln x} \right)$  \\

$= \ln 2 \cdot \dfrac{d}{dx} \left( (\ln x)^{-1} \right)$  \\

$= \ln 2 \cdot \dfrac{-1}{(\ln x)^2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{x}$  \\

$= \dfrac{-\ln 2}{x (\ln x)^2}$  \\
