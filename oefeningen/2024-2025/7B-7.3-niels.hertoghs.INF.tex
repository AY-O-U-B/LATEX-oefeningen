$\underline{f}:\mathbb{R}^2 \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^2:(x,y) \mid\rightarrow (\sin(xy),\sinh(xy))$\\

$Df =\left( \begin{array}{cc}
	y \cos(xy) & x \cos(xy) \\
	y \cosh(xy) & x \cosh(xy)
\end{array}\right)$\\

$\det Df = xy \cos(xy)\cosh(xy) - xy \cos(xy)\cosh(xy) = 0$\\
