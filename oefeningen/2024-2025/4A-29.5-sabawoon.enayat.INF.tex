\newcommand{\dint}{\displaystyle\int}

\normalsize

Opgave: $\dint (3x - 2)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} dx$\\

$\Rightarrow t = 3x - 2$, $t' = 3 \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{dt}{3}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{3}\dint t^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} dx = \dfrac{1}{3}\dfrac{t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{\dfrac{1}{2}} + c$\\

$= \dfrac{2}{3}(3x - 2)^{\tfrac{1}{2}} + c = \dfrac{2 \sqrt{3x - 2}}{3} + c$\\
