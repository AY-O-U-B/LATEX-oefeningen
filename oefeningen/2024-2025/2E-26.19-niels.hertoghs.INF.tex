$\log_2 x = \log_x 2 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_2 x = \dfrac{\log_2 2}{\log_2 x} $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_2 x = \dfrac{1}{\log_2 x} $\\

$\Leftrightarrow (\log_2 x)^2 = 1 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_2 x = \pm 1$\\

$\Rightarrow x_1 = 2$ \\

$\Rightarrow x_2 = \dfrac{1}{2}$\\

$\Rightarrow x \in \left\{\dfrac{1}{2}, 2\right\}$ \\
