REchterzijde $ = \operatorname{Bgtan}\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{3} + \dfrac{1}{7}}{1 - \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \dfrac{1}{7}}\right) $\\

$ = \operatorname{Bgtan} \left(\dfrac{\dfrac{10}{21}}{1 - \dfrac{1}{21}}\right) $\\

\medskip

$ = \operatorname{Bgtan} \left( \dfrac{10}{21} \cdot \dfrac{20}{21} \right) $\\

$ = \operatorname{Bgtan} \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right) $\\

Linkerzijde $ = \operatorname{Bgtan} \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right) $\\

$ \Rightarrow $ L.L $=$ R.L \\
