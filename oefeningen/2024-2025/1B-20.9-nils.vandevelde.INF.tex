$\dfrac{(1+i)^2}{(1-i)^3} + \dfrac{(1-i)^2}{(1+i)^3} = 
\dfrac{1+2i-1}{(1-2i-1)(1-i)} + \dfrac{1-2i-1}{(1+2i-1)(1+i)}$\\

$= \dfrac{2i}{-2i(1-i)} + \dfrac{-2i}{2i(1+i)} = \dfrac{1}{-1+i} + \dfrac{-1}{1+i}$\\

$= \dfrac{2}{(-1-1) + (-1+1)i} = \dfrac{2}{-2} = -1$\\
