Som $3(1 + i)$ en Product $5i$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
z_1 + z_2 = 3 + 3i \\
z_1 z_2 = 5i
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow -z_1^2 + (3 + 3i)z_1 - 5i = 0$ \\

$D = (3 + 3i)^2 - 4(-1)(-5i) = -2i = 2\left(\cos\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}\right) + \sin\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}\right)i\right)$ \\

$\Rightarrow \sqrt{D} = \sqrt{2}\left(\cos\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4} + k\pi\right) + \sin\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4} + k\pi\right)i\right)$ met $k \in \{0,1\}$ \\

$\Rightarrow \sqrt{D} = 1 - i \vee -1 + i$ \\

$z_1 = \dfrac{-3 - 3i \pm \sqrt{D}}{-2} = \dfrac{-4 - 2i}{-2} \vee \dfrac{-2 - 4i}{-2}$ \\

$z_1 = 1 + 2i, z_2 = i + 2$ \\
