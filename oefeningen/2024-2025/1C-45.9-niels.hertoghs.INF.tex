\textbf{Opgave:} \\

\textbf{Oplossing:} \\

$z^3 = 1-i = \sqrt{2} \cis \left(\dfrac{7\pi}{4}\right)$ \\

$\sqrt[3]{r} = \sqrt[3]{\sqrt{2}} = \sqrt[6]{2}$\\

$\theta = \dfrac{\dfrac{7\pi}{12}+k2\pi}{3} \text{ met } k \in \{0,1,2\}$\\

$\Rightarrow z = \sqrt[6]{2} \cis\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{7\pi}{12}+k2\pi}{3}\right) \text{ met } k \in \{0,1,2\}$ \\

$z_0 = \sqrt[6]{2} \cis\left(\dfrac{7\pi}{12}\right)$ \\
