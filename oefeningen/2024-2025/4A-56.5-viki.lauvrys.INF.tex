$ \displaystyle\int \sqrt{1-\cos x} dx $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int \sqrt{2 \sin^2\left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) } dx $ \\

$ = \pm \sqrt{2} \displaystyle\int \sin \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) } dx $ \\

Stel $ t = \dfrac{x}{2} \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{dx}{2} $ \\

$ = \pm 2 \sqrt{2} \displaystyle\int \sin (t) dt $ \\

$ = \pm 2 \sqrt{2} \cos (t) + C $ \\

$ = \pm 2 \sqrt{2} \cos \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) + C $ \\
