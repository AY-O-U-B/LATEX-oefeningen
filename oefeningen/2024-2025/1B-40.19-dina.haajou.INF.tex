We lossen op in $\mathbb{C}$: $2z(z + 1) + z^2 + 1 = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\ 2z^2 + 2z + z^2 + 1 = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\ 3z^2 + 2z + 1 = 0$ \\

$D = b^2 - 4ac = 2^2 - 4 \cdot 3 \cdot 1 = 4 - 12 = -8$ \\

$\sqrt{D} = \sqrt{-8} = \sqrt{8}i = 2\sqrt{2}i$ \\

$z = \dfrac{-2 \pm 2\sqrt{2}i}{6} = \dfrac{-1 \pm \sqrt{2}i}{3}$ \\

Oplossingen: $z_1 = \dfrac{-1 + \sqrt{2}i}{3}$ en $z_2 = \dfrac{-1 - \sqrt{2}i}{3}$ \\
