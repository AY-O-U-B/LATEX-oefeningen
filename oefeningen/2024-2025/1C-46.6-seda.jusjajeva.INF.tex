$\Leftrightarrow z^4 = 16\cdot\operatorname{cis}(k\cdot2\pi)\indent(k \in \mathbb{Z})$ \\

$z_0 = 2 \cdot\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{0\cdot2\pi}{4}\right) = 2$ \\

$z_1 = 2 \cdot\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{1\cdot 2\pi}{4}\right) = 2i$ \\

$z_3 = 2 \cdot\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{3\cdot 2\pi}{4}\right) = -2i$ \\
