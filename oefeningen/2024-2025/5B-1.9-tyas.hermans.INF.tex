$\dint_1^{+\infty}\dfrac{dx}{x\sqrt{x}}$\\

$=\dint_1^{+\infty} x^{\tfrac{-3}{2}}dx$\\

$= \lim\limits_{c\rightarrow +\infty} \left[\dfrac{-2}{\sqrt{x}}\right]_1^c$\\

$= \lim\limits_{c\rightarrow +\infty} \left(\dfrac{-2}{\sqrt{c}} + \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{1}}\right)$\\

$= 2$\\
