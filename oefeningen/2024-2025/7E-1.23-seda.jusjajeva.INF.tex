$f(x, y) = 4xy - 2x^4 - y^2$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{l}
	\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x, y) = 4y - 8x^3 \\
	\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(x, y) = 4x - 2y
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    -8x^3 + 4y = 0 \\
    4x - 2y = 0
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    -8x^3 + 8x = 0 \\
    y = 2x
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    8x(-x^2 + 1) = 0 \\
    y = 2x
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    8x = 0 \vee -x^2 + 1 = 0 \\
    y = 2x
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    x = 0 \vee x = -1 \vee x = 1 \\
    y = 0 \vee y = -2 \vee y = 2
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\nabla f(x, y) = o$ als $ (x, y) = (-1, -2) \vee (x, y) = (0,0) \vee (x, y) = (1, 2)$ \\

Er zijn dus 3 kritieke punten $(-1, -2)$, $(0, 0)$ en $(1, 2)$ \\

$\Delta(x, y) = \left|\begin{array}{cc}
    -24x^2 & 4  \\
    4      & -2
\end{array}\right| = 48x^2 - 16$ \\

$\Delta(-1, -2) = \Delta(1, 2) = 48 - 16 = 32 > 0 \Rightarrow$ minima/maxima \\

$\left(A = -24x^2\right)$ \\

$A(-1, -2) = A(1, 2) = -24\cdot1 = -24 < 0 \Rightarrow$ maxima \\

$\Delta(0, 0) = -16 < 0 \Rightarrow$ zadelpunt \\
