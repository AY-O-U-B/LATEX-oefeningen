$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{e^{2x}}{1 + e^{4x}}dx $ \\

Stel $ t = e^{2x} \Rightarrow dt = 2e^{2x} dx $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{(1 + t^2)}$ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \operatorname{Bgtan}(t) + C $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \operatorname{Bgtan} \left( e^{2x} \right) + C $ \\
