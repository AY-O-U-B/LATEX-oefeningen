Opgave: Los de volgende vergelijking op in $\mathbb{C}$\\

$ z^4 - 5(1 + 2i)z^2 + 24 - 10i = 0 $\\

Stel $g = z^2$. Dit geeft:\\

$g^2 - (5 + 10i)g + (24 - 10i) = 0$\\


\begin{eqnarray*}
D &=& (-(5 + 10i))^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot (24 - 10i)\\
    &=& -171 + 140i
\end{eqnarray*}


Resolvente vergelijking opstellen:\\


$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 

	x^2 - y^2 = -171\\ 
	
	2xy = 140 \Rightarrow x^2(-y^2) = -(70)^2 = -4900 
	
\end{array} \right. $


$\lambda^2 + 171\lambda - 4900 = 0 $

\begin{eqnarray*}
D_{\lambda} &=&171^2 - 4(1)(-4900) \\
		&=& 29241 + 19600 = 48841
\end{eqnarray*}


$\lambda_1 = \dfrac{-171 +  \sqrt{48841}}{2}$ en $\lambda_2 = \dfrac{-171 -  \sqrt{48841}}{2}\Rightarrow \lambda_1 = 25$
 en $\lambda_2 = -196 $ \\

Dus $x^2 = 25$ en $y^2 = 196$  \\

 $xy = 70 > 0$, dus:\\

$g_1 = \dfrac{5 + 10i + (5 + 14i)}{2}\  en \   g_2 = \dfrac{5 + 10i - (5 + 14i)}{2}$\\

$\Rightarrow g_1 = 5 + 12i$ en $g_2 = -2i$\\



Voor $g_1 = 5 + 12i$\\

Resolvente:\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
	x^2 - y^2 = 5\\ 
	2xy = 12 
\end{array} \right. $\\


$\lambda^2 - 5\lambda - 36 = 0$\\

$D_{\lambda} = 25 + 144 = 169 \Rightarrow \sqrt{169} = 13$\\

$\lambda_{1} = \dfrac{5 + 13}{2}$ en $\lambda_{2} = \dfrac{5 - 13}{2}$\\

$\Rightarrow \lambda_1 = 9$ en  $\lambda_2 = -4$\\

$x^2 = 9$ en $y^2 = 4$.\\

Omdat $xy = 6 > 0$: ${z_1 = 3 + 2i}$ en ${z_2 = -3 - 2i}$\\


Voor $g_2 = -2i$\\

Resolvente:\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
	x^2 - y^2 = 0\\
	2xy = -2
\end{array} \right. $\\

$\lambda^2 - 0\lambda - 1 = 0$ \\

$x^2 = 1 \Rightarrow x = \pm 1$ en $y^2 = 1 \Rightarrow y = \pm 1$\\

Omdat $xy = -1 < 0$: $z_3 = 1 - i$ en $z_4 = -1 + i$\\
