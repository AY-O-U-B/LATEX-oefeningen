$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{(2x+1)^2\sqrt{2x+1}}{\sqrt[3]{(2x+1)^2}}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int(2x+1)^{2+\tfrac{1}{2}-\tfrac{2}{3}}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int(2x+1)^{\tfrac{11}{6}}dx$\\

stel $u = 2x+1$\\

$du = 2dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int u^{\tfrac{11}{6}}du$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\cdot\dfrac{u^{\tfrac{17}{6}}}{\dfrac{17}{6}}$

$= \dfrac{3u^{\tfrac{17}{6}}}{17}$

$= \dfrac{3}{17}(2x+1)^{\tfrac{17}{6}} + C$
