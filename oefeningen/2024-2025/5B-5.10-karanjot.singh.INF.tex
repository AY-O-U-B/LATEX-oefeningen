Bereken de volgende bepaalde integraal, indien deze bestaat: \\

$\displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x}{\sin x + \cos x} dx$ \\

Substitutie: $u = \dfrac{\pi}{2} - x \Rightarrow du = -dx$ \\

$\displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x}{\sin x + \cos x} dx = \displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\cos u}{\sin u + \cos u} du$ \\

Neem de twee integralen samen: \\

$\displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x}{\sin x + \cos x} dx + \displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\cos x}{\sin x + \cos x} dx = \displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} 1 dx$ \\

$= \left[ x \right]_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} = \dfrac{\pi}{2}$ \\

Beide integralen zijn gelijk, dus: \\

$\displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x}{\sin x + \cos x} dx = \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{\pi}{2} = \dfrac{\pi}{4}$ \\

Dus, $\displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x}{\sin x + \cos x} dx = \dfrac{\pi}{4}$ \\
