$8(16^{x-1}-4^{2x-1}) = 3(2^{x+2}-4^x-2\cdot 8^x)$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 2^3\left(2^{4x-4} - 2^{4x-2}\right) = 3\left(2^{x+2}-2^{2x}-2^{3x+1}\right)$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 2^{4x-1}-2^{4x+1} = 3\left(2^{x+2}-2^{2x}-2^{3x+1}\right)$ \\

Stel $t = 2^x$ \\

$\dfrac{1}{2}t^4-2t^4 = 3(4t -t^2-2t^3)$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \dfrac{-3}{2}t^4+6t^3+3t^2-12t = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow t = 0$ of $\dfrac{-3}{2}t^3+6t^2+3t-12 = 0$ \\

Oplossen 3de-graadsvergelijking: \\

$\begin{tabular}{c|cccc}
    $4$     & $\dfrac{-3}{2}$ & $6$     & $3$   & $-12$ \\
            &                 & $-6$    & $0$   & $12$ \\ \hline
            & $\dfrac{-3}{2}$ & $0$     & $3$   & $0$
\end{tabular}$ \\

$\dfrac{-3}{2}t^2+3 = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow t^2 = 2$ \\

\medskip

Alle oplossingen van 4de-graadsvergelijking: \\

$t = 0$ of $t = 4$ of $t = +\sqrt{2}$ of $t = -\sqrt{2}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow t = 2^x$ \\

$2^x = 0$ of $2^x = 4$ of $2^x = 2^{\tfrac{1}{2}}$ of $2^x = -\left(2^{\tfrac{1}{2}}\right)$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x = /$ of $x = 2$ of $x = \dfrac{1}{2}$ of $x = /$ \\
