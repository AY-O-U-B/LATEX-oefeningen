$\displaystyle\int 2^x dx = \displaystyle\int e^{x\ln 2} dx$\\

$t=x\ln 2 \left(\Rightarrow dt=\ln 2 dx \Rightarrow dx=\dfrac{dt}{\ln 2}\right)$\\

$\displaystyle\int e^t \dfrac{dt}{\ln 2}$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{\ln 2}\displaystyle\int e^t dt$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{\ln 2}e^t + C$\\

$x \ln 2 = t$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{\ln 2}e^{x\ln 2} + C$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{\ln 2}2^x + C$\\
