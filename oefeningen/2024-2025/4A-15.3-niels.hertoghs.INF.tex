$\displaystyle\int x^3 e^{3x}dx$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	u = x^2 \\
	dv = e^{3x} dx \\
\end{array}
\right . 
\Rightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	du = 2x dx\\
	v = \dfrac{e^{3x}}{3}\\
\end{array}
\right . $ \\

$= \dfrac{x^2 e^{3x}}{3} - \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int 2x e^{3x} dx$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	u = 2x \\
	dv = e^{3x} dx\\
\end{array}
\right . 
\Rightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	du = 2 dx\\
	v = \dfrac{e^{3x}}{3}\\
\end{array}
\right . $ \\

$= \dfrac{x^2 e^{3x}}{3} - \dfrac{2xe^{3x}}{9} - \dfrac{1}{9} \displaystyle\int e^{3x}2 dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^2 e^{3x}}{3} - \dfrac{2xe^{3x}}{9} + \dfrac{2e^{3x}}{27} + C$ \\
