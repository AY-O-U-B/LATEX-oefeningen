$ \displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \dfrac{n+1}{n^3+3n^2} $\\

$\Rightarrow $ verhoudingscriterium\\

$\dfrac{\dfrac{n+1}{(n^3+3n^2}}{\dfrac{1}{n}} = \dfrac{n+1}{n^3+3n^2}\cdot n^2$\\

$= \dfrac{n^3+n^2}{n^3+3n^2} =  \dfrac{1+\dfrac{1}{n}}{1 + \dfrac{3}{n}}$\\

$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{n\rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{1+\dfrac{1}{n}}{1 + \dfrac{3}{n}}  = 1 \Rightarrow $ zelfde convergentie gedrag\\

$\Rightarrow  \displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{1}{n^2} $ convergent $\Rightarrow \displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \dfrac{n+1}{n^3+3n^2}$ convergent\\
