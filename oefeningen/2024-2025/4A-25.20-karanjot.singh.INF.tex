\noindent $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1 - 5x}{1 - 2x} dx$ \\

We herschrijven de teller: \\

$1 - 5x = - (5x - 1), \quad 1 - 2x = - (2x - 1)$ \\

Dus: \\

$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1 - 5x}{1 - 2x} dx = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{-(5x - 1)}{-(2x - 1)} dx = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{5x - 1}{2x - 1} dx$ \\

Stel $u = 2x - 1 \Rightarrow du = 2dx \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{1}{2} du$ \\

Substitueer $x = \dfrac{u + 1}{2}$ in de teller $5x - 1$: \\

$5x - 1 = 5 \cdot \dfrac{u + 1}{2} - 1 = \dfrac{5u + 5 - 2}{2} = \dfrac{5u + 3}{2}$ \\

Dus de integraal wordt: \\

$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{5x - 1}{2x - 1} dx = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\dfrac{5u + 3}{2}}{u} du = \dfrac{1}{4} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{5u + 3}{u} du$ \\

Splits de breuk: \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \left( \displaystyle\int 5 \, du + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{3}{u} du \right)$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \left( 5u + 3 \ln |u| \right) + C$ \\

Substitueer $u = 2x - 1$ terug: \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \left( 5(2x - 1) + 3 \ln |2x - 1| \right) + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{5}{2} x - \dfrac{5}{4} + \dfrac{3}{4} \ln |2x - 1| + C$ \\
