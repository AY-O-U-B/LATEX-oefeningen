$f(y) = y(3-y)$ op het interval [1,3]\\

$\pi\displaystyle \int_{1}^{3} \left(3y-y^2\right)^2 dy$\\

$\pi\displaystyle \int_{1}^{3} (9y^2-6y^3+y^4) dy$\\

$= \pi\left[ 9\cdot\dfrac{y^3}{3} - 6\cdot\dfrac{y^4}{4} + \dfrac{y^5}{5} \right]_1^3$\\

$= \pi\left[ \left(9\cdot\dfrac{27}{3} - 6\cdot\dfrac{81}{4} + \dfrac{243}{5}\right) - \left(9\cdot\dfrac{1}{3} - 6\cdot\dfrac{1}{4} + \dfrac{1}{5}\right) \right]$\\

$= \pi\left[ 3\cdot27 - 81\cdot\dfrac{3}{2} + \dfrac{243}{5} -3+\dfrac{3}{2}-\dfrac{1}{5}\right]$\\

$= \pi\left[ \dfrac{810}{10}-\dfrac{1215}{10}+\dfrac{486}{10}-\dfrac{30}{10}+\dfrac{15}{10}-\dfrac{2}{10}\right]$\\

$= \dfrac{32\pi}{5}$\\
