\textbf{Opgave:} \\

Trek de vierdemachtswortel in $\mathbb{C}$: $z^4 = 16$ \\

\textbf{Oplossing:} \\

$z^4 = 16 = 2^4 \cis 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow z = 2 \cis \dfrac{k\cdot2\pi}{4} = 2 \cis \dfrac{k\pi}{2}$ \\

$z_0 = 2 \cis 0 = 2$ \\

$z_1 = 2 \cis \dfrac{\pi}{2} = 2i$ \\

$z_2 = 2 \cis \pi = -2$ \\

$z_3 = 2 \cis \dfrac{3\pi}{2} = -2i$ \\
