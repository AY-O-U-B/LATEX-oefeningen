$f(x,y) = 8xy -2x^2 - y^4$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 8y - 4x = 0 \leftrightarrow 8y = 4x\Rightarrow x = 2y$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 8x-4y^3 = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow 8(2y)-4y^3 = 0$\\

$= 16y - 4y^3 = 4y (4-y^2)$\\

$ \Rightarrow 4 - y^2 = 0 \Rightarrow y^2 = \pm 2$\\

$\text{Punten: } (0,0), (4,2), (-4,-2)$\\
