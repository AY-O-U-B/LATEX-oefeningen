$ y^2 = x^4 + x^5$\\

$\Rightarrow y = \pm \sqrt{x^4 +x^5}$\\

$V = \pi \displaystyle\int_{-1}^{0}\left(\sqrt{x^4 + x^5}\right)^2dx =\pi \displaystyle\int_{-1}^{0}(x^4 +x^5)dx $\\

$ = \pi \left[\dfrac{x^5}{5} + \dfrac{x^6}{6}\right]_{-1}^{0}$\\

$ = \pi \cdot \left(\dfrac{1}{5} - \dfrac{1}{6}\right)$\\

$ = \pi \cdot \left(\dfrac{6}{30} - \dfrac{5}{30}\right) = \dfrac{\pi}{30}$\\
