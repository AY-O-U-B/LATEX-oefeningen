$f(x) = \ln \left( \tan \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) \right)$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{\tan \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)} \cdot \left( \tan \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) \right)' = \dfrac{\sec^2 \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)'}{\tan \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\tan \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)} \cdot \sec^2 \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right) \cdot \dfrac{1}{2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\sec^2 \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)}{2 \tan \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2 \cos \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)\sin \left( \dfrac{x}{2} \right)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sin x}$ \\
