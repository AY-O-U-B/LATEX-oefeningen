$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2x -3}{\sqrt[3]{x+1}} dx $ \\

Stel $ t = \sqrt[3]{x+1} \Rightarrow dx = 3t^2 dx $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2(t^3 -1) -3}{t} 3t^2 dt $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{(2t^3 -5) 3t^2}{t} dt $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int 6t^4 dt - \displaystyle\int 15t dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{6t^5}{5} - \dfrac{15t^2}{2} +C $ \\

$ = \dfrac{6}{5} \left(\sqrt[3]{x+1} \right)^5 - \dfrac{15}{2} \left(\sqrt[3]{x+1} \right)^2 +C $ \\
