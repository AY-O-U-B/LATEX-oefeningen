$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 9} \dfrac{(\sqrt{x}-3)(\sqrt{x}+3)}{(x-9)(\sqrt{x}+3)} = \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 9} \dfrac{x-9}{(x-9)(\sqrt{x}+3)} = \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 9} \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x} + 3} = \dfrac{1}{6}$\\
