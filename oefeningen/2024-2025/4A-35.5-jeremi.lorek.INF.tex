$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{2^x}{1 + 4^{x}} dx = \int \dfrac{2^x dx}{1 + 2^{2x}}$ \\

Stel $u = 2^x \Rightarrow du = (2^x \ln 2) dx \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{\ln 2} du = 2^x dx $\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\ln 2} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{du}{1 + u^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\ln 2} \operatorname{Bgtan}(u) + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\ln 2} \operatorname{Bgtan}(2^x) + C$ \\
