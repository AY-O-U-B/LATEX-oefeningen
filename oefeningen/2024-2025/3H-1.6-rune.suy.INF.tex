Schrijf in poolcoördinaten: \\

$x^2 + y^2 = 2x(3y^2 - x^2)$\\

$\Rightarrow (r\cos\theta)^2 + (r\sin\theta)^2 = 2r\cos\theta \cdot (3(r\sin\theta)^2 - (r\cos\theta)^2)$\\

$\Leftrightarrow r^2 = 6r^3\sin^2\theta\cos\theta - 2r^3\cos^3\theta$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \dfrac{1}{r} = 6\sin^2\theta \cos\theta - 2\cos^3\theta$ of $r=0$\\

$\Leftrightarrow r = \dfrac{1}{6\sin^2\theta \cos\theta - 2\cos^3\theta}$ of $r=0$\\
