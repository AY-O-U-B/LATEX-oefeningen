$\dint x^2e^{4x}dx$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x^2 \\
dv = e^{4x}dx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 2xdx \\
v = \dfrac{e^{4x}}{4}
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$=\dfrac{x^2e^{4x}}{4}-\dfrac{2}{4}\dint xe^{4x}dx$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x \\
dv = e^{4x}dx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = dx \\
v = \dfrac{e^{4x}}{4}
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$=\dfrac{x^2e^{4x}}{4}-\dfrac{1}{2}\left[\dfrac{xe^{4x}}{4}-\dfrac{1}{4}\dint e^{4x}dx\right]$\\

$=\dfrac{x^2e^{4x}}{4}-\dfrac{xe^{4x}}{8}+\dfrac{1}{8}\left(\dfrac{1}{4}e^{4x}\right)+C$\\

$=\dfrac{x^2e^{4x}}{4}-\dfrac{xe^{4x}}{8}+\dfrac{e^{4x}}{32}+C$\\
