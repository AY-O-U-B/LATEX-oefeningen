Opgave: zoek $\dfrac{dw}{dt}$: $w = \sin xyz$ met $x = t$, $y = t^2$ en $z = t^3$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x} = \cos (xyz) \cdot \dfrac{\partial (xyz)}{\partial x} = \cos (xyz) \cdot (yz)$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y} = \cos (xyz) \cdot (xz)$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z} = \cos (xyz) \cdot (xy)$\\

$\dfrac{dx}{dt} = 1$\\

$\dfrac{dy}{dt} = 2t$\\

$\dfrac{dz}{dt} = 3t^2$\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt} = \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x} \cdot \dfrac{dx}{dt} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y} \cdot \dfrac{dy}{dt} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z} \cdot \dfrac{dz}{dt}$\\

$= \cos\left(xyz\right) \cdot \left(yz + 2t\cdot xz + 3t^2\cdot xy\right)$\\

$= \cos\left(t^6\right) \cdot \left(t^5 + 2t^5 + 3t^5\right) = \cos\left(t^6\right) \cdot \left(6t^5\right)$\\

$$\dfrac{dw}{dt} = \cos\left(t^6\right) \cdot \left(6t^5\right)$$\\
