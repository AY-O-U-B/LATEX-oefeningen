$\displaystyle\int \Bgsin x dx$\\

$\left\{
   \begin{array}{l}
       u = \Bgsin x\\
       dv = dx
   \end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
   \begin{array}{l}
       du = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} dx \\
       v = x
   \end{array}
\right.$

$\Rightarrow x\Bgsin x - \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}dx$\\

$u = 1-x^2 \Rightarrow du = -2x dx$\\

$\Rightarrow x\Bgsin x + \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{u}}du$\\

$= x\Bgsin x + \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int u^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}du = x\Bgsin x + \dfrac{1}{2} \dfrac{u^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{\dfrac{1}{2}} + c$\\

$= x\Bgsin x + \sqrt{1-x^2} + c$\\
