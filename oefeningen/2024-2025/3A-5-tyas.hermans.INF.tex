$\dfrac{d}{dx}(x\sqrt{x})$\\

$f(x+h) = (x+h)\sqrt{x+h}$\\

$f(x+h) - f(x) = (x+h)\sqrt{x+h} - x\sqrt{x} = \dfrac{(x+h)^3-x^3}{(x+h)\sqrt{x+h}+x\sqrt{x}} = \dfrac{3x^2h+3xh^2+h^3}{(x+h)\sqrt{x+h}+x\sqrt{x}}$\\

$\dfrac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h} = \dfrac{3x^2h+O(h^2)}{h((x+h)\sqrt{x+h}+x\sqrt{x})} = \dfrac{3x^2+O(h)}{(x+h)\sqrt{x+h}+x\sqrt{x}}$\\

$\lim\limits_{h\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{3x^2+O(h)}{(x+h)\sqrt{x+h}+x\sqrt{x}} = \dfrac{3x^2}{2x^{\frac{3}{2}}}  = \dfrac{3\sqrt{x}}{2}$\\
