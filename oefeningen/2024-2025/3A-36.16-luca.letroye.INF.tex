$f(x) = \ln(x^3+\sqrt{x^6+1})$\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{x^3+\sqrt{x^6+1}}\cdot \left(3x^2 + \dfrac{3x^5}{\sqrt{x^6+1}} \right)$\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{x^3+\sqrt{x^6+1}}\cdot \left( \dfrac{3x^2(\sqrt{x^6+1}) +3x^5}{\sqrt{x^6+1}} \right)$\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{x^3+\sqrt{x^6+1}}\cdot \left( \dfrac{3x^2(\sqrt{x^6+1}+x^3)}{\sqrt{x^6+1}} \right)$\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{3x^2}{\sqrt{x^6+1}}$\\
