$f(x) = \dfrac{2x^{2}-4x+1}{\sqrt{x}}$ \\[10pt]

$f'(x) = \dfrac{\sqrt{x}\left(4x-4\right)-\left(2x^{2}-4x+1\right)\dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x}$ \\[10pt]

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{\left(4x-4\right)}{2\sqrt{x}}-\dfrac{2x^{2}+4^{x}-1}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x}$ \\[10pt]

$= \dfrac{8x^{2}-8x-2x^{2}+4x-1}{2\sqrt{x}}$ \\[10pt]

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{6x^{2}-4x-1}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x}$ \\[10pt]

$= \dfrac{6x^{2}-4x-1}{\sqrt{x^{3}}}$ \\
