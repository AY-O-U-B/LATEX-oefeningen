$T_6(f,1)(x)$ voor $f(x) = x^6+x^3-x^2+4$ \\

$T_n(f,a)(x) = \displaystyle\sum_{k = 0}^{n} \dfrac{f^{(k)}(a)}{k!}(x - a)^k$ \\

$T_6(f,1)(x) = \displaystyle\sum_{k = 0}^{6} \dfrac{f^k(1)}{k!}(x - 1)^k$ \\

$f'(x)=\left(x^6+x^3-x^2+4\right)' = 6x^5+3x^2-2x$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(1)=7$\\

$f''(x)=\left(x^6+x^3-x^2+4\right)'' = 30x^4+6x-2$ \\

$\Rightarrow f''(1)=34$\\

$f'''(x)=\left(x^6+x^3-x^2+4\right)''' = 120x^3+6$\\

$\Rightarrow f'''(1)=126$\\

$f^{iv}(x)=\left(x^6+x^3-x^2+4\right)^{iv} = 360x^2$ \\

$\Rightarrow f^{iv}(1)=360$\\

$f^{v}(x)=\left(x^6+x^3-x^2+4\right)^{v} = 720x$ \\

$\Rightarrow f^{v}(1)=720$\\

$f^{vi}(x)=\left(x^6+x^3-x^2+4\right)^{vi} = 720$ \\

$\Rightarrow f^{vi}(1)=720$\\

$T_6(f,1)(x) = \dfrac{f\left(1\right)}{0!}\left(x-1\right)^0 + \dfrac{f'\left(1\right)}{1!}\left(x-1\right)^1  + \dfrac{f''\left(1\right)}{2!}\left(x-1\right)^2 + \dfrac{f'''\left(1\right)}{3!}\left(x-1\right)^3  + \dfrac{f^{iv}\left(1\right)}{4!}\left(x-1\right)^4  + \dfrac{f^{v}\left(1\right)}{5!}\left(x-1\right)^5 + \dfrac{f^{vi}\left(1\right)}{6!}\left(x-1\right)^6$ \\

$= 5 + 7\left(x-1\right)  + 17\left(x-1\right)^2 + 21\left(x-1\right)^3  + 15\left(x-1\right)^4  + 6\left(x-1\right)^5 + \left(x-1\right)^6 $ \\
