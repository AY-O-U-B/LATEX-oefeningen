$I=\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sin x}{2\cos x + \sin x} dx$\\

$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\dfrac{\sin x}{\cos x}}{\dfrac{2\cos x + \sin x}{\cos x}} dx = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\tan x}{2+ \tan x} dx$\\



$\overset{\tan x =t}{=} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{t}{2+t} \dfrac{dt}{1+t^2}=\displaystyle\int \dfrac{(2+t)-2}{2+t} \dfrac{dt}{1+t^2}$\\

$=\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{1+t^2} dt -2 \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{(2+t)(1+t^2)} dt $\\

$=\operatorname{Bgtan} t -2\left(\displaystyle\int \dfrac{A}{2+t}dt +\displaystyle\int \dfrac{Bt+C}{1+t^2} dt\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{A(1+t^2)+(Bt+C)(2+t)}{(2+t)(1+t^2)} \Rightarrow (A+B)t^2 +(2B+C)t+(A+2C)=1$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
			\begin{array}{lll}
				A+B=0 \\
				2B+C=0 \\
				A+2C=1
			\end{array}	
			\right.= \left \{
			\begin{array}{lll}
				B=-A \\
				-2A+\dfrac{1-A}{2}=0 \\
				C=\dfrac{1-A}{2}
			\end{array}	
			\right.=\left \{
			\begin{array}{lll}
				A=\dfrac{1}{5} \\ 
				B=-\dfrac{1}{5} \\ 
				C=\dfrac{2}{5}
			\end{array}	
			\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow  I=\operatorname{Bgtan}t -\dfrac{2}{5} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{2+t} dt  -2 \displaystyle\int \dfrac{-\dfrac{t}{5}+\dfrac{2}{5}}{1+t^2} dt$\\

$=\operatorname{Bgtan}t - \dfrac{2}{5} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{2+t} d(2+t) +\dfrac{2}{5} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{t}{1+t^2} dt - \dfrac{4}{5} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{1+t^2} dt$\\

$=\operatorname{Bgtan}t - \dfrac{2}{5} \ln |2+t|  + \dfrac{\cancel{2}}{5} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\cancel{t}}{(1+t^2)\cancel{2}\cancel{t}} d(1+t^2) - \dfrac{4}{5}  \operatorname{Bgtan} t $\\ 

$=\dfrac{1}{5} \operatorname{Bgtan} t -\dfrac{2}{5} \ln |2+t| + \dfrac{1}{5} \ln | 1+t^2| +C$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{5} \left(x - 2 \ln|2+ \tan x| +  \ln | 1+ \tan^2 x| \right)+ C $ \\
