$\displaystyle\int \sqrt[4]{5x^3} dx$ \\

$ = \sqrt[4]{5} \displaystyle\int x^{\tfrac{3}{4}} dx$ \\

$ = \sqrt[4]{5} \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{3}{4} + 1}}{\dfrac{3}{4} + 1} + c$ \\

$ = \sqrt[4]{5} \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{7}{4} }}{\dfrac{7}{4}} + c$ \\

$ = \dfrac{4\sqrt[4]{5}}{7} x^ {\tfrac{7}{4}}  + c$ \\

$ = \dfrac{4\sqrt[4]{5x^7}}{7} + c$ \\
