$f(x)=x^x \quad (x>0)$ \\

$\ln f(x)=\ln(x^x)=x\ln x$ \\

$f(x)=e^{x\ln x}$ \\

$f'(x)=e^{x\ln x}(x\ln x)'$ \\

$(x\ln x)'=1\cdot \ln x+x\cdot\dfrac{1}{x}$ \\

$=\ln x+1$ \\

$f'(x)=e^{x\ln x}(\ln x+1)$ \\

$=x^x(\ln x+1)$ \\

$f'(x)=0$ \\

$x^x(\ln x+1)=0$ \\

$\ln x+1=0$ \\

$\ln x=-1$ \\

$x=e^{-1}$ \\
