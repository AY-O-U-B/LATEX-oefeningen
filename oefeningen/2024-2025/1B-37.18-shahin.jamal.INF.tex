Vierkantswortels van $20i + 21$\\

$20i + 21 = (x + yi)^2$\\

$\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}
    x^2 - y^2 & = & 21 \\
    2xy & = & 20
\end{array}\right.$\\

Tweede vergelijking:\\

$2xy = 20 \Rightarrow xy = 10 \Rightarrow x^2(-y^2) = -100$\\

$\left
\{\begin{array}{rcl}
    x^2 - y^2 & = & 21 \\
    x^2(-y^2) & = & -100
\end{array}
\right. $\\

$\lambda^2 - 21\lambda - 100 = 0$\\

$D = 21^2 - 4 \cdot (-100) = 841$\\

$\lambda_{1,2} = \dfrac{21 \pm 29}{2} \Rightarrow \lambda_{1} = 25, \quad \lambda_{2} = -4$\\

$x^2 = 25, \quad (-y^2) = -4$\\

$x = 5 \ x = -5, \quad y = 2 \ y = -2$\\

De vierkantswortels zijn dus: $5 + 2i \ -5 - 2i$
