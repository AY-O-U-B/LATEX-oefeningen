Bereken de volgende oneigenlijke integraal: \\

$\displaystyle \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{4}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{dx}{\sin^{2}(3x)}$ \\

onbepaalde integraal: \\

$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{\sin^{2}(3x)}$ \\

Stel $u = 3x$, dan $du = 3\,dx$, dus $dx = \dfrac{1}{3}\,du$. \\

$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{1}{\sin^{2} u} \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot du$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \int \csc^{2} u \cdot du$ \\

$= -\dfrac{1}{3} \cot u$ \\

$ = -\dfrac{1}{3} \cot(3x) + c$ \\

verticale asymptoot $x = \dfrac{\pi}{3}$ (omdat $\sin(3 \cdot \dfrac{\pi}{3}) = \sin(\pi) = 0$).

$\displaystyle \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{4}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{dx}{\sin^{2}(3x)} = \lim_{t \to \tfrac{\pi}{3}^{-}} \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{4}}^{t} \dfrac{dx}{\sin^{2}(3x)} + \lim_{s \to \tfrac{\pi}{3}^{+}} \int_{s}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{dx}{\sin^{2}(3x)}$ \\

eerste deel: \\

$\displaystyle \lim_{t \to \tfrac{\pi}{3}^{-}} \left[ -\dfrac{1}{3} \cot(3x) \right]_{\tfrac{\pi}{4}}^{t} = \lim_{t \to \tfrac{\pi}{3}^{-}} \left( -\dfrac{1}{3} \cot(3t) \right) - \left( -\dfrac{1}{3} \cot\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4}\right) \right)$ \\

aangezien $3t \to \pi^{-}$, gaat $\cot(3t) \to -\infty$. \\

$\displaystyle = \left( -\dfrac{1}{3} \cdot (-\infty) \right) - \left( -\dfrac{1}{3} \cdot (-1) \right) = +\infty - \dfrac{1}{3} = +\infty$ \\

tweede deel: \\

$\displaystyle \lim_{s \to \tfrac{\pi}{3}^{+}} \left[ -\dfrac{1}{3} \cot(3x) \right]_{s}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} = \left( -\dfrac{1}{3} \cot\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}\right) \right) - \lim_{s \to \tfrac{\pi}{3}^{+}} \left( -\dfrac{1}{3} \cot(3s) \right)$ \\

aangezien $3s \to \pi^{+}$, gaat $\cot(3s) \to +\infty$. \\

$\displaystyle = 0 - \left( -\dfrac{1}{3} \cdot (+\infty) \right) = 0 - (-\infty) = +\infty$ \\

totaal: \\

$\displaystyle \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{4}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{dx}{\sin^{2}(3x)} = +\infty + (+\infty) = +\infty$ \\
