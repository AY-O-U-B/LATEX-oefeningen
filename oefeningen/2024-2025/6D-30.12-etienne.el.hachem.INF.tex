Opgave: $ \displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \dfrac{n \cdot \sin \left(\dfrac{1}{n}\right)}{\dfrac{1}{n}+5}$ \\

Criterium 1:\\

$\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{n \cdot \sin \left(\dfrac{1}{n}\right)}{\dfrac{1}{n}+5}$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{t \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{t} \cdot \sin t}{t+5}$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{t \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{\sin t}{t}}{t+5} = \dfrac{1}{5}$ \\

$\Rightarrow$ Divergent
