$f(x,y) = x^2 + y^2 -6x + 2y + 5$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 2x - 6 = 0 \Leftrightarrow x = 3 $\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 2y + 2 = 0 \Leftrightarrow y = -1 $\\

In $(3,-1)$ is het raakvlak horizontaal.
