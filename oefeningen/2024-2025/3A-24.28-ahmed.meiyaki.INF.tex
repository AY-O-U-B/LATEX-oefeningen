$f(x) = \tan x - \dfrac{1 - \cos x}{\sin x} $\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{\cos^2x} - \dfrac{\sin x \cdot \sin x - \left(1 - \cos x\right) \cdot \cos x}{\sin^2x} $\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\cos^2x} - \dfrac{\sin^2x - \cos x + \cos^2x}{\sin^2x} $\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\cos^2x} - \dfrac{1 - \cos x}{\sin^2x} $\\
