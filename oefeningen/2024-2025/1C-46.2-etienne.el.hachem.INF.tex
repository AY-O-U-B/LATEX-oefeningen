Opgave: $z^4 = -1$, waarbij $r = 1$ en $\theta = \pi$\\

$w = \sqrt[4]{1} \cdot \cis \left( \dfrac{\pi + k \cdot 2\pi}{4} \right) \quad k \in \{0, 1, 2, 3\}$ \\

\[
\begin{array}{lllr}
    w_0 &= \cis \dfrac{\pi}{4}  & = \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2} + i \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2} & \quad \text{voor } k = 0 \\
		
    w_1 &= \cis \dfrac{3\pi}{4} & = -\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2} + i \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2} & \quad \text{voor } k = 1 \\
		
    w_2 &= \cis \dfrac{5\pi}{4} & = -\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2} - i \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2} & \quad \text{voor } k = 2 \\
		
    w_3 &= \cis \dfrac{7\pi}{4} & = \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2} - i \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}  & \quad \text{voor } k = 3
\end{array}
\]
