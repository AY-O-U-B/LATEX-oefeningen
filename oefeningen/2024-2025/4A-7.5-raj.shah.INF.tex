$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sqrt{x^3} + \sqrt[3]{3x}}{\sqrt {2x}} dx$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt {2}} \cdot \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{x^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}dx - \dfrac{\sqrt[3] 3}{\sqrt 2} \cdot \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{1}{3}}}{x^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}dx $\\

$ = \dfrac{\sqrt {2}}{2} \cdot \displaystyle\int x dx - \dfrac{\sqrt{2}\sqrt[3]{3}}{2} \cdot \displaystyle\int x^{-\tfrac{1}{6}} dx$\\

$ = \dfrac{\sqrt{2}x^2}{4} - \dfrac{\sqrt{2}\sqrt[3]{3}}{2} \cdot \dfrac{6x^{\tfrac{5}{6}}}{5} + c = \dfrac{\sqrt{2}x^2}{4} - \dfrac{3\sqrt{2} \sqrt[3]{3} \sqrt[6]{x^5}}{5} + c$
