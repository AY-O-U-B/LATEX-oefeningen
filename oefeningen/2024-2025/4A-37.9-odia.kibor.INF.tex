$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{7dx}{2\left(\dfrac{(x + 4)^2}{2} + 1\right)}$\\

$= \dfrac{7}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\left(\left(\dfrac{x + 4}{\sqrt{2}}\right)^2 + 1\right)}$\\

$\dfrac{2du}{\sqrt{2}} = \dfrac{2\sqrt{2}du}{2} =dx$\\

$\Rightarrow  \dfrac{7\cdot 2\sqrt{2}}{2\cdot2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{u^2 + 1} du$\\
