$f(x) = \sqrt{\dfrac{x-1}{x+1}}$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{2 \sqrt{\dfrac{x-1}{x+1}}} \cdot \dfrac{(x+1) \cdot 1 - (x-1) \cdot 1}{(x+1)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2 \sqrt{\dfrac{x-1}{x+1}}} \cdot \dfrac{2}{(x+1)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{\dfrac{x-1}{x+1}}} \cdot \dfrac{1}{(x+1)^2}$ \\

$= \sqrt{\dfrac{x+1}{x-1}} \cdot \dfrac{1}{(x+1)^2}$ \\
