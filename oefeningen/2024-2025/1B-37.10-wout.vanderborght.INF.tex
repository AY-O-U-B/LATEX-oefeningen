Bereken in $\mathbb{C}$ de vierkantswortel van de volgende complexe getallen: $ 1 + 2\sqrt{6}i $.

$ 1 + 2\sqrt{6}i = (x + yi)^2 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = 1 \\
    2xy = 2\sqrt{6}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = 1 \\
    xy = \sqrt{6}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = 1 \\
    x^2(-y)^2 = 6
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$x^2$ en $y^2$ zijn oplossingen van:\\

$ \lambda^2 - \lambda - 6 = 0 $\\

$ \Delta = 1 + 4 * 6 = 25 $\\

$ \lambda_{1,2} = \dfrac{1 \pm \sqrt{25}}{2} $\\

$ \lambda_1 = 3 $ en $ \lambda_2 = -2 $\\

$ x = \sqrt{3} $ en $ y = \sqrt{2} $\\

Oplossing: $ \sqrt{3} + \sqrt{2}i $ of $ -\sqrt{3} - \sqrt{2}i $
