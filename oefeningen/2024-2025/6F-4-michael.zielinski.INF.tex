$ f_n : [1,+\infty[ \to \mathbb{R} : x \to \dfrac{1}{n x^2} $\\

$ \lim\limits_{n \to \infty}\dfrac{1}{n x^2} = 0 $\\

$ \Rightarrow f : [1,+\infty[ \to \mathbb{R} : x \to 0 $ en $ f_n \stackrel{p}{\rightarrow} f $\\

$ \left\|f_n - f\right\|_{\infty}
  = \sup\limits_{x \in [1,+\infty[} \left|\dfrac{1}{n x^2} - 0\right|
  = \dfrac{1}{n} $\\

$ \Rightarrow \lim\limits_{n \to \infty}\left\|f_n - f\right\|_{\infty} = 0 $\\

$ \Rightarrow f_n \stackrel{u}{\rightarrow} f $\\
