$ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{9x^2-x+2}{4x^5+x^3}dx = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{9x^2-x+2}{x^3\left(4x^2+1\right)}dx 
= \dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{9x^2-x+2}{x^3\left(x^2+\dfrac{1}{4}\right)}dx $\\

$ \rightarrow\dfrac{9x^2-x+2}{x^3\left(x^2+\dfrac{1}{4}\right)} = \dfrac{A}{x^3}+\dfrac{B}{x^2}+\dfrac{C}{x}+\dfrac{Dx+E}{x^2+\dfrac{1}{4}} $\\

$ \Rightarrow A = \dfrac{2}{\dfrac{1}{4}} = 8 $\\

$ \rightarrow \dfrac{9x^2-x+2}{x^3\left(x^2+\dfrac{1}{4}\right)} -  \dfrac{8}{x^3} = \dfrac{x-1}{x^2\left(x^2+\dfrac{1}{4}\right)} $\\

$ \Rightarrow B = \dfrac{-1}{\dfrac{1}{4}} = -4 $\\

$ \rightarrow \dfrac{x-1}{x^2\left(x^2+\dfrac{1}{4}\right)} - \dfrac{-4}{x^2} = \dfrac{4x+1}{x\left(x^2+\dfrac{1}{4}\right)} $\\

$ \Rightarrow C = \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{1}{4}} = 4 $\\

$ \Rightarrow D\left(\dfrac{1}{2}i\right)+E = \dfrac{-\dfrac{9}{4}-\dfrac{1}{2}i+2}{-\dfrac{1}{4}\cdot\dfrac{1}{2}i} = 4-2i \Rightarrow E = 4 $\\

$ \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{2}Di = -2i \Rightarrow D = -4 $\\
