$S = 4 \displaystyle\int_{\dfrac{\pi}{2}}^0 |a \sin^3 t| \cdot |{-3a \cos^2 t \sin t}| \, dt$ \\

$= -12a^2 \displaystyle\int_{\dfrac{\pi}{2}}^0 \sin^4 t \cos^2 t \, dt = 12a^2 \displaystyle\int_0^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} \sin^4 t \cos^2 t \, dt$ \\

Gebruik: $\sin^2 t = \dfrac{1 - \cos 2t}{2}$ en $\cos^2 t = \dfrac{1 + \cos 2t}{2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow \sin^4 t \cos^2 t = \left( \dfrac{1 - \cos 2t}{2} \right)^2 \cdot \left( \dfrac{1 + \cos 2t}{2} \right) = \dfrac{(1 - \cos 2t)^2 (1 + \cos 2t)}{8}$ \\

$S = 12a^2 \cdot \dfrac{1}{8} \displaystyle\int_0^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} (1 - \cos 2t)^2 (1 + \cos 2t) \, dt = \dfrac{3a^2}{2} \displaystyle\int_0^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} (1 - \cos 2t)^2 (1 + \cos 2t) \, dt$ \\

Bereken de integrand: \\

$(1 - \cos 2t)^2 (1 + \cos 2t) = (1 - 2\cos 2t + \cos^2 2t)(1 + \cos 2t)$ \\

$= 1 - \cos 2t - \cos^2 2t + \cos^3 2t$ \\

$S = \dfrac{3a^2}{2} \displaystyle\int_0^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} \left( 1 - \cos 2t - \cos^2 2t + \cos^3 2t \right) dt$ \\

Gebruik bekende waarden: \\

$\displaystyle\int_0^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} \cos 2t \, dt = 0$, $\displaystyle\int_0^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} \cos^2 2t \, dt = \dfrac{\pi}{4}$, $\displaystyle\int_0^{\dfrac{\pi}{2}} \cos^3 2t \, dt = 0$ \\

$S = \dfrac{3a^2}{2} \left( \dfrac{\pi}{2} - 0 - \dfrac{\pi}{4} + 0 \right) = \dfrac{3a^2}{2} \cdot \dfrac{\pi}{4} = \dfrac{3\pi a^2}{8}$ \\
