$ \displaystyle\int\limits_1^2\dfrac{1}{x\sqrt{x^2-1}}dx =
\stackrel{x = \tfrac{1}{\cos t}}{=} \displaystyle\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{3}}\dfrac{1}{\dfrac{1}{\cos t}\cdot\dfrac{\sin t}{\cos t}}\cdot\dfrac{\sin t}{\cos t}dt
= \displaystyle\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{3}}\dfrac{\sin t}{\sin t}dt $\\

$ = \left[t\right]_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{3}} = \dfrac{\pi}{3} $\\
