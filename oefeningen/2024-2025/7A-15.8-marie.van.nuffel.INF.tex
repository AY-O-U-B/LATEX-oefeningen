$f(x,y) = x^4+y^3-3y$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 4x^3$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 3y^2-3$\\

$4x^3 = 0\Rightarrow x = 0$\\

$3y^2-3 = 0\Rightarrow y = 1$ of $y = -1$\\
