$f\left(x\right) = \dfrac{2 - 3x}{x^2 - 3x + 2}$ \\

$\dfrac{df}{dx} = \dfrac{-3\left(x^2-3x+2\right) - \left(2-3x\right)\left(2x-3\right)}{\left(x^2-3x+2\right)^2}$ \\

$ = \dfrac{-3x^2 + 9x -6-\left(4x-6-6x^2+9x\right)}{\left(x^2-3x+2\right)^2}$ \\

$ = \dfrac{3x^2 -4x}{\left(x^2-3x+2\right)^2}$ \\

$ = \dfrac{x\left(3x - 4\right)}{\left(\left(x-1\right)\left(x-2\right)\right)^2}$ \\

Kritieke punten:\\

$x=0$, $x=\dfrac{4}{3}$, $x=1$, $x=2$ \\

$f\left(0\right) = 1$ \\

$f\left(\dfrac{4}{3}\right) = 9$ \\

\begin{tabular}{c|ccccccccccc}
        $x$     & $-\infty$ & $0$ & & $1$ & & $\dfrac{4}{3}$ &  & $2$ & $+\infty$\\ 
        \hline
        $f'(x)$ & $+$       & $0$ & $-$ & $|$ & $-$ & $0$ & $+$ & $|$ &  $+$ \\ 
        \hline

\end{tabular}\\
