$2^{2x+1} + 4^{x-1} - 9 = 0 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow 2 \cdot 2^{2x} + 2^{2x-2} - 9 = 0 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow 2 \cdot 2^{2x} + \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot 2^{2x} - 9 = 0 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \dfrac{9}{4} \cdot 2^{2x} = 9 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow 2^{2x} = 2^2 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow 2x = 2 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow x = 1 $\\
