Opgave: $\dint \cos 5x \cos x dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\dint (\cos 4x + \cos 6x) dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\left(\dint \cos (4x) dx + \dint \cos (6x) dx\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\left(\dfrac{1}{4}\dint d(\sin 4x) + \dfrac{1}{6}\dint d(\sin 6x)\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}\sin (4x) + \dfrac{1}{12}\sin (6x) + c$\\
