$f(x) = \dfrac{2x^2 - 4x +1}{\sqrt{x}}$\\



$ f'(x) = \dfrac{(4x - 4)\sqrt{x} - (2x^2 - 4x + 1)\left(\dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}\right)}{(\sqrt{x})^2} $\\

$ = \dfrac{(4x - 4)\sqrt{x} - \dfrac{2x^2 - 4x + 1}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x} $\\

$ = \dfrac{\dfrac{2(4x - 4)x - (2x^2 - 4x + 1)}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x}$\\

$ = \dfrac{\dfrac{8x^2 - 8x - 2x^2 + 4x - 1}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x} $\\

$ = \dfrac{\dfrac{6x^2 - 4x - 1}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x} $\\

$ f'(x) = \dfrac{6x^2 - 4x - 1}{2x\sqrt{x}} $\\
