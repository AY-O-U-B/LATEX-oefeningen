$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{\sqrt[4]{x}\sqrt[6]{x^7}}{\sqrt{x^3\sqrt[12]{x^{11}}}}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{x^{\tfrac{1}{4}+\tfrac{7}{6}}}{\sqrt{x^{3+\tfrac{11}{12}}}}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{x^{\tfrac{17}{12}}}{x^{\tfrac{47}{24}}}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int x^{\tfrac{34-47}{24}}dx = \displaystyle\int x^{\tfrac{-13}{24}}dx$\\

$= \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{11}{24}}}{\dfrac{11}{24}} + C = \dfrac{24}{11}x^{\tfrac{11}{24}} + C$\\
