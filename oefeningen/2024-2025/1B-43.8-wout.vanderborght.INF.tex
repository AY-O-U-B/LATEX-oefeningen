Los de volgende complexe vierkantsvergelijking op in $ \mathbb{C} $:

$ z^2 = (1-i)(z-2) $\\

$ \Leftrightarrow z^2 = z - 2 -zi + 2i $\\

$ \Leftrightarrow z^2 - (1-i)z + (2-2i) = 0 $\\

$ \Delta = (1-i)^2 - 4(2-2i) = (1 -i -i +i^2) -8 +8i = -8 + 6i $\\

Wortel van discriminant berekenen:\\


$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = -8 \\
    2xy = 6
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = -8 \\
    xy = 3
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = 8 \\
    x^2(-y)^2 = 9
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$x^2$ en $y^2$ zijn oplossingen van:\\

$ \lambda^2 + 8\lambda -9 = 0 $\\

$ \lambda_{1,2} = \dfrac{-8 \pm \sqrt{100}}{2} $\\

$ \lambda_1 = 1 $ en $ \lambda_2 = -9 $\\

$ x = 1 $ en $ y = 3 $\\

Oplossing wortel discriminant: $ 1 + 3i $ of $ -1 - 3i $\\

$ z_1 = \dfrac{1-i + 1 + 3i}{2} = 1 + i $\\

$ z_2 = \dfrac{1-i - 1 - 3i}{2} = -2i $
