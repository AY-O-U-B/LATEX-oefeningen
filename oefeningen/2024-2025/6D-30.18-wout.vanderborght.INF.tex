$ \displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \dfrac{n^2}{2^n} $ \\

Klein criterium van Cauchy: \\

$ \Limit{n \rightarrow +\infty} \sqrt[n]{\dfrac{n^2}{2^n}} $ \\

$ = \Limit{n \rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{\sqrt[n]{n^2}}{2} $ \\

$ = \Limit{n \rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{n^{\tfrac{2}{n}}}{2} $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} < 1 \Rightarrow $ convergent \\
