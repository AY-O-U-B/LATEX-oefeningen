Trek de vierdemachtswortels in $\mathbb{C}$:\\

$z^5=\dfrac{-1+i\sqrt3}{2}$\\

$\dfrac{-1+i\sqrt3}{2}=\dfrac{-1}{2} + i\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{2\pi}{3}\right)$\\


$\Rightarrow z_k=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{2\pi}{3}+2k\pi}{5}\right),k=0,1,2,3,4$\\

$\Rightarrow z_k=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{2\pi}{15}+\dfrac{2k\pi}{5}\right),k=0,1,2,3,4$\\


$\Rightarrow z_0=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{2\pi}{15}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_1=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{8\pi}{15}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_2=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{14\pi}{15}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_3=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{4\pi}{3}\right)=\cos\left({\dfrac{4\pi}{3}}\right)+i\sin\left({\dfrac{4\pi}{3}}\right)=\dfrac{-1}{2}-\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}i$\\

$\Rightarrow z_4=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{26\pi}{15}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow \boxed{z_0=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{2\pi}{15}\right),z_1=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{8\pi}{15}\right),z_2=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{14\pi}{15}\right),z_3=\dfrac{-1}{2}-\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}i \ , \ z_4=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{26\pi}{15}\right)}$\\
