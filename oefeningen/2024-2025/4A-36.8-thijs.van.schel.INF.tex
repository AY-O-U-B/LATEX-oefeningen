$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{5dx}{9x^2+1} = 5\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{9x^2+1}$\\

Stel $t = 3x\Rightarrow dt = 3dx$\\

$ = \dfrac{5}{3} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t^2+1}$\\\

$ = \dfrac{5}{3} \operatorname{Bgtan}(t)$ + C\\

$ = \dfrac{5}{3} \operatorname{Bgtan}(3x)$ + C
