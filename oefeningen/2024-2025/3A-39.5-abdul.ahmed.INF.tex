$\dfrac{d}{dx} \left(x^{\tfrac{1}{x}}\right)$\\

$=\dfrac{d}{dx} \left(e^ {\tfrac{1}{x} \cdot\ln(x)}\right)$\\

$=e^ {\tfrac{1}{x} \cdot\ln(x)} \left(\dfrac{1}{x^2} - \dfrac{\ln x}{x^2}\right)$\\

$=x^{\tfrac{1}{x}-2}(1-\ln x)$\\
