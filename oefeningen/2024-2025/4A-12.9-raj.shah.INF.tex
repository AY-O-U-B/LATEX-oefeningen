$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{2^{x+2} + 5^{x-2}}{4^{x-1}}dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2^{x+2}}{4^{x-1}}dx + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{ 5^{x-2}}{4^{x-1}}dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2^{x+2}}{2^{2x-2}}dx + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{ 5^{x}\cdot 5^{-2}}{4^{x}\cdot 4^{-1}}dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int 2^{-x+4} dx +  \dfrac{4}{5^2} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{5^x}{4^x}dx$\\

$ = 16 \displaystyle\int 2^{-x} dx + \dfrac{4}{25} \displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x dx$\\

$ = \dfrac{16 \cdot 2^{-x}}{\ln 2} + \dfrac{4}{25} \cdot \left(\dfrac{1}{ \ln \dfrac{5}{4}} \cdot \left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x\right) + c$\\

$ = \dfrac{16 \cdot 2^{-x}}{\ln 2} + \dfrac{4 \left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x}{25 \ln \dfrac{5}{4}} + c$\\
