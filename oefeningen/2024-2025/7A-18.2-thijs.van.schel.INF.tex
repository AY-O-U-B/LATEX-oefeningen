$f(x,y,z) = 
\left\{ 
\begin{array}{ll} 
\dfrac{xyz}{x^2+y^2+z^2} & \text{als } (x,y,z) \neq (0,0,0)\\ 
0 & \text{als } (x,y,z) = (0,0,0) 
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

Continuïteit:\\

Stel $x = r \sin \phi \cos \theta, y = r \sin \phi \sin \theta$ en $z = r \cos \phi$\\

$\dlim_{(x,y,z)\rightarrow (0,0,0)} f(x,y,z) = \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{(r \sin \phi \cos \theta)(r \sin \phi \sin \theta)(r \cos \phi)}{r^2 \sin^2 \phi \cos^2 \theta + r^2 \sin^2 \phi \sin^2 \theta + r^2 \cos^2 \phi}$\\

$= \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{r^3 \sin^2 \phi \cos \phi \sin \theta \cos \theta}{r^2 (\sin^2 \phi (\cos^2 \theta + \sin^2 \theta) + \cos^2 \phi)}$\\

$= \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{r^3 \sin^2 \phi \cos \phi \sin \theta \cos \theta}{r^2 (\sin^2 \phi + \cos^2 \phi)}$\\

$= \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{r^3 \sin^2 \phi \cos \phi \sin \theta \cos \theta}{r^2}$\\

$= \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} r (\sin^2 \phi \cos \phi \sin \theta \cos \theta) = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow f$ is continu in $(0,0,0)$ \\

Partiële afgeleiden:\\

$\left\{ 
\begin{array}{l} 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(0,0,0) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(\lambda,0,0) - f(0,0,0)}{\lambda} = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{0}{\lambda} = 0 \\ 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(0,0,0) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(0,\lambda,0) - f(0,0,0)}{\lambda} = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{0}{\lambda} = 0 \\
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial z}(0,0,0) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(0,0,\lambda) - f(0,0,0)}{\lambda} = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{0}{\lambda} = 0 
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow f$ is partieel afleidbaar in $(0,0,0)$\\

Afleidbaarheid:\\

Stel $\overline{h} = (h_1,h_2,h_3)$\\

$Df(\overline{0}, \overline{h}) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(\lambda h_1, \lambda h_2, \lambda h_3) - f(0,0,0)}{\lambda}$\\

$= \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{(\lambda h_1)(\lambda h_2)(\lambda h_3)}{\lambda^2 h_1^2 + \lambda^2 h_2^2 + \lambda^2 h_3^2}}{\lambda}$\\

$= \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\lambda^3 h_1 h_2 h_3}{\lambda (\lambda^2 h_1^2 + \lambda^2 h_2^2 + \lambda^2 h_3^2)}$\\

$= \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\lambda^3 h_1 h_2 h_3}{\lambda^3 (h_1^2 + h_2^2 + h_3^2)} = \dfrac{h_1 h_2 h_3}{h_1^2 + h_2^2 + h_3^2}$\\

$\Rightarrow f$ is afleidbaar in $(0,0,0)$ (in alle richtingen $\overline{h}$)\\

Differentieerbaarheid:\\

Voor differentieerbaarheid in $(0,0,0)$ moet gelden:\\

$Df(\overline{0}, \overline{h}) = \left(\begin{array}{c c c} \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(0,0,0) & \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(0,0,0) & \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial z}(0,0,0) \end{array}\right) \left(\begin{array}{c} h_1 \\ h_2 \\ h_3 \end{array}\right)$\\

$\left(\begin{array}{c c c} 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right) \left(\begin{array}{c} h_1 \\ h_2 \\ h_3 \end{array}\right) = 0$\\

$Df(\overline{0}, \overline{h}) = \dfrac{h_1 h_2 h_3}{h_1^2 + h_2^2 + h_3^2} \neq 0$\\

$\Rightarrow f$ is niet differentieerbaar in $(0,0,0)$\\
