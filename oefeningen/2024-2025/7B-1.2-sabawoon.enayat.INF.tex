Opgave: zoek $\dfrac{dw}{dt}$: $w = \dfrac{1}{u^2 + v^2}$ met $u = \cos 2t$ en $v = \sin 2t$\\

$w = \left(u^2 + v^2\right)^{-1}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial u} = -1\cdot\left(u^2 + v^2\right)^2\cdot 2u = \dfrac{-2u}{\left(u^2 + v^2\right)^2}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial v} = -1\cdot\left(u^2 + v^2\right)^2\cdot 2v = \dfrac{-2v}{\left(u^2 + v^2\right)^2}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{du}{dt} = \dfrac{d}{dt}\left(\cos 2t\right) = -2\sin 2t$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{dv}{dt} = \dfrac{d}{dt}\left(\sin 2t\right) = 2\cos 2t$\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt} = \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial u}\cdot\dfrac{du}{dt} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial v}\cdot\dfrac{dv}{dt}$\\

$= \dfrac{-2u}{\left(u^2 + v^2\right)^2} \cdot (-2\sin 2t) - \dfrac{2v}{\left(u^2 + v^2\right)^2} \cdot (2\cos 2t)$\\

$= \dfrac{4\cos 2t\cdot \sin 2t}{\left(\cos^2 2t + \sin^2 2t\right)^2} - \dfrac{4\sin 2t\cdot \cos 2t}{\left(\cos^2 2t + \sin^2 2t\right)^2}$\\

$= 4\cos 2t\cdot \sin 2t - 4\sin 2t\cdot \cos 2t = 0$\\

$$\dfrac{dw}{dt} = 0$$\\
