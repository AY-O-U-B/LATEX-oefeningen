	$\lim\limits_{x\to\infty}$$\left(\dfrac{ax^2+bx+c}{ax^2+dx+f}\right)^x = \lim\limits_{x\to\infty}\left(\dfrac{x^2}{x^2}\right)^x=\left(\dfrac{\infty}{\infty}\right)^\infty = 1^\infty$ \\
	
	$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{x\to\infty}$$\left(\dfrac{ax^2+bx+c}{ax^2+dx+f}\right)^x = \lim\limits_{x\to\infty}$$\left(\dfrac{ax^2+dx+f}{ax^2+dx+f} + \dfrac{bx+c-dx-f}{ax^2+dx+f} \right)^x$ \\  
	
	$ = \lim\limits_{x\to\infty}\left(1 + \dfrac{(b-d)x + (c-f)}{ax^2+dx+f} \right)^x = e^{\lim\limits_{x\to\infty}x\tfrac{(b-d)x + (c-f)}{ax^2+dx+f}}
	= e^{\lim\limits_{x\to\infty}\tfrac{(b-d)x^2 + (c-f)x}{ax^2+dx+f}}$ \\
	
	$= e^{\lim\limits_{x\to\infty}\tfrac{(b-d)x^2}{ax^2}} = e^{\tfrac{b-d}{a}}$
