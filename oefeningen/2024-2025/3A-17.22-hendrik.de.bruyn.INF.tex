\textbf{Opgave:} Bereken de afgeleide functie van $f(x) = \dfrac{x^2}{x+1} - \dfrac{3x}{x-2}$. \\

\textbf{Oplossing:} \\

$f\left(x\right)
    = \dfrac{x^2}{x+1} - \dfrac{3x}{x-2}
    = \dfrac{x^2\left(x-1\right) - 3x\left(x+1\right)}{x^2 - 1}
    = \dfrac{x^3 -4x^2 -3x}{x^2 - 1}$ \\

$f'\left(x\right)
    = \dfrac{\left(3x^2 -8x -3\right)\left(x^2 -1\right) - 2x\left(x^3 -4x^2 -3x\right)}{\left(x^2 - 1\right)^2}
    = \dfrac{x^4 + 8x + 3}{\left(x^2 - 1\right)^2}$ \\
