$f(x)=\left(\sin x\right)^{\cos x}$\\

$\Rightarrow y=\left(\sin x\right)^{\cos x}$\\

$\Rightarrow  \ln y=\ln \left(\left(\sin x\right)^{\cos x}\right) = \left(\cos x\right) \ln \left(\sin x\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow \left(\ln y\right)'= \left(\left(\cos x\right) \ln \left(\sin x\right) \right)'$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{y'}{y} = \left(-\sin x\right) \ln \left(\sin x\right)+\left(\cos x\right) \dfrac{\cos x}{\sin x}  $\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{y'}{y} = -\sin x\ln\left(\sin x\right)+\cos x\dfrac{\cos x}{\sin x}$\\

$\Rightarrow y' = y\left(-\sin x\ln\left(\sin x\right)+\dfrac{\cos^2 x}{\sin x}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \left(\sin x\right)^{\cos x}\left(-\sin x\ln\left(\sin x\right)+\dfrac{\cos^2 x}{\sin x}\right)$\\
