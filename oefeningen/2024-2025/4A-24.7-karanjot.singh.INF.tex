$I = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\ln^3 x}{x} dx$ \\

Substitutie: $u = \ln x$, dan is $du = \dfrac{1}{x} dx$ \\

$\Rightarrow I = \displaystyle\int u^3 du$ \\

$\displaystyle\int u^3 du = \dfrac{u^4}{4} + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{(\ln x)^4}{4} + C$ \\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\ln^3 x}{x} dx = \dfrac{(\ln x)^4}{4} + C$ \\
