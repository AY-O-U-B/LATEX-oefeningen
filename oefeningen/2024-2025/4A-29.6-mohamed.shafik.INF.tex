Bereken $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{4}{\sqrt{x-5}} dx$\\

Substitutie: $t = \sqrt{x-5}$, $t^2 = x - 5$, $dx = 2t dt$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int \dfrac{4}{\sqrt{x-5}} dx &=& \displaystyle\int \dfrac{4}{t} \cdot 2t dt\\
&=& 8 \displaystyle\int dt\\
&=& 8t + c\\
&=& 8\sqrt{x-5} + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
