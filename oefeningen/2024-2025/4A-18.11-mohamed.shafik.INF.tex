Bereken $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x}{\sin^2 x} dx$ door parti\"ele integratie\\

Stel\\

$\left\{\begin{array}{l} u = x \\ dv = \dfrac{dx}{\sin^2 x} \end{array}\right. \Rightarrow \left\{\begin{array}{l} du = dx \\ v = -\cot x \end{array}\right.$\\

dan wordt\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
I &=& -x \cot x - \displaystyle\int (-\cot x) dx\\
&=& -x \cot x + \displaystyle\int \cot x dx\\
&=& -x \cot x + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\cos x}{\sin x} dx\\
&=& -x \cot x + \ln|\sin x| + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
