Opgave: $z^2 - 2z + 1 - zi = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow \Delta = (-2)^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot (1 - 2i) = 4 - (4 - 8i) = (4 + 0i) + (-4 + 8i) = 8i$\\

$\Rightarrow 8i = (x + yi)^2 = x^2 - y^2 + 2xyi$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
	\begin{array}{l}
		x^2 + (- y^2) = 0 \\
		2xy = 8
	\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
	\begin{array}{l}
		x^2 + (- y^2) = 0 \\
		x^2 (- y^2) = - 4^2 = - 16
	\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\lambda^2 - 0 \cdot \lambda - 16 = 0$\\

$\Delta = - 4 \cdot (- 16) = 64 \Rightarrow \sqrt{\Delta} = \pm 4$\\

$\Rightarrow \lambda_{1,2} = \dfrac{a \pm \sqrt{\Delta}}{2} = \pm \dfrac{8}{2} = \pm 4$\\

$\Rightarrow x^2 = \lambda_{1} = 4 \Rightarrow x = \pm 2$\\

$\Rightarrow -y^2 = \lambda_{2} = 4 \Rightarrow y = \sqrt{- 4} = \sqrt{- 1} \cdot \sqrt{4} = \pm 2i$\\

$x = 2$ en $y = 2i$ of $x = -2$ en $y = -2i \Rightarrow k = (2 + 2i)$\\

$z_1 = \dfrac{-b + k}{2a} = \dfrac{2 + (2 + 2i)}{2} = \dfrac{4 + 2i}{2} = 2 + i$\\

$z_2 = \dfrac{-b - k}{2a} = \dfrac{2 - (2 + 2i)}{2} = \dfrac{- 2i}{2} = - i$\\
