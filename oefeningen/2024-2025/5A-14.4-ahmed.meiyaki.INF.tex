$I = \displaystyle\int_{0}^{1} \dfrac{x^3}{1+x^4} dx  $\\

Stel $t = 1 + x^4 $\\

$\Rightarrow dt = 4x^3 $\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{4}dt = x^3 $\\

$I = \dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int_{1}^{2} \dfrac{1}{t} dt  $\\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \left[\ln|t|\right]_{1}^{2} = \dfrac{1}{4} \ln2$\\
