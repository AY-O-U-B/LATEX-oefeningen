$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{9x^2+6x}{9x^2+6x+1}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{9x^2+6x+1}{9x^2+6x+1}dx- \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{9x^2+6x+1}dx$\\

$= x -\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{9x^2+6x+1}dx$\\

stel $u = 3x+1$\\

$du = 3dx$\\

$= x -\dfrac{1}{3}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{du}{u^2}$\\

$= x + \dfrac{1}{3u} + C$\\

$= x + \dfrac{1}{9x+3} + C$\\
