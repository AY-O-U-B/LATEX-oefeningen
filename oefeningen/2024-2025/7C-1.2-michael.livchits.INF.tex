$f(x,y,z,t)= \ln \dfrac{xy}{zt}$\\
	
Eerste orde:\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \dfrac{zt}{xy}\cdot\dfrac{y}{zt} = \dfrac{1}{x}$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \dfrac{zt}{xy}\cdot\dfrac{x}{zt} = \dfrac{1}{y}$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial z} = \dfrac{zt}{xy}\cdot\left(-\dfrac{xy}{z^2t}\right) = -\dfrac{1}{z}$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial t} = \dfrac{zt}{xy}\cdot\left(-\dfrac{xy}{zt^2}\right) = -\dfrac{1}{t}$\\

Tweede orde:\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} = -\dfrac{1}{x^2}$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} = -\dfrac{1}{y^2}$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial z^2} = \dfrac{1}{z^2}$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial t^2} = \dfrac{1}{t^2}$\\

$\Rightarrow D^2f(x,y,z,t) = \left(\begin{array}{cccc}
-\dfrac{1}{x^2}&0&0&0 \\
0&-\dfrac{1}{y^2}&0&0 \\
0&0&\dfrac{1}{z^2}&0 \\
0&0&0&\dfrac{1}{t^2}
\end{array}\right)$\\
