$f(x)=\dfrac{x^2-1}{x}$\\

$f'(x)=\dfrac{\left(x^2-1\right)'x-(x^2-1)\left(x\right)'}{x^2}=\dfrac{2x^2-x^2+1}{x^2}=\dfrac{x^2+1}{x^2}$\\

$f''(x)=\left(1+\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)'=-\dfrac{2}{x^3}$\\

$f''(x)=0 \Leftrightarrow -\dfrac{2}{x^3}=0 \Leftrightarrow x \in \emptyset$\\

$\begin{array}{r|ccc}
           & -\infty &        0       & +\infty \\
    \hline  
    f''(x) &    +      & |     &   -      \\
    f(x)   &     \smile  &  | &    \frown     \\
\end{array}$ \\
