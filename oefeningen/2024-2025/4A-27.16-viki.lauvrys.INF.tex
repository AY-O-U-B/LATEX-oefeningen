$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^5}{x^3-1} dx $ \\

Stel $ t = x^3 - 1 \Rightarrow dt = 3x^2 dx$ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{t+1}{3t} dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{t+1}{t} dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int dt + \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t} $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3}t + \dfrac{1}{3} \ln |t| + C $ \\

$ = \dfrac{x^3 - 1 + \ln |x^3-1|}{3} + C $ \\
