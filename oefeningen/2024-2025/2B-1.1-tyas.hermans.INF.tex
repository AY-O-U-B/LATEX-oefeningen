$\sin(\operatorname{Bgcos}x)$\\

$y = \operatorname{Bgcos}x$\\

$x = \cos y$ voor $y \in \left[\dfrac{-\pi}{2},\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right]$\\

$x^2 = \cos^2 y$\\

$x^2 = 1 - \sin^2 y$\\

$1-x^2 = \sin^2 y$\\

$\sqrt{1-x^2} = \sin y$\\

$\sqrt{1-x^2} = \sin(\operatorname{Bgcos}x)$\\
