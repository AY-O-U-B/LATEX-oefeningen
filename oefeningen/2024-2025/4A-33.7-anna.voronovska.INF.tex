$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{e^xdx}{\sqrt{1-e^{2x}}} = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{e^xdx}{\sqrt{1-(e^x)^2}}$\\

$u = e^x \Rightarrow du = e^xdx$\\

$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{e^xdx}{\sqrt{1-e^{2x}}} = \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-u^2}}du$\\

$= \operatorname{Bgsin}(u) + c = \operatorname{Bgsin}(e^x) + c$\\
