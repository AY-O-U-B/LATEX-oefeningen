Opgave: $\dint \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{1 - 3x^2}}$\\

$= \dint \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{3\left(\left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\right)^2 - x^2\right)}}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\dint \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3}\right)^2 - x^2}}$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \Bgsin \left(\dfrac{x}{\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3}}\right) + c$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \Bgsin \left(\dfrac{3x}{\sqrt{3}}\right) + c$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \Bgsin \left(\dfrac{3\sqrt{3} \cdot x}{3}\right) + c$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \Bgsin \left(\sqrt{3} \cdot x\right) + c$\\
