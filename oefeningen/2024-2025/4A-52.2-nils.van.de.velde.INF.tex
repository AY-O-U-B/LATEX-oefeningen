$\dint\sin^2x\cos^4xdx=\dint(\sin x\cos x)^2\cos^2xdx$\\

$=\dint\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\sin2x\right)^2\cos^2xdx=\dfrac{1}{4}\dint\sin^22x\cos^2xdx$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{16}\dint(1-\cos4x)(1+\cos2x)dx=\dfrac{1}{16}\dint(1+\cos2x-\cos4x-\cos4x\cos2x)dx$\\

$=\dfrac{x}{16}+\dfrac{1}{32}\dint\cos2xdx-\dfrac{1}{64}\sin4x-\dfrac{1}{32}\dint\cos6xdx$\\

$=\dfrac{x}{16}+\dfrac{1}{64}\sin2x-\dfrac{1}{64}\sin4x-\dfrac{1}{192}\sin6x+c$\\
