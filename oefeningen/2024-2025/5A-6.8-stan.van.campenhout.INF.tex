$ \displaystyle\int\limits_0^1x\left(x-1\right)^8dx $\\

O.I. $ =  \displaystyle\int x\left(x-1\right)^8dx \stackrel{t = 1-x}{=} -\displaystyle\int\left(1-t\right)t^8dt = \displaystyle\int t^9-t^8dt $\\

$ = \dfrac{t^{10}}{10}-\dfrac{t^9}{9} + c \stackrel{t = 1-x}{=} \dfrac{\left(1-x\right)^{10}}{10}-\dfrac{\left(1-x\right)^9}{9} + c $\\

$ \Rightarrow $ B.I. $ = \left[\dfrac{\left(1-x\right)^{10}}{10}-\dfrac{\left(1-x\right)^9}{9}\right]_0^1 = -\left(\dfrac{1}{10}-\dfrac{1}{9}\right) = \dfrac{1}{90} $\\
