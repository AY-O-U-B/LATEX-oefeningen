Opgave: $\dint \dfrac{3}{x-2}dx$\\

$\Rightarrow \dint \dfrac{3}{x-2}dx = 3\dint \dfrac{1}{x-2}dx$\\

$\Rightarrow t = x - 2 \Rightarrow dx = dt $\\

$= 3\dint \dfrac{dt}{t} = 3 \ln |t| + c = 3 \ln |x-2| + c$\\
