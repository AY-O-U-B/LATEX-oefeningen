Bereken $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt[3]{3x-1}}$\\

Substitutie: $t = \sqrt[3]{3x-1}$, $t^3 = 3x - 1$, $dx = t^2 dt$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt[3]{3x-1}} &=& \displaystyle\int \dfrac{t^2 dt}{t}\\
&=& \displaystyle\int t dt\\
&=& \dfrac{t^2}{2} + c\\
&=& \dfrac{(3x-1)^{2/3}}{2} + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
