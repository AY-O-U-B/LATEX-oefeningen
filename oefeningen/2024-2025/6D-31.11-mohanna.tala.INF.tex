$\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{|\sin 2^n|+5\ln n}{n^3}$\\

criterium 5:\\

$|\sin 2^n|\leq 1$ en $\ln n \leq n$ voor $n\geq 2$\\

$\displaystyle \dfrac{|\sin 2^n|+5\ln n}{n^3} \leq \dfrac{1+5n}{n^3} = \dfrac{1}{n^3}+\dfrac{5}{n^2}$\\

$\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n^3} \rightarrow$ convergent\\

$\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty}\dfrac{5}{n^2} \rightarrow$ convergent\\

dus $\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{|\sin 2^n|+5\ln n}{n^3}$ is convergent\\
