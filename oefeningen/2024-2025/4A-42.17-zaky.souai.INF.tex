$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{6x^2+7x+5}{3x^2+7x+2} dx$\\

$3x^2+7x+2=(3x+1)(x+2)$\\

$\dfrac{6x^2+7x+5}{3x^2+7x+2}=2 + \dfrac{-7x+1}{3x^2+7x+2}$\\

$\dfrac{-7x+1}{3x^2+7x+2}= \dfrac{-7x+1}{(3x+1)(x+2)} = \dfrac{A}{3x+1}+ \dfrac{B}{x+2}$\\

$A=\left.\dfrac{-7x+1}{x+2}\right|_{x=-\dfrac{1}{3}}=\dfrac{\dfrac{10}{3}}{\dfrac{5}{3}}=2$\\

$B=\left.\dfrac{-7x+1}{3x+1}\right|_{x=-2}=\dfrac{15}{-5}=-3$\\

$\dfrac{6x^2+7x+5}{3x^2+7x+2}=2+\dfrac{2}{3x+1}-\dfrac{3}{x+2}$\\

$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{6x^2+7x+5}{3x^2+7x+2} dx = \displaystyle \int 2 dx + 2 \displaystyle \int\dfrac{dx}{3x+1} - 3 \displaystyle \int\dfrac{dx}{x+2}$\\

$=2x+\dfrac{2}{3}\ln|3x+1|-3\ln|x+2|+C$
