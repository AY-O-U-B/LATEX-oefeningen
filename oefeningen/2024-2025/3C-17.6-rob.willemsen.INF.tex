$\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\sin x - x\cos x}{x(1-\cos x)} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\overset{H}{=}\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\cos x - \cos x + x\sin x}{1-(\cos x + x\sin x)}$ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{x\sin x}{1-\cos x + x\sin x} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\overset{H}{=}\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\sin x + x\cos x}{\sin x + \sin x + x\cos x}$ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\sin x + x\cos x}{2\sin x + x\cos x} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\overset{H}{=}\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\cos x + \cos x - x\sin x}{2\cos x + \cos x - x\sin x}$ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{2\cos x - x\sin x}{3\cos x - x\sin x} = \dfrac{2}{3}$ \\
