\textbf{De poolcoordinaten van} $(4, -4)$; $(r, \theta) - ?$ \\

$r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \Rightarrow r  = \sqrt{4^2 + (-4)^2} = \sqrt{32} = 4\sqrt{2}$\\

$\tan\theta = \dfrac{-y}{x} \Rightarrow \tan\theta = \dfrac{-4}{4} = -1 \Rightarrow \theta = \tan^-1(-1) = -\dfrac{\pi}{4} \Rightarrow \theta = 2\pi -\dfrac{\pi}{4}$\\ 

$\Rightarrow
 \theta = \dfrac{8\pi}{4} 
-\dfrac{\pi}{4} = \dfrac{7\pi}{4}$\\

\textbf{\emph{Resultaat:}} $(r, \theta) = \left(4\sqrt{2}, \dfrac{7\pi}{4}\right)$
