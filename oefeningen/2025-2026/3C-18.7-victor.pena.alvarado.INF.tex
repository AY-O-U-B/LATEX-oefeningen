	$\lim\limits_{x\to0} \dfrac{e^{x}-e^{-x}}{x} = \dfrac{1-1}{0} = \dfrac{0}{0} $\\
	
	$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{x\to0} \dfrac{e^{x}-e^{-x}}{x} = \lim\limits_{x\to0} \dfrac{\dx\left(e^{x}-e^{-x}\right)}{\dx x} = \lim\limits_{x\to0}\dfrac{e^x + e^{-x}}{1} = 2 $ \\
