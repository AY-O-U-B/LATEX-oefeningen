Bepaal van de volgende functie gegeven in poolcoordinaten de oppervlakte begrensd door de grafiek. \\

$ r = - \cos \theta $ \\

r is positief op interval $ \left[ \dfrac{\pi}{2}, \dfrac{3\pi}{2} \right] $ \\

$ S = \dfrac{1}{2} \dint_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{3\pi}{2}} \cos^2 \theta d\theta  $ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \dint_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{3\pi}{2}} \left( \dfrac{1 + \cos 2\theta}{2} \right) d\theta  $ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \dint_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{3\pi}{2}} \left( 1 + \cos 2\theta \right) d\theta  $ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \dint_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{3\pi}{2}} d\theta + \dfrac{1}{4} \dint_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{3\pi}{2}} \cos 2\theta d\theta  $ \\

$ u = 2 \theta \Rightarrow du = 2 d\theta \Rightarrow d\theta = \dfrac{du}{2} $ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \dint_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{3\pi}{2}} d\theta + \dfrac{1}{8} \dint_{\pi}^{3\pi} \cos u du  $ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \left[ \theta \right]_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{3\pi}{2}} + \dfrac{1}{8} \left[ \sin u \right]_{\pi}^{3\pi} $ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \left[ \dfrac{3\pi}{2} - \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right] + \dfrac{1}{8} \left[ 0 - 0 \right] $ \\

$= \dfrac{\pi}{4} $ \\
