$f(-x) = -\sin x = -f(x) \Rightarrow$  f(x) is oneven $\Rightarrow a_0 = a_n = 0 $\\

$b_n = \dfrac{2}{\pi}\integral_{0}^{\pi}\sin x\sin nxdx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\pi}\integral_{0}^{\pi} (\cos(x - nx) - \cos (x + nx))dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\pi}\left[\dfrac{\sin (x - nx)}{1-n} - \dfrac{\sin (x + nx)}{1+n}\right]_{0}^{\pi}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\pi}\left(\dfrac{\sin (\pi - n\pi)}{1 - \pi} - \dfrac{\sin (\pi + n\pi)}{1 + n}\right)$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\pi}\left(\dfrac{0}{1 - \pi} - \dfrac{0}{1 + n}\right) = 0\Rightarrow b_n = 0$ voor $n \neq 1$ \\

$b_1 = \dfrac{2}{\pi}\integral_{0}^{\pi}\sin^2xdx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\pi} \integral_{0}^{\pi} ( 1- \cos2x)dx$ \\

$=\dfrac{1}{\pi}\left[x-\dfrac{\sin 2x}{2}\right]_0^{\pi} = 1$ \\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\sum_{n = 1}^\infty b_n\sin nx$ met $b_n = 0$, $b_1 = 1 $ \\

$\Rightarrow$ fourierreeks: $\sin x$
