$ -1 - 2\sqrt{2}i = (x + yi)^2 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = -1 \\
    2xy = -2\sqrt{2}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = -1 \\
    xy = -\sqrt{2}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = -1 \\
    x^2y^2 = (-\sqrt{2})^2 = 2
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$x^2$ en $y^2$ zijn oplossingen van:\\

$ \lambda^2 - \lambda - 2 = 0 $\\

$ \Delta = 1 + 4 * 2 = 9 $\\

$ \lambda_{1,2} = \dfrac{1 \pm \sqrt{9}}{2} $\\

$ \lambda_1 = \dfrac{4}{2}=2 $ en $ \lambda_2 = -1 $\\

$ x = \sqrt{2} $ en $ y = 1 $\\
