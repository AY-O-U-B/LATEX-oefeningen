$I = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{\tan x-1}dx$\\

Stel $ t=\tan x \Rightarrow dx=\dfrac{1}{1+t^2}dt$\\

Dus:\\

$ I=\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{t-1}\cdot\dfrac{1}{1+t^2}dt =\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{(t-1)(t^2+1)}dt$\\

$ \dfrac{1}{(t-1)(t^2+1)}=\dfrac{A}{t-1}+\dfrac{Bt+C}{t^2+1}$\\

$ A=\left.\dfrac{1}{t^2+1}\right|_{t=1}=\dfrac{1}{2}$\\

$ Bi+C=\left.\dfrac{1}{t-1}\right|_{t=i}=\dfrac{1}{i-1}$\\

$ =\dfrac{-1-i}{(-1+i)(-1-i)}=\dfrac{-1-i}{2}$\\

$ =-\dfrac{1}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}i$\\

$ B=-\dfrac{1}{2},C=-\dfrac{1}{2}$\\

$ I=\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{t-1}dt-\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{t+1}{t^2+1}dt$\\

$ =\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{t-1}dt-\dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{2t}{t^2+1}dt-\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{t^2+1}dt$\\

$ =\dfrac{1}{2}\ln|t-1|-\dfrac{1}{4}\ln(t^2+1)-\dfrac{1}{2}\operatorname{Bgtan}t+c$\\

Terug substitueren:\\

$ I=\dfrac{1}{2}\ln|\tan x-1|-\dfrac{1}{4}\ln(\tan^2x+1)-\dfrac{1}{2}x+c$\\

$ 1+\tan^2x=\dfrac{1}{\cos^2x}$\\

Dus:\\

$ I=\dfrac{1}{2}\ln\left|\dfrac{\sin x-\cos x}{\cos x}\right|-\dfrac{1}{4}\ln\left(\dfrac{1}{\cos^2x}\right)-\dfrac{1}{2}x+c$\\

$ =\dfrac{1}{2}\ln|\sin x-\cos x|-\dfrac{1}{2}x+c$\\
