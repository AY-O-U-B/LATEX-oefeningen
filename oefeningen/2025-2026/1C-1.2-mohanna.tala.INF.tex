$\left(-2,-\dfrac{\pi}{6} \right)$\\

Punt: $\left( r,\theta \right) = \left(-2, -\dfrac{\pi}{6}\right)$\\

$x = r\cos\theta,\qquad y= r\sin\theta$\\

$x = -2\cos\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{6} \right)$\\

$ =-2\cdot\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2} $\\

$ =-\sqrt{3}$\\

$y = -2\sin \left(-\dfrac{\pi}{6}\right) $\\

$ = -2\cdot\left(-\dfrac{1}{2}\right) $\\

$ =1$\\

$\boxed{\left(-\sqrt{3},1 \right)}$\\
