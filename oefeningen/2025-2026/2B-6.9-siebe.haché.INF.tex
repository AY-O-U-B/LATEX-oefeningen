\newcommand{\Bgtan}{\operatorname{Bgtan}}
\medskip
\normalsize

Toon aan dat $5\Bgtan\dfrac{1}{7} + 2\Bgtan\dfrac{3}{79} = \dfrac{\pi}{4}$. \\

$\alpha = \Bgtan\dfrac{1}{7}$ en $\beta = \Bgtan\dfrac{3}{79} \Rightarrow \tan \alpha = \dfrac{1}{7}$ en $\tan \beta = \dfrac{3}{79}$ \\

We gebruiken: $\tan 2\theta = \dfrac{2\tan\theta}{1-\tan^2\theta}$ \\

$\tan 2\alpha = \dfrac{2\cdot\dfrac{1}{7}}{1-\left(\dfrac{1}{7}\right)^2}$\\

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{2}{7}}{1-\dfrac{1}{49}} = \dfrac{\dfrac{2}{7}}{\dfrac{48}{49}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{7}{24}$ \\

Somformule geeft: \\

$\tan 3\alpha = \tan(2\alpha + \alpha)$ \\

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{7}{24} + \dfrac{1}{7}}{1 - \dfrac{7}{24}\cdot\dfrac{1}{7}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{73}{168}}{\dfrac{23}{24}} = \dfrac{73}{161}$\\

$\tan 4\alpha = \tan(3\alpha + \alpha)$ \\

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{73}{161} + \dfrac{1}{7}}{1 - \dfrac{73}{161}\cdot\dfrac{1}{7}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{672}{1127}}{\dfrac{1054}{1127}} = \dfrac{336}{527}$ \\

$\tan 5\alpha = \tan(4\alpha + \alpha)$ \\

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{336}{527} + \dfrac{1}{7}}{1 - \dfrac{336}{527}\cdot\dfrac{1}{7}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2879}{3353}$\\

Nu $\tan 2\beta$: \\

$\tan 2\beta = \dfrac{2\cdot\dfrac{3}{79}}{1-\left(\dfrac{3}{79}\right)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{6}{79}}{1-\dfrac{9}{6241}} = \dfrac{\dfrac{6}{79}}{\dfrac{6232}{6241}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{237}{3116}$\\

We bepalen $\tan(5\alpha + 2\beta)$. \\

Somformule geeft: \\

$\tan(5\alpha + 2\beta) = \dfrac{\dfrac{2879}{3353} + \dfrac{237}{3116}}{1 - \dfrac{2879}{3353}\cdot\dfrac{237}{3116}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{9765625}{10447948}}{\dfrac{9765625}{10447948}}$\\

$= 1$ \\

Dus \\

$5\alpha + 2\beta = \Bgtan(1) = \dfrac{\pi}{4}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 5\Bgtan\dfrac{1}{7} + 2\Bgtan\dfrac{3}{79} = \dfrac{\pi}{4}$\\
