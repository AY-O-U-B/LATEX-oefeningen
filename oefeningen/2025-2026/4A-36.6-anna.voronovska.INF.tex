$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{4x^2+9} = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{4\left(x^2 + \dfrac{9}{4}\right)} = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{4\left(x^2 + \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^2\right)} = \dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x^2 + \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^2}$\\

Formule is:  $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x^2+a^2} = \dfrac{1}{a}\operatorname{Bgtan}\dfrac{x}{a} + c$:\\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{3}{2}} \operatorname{Bgtan}\dfrac{x}{\dfrac{3}{2}} + c$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{6}\operatorname{Bgtan}\dfrac{2x}{3} + c$\\
