$f(x) = x^2-3x, \text{punt} \left(\dfrac{3}{2}, ...\right)$\\

$f\left( \dfrac{3}{2} \right) = \left (\dfrac{3}{2} \right)^2 -3\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)$\\

$ = \dfrac{9}{4} - \dfrac{9}{2}$\\

$ = \dfrac{-9}{4}$\\

$f'(x) = 2x-3$\\

$f'\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right) = 2\dfrac{3}{2}-3$\\

$ = 0$\\

$y - \dfrac{9}{4} = 0$\\

$y = \dfrac{-9}{4}$\\
