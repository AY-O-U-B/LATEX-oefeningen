$-2i = (a+bi)^2 = a^2-b^2+2abi$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    a^2 - b^2 = 0 \\
    ab = -1
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    a^2 = b^2 \\
    b = \dfrac{-1}{a}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    a^2 = \dfrac{1}{a^2} \\
    b = \dfrac{-1}{a}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    a^4 = 1 \\
    b = \dfrac{-1}{a}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow a = 1$, $b = -1$ of $a = -1$, $b=1$\\
