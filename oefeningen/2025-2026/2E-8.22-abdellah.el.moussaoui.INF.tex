$3^{x+1} - 3^{x-1} = 8$\\

$\Rightarrow 3^x \left(3-\dfrac{1}{3}\right) = 8$\\

$\Rightarrow 3^x = 3$\\

$\Rightarrow \log_33^x = \log_33^1$\\

$\Rightarrow \boxed{x = 1}$\\
