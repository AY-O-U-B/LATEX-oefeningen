$\displaystyle\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{dx}{2+\sin x}$\\


$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{2+\sin x}
=
\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{2+\dfrac{2t}{1+t^2}} \cdot \dfrac{2}{1+t^2}dt$\\

$=
\displaystyle\int
\dfrac{2}{\left(2+\dfrac{2t}{1+t^2}\right)(1+t^2)}dt$\\

$=
\displaystyle\int
\dfrac{2}{2(1+t^2)+\dfrac{2t}{1+t^2}(1+t^2)}dt$\\

$=
\displaystyle\int
\dfrac{2}{2+2t^2+2t}dt$\\

$=
\displaystyle\int
\dfrac{1}{t^2+t+1}dt$\\

$=
\displaystyle\int
\dfrac{1}{\left(t+\dfrac12\right)^2+\dfrac34}dt$\\

$=
\dfrac{2\sqrt3}{3}\operatorname{Bgtan}\dfrac{2t+1}{\sqrt3}+c$\\

$=
\dfrac{2\sqrt3}{3}\operatorname{Bgtan}\dfrac{2\tan\dfrac{x}{2}+1}{\sqrt3}+c$\\

$\Rightarrow
\displaystyle\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}
\dfrac{dx}{2+\sin x}
=
\left[
\dfrac{2\sqrt3}{3}\operatorname{Bgtan}
\dfrac{2\tan\dfrac{x}{2}+1}{\sqrt3}
\right]_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}$\\

$=
\dfrac{2\sqrt3}{3}
\left(
\dfrac{\pi}{3}-\dfrac{\pi}{6}
\right)$\\

$=
\dfrac{\pi\sqrt3}{9}$
