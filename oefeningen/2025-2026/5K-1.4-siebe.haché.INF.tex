Bereken de booglengte $L$ van de kromme $r = \sin^2\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right)$ op het interval $[0, 2\pi]$. \\

afgeleide: \\

$r' = 2 \sin\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \cdot \cos\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} = \sin\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \cos\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right)$ \\

$r^2 + \left( r' \right)^2 = \left( \sin^2\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \right)^2 + \left( \sin\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \cos\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \right)^2$ \\

$= \sin^4\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) + \sin^2\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \cos^2\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right)$ \\

$= \sin^2\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \left( \sin^2\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) + \cos^2\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \right)$ \\

$= \sin^2\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \cdot 1 = \sin^2\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right)$ \\

Integraal: \\

$L = \displaystyle\int_{0}^{2\pi} \sin\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right)d\theta$ \\

$= \left[ -2 \cos\left(\dfrac{\theta}{2}\right) \right]_{0}^{2\pi}$ \\

$= \left( -2 \cos\left(\dfrac{2\pi}{2}\right) \right) - \left( -2 \cos\left(\dfrac{0}{2}\right) \right)$ \\

$= \left( -2 \cos \pi \right) - \left( -2 \cos 0 \right)$\\

$= \left( -2 \cdot\left( -1 \right) \right) - \left( -2 \cdot 1 \right) = 2 + 2 = 4$ \\
