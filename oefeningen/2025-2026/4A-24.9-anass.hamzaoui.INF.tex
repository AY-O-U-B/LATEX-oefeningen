$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x} \ln (\ln x) dx$ \\

$t = \ln x, dt = \dfrac{dx}{x}$ \\

$\displaystyle\int \ln t dt$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    u = \ln t \\
    dv = dt \\
\end{array}
\right. 
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    du = \dfrac{dt}{t} \\
    v = t \\
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$= t \ln t - \displaystyle\int dt$ \\

$= t \ln t - t + c$ \\

$= \ln x \ln (\ln x) - \ln x + c$ \\
