$\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{n+1}{n!}$\\

$ \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \left| \dfrac{\dfrac{n+2}{\left(n+1\right)!}}{\dfrac{n+1}{n!}} \right| $\\

$= \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \left| \dfrac{n+2}{\left(n+1\right)!} \cdot \dfrac{n!}{n+1}\right|$\\

$= \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \left| \dfrac{n+2}{\left(n+1\right) \left(n+1\right)} \right| $\\

$= \lim\limits_{n\to+\infty} \dfrac{n+2}{\left(n+1\right)^2}$\\

$= 0$\\

Omdat $0 < 1$, is de reeks convergent volgens het Criterium van d'Alembert.\\
