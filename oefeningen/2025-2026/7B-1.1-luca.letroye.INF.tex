$w = e^{-x^2-y^2}$\\

$x= t$\\

$y = \sqrt{t}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x} = -2xe^{-x^2-y^2}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y} = -2ye^{-x^2-y^2}$\\

$\dfrac{dx}{dt} = 1$\\

$\dfrac{dy}{dt} = \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{t}}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} = -2xe^{-x^2-y^2}\cdot (1) + -2ye^{-x^2-y^2}\cdot \left(\dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{t}}\right)$\\

$ = -te^{-t^2-t} - e^{-t^2-t}$\\

$ = (-2t-1)e^{-t^2-t}$\\
