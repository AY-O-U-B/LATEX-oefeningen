$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 2x \Rightarrow\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(5,2) = 10$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = -8y \Rightarrow\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(5,2) = -16$\\
