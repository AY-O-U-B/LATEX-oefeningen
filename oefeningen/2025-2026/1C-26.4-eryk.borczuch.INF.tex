$6 \operatorname{cis}80^\circ : 2 \operatorname{cis}20^\circ$\\

$= \dfrac{6}{2} \operatorname{cis}\left(80^\circ - 20^\circ \right)$\\

$= 3 \operatorname{cis}\left(60^\circ \right) $\\

$= 3 \cos (60^\circ) + 3i \sin (60^\circ)$\\

$= \dfrac{3}{2} + \dfrac{3\sqrt{3}}{2}i$\\
