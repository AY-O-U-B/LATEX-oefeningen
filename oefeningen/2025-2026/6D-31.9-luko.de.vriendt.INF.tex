$\sum \limits_{n = 1}^{\infty} \dfrac{n!}{7^n}$ \\

criterium d'Alembert \\

$\lim \limits_{n \rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{(n+1)! \cdot 7^n}{7^{n+1} \cdot n!} = \lim \limits_{n \rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{(n+1) \cdot \cancel{n!\cdot7^n}}{\cancel{7^n} \cdot 7 \cdot \cancel{n!}} = +\infty > 1$ \\

$\Rightarrow$ reeks is divergent\\
