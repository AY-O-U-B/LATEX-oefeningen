$2^{x-1} + 2^{1-x} = \dfrac{5}{2}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{2^x}{2} + \dfrac{2}{2^x} = \dfrac{5}{2}$\\

Stel $t = 2^x$ met $(t > 0)$:\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{t}{2} + \dfrac{2}{t} = \dfrac{5}{2}$\\

$\Rightarrow t^2 + 4 = 5t$\\

$\Rightarrow t^2 - 5t + 4 = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow (t-1)(t-4) = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow t = 1$ of $t = 4$\\

$t = 1 \Rightarrow 2^x = 1 \Rightarrow x = 0$\\

$t = 4 \Rightarrow 2^x = 4 \Rightarrow x = 2$\\

$x \in \{0, 2\}$
