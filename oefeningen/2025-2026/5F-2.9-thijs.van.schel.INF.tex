$A = \dfrac{1}{2} \dint_{0}^{2\pi} (r_1^2 - r_2^2) d\theta$\\

$\left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        r_1 = 3 + \sin(\theta)\\
        r_2 = 2 + \sin(\theta)\\
    \end{array}
\right.$\\

$A = \dfrac{1}{2} \dint_{0}^{2\pi} \left((3 + \sin(\theta))^2 - (2 + \sin(\theta))^2\right) d\theta$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2} \dint_{0}^{2\pi} \left((9 + 6\sin(\theta) + \sin^2(\theta)) - (4 + 4\sin(\theta) + \sin^2(\theta))\right) d\theta$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2} \dint_{0}^{2\pi} (5 + 2\sin(\theta)) d\theta$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2} \left[ 5\theta - 2\cos(\theta) \right]_{0}^{2\pi}$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2} \left( (10\pi - 2\cos(2\pi)) - (0 - 2\cos(0)) \right)$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2} \left( (10\pi - 2) - (-2) \right)$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2} (10\pi)$\\

$A = 5\pi$\\
