Bereken: $\forall x>0: \Bgtan x+\Bgcot x$\\[6pt]

$\Bgtan\left(\tan\alpha\right)+\Bgcot\left(\tan\alpha\right)$\\

met $\tan\alpha=x$ en $\alpha\in\left]0,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right[$\\

$=\alpha+\Bgcot\left(\tan\alpha\right)
   =\alpha+\dfrac{\pi}{2}-\alpha
   =\dfrac{\pi}{2}$\\

want $\cot\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}-\alpha\right)=\tan\alpha
      \Rightarrow
      \Bgcot\left(\tan\alpha\right)=\dfrac{\pi}{2}-\alpha$.\\
