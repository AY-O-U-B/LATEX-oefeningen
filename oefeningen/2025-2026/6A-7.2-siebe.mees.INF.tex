$x_1, x_2, x_3 = ?? $ als $ x_4 = 24 $ en $ x_6 = 96$\\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
x_4 = 24\\
ar^3 = 24
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
x_6 = 96\\
ar^5 = 96
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\rightarrow \dfrac{ar^5}{ar^3} = \dfrac{96}{24}$\\

$r^2 = 4$\\

$r = 2 $ of $ r = -2$\\

$\Rightarrow $ als $ r = 2:$\\

$ar^3 = 24$\\

$a \cdot 2^3 = 24$\\

$8a = 24$\\

$a = 3$\\

$\Rightarrow x_1 = 3, x_2 = 6, x_3 = 12$\\

$\Rightarrow $ als $ r = -2:$\\

$ar^3 = 24$\\

$a \cdot (-2)^3 = 24$\\

$-8a = 24$\\

$a = -3$\\

$\Rightarrow x_1 = -3, x_2 = 6, x_3 = -12$\\
