$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sqrt{3x}}{5x}dx $\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sqrt{3}\sqrt{x}}{5x}dx $\\

$ = \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{5}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{x}dx $\\

$ = \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{5}\displaystyle\int x^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}dx $\\

$ = \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{5}\cdot 2x^{\tfrac{1}{2}} + C $\\

$ = \dfrac{2\sqrt{3}}{5}x^{\tfrac{1}{2}} + C $\\
