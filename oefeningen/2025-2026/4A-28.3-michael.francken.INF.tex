$\dint\dfrac{5xdx}{9x^4+1}$\\

$u = 3x^2$\\

$du = 6xdx$\\


$\Rightarrow \dint\dfrac{5x}{u^2+1}du$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{5}{6}\dint\dfrac{du}{u^2+1}$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{6}\operatorname{Bgtan}(u) +c$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{6}\operatorname{Bgtan}(3x^2) +c$\\
