$f_n : [1, +\infty] \to \mathbb{R}: x \to \dfrac{1}{nx^2}$ \\

$ \forall x \in \mathbb{R}: \lim\limits_{n \to \infty}\left(\dfrac{1}{nx^2}\right) = 0 \Rightarrow $ puntgewijs convergent \\

$\sup\limits_{x \in [1, +\infty[}\left|\dfrac{1}{nx^2} - 0\right| = \sup\limits_{x \in [1, +\infty[}\left|\dfrac{1}{nx^2}\right| = \dfrac{1}{n} $ \\

$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{n \to \infty}\left||f_n - f||\right_\infty = \lim\limits_{n \to \infty}\left \dfrac{1}{n} = 0 \Rightarrow $  uniform convergent \\
