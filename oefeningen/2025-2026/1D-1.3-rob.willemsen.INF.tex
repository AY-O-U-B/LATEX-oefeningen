$a(z-i) + b(z+2i) = 3z - i$ \\

$az - ai + bz + 2bi = 3z - i$ \\

$(a+b)z + (-a+2b)i = 3z - 1i$ \\

$\left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        a + b = 3 \\
        -a + 2b = -1 
    \end{array}
  \right.
  \Rightarrow
  \left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        a = 3 - b \\
        -(3 - b) + 2b = -1 
    \end{array}
  \right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow
  \left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        a = 3 - b \\
        3b - 3 = -1 \Rightarrow 3b = 2 
    \end{array}
  \right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow
  \left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        a = \dfrac{7}{3} \\
        b = \dfrac{2}{3} 
    \end{array}
  \right.$ \\

\textbf{Antwoord:} $a = \dfrac{7}{3}, b = \dfrac{2}{3}$ \\
