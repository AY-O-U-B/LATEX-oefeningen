$w = e^{-x^2 - y^2}$ met $x = t$ en $y = \sqrt{t}$ \\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt} = \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x} \dfrac{dx}{dt} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y} \dfrac{dy}{dt}$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x} = -2xe^{-x^2 - y^2}$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y} = -2ye^{-x^2 - y^2}$ \\

$\dfrac{dx}{dt} = 1$ \\

$\dfrac{dy}{dt} = \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{t}}$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} = \left(-2xe^{-x^2 - y^2}\right) \cdot 1 + \left(-2y e^{-x^2 - y^2}\right) \cdot \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{t}}$ \\

$= -2x e^{-x^2 - y^2} - t^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}ye^{-x^2 - y^2}$ \\

$= -2t e^{-t^2-t} - t^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}e^{-t^2-t}$ \\

$= -2t e^{-t^2-t} - e^{-t^2-t}$ \\

$ = (-2t - 1)e^{-t^2-t}$ \\
