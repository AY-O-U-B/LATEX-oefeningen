$\dint \dfrac{8}{1-3x} dx $ \\

$= 8 \dint \dfrac{dx}{1-3x} $ \\

Stel $ t = 1 - 3x \Rightarrow dt = -3dx \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{-dt}{3} $ \\

$= -\dfrac{8}{3} \dint \dfrac{dt}{t} $ \\

$= -\dfrac{8}{3} \ln|t| + c $ \\

$= -\dfrac{8}{3} \ln|1-3x| + c $ \\
