$\displaystyle\int e^{\sqrt{x}}dx$\\

stel $t = \sqrt{x}$\\

$t^2 = x$\\

$2tdt = dx$\\

$= 2\displaystyle\int te^tdt$\\

$\left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        u =  t \\
        dv = e^tdt
    \end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left\{
    \begin{array}{l}
        du = dt \\
        v = e^t
    \end{array}
\right .$\\

$= 2te^t-2\displaystyle\int e^tdt$\\

$= 2te^t-2e^t + C$\\

$= 2\sqrt{x}e^{\sqrt{x}}-2e^{\sqrt{x}} + C$\\
