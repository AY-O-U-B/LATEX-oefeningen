$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x^2+5} $\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{5\left(\dfrac{x^2}{5}+1\right)} $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{5}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\left(\dfrac{x}{\sqrt{5}}\right)^2+1} $\\

$t = \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{5}}\Rightarrow dx = \sqrt{5}dt $\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{\sqrt{5}}{5} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{t^2+1}$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{5}}{5} \bgtan(t) +C$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{5}}{5} \bgtan\left(\dfrac{x}{\sqrt{5}}\right) +C$\\
