$f : \Omega \subseteq \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}^4: x\rightarrow \left(2^{\sec{x}}, \coth{x}, \log_5 \sqrt{\cos{x}},x^{{x}^x}\right) $ \\

$\dfrac{\partial }{\partial x}(2^{\sec{x}}) = 2^{\sec x} \cdot \ln 2 \cdot \sec x \tan x$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial }{\partial x}(\coth{x}) = -\csc^2{x} $ \\

$\dfrac{\partial }{\partial x}(\log_5 \sqrt{\cos{x}}) = \dfrac{-\tan{x}}{2\ln{5}} $ \\

$\dfrac{\partial }{\partial x}\left(x^{{x}^x}\right) = \dfrac{\partial }{\partial x}(e^{x{^x}\ln{x}})$ \\ 

$ = e^{x{^x}\ln{x}} \cdot \dfrac{\partial }{\partial x}(x^{x}\ln{x}) $ \\

$ = x^{{x}^x} \cdot \dfrac{\partial }{\partial x}(x^{x}\ln{x}) $ \\

$ = x^{{x}^x} \left(\dfrac{x^x}{x} + x^x \cdot (\ln{x} + 1)\cdot \ln{x}\right) $ \\

$ = x^{{x}^x} \left(x^{x}(\ln{x}+1)\ln{x} + x^{x-1} \right)$ \\ 


$Df(x)= \left(\begin{array}{c}
2^{\sec x} \cdot\ln 2  \cdot\sec x \tan x \\
-\csc^2 x \\
-\dfrac{\tan x}{2\ln 5} \\
x^{x^x}\left(x^{x}(\ln x+1)\ln x + x^{x-1}\right)
\end{array}\right)$ \\
