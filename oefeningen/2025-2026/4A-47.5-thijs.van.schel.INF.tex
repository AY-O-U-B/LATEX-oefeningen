$\dint \dfrac{2x^5+6x^3-2x}{x^6-3x^2-2}dx = \dint \dfrac{2x(x^4+3x^2-1)}{x^6-3x^2-2}dx$\\

$t=x^2 \Rightarrow dt = 2xdx$\\

$\dint \dfrac{t^2+3t-1}{t^3-3t-2}dt = \dint \dfrac{t^2+3t-1}{(t+1)(t^2-t-2)}dt$\\

$= \dint \dfrac{t^2+3t-1}{(t+1)^2(t-2)}dt \Rightarrow \dfrac{A}{(t+1)^2} + \dfrac{B}{(t+1)}+ \dfrac{C}{(t-2)}$\\

$A = \left . \dfrac{t^2+3t-1}{t-2} \right |_{x=-1}=1$\\

$C = \left . \dfrac{t^2+3t-1}{(t+1)^2} \right |_{x=2}=1$\\

$\dfrac{t^2+3t-1}{(t+1)^2(t-2)}-\dfrac{1}{(t+1)^2}= \dfrac{t^2+3t-1-(t-2)}{(t+1)^2(t-2)}$\\

$= \dfrac{t^2+2t+1}{(t+1)^2(t-2)}=\dfrac{(t+1)^2}{(t+1)^2(t-2)}=\dfrac{1}{(t-2)}$\\

$B = \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{t-2}}{t+1}=\left .\dfrac{t+1}{t-2}\right |_{x=-1}=0$\\

$\dint \dfrac{1}{(t+1)^2}dt + \dint \dfrac{1}{t-2}dt= -\dfrac{1}{t+1}+\ln|t-2|+C$\\

$=-\dfrac{1}{x^2+1}+\ln|x^2-2|+C$
