$\limit_{x\to0}\dfrac{1 - \cos x + x\sin x}{2x^2} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\stackrel{(H)}{=}\limit_{x\to0}\dfrac{\sin x + x\cos x + \sin x}{4x} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\stackrel{(H)}{=}\limit_{x\to0}\dfrac{\cos x + \cos x - x\sin x + \cos x}{4}$

$= \limit_{x\to0}\dfrac{3\cos x - x\sin x}{4} = \dfrac{3}{4}$
