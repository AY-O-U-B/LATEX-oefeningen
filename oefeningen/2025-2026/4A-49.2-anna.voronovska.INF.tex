$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{\sin xdx}{(1-\cos x)^3}$\\

$t = 1-\cos x \Rightarrow \dfrac{dt}{dx} = \sin x$\\

$= \displaystyle\int t^{-3}dt$\\

$= \dfrac{t^{-2}}{-2} + c$\\

$= \dfrac{-1}{2(1-\cos x)^2} + c$\\
