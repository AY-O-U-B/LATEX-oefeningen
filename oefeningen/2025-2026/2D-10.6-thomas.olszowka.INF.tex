$\llim_{x \rightarrow -\sqrt{3}} \dfrac{x^2 + 2\sqrt{3}x + 3}{x^2 - 3} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

We noemen de vergelijking in de teller en noemer respectievelijk $t(x)$ en $n(x)$\\

$t(x)$ is van de vorm $(a+b)^2$ met: \\

$\Rightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{lll}
a^2 = x^2 &\Rightarrow& a = x    \\

b^2 = 3 &\Rightarrow& b = \sqrt{3}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$t(x) = \left(x+\sqrt{3}\right)^2 =\left(x+\sqrt{3}\right)\left(x+\sqrt{3}\right) $\\

$n(x)$ is van de vorm $a^2 - b^2 = (a+b)(a-b)$ met:

$\Rightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{lll}
a^2 = x^2 &\Rightarrow& a = x    \\

b^2 = 3 &\Rightarrow& b = \sqrt{3}  \\
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$n(x) = \left(x+\sqrt{3}\right) \left(x-\sqrt{3}\right)$ \\

$\llim_{x \rightarrow -\sqrt{3}} \dfrac{t(x)}{n(x)}$ \\

$= \llim_{x \rightarrow -\sqrt{3}} \dfrac{\cancel{\left(x+\sqrt{3}\right)}\left(x+\sqrt{3}\right)}{\cancel{\left(x+\sqrt{3}\right)}\left(x-\sqrt{3}\right)}$ \\

$=\llim_{x \rightarrow -\sqrt{3}} \dfrac{x+\sqrt{3}}{x-\sqrt{3}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{-\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{3}}{-\sqrt{3} - \sqrt{3}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{0}{-2\sqrt{3}}$ \\

$= 0$ \\
