$\dint\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3x^2+2}} dx$\\

$t = \sqrt{3}x \Rightarrow dt = \sqrt{3} dx \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} dt$\\

$= \dint \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{t^2+2}} \cdot \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} dt$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \dint \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{t^2+2}} dt$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \ln|t + \sqrt{t^2+2}| + C$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \ln|\sqrt{3}x + \sqrt{3x^2+2}| + C$\\
