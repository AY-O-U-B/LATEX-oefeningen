$ \displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{\infty}\dfrac{\ln n}{n} $\\

\begin{enumerate}

\item Op $ [2,+\infty[ : f(x) > 0 $\\

\item Dalend: $ f'(x)=\dfrac{1-\ln x}{x^2}<0 $\\

\end{enumerate}

$ \Rightarrow $ integraalcriterium \\

$ \displaystyle\int_{2}^{+\infty}\dfrac{\ln x}{x}dx
=\lim\limits_{b \rightarrow +\infty}\displaystyle\int_{2}^{b}\dfrac{\ln x}{x}dx =\lim\limits_{b \rightarrow +\infty}\left[\dfrac{(\ln x)^2}{2}\right]_{2}^{+\infty} =+\infty $\\

Dus $ \displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{\infty}\dfrac{\ln n}{n} $ is divergent \\
