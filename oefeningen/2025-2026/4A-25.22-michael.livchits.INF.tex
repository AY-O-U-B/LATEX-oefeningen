$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{2x^2-x+2}{2x-3}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\left(x+1+\dfrac{5}{2x-3}\right)dx$\\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2}+x+\displaystyle\int\left(\dfrac{5}{2x-3}\right)dx$\\

stel $u = 2x-3$\\

$du = 2dx$\\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2}+x+\displaystyle\dfrac{5}{2}\int\dfrac{du}{u}$\\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2}+x+\displaystyle\dfrac{5}{2}\ln|u|$\\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2}+x+\displaystyle\dfrac{5}{2}\ln|2x-3|+C$\\
