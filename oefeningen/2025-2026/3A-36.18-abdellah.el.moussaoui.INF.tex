$f(x)=\dfrac{\ln x}{x}$\\

$f'(x)=\dfrac{\left(\ln x\right)'x-\left(\ln x\right)x'}{x^2}$\\

$=\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{x}\cdot x- \ln x}{x^2}$\\

$=\dfrac{1- \ln x}{x^2}$\\
