$f(x) = (x^2+5x-6)(x^2-2x+4)$\\

$f(x) = x^4 -2x^3 +4x^2 +5x^3 -10x^2 +20x -6x^2 +12x -24$\\

$= x^4 +3x^3 -12x^2 +32x$\\

$f'(x) = 4x^3 +9x^2 -24x + 32$\\

formule: \\

$f'(x) = (2x+5)(x^2-2x+4)+(2x-2)(x^2+5x-6)$\\

$= 2x^3 -4x^2 +8x +5x^2 -10x +20 +2x^3 +10x^2 -12x -2x^2 -10x +12$\\

$= 4x^3 +9x^2 -24x + 32$\\
