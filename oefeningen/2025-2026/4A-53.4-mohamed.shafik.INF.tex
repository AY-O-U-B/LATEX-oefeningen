Bereken $\displaystyle\int \sin x \cos^{-3} x\, dx$\\

Substitutie: $t = \cos x$, $dt = -\sin x\, dx$, $\sin x\, dx = -dt$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int \sin x \cos^{-3} x\, dx &=& \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sin x}{\cos^3 x}\, dx\\
&=& \displaystyle\int \dfrac{-dt}{t^3}\\
&=& -\displaystyle\int t^{-3}\, dt\\
&=& -\dfrac{t^{-2}}{-2} + c\\
&=& \dfrac{1}{2t^2} + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}

Terugsubstitueren: $t = \cos x$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
&=& \dfrac{1}{2\cos^2 x} + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
