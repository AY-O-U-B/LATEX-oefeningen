$\dint\limits_0^{\tfrac{1}{2}}\dfrac{\Bgsin x}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}dx$\\

$t=\Bgsin x \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}dx$\\

$x=0 \Rightarrow t=0$\\

$x=\dfrac{1}{2} \Rightarrow t=\dfrac{\pi}{6}$\\

$\dint\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{6}}tdt = \left[\dfrac{t^2}{2}\right]_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{6}}$\\

$=\dfrac{\pi^2}{72}-0 = \dfrac{\pi^2}{72}$
