RR:
$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
     x_{n-1} + x_n + x_{n+1} = 51 \space(1) \\
     x_{n-1} \cdot x_n \cdot x_{n+1} = 4641 \space(2)
\end{array}
\right .$ \\

$x_{n+1} = x_n + v \text{ en } x_{n-1} = x_n - v$ \\

$(1): x_n \cancel{-v} + x_n + x_n \cancel{+v} = 51$ \\

$\Rightarrow 3x_n = 51$ \\

$\Rightarrow x_n = 17$ \\

$(2): (x_n-v)x_n(x_n+v) = 4641$ \\

$\Rightarrow x_n\left(x_n^2-v^2\right) = 4641$ \\

$\Rightarrow 17\left(17^2 - v^2\right) = 4641$ \\

$\Rightarrow \cancelto{+}{-}v^2 = \cancelto{+}{-}16$ \\

$\Rightarrow v = \pm 4$ \\

$\Rightarrow (x_n)_n = (13, 17, 21) \text{ of } (x_n)_n = (21, 17, 13)$ \\
