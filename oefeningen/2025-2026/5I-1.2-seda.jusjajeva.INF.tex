$L = \integral_{0}^{1}\sqrt{1 + \left(\left(2\sqrt{x}\right)'\right)^2}dx$ \\

$\integral\sqrt{1 + \left(\left(2\sqrt{x}\right)'\right)^2}dx$ \\

$= \integral\sqrt{1 + \left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\right)^2}dx$ \\

$= \integral\sqrt{1 + \dfrac{1}{x}}dx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{\sqrt{x + 1}}{\sqrt{x}}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $x = t^2 \Rightarrow dx = 2tdt \Leftrightarrow t = \sqrt{x}$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{\sqrt{t^2 + 1}}{t}2tdt$ \\

$= 2\integral\sqrt{t^2 + 1}dt$ \\

(Hyperbolic) substitutie: $t = \sinh u \Rightarrow dt = \cosh udu$ \\

$= 2\integral\sqrt{\sinh^2u + 1}\cosh udu$ \\

$= 2\integral\cosh^2udu$ \\

$= 2\integral\dfrac{\cosh2u + 1}{2}du$ \\

$= \integral\left(\cosh2u + 1\right)du$ \\

$= \dfrac{\sinh2u}{2} + u + c$ \\

$= \sinh u\cosh u + u + c$ \\

$\overset{\cosh u = \sqrt{t^2 + 1}, u = \operatorname{Bgsinh} t}{=} t\sqrt{t^2 + 1} + \ln |t + \sqrt{t^2 + 1}| + c$ \\

$= \sqrt{x}\sqrt{x + 1} + \ln |\sqrt{x} + \sqrt{x + 1}| + c$ \\

$\Rightarrow L = \left[\sqrt{x}\sqrt{x + 1} + \ln |\sqrt{x} + \sqrt{x + 1}|\right]_0^1$ \\

$= \sqrt{2} + \ln |1 + \sqrt{2}| - \ln 1$ \\

$= \sqrt{2} + \ln \left(1 + \sqrt{2}\right)$ \\
