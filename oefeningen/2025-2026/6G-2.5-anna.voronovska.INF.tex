$f(x) = x^3 - x^2$\\

$f(-x)=(-x)^3-(-x)^2=-x^3-x^2$\\

$f(-x)\neq f(x)$\\

Dus $f$ is niet even.\\

$-f(x)=-(x^3-x^2)=-x^3+x^2$\\

$f(-x)\neq -f(x)$\\

Dus $f$ is ook niet oneven.\\

We ontbinden:\\

$f_E(x) = \dfrac{f(x)+f(-x)}{2} = -x^2,$\\

$f_O(x) = \dfrac{f(x)-f(-x)}{2} = x^3$\\

$a_0$ berekenen:\\

$a_0 = \dfrac{2}{\pi}\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi} (-x^2)dx
= -\dfrac{2}{\pi}\left[\dfrac{x^3}{3}\right]_0^{\pi}
= -\dfrac{2\pi^2}{3}$\\

$a_n$ berekenen:\\

$a_n = \dfrac{2}{\pi}\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi} (-x^2)\cos(nx)dx$\\

Parti\"ele integratie:\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    u = x^2 \\ 
    dv = \cos(nx)  dx 
\end{array} \right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    du = 2x  dx \\ 
    v = \dfrac{\sin(nx)}{n} 
\end{array} \right.$:\\

$= -\dfrac{2}{\pi}\left(
\left[\dfrac{x^2 \sin(nx)}{n}\right]_0^{\pi}
- \dfrac{2}{n}\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi} x\sin(nx)dx
\right)
= \dfrac{4}{n\pi}\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi} x\sin(nx)dx$\\

Parti\"ele integratie:\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    u = x \\ 
    dv = \sin(nx)  dx 
\end{array} \right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    du = 1  dx \\ 
    v = -\dfrac{\cos(nx)}{n} 
\end{array} \right.$:\\

$\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi} x\sin(nx)dx
= \left[-\dfrac{x\cos(nx)}{n}\right]_0^{\pi}
+ \dfrac{1}{n}\left[\dfrac{\sin(nx)}{n}\right]_0^{\pi}
= \dfrac{(-1)^{n+1}\pi}{n}$\\

Dus:\\

$a_n = \dfrac{4}{n\pi} \cdot \dfrac{(-1)^{n+1}\pi}{n} = \dfrac{4(-1)^{n+1}}{n^2}$\\

$b_n$ berekenen:\\

$b_n = \dfrac{2}{\pi}\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi} x^3\sin(nx)dx$\\

Parti\"ele integratie:\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    u = x^3 \\ 
    dv = \sin(nx)  dx 
\end{array} \right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    du = 3x^2  dx \\ 
    v = -\dfrac{\cos(nx)}{n} 
\end{array} \right.$:\\

$= \dfrac{2}{\pi}\left(
\left[-\dfrac{x^3\cos(nx)}{n}\right]_0^{\pi}
+ \dfrac{3}{n}\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi} x^2\cos(nx)dx
\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{2}{\pi}\left(
\dfrac{(-1)^{n+1}\pi^3}{n}
+ \dfrac{3}{n} \cdot \dfrac{2(-1)^n\pi}{n^2}
\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{2(-1)^{n+1}\pi^2}{n}
+ \dfrac{12(-1)^n}{n^3}
= \dfrac{-2(-1)^n\pi^2}{n}
+ \dfrac{12(-1)^n}{n^3}
= \dfrac{2(-1)^n(6 - n^2\pi^2)}{n^3}$\\

Fourierreeks:\\

$f(x) = \dfrac{a_0}{2} + \displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\bigl(a_n\cos(nx)+b_n\sin(nx)\bigr)$\\

$f(x) = -\dfrac{\pi^2}{3}
+ \displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left(
\dfrac{4(-1)^{n+1}}{n^2}\cos(nx)
+\dfrac{2(-1)^n(6-n^2\pi^2)}{n^3}\sin(nx)
\right)$
