$\integral x^2\sqrt[3]{x}dx$ \\

$= \integral x^2 \cdot x^{\tfrac{1}{3}}dx$ \\

$= \integral x^{2 + \tfrac{1}{3}}dx$ \\

$= \integral x^{\tfrac{7}{3}}dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{10}{3}}}{\dfrac{10}{3}} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{3x^{\tfrac{10}{3}}}{10} + c$ \\
