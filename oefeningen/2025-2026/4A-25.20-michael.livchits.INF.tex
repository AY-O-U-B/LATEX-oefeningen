$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1-5x}{1-2z}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\left(\dfrac{5}{2}+\dfrac{\dfrac{-3}{2}}{1-2x}\right)dx$\\

stel $u = 1-2x$\\

$du = -2dx$\\

$= \dfrac{5}{2}x+\dfrac{3}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{du}{u}$\\

$= \dfrac{5}{2}x+\dfrac{3}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{du}{u}$\\

$= \dfrac{5}{2}x+\dfrac{3}{4}\ln|1-2x|+C$\\
