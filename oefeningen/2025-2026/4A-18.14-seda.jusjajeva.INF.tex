$\integral x\sin x\cos xdx$ \\

$= \integral\left(x\cdot\dfrac{1}{2}\left(\sin 0 + \sin 2x\right)\right)dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\integral x\sin 2xdx$ \\

Partiële integratie: $\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    u = x \\ 
    dv = \sin 2xdx \\
\end{array}\right. \Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    du = dx \\
    v = -\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 2x \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\left(-\dfrac{x\cos 2x}{2} + \dfrac{1}{2}\integral\cos 2xdx\right)$ \\

Substitutie: $t = 2x \Rightarrow dt = 2dx$ \\

$= -\dfrac{x\cos 2x}{4} + \dfrac{1}{8}\integral\cos tdt$ \\

$= -\dfrac{x\cos 2x}{4} + \dfrac{1}{8}\sin t + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{-2x\cos 2x + \sin 2x}{8} + c$ \\
