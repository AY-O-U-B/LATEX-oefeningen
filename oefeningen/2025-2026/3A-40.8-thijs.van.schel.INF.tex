$f(x) = e^{\tfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin x}}{x}}$\\

$f'(x) = e^{\tfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin x}}{x}} \cdot \left(\dfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin x}}{x}\right)'$\\

$\left(\dfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin x}}{x}\right)' = \dfrac{x \cdot (\sqrt[3]{\sin x})' - \sqrt[3]{\sin x} \cdot (x)'}{x^2}$\\

$(\sqrt[3]{\sin x})' = \left((\sin x)^{\tfrac{1}{3}}\right)' = \dfrac{1}{3}(\sin x)^{-\tfrac{2}{3}} \cdot \cos x = \dfrac{\cos x}{3\sqrt[3]{\sin^2 x}}$\\

$\left(\dfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin x}}{x}\right)' = \dfrac{x \cdot \dfrac{\cos x}{3\sqrt[3]{\sin^2 x}} - \sqrt[3]{\sin x}}{x^2}$\\

$\left(\dfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin x}}{x}\right)' = \dfrac{\dfrac{x \cos x - 3 \sqrt[3]{\sin x} \cdot \sqrt[3]{\sin^2 x}}{3\sqrt[3]{\sin^2 x}}}{x^2}$\\

$\left(\dfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin x}}{x}\right)' = \dfrac{x \cos x - 3\sin x}{3x^2\sqrt[3]{\sin^2 x}}$\\

$f'(x) = e^{\tfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin x}}{x}} \cdot \dfrac{x \cos x - 3\sin x}{3x^2\sqrt[3]{\sin^2 x}}$\\
