$\dint \dfrac{x^2 + \cos^2x}{x^2\sin^2x + \sin^2x} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x^2 + \cos^2x}{\sin^2x(x^2 + 1)} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x^2 + 1 - \sin^2x}{\sin^2x(x^2 + 1)} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x^2 + 1}{\sin^2x(x^2 + 1)} dx - \dint \dfrac{\sin^2x}{\sin^2x(x^2 + 1)} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{1}{\sin^2x} dx - \dint \dfrac{1}{x^2 + 1} dx$ \\

$= -\cot x - \Bgtan x + C$ \\
