$f(x, y, z) = 2x^2 + 6y^2 + 4z^2 - 4xy + 4xz$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{l}
	\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x, y, z) = 4x - 4y + 4z \\
	\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(x, y, z) = 12y - 4x     \\
	\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial z}(x, y, z) = 8z + 4x
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
	4x - 4y + 4z = 0 \\
	-4x + 12y = 0    \\
	4x + 8z = 0
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
	4x - 4y + 4z = 0 \\
	y = \dfrac{1}{3}x    \\
	z = -\dfrac{1}{2}x
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
	4x - \dfrac{4}{3}x - 2x = 0 \\
	y = \dfrac{1}{3}x    \\
	z = -\dfrac{1}{2}x
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
	x = 0 \\
	y = \dfrac{1}{3}x    \\
	z = -\dfrac{1}{2}x
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
	x = 0 \\
	y = 0 \\
	z = 0
\end{array}\right.$ \\


$\nabla f(x, y, z) = o$ als $(x, y, z) = (0, 0, 0)$ \\

$H(x, y, z) = \left(\begin{array}{ccc}
    4  & -4 & 4 \\
    -4 & 12 & 0 \\
    4  & 0  & 8
\end{array}\right)$ \\

Eigenwaarden zoeken: \\

$\lambda I - H = \left(\begin{array}{ccc}
    \lambda - 4 & -4           & 4           \\
    -4          & \lambda - 12 & 0           \\
    4           & 0            & \lambda - 8
\end{array}\right)$ \\

$\det(\lambda I - H) = \lambda^{3} - 24\lambda^{2} + 144\lambda - 64$ \\


Stel nu $\lambda \leq 0$. Dan geldt: \\


$\lambda^3 \leq 0, -24\lambda^2 \leq 0,
144\lambda \leq 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow \lambda^{3} - 24\lambda^{2} + 144\lambda - 64 < 0$ voor alle $\lambda \leq 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow$ er zijn geen niet-positieve nulpunten \\

$\Rightarrow$ alle eigenwaarden van $H$ zijn strikt positief \\

$\Rightarrow$ $H$ is positief definiet en $(0,0,0)$ is een strikt lokaal minimum. \\
