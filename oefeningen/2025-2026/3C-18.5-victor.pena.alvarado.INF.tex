	$\lim\limits_{x\to0} \dfrac{e^{5x}-1}{x} = \dfrac{1-1}{0} = \dfrac{0}{0} $\\
	
	$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{x\to0} \dfrac{e^{5x}-1}{x} = \lim\limits_{x\to0} \dfrac{\dx\left(e^{5x}-1\right)}{\dx x} = \lim\limits_{x\to0}\dfrac{5e^{5x}}{1} = 5 $
