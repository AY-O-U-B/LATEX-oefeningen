$ 1^5 - 1 = a(1) + b$\\

$ 0 = a + b$\\

Voor $z = -3$:\\

$ (-3)^5 - 1 = a(-3) + b$\\

$ -243 - 1 = -3a + b$\\

$ -3a + b = -244$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{lll}
a + b &=& 0 \Rightarrow b=-a\\
-3a + b &=& -244 \Rightarrow -3a - a=-244 \Rightarrow -4a = -244\\
\end{array}
\right.$\\
