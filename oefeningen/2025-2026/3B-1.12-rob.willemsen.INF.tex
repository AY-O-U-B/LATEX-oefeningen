$f(x) = \operatorname{Bgtan}(\dfrac{1}{x})$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{1+\left(\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^2} \cdot \dfrac{0 \cdot 1 - 1 \cdot 1}{x^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{1+\dfrac{1}{x^2}} \cdot \left(-\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{x^2}{x^2} \cdot {\dfrac{1}{x^2}}} \cdot \left(-\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{x^2+1}{x^2}} \cdot \left(-\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{x^2+1} \cdot \left(-\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)$ \\

$= -\dfrac{x^2 \cdot 1}{(x^2+1) \cdot x^2}$ \\

$= -\dfrac{1}{x^2+1}$ \\

$f''(x) = -\dfrac{0 \cdot (x^2+1) - (1 \cdot 2x)}{(x^2+1)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2x}{(x^2+1)^2}$ \\
