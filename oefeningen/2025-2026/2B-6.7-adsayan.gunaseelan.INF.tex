6.7) $ 4\bgtan \dfrac{1}{5}-\bgtan \dfrac{1}{239}$ toon aan dat de uitdrukking gelijk is aan $\dfrac{\pi}{4}.$\\

$ 4\bgtan \dfrac{1}{5}-\bgtan \dfrac{1}{239}$\\

$= 2\bgtan \left(\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{5}+\dfrac{1}{5}}{1-\dfrac{1}{5}\cdot \dfrac{1}{5}}\right)- \bgtan \left(\dfrac{1}{239}\right)$\\

$= 2\bgtan \left(\dfrac{10}{24}\right)- \bgtan \left(\dfrac{1}{239}\right)$\\

$= \bgtan \left(\dfrac{\dfrac{10}{24}+\dfrac{10}{24}}{1-\dfrac{10}{24}\cdot \dfrac{10}{24}}\right)- \bgtan \left(\dfrac{1}{239}\right)$\\

$= \bgtan \left(\dfrac{120}{119}\right)- \bgtan \left(\dfrac{1}{239}\right)$\\

$= \bgtan \left(\dfrac{\dfrac{120}{119}-\dfrac{1}{239}}{1+\dfrac{120}{119}\cdot \dfrac{1}{239}}\right)$\\

$= \bgtan \left(\dfrac{\dfrac{28680}{28441}-\dfrac{119}{28441}}{\dfrac{28441}{28441} +\dfrac{120}{28441}}\right)$\\

$= \bgtan \left(1\right) = \dfrac{\pi}{4}$\\
