$\dint \dfrac{\fourtroot{x} \sixthroot{x^7}}{\sqrt{x^3 \twelfthroot{x^{11}}}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{1}{4}} x^{\tfrac{6}{7}}}{\sqrt{x^3 x^{\tfrac{11}{12}}}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{3}{12}} x^{\tfrac{14}{12}}}{\left( x^{\tfrac{36}{12}} x^{\tfrac{11}{12}}\right)^{\tfrac{1}{2}}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{17}{12}}}{\left( x^{\tfrac{47}{12}} \right)^{\tfrac{1}{2}}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{34}{24}}}{x^{\tfrac{47}{24}}} dx$ \\

$= \dint x^{\tfrac{34}{24}} x^{\tfrac{-47}{24}} dx$ \\

$= \dint x^{\tfrac{-13}{24}}dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{11}{24}}}{\dfrac{11}{24}} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{24 \twentyfourthroot{x^{11}}}{11} + c$ \\
