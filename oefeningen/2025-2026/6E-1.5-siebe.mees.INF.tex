$T_7\left(f,2\right)(x)$ voor $f(x)=\dfrac{1}{x-1}$\\

$f(x)=(x-1)^{-1}$\\

$f'(x)=-1(x-1)^{-2}$\\

$f''(x)=2(x-1)^{-3}$\\

$f'''(x)=-6(x-1)^{-4}$\\

$f^{iv}(x)=24(x-1)^{-5}$\\

$f^{v}(x)=-120(x-1)^{-6}$\\

$f^{vi}(x)=720(x-1)^{-7}$\\

$f^{vii}(x)=-5040(x-1)^{-8}$\\

$f(2)=1$\\

$f'(2)=-1$\\

$f''(2)=2$\\

$f'''(2)=-6$\\

$f^{iv}(2)=24$\\

$f^{v}(2)=-120$\\

$f^{vi}(2)=720$\\

$f^{vii}(2)=-5040$\\

$\Rightarrow T_7\left(f,2\right)(x)=\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^{7}\dfrac{f^{(k)}(2)}{k!}(x-2)^k$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{0!}+\dfrac{-1}{1!}(x-2)+\dfrac{2}{2!}(x-2)^2+\dfrac{-6}{3!}(x-2)^3+\dfrac{24}{4!}(x-2)^4$\\

$+\dfrac{-120}{5!}(x-2)^5+\dfrac{720}{6!}(x-2)^6+\dfrac{-5040}{7!}(x-2)^7$\\

$=1-(x-2)+(x-2)^2-(x-2)^3+(x-2)^4-(x-2)^5+(x-2)^6-(x-2)^7$\\
