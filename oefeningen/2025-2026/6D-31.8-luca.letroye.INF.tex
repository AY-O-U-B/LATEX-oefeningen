$ \displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{2^{2n}}{2n!} $\\

$\Rightarrow $ D'Alembert\\

$\dfrac{\dfrac{2^{2n+2}}{(2n+2)!}}{\dfrac{2^{2n}}{2n!}} = \dfrac{2^{2n+2}}{(2n+2)!}\cdot \dfrac{2n!}{2^{2n}}$\\

$= \dfrac{4}{(2n+2)(2n+1)} $\\

$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{n\rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{4}{(2n+2)(2n+1)}  = 0 < 1 \Rightarrow $ convergent\\
