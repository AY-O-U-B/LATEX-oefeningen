$\dfrac{z+1-i}{z-1-i} - \dfrac{2+2i}{z^2-z-iz} - i = 2 $\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{(z-1-i)+2}{z-1-i} - \dfrac{2+2i}{z^2-z-iz} = 2 + i $\\

$\Rightarrow 1+ \dfrac{2}{z-1-i} - \dfrac{2+2i}{z^2-z-iz} = 2 + i $\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{2}{z-1-i} - \dfrac{2+2i}{z^2-z-iz} = 1 + i $\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{2z-2-2i}{z(z-1-i)} = 1 + i $\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{2}{z} = 1 + i  \Rightarrow z = \dfrac{2}{1+i} = 1-i$\\
