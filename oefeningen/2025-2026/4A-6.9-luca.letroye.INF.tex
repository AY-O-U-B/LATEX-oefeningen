$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^2+4}{x^2+1}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^2}{x^2+1}dx + 4\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x^2+1}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^2+1-1}{x^2+1}dx + 4\operatorname{Bgtan} x$\\

$= x -\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x^2+1}dx + 4\operatorname{Bgtan} x$\\

$= x + 3\operatorname{Bgtan} x+C $\\
