$\dint \dfrac{x - \sin x}{2\sin^2\dfrac{x}{2}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x-2\sin\dfrac{x}{2} \cdot \cos\dfrac{x}{2}}{2\sin^2\dfrac{x}{2}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x}{2\sin^2\dfrac{x}{2}} dx - \dint \dfrac{\cos\dfrac{x}{2}}{\sin\dfrac{x}{2}} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \dint x \cdot \dfrac{1}{\sin^2 \dfrac{x}{2}} dx - \dint \dfrac{\cos\dfrac{x}{2}}{\sin\dfrac{x}{2}} dx$ \\

$ \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x \\
dv = \dfrac{1}{\sin^2\dfrac{x}{2}} dx
\end{array} \right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 1dx \\
v = *
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

$* \Rightarrow t = \dfrac{x}{2} \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{1}{2}dx \Rightarrow 2dt = dx$ \\

$ \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{2} \dint \dfrac{1}{\sin^2 t} \cdot 2dt = \dint \dfrac{1}{\sin^2 t} dt = -\cot t = -\cot \dfrac{x}{2} $\\

$= \left[ x \cdot \left(- \cot \dfrac{x}{2}\right) - \dint -\cot \dfrac{x}{2} \cdot 1dx \right] - \left[ \dint \dfrac{\cos \dfrac{x}{2}}{\sin \dfrac{x}{2}} dx\right]$

$= -x \cot \dfrac{x}{2} + \dint \cot \dfrac{x}{2} dx - \dint \cot \dfrac{x}{2} dx$\\

$= -x \cot \dfrac{x}{2} + C$
