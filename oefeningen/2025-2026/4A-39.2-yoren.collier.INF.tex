$ I =  \intgrl \dfrac{2x+7}{\Lh x^2 +1 \Rh^2} dx = \intgrl \dfrac{2x}{\Lh x^2 +1 \Rh^2} dx + 7\intgrl \dfrac{1}{\Lh x^2 +1 \Rh^2} dx$\\

Stel: $t = x^2 +1 \adR dt = 2x dx$\\

$= \intgrl \dfrac{1}{t^2}dt +7 \intgrl \dfrac{1}{\Lh x^2 +1 \Rh^2 dx}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{-1t} +c +7 \Lv \dfrac{2 \cdot 2 -3}{2 \cdot 2 -2} \cdot \intgrl \dfrac{1}{\Lh x^2 +1 \Rh} dx + \dfrac{x}{\Lh 2 \cdot 2 -2 \Rh \Lh x^2 +1 \Rh}\Rv$\\

$= \dfrac{-1}{x^2 +1} +c +7 \Lv \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \operatorname{Bgtan} \Lh x \Rh +c + \dfrac{x}{2 \Lh x^2 +1 \Rh} \Rv $\\

$= \dfrac{-1}{x^2 +1} + \dfrac{7}{2} \operatorname{Bgtan} \Lh x \Rh + \dfrac{7x}{2 \Lh x^2 +1 \Rh} +c$\\

$= \dfrac{-2 +7x}{2 \Lh x^2 +1 \Rh} + \dfrac{7}{2} \operatorname{Bgtan} \Lh x \Rh +c$\\
