$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{dx}{\left(3x+7\right)\sqrt{x+2}}$ \\

$$t = x+2$$ $$dt = dx$$ $$\left( x = t-2 \right)$$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dt}{\left(3x+7\right)\sqrt{t}}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dt}{\left(3\left(t-2\right)+7\right)t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dt}{\left(3t-6+7\right)t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dt}{\left(3t+1\right)t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dt}{\left(3t+1\right)t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}$ \\

$$u = t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}$$ $$u^2 = t$$ $$2udu = dt$$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int 2u\left( \dfrac{du}{u\left(3t+1\right)}\right)$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int 2\left( \dfrac{du}{3u^2+1}\right)$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \dfrac{2du}{3u^2+1}$ \\

$= \displaystyle 2 \int \dfrac{du}{3u^2+1}$ \\

$$v = \sqrt{3}u$$ $$dv = \sqrt{3}du$$ $$\dfrac{dv}{\sqrt{3}} = du$$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{3}} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{dv}{v^2+1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{3}} \operatorname{Bgtan}(v)+c$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{3}} \operatorname{Bgtan}(\sqrt{3}u)+c$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{3}} \operatorname{Bgtan}(\sqrt{3t})+c$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{3}} \operatorname{Bgtan}(\sqrt{3(x+2)})+c$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{3}} \operatorname{Bgtan}(\sqrt{3x+6})+c$ \\
