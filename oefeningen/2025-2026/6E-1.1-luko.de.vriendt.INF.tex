$T_4 (f,0)(x)$ voor $ f(x) = \dfrac{1}{1+x}$ \\

$f(x) = (1+x)^{-1} \hspace{2cm}  f(0) = 1$ \\

$f'(x) = -1(1+x)^{-2} \hspace{2cm}  f'(0) = -1$ \\

$f''(x) = 2(1+x)^{-3} \hspace{2cm}  f''(0) = 2$ \\

$f'''(x) = -6(1+x)^{-4} \hspace{2cm}  f'''(0) = -6$ \\

$f^{iv}(x) = 24(1+x)^{-5} \hspace{2cm}  f^{iv}(0) = 24$ \\

$\Rightarrow T_4\left (\dfrac{1}{1+x}, 0\right)(x) = \displaystyle \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \dfrac{f^{(k)}(0)}{k!}x^k$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{0!}x^0 + \dfrac{-1}{1!}x^1 + \dfrac{2}{2!}x^2 + \dfrac{-6}{3!}x^3 + \dfrac{24}{4!}x^4$ \\

$= 1 - x + x^2 - x^3 + x^4$ \\
