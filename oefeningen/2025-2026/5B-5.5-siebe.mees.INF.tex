$ \displaystyle\int_0^1 x^2 \ln x dx $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \displaystyle\int_a^1 x^2 \ln x dx $\\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u = \ln x \\
dv = x^2 dx
\end{array}
\right . 
\Rightarrow 
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du = \dfrac{1}{x} dx \\
v = \dfrac{x^3}{3}
\end{array}
\right . $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \ln x \dfrac{x^3}{3} - \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^3}{3} \cdot \dfrac{1}{x} dx $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \ln x \dfrac{x^3}{3} - \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int x^2 dx $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \ln x \dfrac{x^3}{3} - \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \dfrac{x^3}{3} + C $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \left[ \ln x \dfrac{x^3}{3} - \dfrac{x^3}{9} \right]_a^1 $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \left( \ln(1)\dfrac{1^3}{3} - \dfrac{1^3}{9} \right) - \left( \ln(a)\dfrac{a^3}{3} - \dfrac{a^3}{9} \right) $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \left( -\dfrac{1}{9} - \left( \ln(a)\dfrac{a^3}{3} - \dfrac{a^3}{9} \right) \right) $\\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
\lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} a^3 = 0 \rightarrow \dfrac{a^3}{9} = 0 \\

\lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} a^3 \ln(a) = 0\cdot (-\infty) \text{ onbepaald} \\ 
\Rightarrow \dfrac{\ln(a)}{\dfrac{1}{a^3}} = \dfrac{-\infty}{+\infty} \overset{\text{L'Hôpital}}{=} \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{a}}{-\dfrac{3}{a^4}} = \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \dfrac{1}{a} \cdot \dfrac{a^4}{-3} = \lim\limits_{a \rightarrow 0^+} \dfrac{-a^3}{3} = 0
\end{array}
\right . $\\

$ = -\dfrac{1}{9} - \left(0\cdot0\right) = -\dfrac{1}{9} $\\
