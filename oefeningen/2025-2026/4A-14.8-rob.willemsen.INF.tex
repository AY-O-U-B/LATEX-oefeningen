$\dint x^2 \ln^2 x dx$ \\

$ \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = \ln^2 x \\
dv = x^2 dx
\end{array} \right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 2 \ln x \cdot \dfrac{1}{x} dx \\
v = \dfrac{1}{3} x^3
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

$= \ln^2 x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dint \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 \cdot 2 \ln x \cdot \dfrac{1}{x} dx$ \\

$= \ln^2 x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{2}{3} \dint \ln x \cdot \dfrac{x^3}{x} dx$ \\

$= \ln^2 x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{2}{3} \dint x^2 \ln x dx$ \\

$ \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = \ln x \\
dv = x^2 dx
\end{array} \right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = \dfrac{1}{x} dx \\
v = \dfrac{1}{3} x^3
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

$= \ln^2 x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{2}{3} \cdot \left[\ln x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dint \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 \cdot \dfrac{1}{x} dx\right]$ \\

$= \ln^2 x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{2}{3} \cdot \left[\ln x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{1}{3} \dint x^2 dx\right]$ \\

$= \ln^2 x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{2}{3} \cdot \left[\ln x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3\right] + C$ \\

$= \ln^2 x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{2}{3} \cdot \left[\ln x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{1}{9} x^3\right] + C$ \\

$= \ln^2 x \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} x^3 - \dfrac{2}{9}x^3 \ln x + \dfrac{2}{27} x^3 + C$ \\
