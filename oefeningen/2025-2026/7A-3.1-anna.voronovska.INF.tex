Via de driehoeksongelijkheid: $|x^2-y^2| \leq |x^2|+|y^2| = x^2+y^2$, dus:\\

$0 \leq \left|\dfrac{x^2-y^2}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\right| \leq \dfrac{x^2+y^2}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} = \sqrt{x^2+y^2}$\\

$\lim\limits_{(x,y)\to(0,0)} \sqrt{x^2+y^2} = 0$:\\

$\lim\limits_{(x,y)\to(0,0)} \dfrac{x^2-y^2}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} = 0$
