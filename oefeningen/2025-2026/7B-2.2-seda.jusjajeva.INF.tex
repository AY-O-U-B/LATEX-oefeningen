$w = pq\sin r$ met $p = 2s + t$, $q = s - t$ en $r = st$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} = \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial p} \dfrac{\partial p}{\partial t} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial q} \dfrac{\partial q}{\partial t} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial r} \dfrac{\partial r}{\partial t}$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial p} = q\sin r$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial q} = p\sin r$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial r} = pq\cos r$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial p}{\partial t} = 1$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial q}{\partial t} = -1$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial r}{\partial t} = s$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} = q\sin r - p\sin r + pqs\cos r $ \\

$= (q - p)\sin r + pqs\cos r $ \\

$= ((s - t) - (2s + t))\sin st + (2s + t)(s - t)s\cos st $ \\

$= (-s - 2t)\sin st + (2s^2 - st - t^2)s\cos st $ \\


$= (-s - 2t)\sin st + (2s^3 - s^2t - st^2)\cos st $ \\



$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s} = \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial p} \dfrac{\partial p}{\partial s} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial q} \dfrac{\partial q}{\partial s} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial r} \dfrac{\partial r}{\partial s}$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial p}{\partial s} = 2$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial q}{\partial s} = 1$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial r}{\partial s} = t$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s} = 2q\sin r + p\sin r + pqt\cos r $ \\

$= 2(s - t)\sin st + (2s + t)\sin st + (2s + t)(s - t)t\cos st $ \\

$= (2s - 2t + 2s + t)\sin st + (2s + t)(s - t)t\cos st $ \\

$= (4s - t)\sin st + (2s + t)(s - t)t\cos st $ \\

$\Rightarrow Dw = \left(\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} \quad \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial s}\right)$

$= \left((-s - 2t)\sin st + (2s^3 - s^2t - st^2)\cos st \quad (4s - t)\sin st + (2s + t)(s - t)t\cos st\right)$
