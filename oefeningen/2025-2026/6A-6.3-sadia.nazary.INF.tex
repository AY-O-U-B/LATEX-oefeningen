Bepaal de eerste drie termen van de rekenkundige rij als \\

$ x_{1} + x_{4} = 16 $ \\

$ x_{3} + x_{5} = 28 $ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    x_{4} = x_{1} + 3v \\
	
    x_{3} = x_{1} + 2v \\
	
	x_{5} = x_{1} + 4v \\
	
	x_{1} + x_{4} = 16 \\
	
	x_{3} + x_{5} = 28 \\
	
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    x_{4} = x_{1} + 3v \\
	
    x_{3} = x_{1} + 2v \\
	
	x_{5} = x_{1} + 4v \\
	
	x_{1} + \left( x_{1} + 3v \right) = 16 \\
	
	\left( x_{1} + 2v \right) + \left( x_{1} + 4v \right) = 28 \\
	
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
    2x_{1} + 3v = 16 \\
	
	2x_{1} + 6v = 28 \\
	
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 3v = 12$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow v = 4$ \\

$2x_{1} + 3 \cdot 4 = 16$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 2x_{1} = 4$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x_{1} = 2$ \\

$ x_{2} = x_{1} + v = 2 + 4 = 6 $ \\

$ x_{3} = x_{1} + 2v = 2 + 8 = 10 $ \\
