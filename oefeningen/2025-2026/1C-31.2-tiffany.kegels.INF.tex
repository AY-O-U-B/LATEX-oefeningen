$r = \sqrt{(-1)^2 + 1} = \sqrt{2}$\\

$\tan \alpha = \dfrac{-1}{1} = -1$\\

$\alpha = -\dfrac{\pi}{4} + \pi = \dfrac{3\pi}{4}$\\

$\left(\sqrt{2} \operatorname{cis} \left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4}\right) \right) ^{10}$\\

$= (\sqrt{2})^{10} \operatorname{cis} \left( 10 \cdot \dfrac{3\pi}{4} \right) $\\

$= 32 \operatorname{cis} \left(\dfrac{15\pi}{2} \right)$\\

$\dfrac{15\pi}{2} - 2\pi \cdot 3 = \dfrac{15\pi}{2} - \dfrac{12\pi}{2} = \dfrac{3\pi}{2}$\\

$= 32 \operatorname{cis} \left(\dfrac{3\pi}{2} \right)$\\

$= 32 \cdot (-i) = -32i$\\
