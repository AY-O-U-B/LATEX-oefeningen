$\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{\log_a (1 + x)}{x} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

Toepassen van de regel van l'Hôpital: \\

$\dfrac{d}{dx} \log_a (1 + x) = \dfrac{1}{(1 + x) \ln a}$ \\

$\dfrac{d}{dx} x = 1$ \\

$\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{(1 + x) \ln a}}{1} = \dfrac{1}{\ln a}$ \\
