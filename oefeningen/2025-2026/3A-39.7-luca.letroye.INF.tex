$f(x) = x^{\ln x}$\\

$= \left(e^{\ln x} \right)^{\ln x}$\\

$= e^{(\ln x)^2} $\\

$f'(x) = e^{(\ln x)^2} \cdot \dfrac{2\ln x}{x}$\\

$= \dfrac{x^{\ln x} \cdot 2\ln x}{x}$\\

$= x^{\ln x- 1} \cdot 2\ln x$\\
