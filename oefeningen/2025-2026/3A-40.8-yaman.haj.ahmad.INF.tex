$f(x) = e^{\left(\tfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}}{x}\right)}$\\\\

$f'(x) = e^{\left(\tfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}}{x}\right)} .\frac{d}{dx}\left(\dfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}}{x}\right)$\\\\

$= e^{\left(\tfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}}{x}\right)} . \dfrac{\left(\left(x.\frac{d}{dx}\left(\sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}\right)\right)-\sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}\right)}{x^2}$\\\\

$= e^{\left(\tfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}}{x}\right)} . \dfrac{\dfrac{x\cos\left(x\right)}{3\sqrt[3]{\sin^2\left(x\right)}} - \sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}}{x^2}$\\\\

$= e^{\left(\tfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}}{x}\right)} . \dfrac{\dfrac{x\cos\left(x\right)-3\sin\left(x\right)}{3\sqrt[3]{\sin^2\left(x\right)}}}{x^2}$\\\\

$= \dfrac{e^{\left(\tfrac{\sqrt[3]{\sin\left(x\right)}}{x}\right)}\left(x\cos\left(x\right)-3\sin\left(x\right)\right)}{3x^2\sqrt[3]{\sin^2\left(x\right)}}$
