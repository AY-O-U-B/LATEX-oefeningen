$3 \cdot \left(\dfrac{27}{8}\right)^{\tfrac{2x}{3}-1} = 2 \cdot \left(\dfrac{32}{243}\right)^{2x}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 3 \cdot \left(\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^3\right)^{\tfrac{2x}{3}-1} = 2 \cdot \left(\left(\dfrac{2}{3}\right)^5\right)^{2x}$\\

$\Rightarrow 3 \cdot \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^{3 \cdot \left(\tfrac{2x}{3}-1\right)} = 2 \cdot \left(\dfrac{2}{3}\right)^{5 \cdot 2x}$\\

$\Rightarrow 3 \cdot \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^{2x-3} = 2 \cdot \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^{-10x}$\\

$\Rightarrow \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^1 \cdot \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^{2x-3} = \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^{-10x}$\\

$\Rightarrow \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^{2x-2} = \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^{-10x}$\\

$\Rightarrow 2x - 2 = -10x$\\

$\Rightarrow 2x + 10x = 2$\\

$\Rightarrow 12x = 2$\\

$\Rightarrow x = \dfrac{2}{12} = \dfrac{1}{6}$\\
