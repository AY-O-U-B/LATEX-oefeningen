$f(x) = \dfrac{x - 4}{x + 2}$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{(x + 2) \cdot 1 - (x - 4) \cdot 1}{(x + 2)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{x + 2 - x + 4}{(x + 2)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{6}{(x + 2)^2}$
