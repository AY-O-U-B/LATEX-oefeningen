$\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{n\sin\left(\dfrac{1}{n}\right)}{\left(\dfrac{1}{n}+5\right)}$\\

criterium 1: $\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow +\infty} x_n \neq 0$ \\

$ = \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow +\infty}  \dfrac{n\sin\left(\dfrac{1}{n}\right)}{\left(\dfrac{1}{n}+5\right)}$

$=\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow +\infty}  \dfrac{n\sin\left(\dfrac{1}{n}\right)\left(\dfrac{1}{n}\right)}{\left(\dfrac{1}{n}+5\right)\left(\dfrac{1}{n}\right)}$

$= \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow +\infty}  \dfrac{1}{\left(\dfrac{1}{n}+5\right)} = \dfrac{1}{5} \Rightarrow $ divergent
