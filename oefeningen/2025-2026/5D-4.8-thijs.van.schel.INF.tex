$y = x^3-3x+2, y = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow x^3-3x+2 = 0 \Rightarrow (x-1)(x^2+x-2)=0\Rightarrow (x-1)^2(x+2)=0$\\

$S = \dint\limits_{-2}^1(x^3-3x+2)dx$\\

$= \left[\dfrac{x^4}{4}-\dfrac{3x^2}{2}+2x\right]_{-2}^1$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{4}-\dfrac{6}{4}+2-4+6+4 = \dfrac{27}{4}$\\
