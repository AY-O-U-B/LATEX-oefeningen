$\dint 4x \sqrt[3]{3x}dx$\\

$=\dint 4x \cdot 3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} \cdot x^{\tfrac{1}{3}} dx = 4 \cdot 3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} \dint x^{1 + \tfrac{1}{3}} dx = 4 \cdot 3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} \dint x^{\tfrac{4}{3}} dx$ \\

$= 4 \cdot 3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} \cdot \dfrac{3}{7} x^{\tfrac{7}{3}} + C = \dfrac{12}{7} 3^{\tfrac{1}{3}} x^{\tfrac{7}{3}} + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{12}{7} \sqrt[3]{3} \cdot x^2\sqrt[3]{x} + C = \dfrac{12}{7}x^2 \sqrt[3]{3x} + C$ \\
