$\displaystyle\int (e^x+1)^2dx $\\

$t = e^x +1 \Rightarrow e^x = t-1$\\

$\dfrac{dt}{e^x}$\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \dfrac{t^2}{t-1}dt $\\

$= \displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{t^2-1+1}{t-1}\right)dt $\\

$= \displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{(t-1)(t+1)+1}{t-1}\right)dt $\\

$= \displaystyle\int  \left((t+1)+ \dfrac{1}{t-1}\right)dt $\\

$\dfrac{1}{2}t^2+t+\ln|t-1|+C$\\

$\dfrac{1}{2}(e^x+1)^2+(e^x+1)+\ln|e^x|+C$\\
