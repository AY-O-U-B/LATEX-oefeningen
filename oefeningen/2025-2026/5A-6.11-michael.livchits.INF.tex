$I = \displaystyle\dint_{2}^{9} \dfrac{3x}{\sqrt[3]{x-1}}dx$\\

Eerst vinden we de onbepaalde integraal\\

Stel $t = \sqrt[3]{x-1}$\\

$t^3 + 1 = x$\\

$dx = 3t^2dt$\\

$3\displaystyle\int \dfrac{t^3+1}{t}\cdot3t^2dt$\\

$= 9\displaystyle\int (t^4+t)dt$\\

$= \dfrac{9}{5}t^5 + \dfrac{9}{2}t^2 + C$

Pas grenzen aan en vul in\\

$I = 9\left[\dfrac{t^5}{5} + \dfrac{t^2}{2}\right]_{1}^{2}$\\

$I = \dfrac{693}{10}$\\
