$I = \dint x^2 \ln^2 x dx$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    u =& \ln^2 x \\ 
    dv =& x^2 dx \\ 
\end{array}\right. \Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    du =& \dfrac{2 \ln x}{x} dx \\
    v =& \dfrac{x^3}{3} 
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$I = \ln^2 x \cdot \dfrac{x^3}{3} - \dint \dfrac{x^3}{3} \cdot \dfrac{2 \ln x}{x} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3}x^3 \ln^2 x - \dfrac{2}{3} \dint x^2 \ln x dx$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    u =& \ln x \\ 
    dv =& x^2 dx \\ 
\end{array}\right. \Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    du =& \dfrac{1}{x} dx \\
    v =& \dfrac{x^3}{3} \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$I = \dfrac{1}{3}x^3 \ln^2 x - \dfrac{2}{3} \left(\dfrac{1}{3}x^3 \ln x - \dint \dfrac{x^3}{3} \cdot \dfrac{1}{x} dx \right) $ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3}x^3 \ln^2 x - \dfrac{2}{3} \left(\dfrac{1}{3}x^3 \ln x - \dfrac{1}{3} \dint x^2 dx \right) $ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3}x^3 \ln^2 x - \dfrac{2}{3} \left(\dfrac{1}{3}x^3 \ln x - \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \dfrac{x^3}{3} \right)$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3}x^3 \ln^2 x - \dfrac{2}{9}x^3 \ln x + \dfrac{2}{27}x^3 + c $ \\
