$\sin (2x) = 2\cot(x)$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \bcancel{2}\sin(x)\cos(x) = \bcancel{2} \dfrac{\cos(x)}{\sin(x)} \rightarrow \sin(x) \not = 0 \Rightarrow x \not = k\pi$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \sin^2(x)\cos(x) = \cos(x)$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow -\cos(x)\left(-\sin^2(x)+1\right) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow -\cos^3(x) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \cos(x) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x = \dfrac{\pi}{2} + k\pi \text{ met } k \in \mathbb{Z}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x = \dfrac{\pi}{2} \text{ of } \bcancel{\dfrac{3\pi}{2}} \text{, want } \left[\dfrac{\pi}{3}, \dfrac{2\pi}{3}\right]$ \\

$S = \displaystyle \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{3}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \left ( 2\cot(x) - \sin(2x)\right)dx + \displaystyle \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{2\pi}{3}} \left ( \sin(2x) - 2\cot(x)\right)dx$ \\

$= 2\displaystyle \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{3}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \cot(x)dx - \displaystyle \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{3}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \sin(2x)dx + \displaystyle \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{2\pi}{3}} \sin(2x)dx - 2\displaystyle \int_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{2\pi}{3}} \cot(x)dx$ \\

$= 2\left[\ln|\sin(x)|\right]_{\tfrac{\pi}{3}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} + \dfrac{1}{2}\left[\cos(2x)\right]_{\tfrac{\pi}{3}}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} - \dfrac{1}{2}\left[\cos(2x)\right]_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{2\pi}{3}} - 2\left[\ln|\sin(x)|\right]_{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}^{\tfrac{2\pi}{3}}$ \\

$= 2\left(\cancelto{0}{\ln(1)} - \ln\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)\right) + \dfrac{1}{2}\left(-1 + \dfrac{1}{2}\right) - \dfrac{1}{2}\left(\dfrac{-1}{2} + 1\right) -2\left(\ln\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)- \cancelto{0}{\ln(1)}\right)$ \\

$= -2\ln\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right) - \dfrac{1}{4} - \dfrac{1}{4} - 2\ln\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)$ \\

$= -4\ln\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right) - \dfrac{1}{2}$ \\

$= -2\ln\left(\dfrac{3}{4}\right) - \dfrac{1}{2} \text{, want oppervlakte is positief}$ \\
