Gegeven is het complex getal $z = \sqrt{3} - i$. \\

$r = \sqrt{(\sqrt{3})^2 + (-1)^2} = \sqrt{3 + 1} = \sqrt{4} = 2$ \\

$\tan \alpha = \dfrac{-1}{\sqrt{3}} = -\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3}$ \\

$\alpha = -\dfrac{\pi}{6}$ \\

\textbf{Antwoord:} $z = 2 \operatorname{cis} \left(-\dfrac{\pi}{6}\right)$ \\
