Bereken $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^4 dx}{\sqrt{x^5-6}}$\\

Substitutie: $t = x^5 - 6$, $dt = 5x^4 dx$, $x^4 dx = \dfrac{dt}{5}$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^4 dx}{\sqrt{x^5-6}} &=& \dfrac{1}{5} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{\sqrt{t}}\\
&=& \dfrac{1}{5} \displaystyle\int t^{-1/2} dt\\
&=& \dfrac{1}{5} \cdot 2t^{1/2} + c\\
&=& \dfrac{2\sqrt{x^5-6}}{5} + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
