$f(x)=\sqrt{x^3+2x^2+2-\dfrac{1}{x}-\dfrac{3}{x^2}}$ \\

$f'(x)=\dfrac{3x^2+4x+\dfrac{1}{x^2}+\dfrac{6}{x^3}}{2\sqrt{x^3+2x^2+2-\dfrac{1}{x}-\dfrac{3}{x^2}}}$ \\

tussenstap 1: \\

$\dfrac{3x^2 \cdot x^3}{x^3} + \dfrac{4x \cdot x^3}{x^3} + \dfrac{x}{x^3} + \dfrac{6}{x^3}$ \\

$= \dfrac{3x^5 + 4x^4 + x + 6}{x^3}$ \\

tussenstap 2: \\

$\sqrt{x^3+2x^2+2-\dfrac{1}{x}-\dfrac{3}{x^2}}$ \\

$= \sqrt{\dfrac{x^3 \cdot x^2}{x^2} + \dfrac{2x^2 \cdot x^2}{x^2} + \dfrac{2x^2}{x^2}-\dfrac{x}{x^2} - \dfrac{3}{x^2}}$ \\

$= \sqrt{\dfrac{x^5+2x^4+2x^2-x-3}{x^2}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{x^5+2x^4+2x^2-x-3}}{\sqrt{x^2}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{x^5+2x^4+2x^2-x-3}}{\mid x \mid}$ \\

Pas deze twee tussenstappen toe: \\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{\dfrac{3x^5 + 4x^4 + x + 6}{x^3}}{2\dfrac{\sqrt{x^5+2x^4+2x^2-x-3}}{\mid x \mid}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{3x^5 + 4x^4 + x + 6}{x^3} \cdot \dfrac{x}{2\sqrt{x^5+2x^4+2x^2-x-3}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{3x^5 + 4x^4 + x + 6}{2x \cdot \mid x \mid \cdot \sqrt{x^5+2x^4+2x^2-x-3}}$ \\
