Stel $\theta = \operatorname{Bgtan} x$ met $x \in \mathbb{R}$ en $\theta \in \left] -\dfrac{\pi}{2}, \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right[$, dan is $\tan \theta = x$\\

$\cos(2 \operatorname{Bgtan} x) = \cos(2\theta) = \dfrac{\cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta}{1}= \dfrac{\cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta}{\cos^2 \theta + \sin^2 \theta} = \dfrac{\dfrac{\cos^2 \theta}{\cos^2 \theta} - \dfrac{\sin^2 \theta}{\cos^2 \theta}}{\dfrac{\cos^2 \theta}{\cos^2 \theta} + \dfrac{\sin^2 \theta}{\cos^2 \theta}}  = \dfrac{1 - \tan^2 \theta}{1 + \tan^2 \theta} = \dfrac{1 - x^2}{1 + x^2}$\\
