$f(x,y) = e^x\sin y$ in $a\left(0,\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)$ in de richting $h(1,-1)$\\

$f\left(0,\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right) = \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$\\

$Df(a,h) = \lim\limits_{\lambda\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{f\left(\lambda,\dfrac{\pi}{4}-\lambda\right) - f\left(0,\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)}{\lambda}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{\lambda\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{e^\lambda\sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}-\lambda\right) - \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}{\lambda}$\\

Met l'hôpital:\\

$= \lim\limits_{\lambda\rightarrow 0}\left(e^\lambda\sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}-\lambda\right)-e^\lambda\cos\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}-\lambda\right)\right)$\\

$= \sin\dfrac{\pi}{4} - \cos\dfrac{\pi}{4} = 0$\\
