$\left(\dfrac{-3-5i}{i+4}\right)^5$\\

$\dfrac{-3-5i}{i+4}$  vereenvoudigen: \\

$\dfrac{-3-5i}{i+4} \cdot \dfrac{i-4}{i-4}$ \\

$= -1-i$\\

$\Rightarrow (-1-i)^5$\\

$r = \sqrt{(-1)^2 + (-1)^2} = \sqrt{2}$\\

$\sin\theta = \dfrac{-1}{\sqrt{2}}$\\

$\cos\theta = \dfrac{-1}{\sqrt{2}}$\\

Derde kwadrant:\\

$\theta = \dfrac{5\pi}{4}$\\

$\Rightarrow z = \left(\sqrt{2}\right)^{5}\cdot\left(\cos\left(\dfrac{25\pi}{4}\right)\sin\left(\dfrac{25\pi}{4}\right) \right)$\\

$ = \left(\sqrt{2}\right)^{5}\cdot\left(\cos\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)\sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right) \right)$\\

$ = 4\sqrt{2}\cdot\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}+\dfrac{\sqrt{2}i}{2}\right)$

$= 4+4i$\\
