$ \dint \dfrac{8x^2}{x^4+2x^3-2x-1}$\\

$  = \dint \dfrac{8x^2}{(x-1)(x+1)^3}$\\

$ \dfrac{A}{x-1} + \dfrac{B}{x+1} + \dfrac{C}{(x+1)^2} + \dfrac{D}{(x+1)^3}$ \\

$ A = \dfrac{8x^2}{(x+1)^3} = \dfrac{8}{8} = 1 $ voor $x=1$ \\

$ D = \dfrac{8x^2}{x-1} = \dfrac{8}{-2} = -4$ voor $x = -1$ \\

$ C = \dfrac{8x^2}{(x+1)^3(x-1)} - \dfrac{D}{(x+1)^3} = \dfrac{8x^2}{(x+1)^3(x-1)} + \dfrac{4}{(x+1)^3}$ \\

$  = \dfrac{8x^2 + 4x - 4}{(x+1)^3(x-1)} = \dfrac{8x-4}{(x+1)^2(x-1)}$ \\

$ C = \dfrac{8x-4}{(x-1)} = \dfrac{12}{2} = 6 $ voor $x= -1$ \\

$ B = \dfrac{8x-4}{(x+1)^2(x-1)} - \dfrac{C}{(x+1)^2}$ \\

$ = \dfrac{8x-4}{(x+1)^2 (x-1)} - \dfrac{6}{(x+1)^2}$ \\

$ = \dfrac{8x-4-6x+6}{(x+1)^2(x-1)} = \dfrac{2}{x-1}$ \\

$ B = \dfrac{2}{x-1} = -1$ voor $x = -1$ \\

$ \dfrac{1}{x-1} + \left(\dfrac{-1}{x+1}\right) + \dfrac{6}{(x+1)^2} + \left(\dfrac{-4}{(x+1)^3}\right)$ \\

$ \dint\dfrac{1}{x-1}dx -\dint\dfrac{1}{x+1}dx + 6 \dint\dfrac{1}{(x+1)^2}dx - 4\dint\dfrac{1}{(x+1)^3}$ \\

$ = \ln|x-1| - \ln|x+1| + 6 \cdot \left(-\dfrac{1}{x+1}\right) - 4 \cdot \left(-\dfrac{1}{2(x+1)^2}\right)$\\

$ = \ln|x-1| - \ln|x+1| - \dfrac{6}{x+1} + \dfrac{2}{(x+1)^2} + C $ \\
