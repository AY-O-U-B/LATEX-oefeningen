$ f_n : \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{R} : x \rightarrow 
\left\{\begin{array}{ll}
\dfrac{\sqrt{n^2-x^2}}{n} & $als$ |x| \leq n\\
0 & $elders$
\end{array}\right.$\\

$ \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\dfrac{\sqrt{n^2-x^2}}{n} = \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\sqrt{1-\dfrac{x^2}{n^2}} = 1 $\\

$ f : \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{R} : x \rightarrow 1 $en$ f_n \stackrel{p}{\rightarrow} f $\\

$ \left|\left|f_n - f\right|\right|_{\infty} = \sup\limits_{x \in \mathbb{R}}\left|f_n(x)-1\right| = \sup\limits_{|x|>n}|0-1| = 1 \neq 0 $\\

$ \Rightarrow f_n \stackrel{u}{\not\rightarrow} f $\\
