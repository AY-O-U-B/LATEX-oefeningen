$\lim\limits_{x\to3} \dfrac{\sqrt{3x-1}-\sqrt{5x-7}}{\sqrt{13-2x}-\sqrt{3x-2}}$\\


$= \lim\limits_{x\to3} \dfrac{\left(\left(3x-1\right) - \left(5x-7\right)\right)\left(\sqrt{13-2x} + \sqrt{3x-2}\right)}{\left(\sqrt{3x-1}+\sqrt{5x-7}\right)\left(\left(13-2x\right) - \left(3x-2\right)\right)}$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x\to3} \dfrac{\left(-2x+6\right)\left(\sqrt{13-2x}+\sqrt{3x-2}\right)}{\left(\sqrt{3x-1}+\sqrt{5x-7}\right)\left(15-5x\right)}$\\


$= \lim\limits_{x\to3} \dfrac{-2\left(x-3\right)\left(\sqrt{13-2x}+\sqrt{3x-2}\right)}{-5\left(x-3\right)\left(\sqrt{3x-1}+\sqrt{5x-7}\right)}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{x\to3} \dfrac{2\left(\sqrt{13-2x}+\sqrt{3x-2}\right)}{5\left(\sqrt{3x-1}+\sqrt{5x-7}\right)}$\\


$= \dfrac{2\left(\sqrt{7} + \sqrt{7}\right)}{5\left(\sqrt{8}+\sqrt{8}\right)}$\\

$= \dfrac{4\sqrt{7}}{10\sqrt{8}}$\\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{14}}{10}$\\
