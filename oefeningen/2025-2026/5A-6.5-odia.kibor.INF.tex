$\displaystyle\int_{0}^{9} e^{3x} dx $\\

$u = 3x$\\

$du = 3dx$\\

$\dfrac{du}{3} = dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \displaystyle\int_{0}^{27} e^u du$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{3}\left[e^u \right]_{0}^{27} $\\

$= \dfrac{e^{27}}{3} - \dfrac{1}{3}$\\

$ = \dfrac{e^{27} - 1}{3}$\\
