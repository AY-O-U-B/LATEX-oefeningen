$\dint x^3 e^x dx$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x^3 \\
dv = e^x dx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 3x^2 dx \\
v = e^x
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dint x^3 e^x dx = x^3 e^x - 3 \dint x^2 e^x dx$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x^2 \\
dv = e^x dx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 2x \, dx \\
v = e^x
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dint x^2 e^x dx = x^2 e^x - 2 \dint x e^x dx$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x \\
dv = e^x dx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = dx \\
v = e^x
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dint x e^x dx = x e^x - e^x + c$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dint x^3 e^x dx = x^3 e^x - 3\bigl(x^2 e^x - 2(x e^x - e^x)\bigr) + c$ \\

$= e^x(x^3 - 3x^2 + 6x - 6) + c$
