$\limit_{x\to0} \left(\dfrac{\ln(1 - 3x)}{\tan5x}\right) = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\stackrel{(H)} = \limit_{x\to0}\left(\dfrac{-\dfrac{3}{1 - 3x}}{5\sec^2(5x)}\right)$ \\

$= \limit_{x\to0}\left(-\dfrac{3}{(1 - 3x)\cdot5\sec^2(5x)}\right)$ \\

$= -\dfrac{3}{(1 - 3 \cdot 0) \cdot 5 \sec^2 (5 \cdot 0)}$ \\

$= -\dfrac{3}{5}$ \\
