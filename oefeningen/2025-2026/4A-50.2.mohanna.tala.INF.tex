$ \dint \cos x \cos 2x dx $\\

$ = \dint \dfrac{1}{2} (\cos(x-2x)+\cos(x+2x)) dx $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \dint (\cos(-x)+\cos 3x) dx $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \dint (\cos x+\cos 3x) dx $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \left( \dint \cos x dx+\dint \cos 3x dx \right) $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \left( \sin x+\dfrac{1}{3}\sin 3x \right)+c $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2}\sin x+\dfrac{1}{6}\sin 3x+c $
