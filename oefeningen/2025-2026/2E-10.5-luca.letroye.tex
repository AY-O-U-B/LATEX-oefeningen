$8^x+4^x = 5\cdot 2^{x-4}$\\

$\Rightarrow 2^{3x} + 2^{2x} = \dfrac{5}{16}\cdot 2^x$\\

$\Rightarrow \left(2^x \right)^3 + \left(2^x \right)^2 =   \dfrac{5}{16}\cdot 2^x$\\

stel $t = 2^x$\\

$\Rightarrow t^3 +t^2 - \dfrac{5}{16}t$\\

$\Rightarrow 16t^3 +16t^2 - 5t$\\

$\Rightarrow t(16t^2 +16t - 5)$\\

$D = 16^2-4\cdot 16 \cdot (-5) = 576$\\

$t_1 = \dfrac{-16+24}{32} = \dfrac{1}{4}$\\

$t_2 = \dfrac{-16-24}{32} = \dfrac{-5}{4}$\\

mogelijke oplossingen van t: $0, \dfrac{-5}{4}, \dfrac{1}{4}$\\

doordat $t = 2^x$ kan alleen $\dfrac{1}{4}$ een oplossing zijn\\

$\Rightarrow2^x = \dfrac{1}{4}$\\

$\Rightarrow x = -2$\\
