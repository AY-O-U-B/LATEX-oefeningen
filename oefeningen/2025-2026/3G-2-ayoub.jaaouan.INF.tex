$f(x) = (x-2)^3 + 1$ \\

Nulpunten: \\

$f'(x) = 3(x-2)^2 $\\

$f'(x) = 0 \Rightarrow f(2) =  3(2-2)^2 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 2 $ \\

\bigskip

$f''(x) = 6(x-2) $\\

Buigpunten: \\

$f''(x) = 0 \Rightarrow f''(2) =  3(2-2)^2 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 2 $ \\
