$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x}{\sin^2 x}dx$\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    u = x \\ 
    dv = \dfrac{1}{\sin^2 x} \, dx 
\end{array} \right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    du = dx \\ 
    v = -\cot x 
\end{array} \right.$\\

$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x}{\sin^2 x}dx = -x \cot x - \displaystyle\int (-\cot x)dx = -x \cot x + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\cos x}{\sin x}dx $\\

$= -x \cot x + \ln|\sin x| + c$\\

Want $ \displaystyle\int \cot xdx = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\cos x}{\sin x}dx $\\

$u = \sin x \Rightarrow du = \cos xdx$\\

$ \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{u}du = \ln|u| + c $\\

$ \displaystyle\int \cot xdx = \ln|\sin x| + c $\\
