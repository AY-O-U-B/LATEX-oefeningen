$A(z)=(z-a)(z-b)Q(z)+cz+d$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
A(a)=ac+d=0\\
A(b)=bc+d
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
d=-ac\\
A(b)=bc-ac
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
d=-ac\\
A(b)=c(b-a)
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
c=\dfrac{A(b)}{b-a}\\
d=-\dfrac{aA(b)}{b-a}
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow R(z)=\dfrac{A(b)}{b-a}(z-a)$\\
