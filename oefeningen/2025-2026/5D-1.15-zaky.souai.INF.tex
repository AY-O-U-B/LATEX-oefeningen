$5^x>0 \Rightarrow f(x)>0$\\

Dus de oppervlakte tussen de grafiek en de $X$-as is:

$A=\displaystyle\int_0^1 5^x dx$\\

$A=\left[\dfrac{5^x}{\ln 5}\right]_0^1$\\

$=\dfrac{5^1}{\ln 5}-\dfrac{5^0}{\ln 5}$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{\ln 5}-\dfrac{1}{\ln 5}$\\

$=\dfrac{4}{\ln 5}$\\
