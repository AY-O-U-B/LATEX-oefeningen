$9^x+3^{x-3}=1+27^{x-1}$\\

$3^{2x}+3^{x-3}-3^{3x-3}-1=0$\\

$\left(3^x\right)^2+3^x\cdot3^{-3}-\left(3^x\right)^3\cdot3^{-3}-1=0$\\

Laat $X=3^x$\\

$X^2+3^{-3}X-3^{-3}X^3-1=0$\\

$-X^3+27X^2+X-27=0$\\

$-X\left(X^2-1\right)+27\left(X^2-1\right)=0$\\

$\left(X^2-1\right)\left(27-X\right)=0$\\

$\Rightarrow X=1,-1,27$\\

$\Rightarrow 3^x=1,-1,27$\\

$\Rightarrow \boxed{x\in\{0, 3\}}$\\
