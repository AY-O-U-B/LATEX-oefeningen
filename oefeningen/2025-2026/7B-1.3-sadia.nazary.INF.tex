Gegeven:\\

$w=\sin xyz$\\

$x=t$\\

$y=t^2$\\

$z=t^3$\\

Gevraagd:\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt}$\\

Volgens de kettingregel geldt:\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt}
=
\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x}\dfrac{dx}{dt}
+
\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y}\dfrac{dy}{dt}
+
\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z}\dfrac{dz}{dt}$\\

We berekenen eerst de partiele afgeleiden:\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial x}
=
yz\cos xyz$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial y}
=
xz\cos xyz$\\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial z}
=
xy\cos xyz$\\

Ook geldt:\\

$\dfrac{dx}{dt}=1$\\

$\dfrac{dy}{dt}=2t$\\

$\dfrac{dz}{dt}=3t^2$\\

Dus:\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt}
=
yz\cos xyz
+
2txz\cos xyz
+
3t^2xy\cos xyz$\\

$=
\left(yz+2txz+3t^2xy\right)\cos xyz$\\

Nu vullen we $x=t$, $y=t^2$ en $z=t^3$ in:\\

$yz=t^2t^3=t^5$\\

$2txz=2t\cdot t\cdot t^3=2t^5$\\

$3t^2xy=3t^2\cdot t\cdot t^2=3t^5$\\

$xyz=t\cdot t^2\cdot t^3=t^6$\\

Bijgevolg:\\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt}
=
\left(t^5+2t^5+3t^5\right)\cos t^6$\\

$=
6t^5\cos t^6$\\

$\boxed{\dfrac{dw}{dt}=6t^5\cos t^6}$\\
