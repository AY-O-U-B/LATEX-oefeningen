$\displaystyle\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\dfrac{x}{x^2+4}dx$\\

$=\displaystyle\int_{-\infty}^{0}\dfrac{x}{x^2+4}dx+ \displaystyle \int_{0}^{+\infty}\dfrac{x}{x^2+4}dx$\\

$u=x^2+4 \Rightarrow du=2xdx \Rightarrow \dfrac{du}{2} = xdx$

$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{x}{x^2+4}dx=\dfrac{1}{2}\ln(x^2+4) + C$\\

$\displaystyle\int_{0}^{+\infty}\dfrac{x}{x^2+4}dx=\lim_{b\to+\infty}\left[\dfrac{1}{2}\ln(x^2+4)\right]_{0}^{b}$\\

$=\lim_{b\to+\infty}\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\ln(b^2+4)-\dfrac{1}{2}\ln4\right) =+\infty$\\

$\displaystyle\int_{-\infty}^{0}\dfrac{x}{x^2+4}dx=\lim_{a\to-\infty}\left[\dfrac{1}{2}\ln(x^2+4)\right]_{a}^{0}$\\

$=\lim_{a\to-\infty}\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\ln4-\dfrac{1}{2}\ln(a^2+4)\right) =-\infty$\\

$\displaystyle\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\dfrac{x}{x^2+4}dx$ bestaat niet\\
