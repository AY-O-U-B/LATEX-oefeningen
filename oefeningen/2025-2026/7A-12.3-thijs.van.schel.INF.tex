$f(x,y) = x^3 - x^2y + xy^2 - y^3$ \\

$a(1,-1)$ en $h(2,3)$. \\

$
\left\{ 
\begin{array}{l} 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x,y) = 3x^2 - 2xy + y^2 \\[3ex] 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(x,y) = -x^2 + 2xy - 3y^2 
\end{array} 
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{ 
\begin{array}{l} 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(1,-1) = 3(1)^2 - 2(1)(-1) + (-1)^2 = 6 \\[3ex] 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(1,-1) = -(1)^2 + 2(1)(-1) - 3(-1)^2 = -6 
\end{array} 
\right.
$ \\

$\Rightarrow Df(a,h) = \left(\begin{array}{c c} 6 & -6 \end{array}\right) \left(\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \end{array}\right) = -6$
