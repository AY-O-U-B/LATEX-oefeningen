$f(x) = \dfrac{ax^2 + bx + c}{2x}$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{(2ax + b) \cdot 2x - (ax^2 + bx + c) \cdot 2}{(2x)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{4ax^2 + 2bx - 2ax^2 - 2bx - 2c}{4x^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2ax^2 - 2c}{4x^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{ax^2 - c}{2x^2}$ \\
