Trek de vierdemachtswortels in $\mathbb{C}$:\\

$z^4=16\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{4\pi}{9}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_k=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\dfrac{4\pi}{9}+2k\pi}{4}\right),k=0,1,2,3$\\

$\Rightarrow z_k=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{9}+\dfrac{k\pi}{2}\right),k=0,1,2,3$\\

$\Rightarrow z_0=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{9}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_1=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{11\pi}{18}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_2=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{10\pi}{9}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_3=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{29\pi}{18}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow \boxed{z_0=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{9}\right),z_1=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{11\pi}{18}\right),z_2=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{10\pi}{9}\right),z_3=2\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{29\pi}{18}\right)}$\\
