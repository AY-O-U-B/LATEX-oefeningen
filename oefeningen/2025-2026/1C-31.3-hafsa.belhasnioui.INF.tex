$\left(-\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} -i\right)^8$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
r = \sqrt{\left(-\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\right)^2 + (-1)^2} \\
\tan \theta = \dfrac{-1}{-\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}}}\\ 
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
r = \dfrac{2\sqrt{3}}{3}\\
\theta = \dfrac{\pi}{3} + \pi \\
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
r = \dfrac{2\sqrt{3}}{3} \\
\theta = \dfrac{4}{3}\pi \\
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow \left(\dfrac{2\sqrt{3}}{3} \cis \dfrac{4}{3}\pi \right)^8$\\

$= \left(\dfrac{2\sqrt{3}}{3}\right)^8 \cis \dfrac{4\pi}{3} \cdot 8$\\

$= \dfrac{256}{81} \left(\cos \dfrac{32\pi}{3} + i \cdot \sin \dfrac{32\pi}{3}\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{256}{81} \left(-\dfrac{1}{2} + \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}i\right)$\\

$= -\dfrac{128}{81} + \dfrac{128 \sqrt{3}}{81}i $\\
