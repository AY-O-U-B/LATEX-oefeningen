$\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(\dfrac{ax+b}{ax+c}\right)^x$\\

$= \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(\dfrac{(ax+c)+(b-c)}{ax+c}\right)^x$\\

$= \lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} \left(1+\dfrac{b-c}{ax+c}\right)^x$\\

Stel $\dfrac{1}{u} = \dfrac{b - c}{ax + c} \Rightarrow u = \dfrac{ax + c}{b - c} \Rightarrow x = \dfrac{u(b-c) - c}{a}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{u \rightarrow \infty} \left(1+\dfrac{1}{u}\right)^{\tfrac{u(b-c)-c}{a}}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{u \rightarrow \infty} \left(1+\dfrac{1}{u}\right)^{\tfrac{u(b-c)}{a}-\tfrac{c}{a}}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{u \rightarrow \infty} \left(1+\dfrac{1}{u}\right)^{\tfrac{u(b-c)}{a}}\cdot \left(1+\dfrac{1}{u}\right)^{-\tfrac{c}{a}}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{u \rightarrow \infty} \left
(\left(1+\dfrac{1}{u}\right)^u\right)^{\tfrac{b-c}{a}}\cdot \lim\limits_{u \rightarrow \infty}\left(1+\dfrac{1}{u}\right)^{-\tfrac{c}{a}}$\\

$= e^{\tfrac{b-c}{a}}\cdot 1$\\

$= e^{\tfrac{b-c}{a}}$
