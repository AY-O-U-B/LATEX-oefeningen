$f(x) = \ln(\tan x)$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{\tan x}\cdot\dfrac{d}{dx}(\tan x)$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\tan x}\cdot\dfrac{1}{\cos^2 x}$ \\

$= \cot x\cdot\dfrac{1}{\cos^2 x}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\cos x}{\sin x}\cdot\dfrac{1}{\cos^2 x}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sin x\cos x}$ \\
