$r^2(\cos^2\theta - 9\sin^2\theta) = 9$ \\

$\left \{
	\begin{array}{l}
		x = r\cos\theta\\
		y = r\sin\theta
	\end{array}
\right .$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow r^2\left(\dfrac{x^2}{r^2} - 9\dfrac{y^2}{r^2}\right) = 9$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x^2 - 9y^2 = 9$ \\
