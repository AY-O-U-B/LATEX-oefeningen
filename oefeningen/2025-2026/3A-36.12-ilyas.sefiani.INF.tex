$\dfrac{df}{dx}(x\ln(x)-x)$\\

$= (x)'\ln(x)+x(\ln(x))'-1$\\

$= \ln(x)+\dfrac{x}{x}-1$\\

$= \ln(x)+1-1$\\

$= \ln(x)$\\
