$f(x) = \dfrac{-2}{x^2 \cos(x)}$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{0 - (-2) \cdot \left(2x \cos(x) + x^2 (-\sin(x))\right)}{(x^2 \cos(x))^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{4x \cos(x) - 2x^2 \sin(x)}{x^4 \cos^2(x)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{x(4 \cos(x) - 2x \sin(x))}{x^4 \cos^2(x)}$ \\

$= \dfrac{4 \cos(x) - 2x \sin(x)}{x^3 \cos^2(x)}$ \\
