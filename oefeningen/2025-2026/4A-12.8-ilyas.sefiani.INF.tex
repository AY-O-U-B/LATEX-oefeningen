$\displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{5^{x-1}+4^{x+1}}{3^x}\right)dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{\dfrac{5^x}{5}+4\cdot4^x}{3^x}\right)dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{5}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{5^x}{3^x}dx+ 4\displaystyle\int \dfrac{4^x}{3^x}dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{5}\displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{5}{3}\right)^xdx+ 4\displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{4}{3}\right)^xdx$\\

$= \dfrac{\left(\dfrac{5}{3}\right)^x}{5\ln\left(\dfrac{5}{3}\right)} + \dfrac{4\left(\dfrac{4}{3}\right)^x}{\ln\left(\dfrac{4}{3}\right)} + C$\\
