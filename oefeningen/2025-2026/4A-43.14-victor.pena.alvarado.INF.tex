	$f(x)=\dfrac{8x^2}{x^4+2x^3-2x-1}=   \dfrac{A}{(x+1)^3} +  \dfrac{B}{(x+1)^2} + \dfrac{C}{(x+1)} + \dfrac{D}{(x-1)}$ \\
 
	
	$$\Rightarrow \begin{array}{ccc}
	A=&\left.\dfrac{8x^2}{(x-1)}\right|_{x=-1}&=-4 \\
	D=&\left.\dfrac{8x^2}{(x+1)^3}\right|_{x=1}&=1 \\
	\end{array}$$
	
	$f(x) - \dfrac{-4}{(x+1)^3} = \dfrac{8x-4}{(x+1)^2(x-1)}$\\
	
	$$\Rightarrow \begin{array}{ccc}
	B=&\left.\dfrac{8x-4}{(x-1)}\right|_{x=-1}&=6 \\
	\end{array}$$ 
	
	$\dfrac{8x-4}{(x+1)^2(x-1)} - \dfrac{6}{(x+1)^2} = \dfrac{2}{(x+1)(x-1)}$\\
	
	$$\Rightarrow \begin{array}{ccc}
	C=&\left.\dfrac{2}{(x-1)}\right|_{x=-1}&=-1 \\
	\end{array}$$ 
	
	$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \dfrac{8x^2}{x^4+2x^3-2x-1} = -4\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{(x+1)^3} +  6\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{(x+1)^2} - \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{(x+1)} + \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{(x-1)} $ \\ 
	
	$= \dfrac{2}{(x+1)^2} - \dfrac{6}{(x+1)} - \ln|x+1| + \ln|x-1| + k$\\
	
	$= \dfrac{-6x-4}{(x+1)^2} + \ln\left|\dfrac{x-1}{x+1}\right| + k$
