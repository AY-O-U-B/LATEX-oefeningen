$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt[3]{x^4} - \sqrt[3]{x^2}}$ \\

$\left|\begin{array}{ll}
    & x = t^3 \\
    
    & dx = 3t^2 dt \\
    
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\begin{array}{ll}
&= 3\displaystyle\int \dfrac{t^2}{t^4 - t^2} dt = 3\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t^2 - 1} \\

\dfrac{1}{t^2-1} &= \dfrac{A}{t-1} + \dfrac{B}{t+1} \\

\end{array}$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{l}

    A = \dfrac{1}{t+1}\bigg|_{t=1} = \dfrac{1}{2} \\
    
    B = \dfrac{1}{t-1}\bigg|_{t=-1} = \dfrac{-1}{2} \\
    
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$\begin{array}{ll}
&= \dfrac{3}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t-1} - \dfrac{3}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t+1}\\

&= \dfrac{3}{2}\ln|t-1| - \dfrac{3}{2}\ln|t+1| + c \\

&= \dfrac{3}{2}\ln|\sqrt[3]{x}-1| - \dfrac{3}{2}\ln|\sqrt[3]{x}+1| + c \\

\end{array}$
