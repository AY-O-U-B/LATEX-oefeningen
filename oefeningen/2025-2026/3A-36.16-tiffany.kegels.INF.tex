Bereken de afgeleide functie van $f(x) = \ln (x^3 + \sqrt{x^6 + 1})$\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{x^3 + \sqrt{x^6 + 1}} \cdot \left( 3x^2 + \dfrac{1}{2}(x^6 + 1)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} \cdot 6x^5 \right)$\\\\

$= \dfrac{3x^2 \cdot \dfrac{x^3 + \sqrt{x^6 + 1}}{\sqrt{x^6 +1}}}{x^3 + \sqrt{x^6 + 1}}$\\\\

$= \dfrac{3x^2}{\sqrt{x^6 +1}}$\\
