Gegeven poolcoördinaat: $\left(-2,\dfrac{-\pi}{6}\right)$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{l}
x = -2\cos\left(\dfrac{-\pi}{6}\right) \\
y = -2\sin\left(\dfrac{-\pi}{6}\right)
\end{array}\right.
\Leftrightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
x = -2 \cdot \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \\
y = -2 \cdot \dfrac{-1}{2}
\end{array}\right.
\Leftrightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
x = -\sqrt{3} \\
y = 1
\end{array}\right.$ \\

Uitkomst cartesische coördinaten: $(-\sqrt{3}, 1)$
