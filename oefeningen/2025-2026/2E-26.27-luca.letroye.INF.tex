$\log_6x = \log_{\tfrac{1}{5}}x$\\

$\Rightarrow \log_6x = \dfrac{\log_6x}{\log_6\dfrac{1}{5}}$\\

$\Rightarrow \log_6x\left(1-\dfrac{1}{\log_6\dfrac{1}{5}}\right) = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow \log_6x = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow x = 6^0 = 1$\\
