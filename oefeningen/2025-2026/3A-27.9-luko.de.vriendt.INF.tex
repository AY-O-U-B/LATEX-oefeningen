$f(x) = x^2 - 3x$ \\

$f'(x) = 2x - 3$ \\

$f\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)= \left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^2 -3\cdot\dfrac{3}{2} = \dfrac{9}{4} - \dfrac{9}{2} = -\dfrac{9}{4}$ \\

$f'\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\right) = 2\cdot\dfrac{3}{2} - 3 = 0$ \\

$y = f'(a)(x-a) + f(a) = 0\cdot(x-\dfrac{3}{2})-\dfrac{9}{4} = -\dfrac{9}{4}$
