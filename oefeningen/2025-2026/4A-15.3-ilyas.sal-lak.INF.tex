$\dint x^2 e^{3x} dx$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x^2 \\
dv = e^{3x} dx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = 2x \, dx \\
v = \dfrac{e^{3x}}{3}
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dint x^2 e^{3x} dx = \dfrac{x^2 e^{3x}}{3} - \dfrac{2}{3} \dint x e^{3x} dx$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x \\
dv = e^{3x} dx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du = dx \\
v = \dfrac{e^{3x}}{3}
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dint x e^{3x} dx = \dfrac{x e^{3x}}{3} - \dfrac{e^{3x}}{9} + c$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dint x^2 e^{3x} dx = \dfrac{x^2 e^{3x}}{3} - \dfrac{2}{3}\left(\dfrac{x e^{3x}}{3} - \dfrac{e^{3x}}{9}\right) + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{e^{3x}}{27}(9x^2 - 6x + 2) + c$
