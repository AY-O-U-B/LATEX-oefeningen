$V = \pi \displaystyle\int^1_0 \left(\sqrt{x} \sqrt[4]{1-x}\right)^2 dx$\\

$V = \pi \displaystyle\int^1_0 x\left(1-x\right)^{\tfrac{1}{2}} dx$\\

Laat $t = \left(1-x\right)^{\tfrac{1}{2}}$\\

$x = 1 - t^2 \Rightarrow dx=-2tdt$ \\

$V = -2\pi \displaystyle\int^0_1 \left(1 - t^2\right)t^2dt$\\

$ = 2\pi \displaystyle\int^1_0 t^2 - t^4 dt$\\

$ = 2\pi \left[ \dfrac{1}{3}t^3 - \dfrac{1}{5}t^5\right]^1_0$\\

$ = \dfrac{4}{15}\pi$\\
