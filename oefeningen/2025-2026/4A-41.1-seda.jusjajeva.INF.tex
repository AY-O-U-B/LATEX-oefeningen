$\integral\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x^2 - 4}}dx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{4\left(\dfrac{x^2}{4} - 1\right)}}dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\integral\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{\dfrac{x^2}{4} - 1}}dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\integral\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right)^2 - 1}}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $t = \dfrac{x}{2} \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{1}{2}dx$ \\

$= \integral\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{t^2 - 1}}dt$ \\

$= \ln|t + \sqrt{t^2 - 1}| + c$ \\

$= \ln\left|\dfrac{x}{2} + \sqrt{\dfrac{x^2}{4} - 1}\right| + c$ \\
