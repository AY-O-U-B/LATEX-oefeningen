$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\ln x}{\sqrt{x}}dx$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	u=\ln x \\ 
	dv=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x}}dx
\end{array}\right.
\Rightarrow 
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	du= 	\dfrac{1}{x}\\
 	v=2\sqrt{x}
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$= 2\sqrt{x}\ln x - \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2\sqrt{x}}{x}dx$\\

$= 2\sqrt{x}\ln x - 2\displaystyle\int x^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}dx$\\

$= 2\sqrt{x}\ln x - \dfrac{2x^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{\dfrac{1}{2}}+C$\\

$= 2\sqrt{x}\ln x - 4\sqrt{x}+C$\\
