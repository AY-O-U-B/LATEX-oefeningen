$ = \left[\dfrac{2}{3}x^{3}{2}\right]_{1}^{4} + \dfrac{1}{2}\left[\sqrt{x}\right]_{1}^{4} $ \\

$ = \dfrac{2}{3} \cdot 8 - \dfrac{2}{3} + 1 - \dfrac{1}{2} = \dfrac{31}{6} $ \\
