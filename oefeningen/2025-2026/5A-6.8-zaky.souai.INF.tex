$ \displaystyle\int\limits_{-1}^{0} \Sinh(5x)dx $\\

$ u=5x \Rightarrow du=5dx \Rightarrow dx=\dfrac{du}{5}$\\

$ x=-1 \Rightarrow u=-5 $\\

$ x=0 \Rightarrow u=0 $\\

$ \displaystyle\int\limits_{-1}^{0}\Sinh(5x)dx = \displaystyle\int\limits_{-5}^{0}\Sinh(u)\dfrac{du}{5} $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{5}\displaystyle\int\limits_{-5}^{0}\Sinh(u)du $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{5}\left[\Cosh(u)\right]_{-5}^{0} $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{5}\left(\Cosh(0)-\Cosh(-5)\right) $\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{5}\left(1-\Cosh(5)\right) $\\

$ = \dfrac{1-\Cosh(5)}{5} $\\
