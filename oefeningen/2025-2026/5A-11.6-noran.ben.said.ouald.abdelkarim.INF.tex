$ I = \displaystyle\int_0^1 \dfrac{xe^x}{(x+1)^2}\,dx$\\

Partiële integratie:\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    u = xe^x \\ 
    dv = \dfrac{1}{(x+1)^2}dx 
\end{array} \right. 
\Rightarrow 
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
    du = (e^x+xe^x)dx \\ 
    v = -\dfrac{1}{x+1} 
\end{array} \right.$\\

$ I = \left[ -\dfrac{xe^x}{x+1} \right]_0^1 - \displaystyle\int_0^1 \left( -\dfrac{1}{x+1} \right)e^x(1+x)\,dx$\\

$ = \left[ -\dfrac{xe^x}{x+1} \right]_0^1 + \displaystyle\int_0^1 e^x\,dx$\\

$ = \left[ -\dfrac{xe^x}{x+1} + e^x \right]_0^1$\\

$ = \left[ \dfrac{e^x}{x+1} \right]_0^1$\\

$ = \dfrac{e}{2} - 1$\\
