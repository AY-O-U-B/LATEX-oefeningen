$f(x) = \dfrac{x^3 + 4x^2 + x + 1}{x - 3}$ \\

VA: \\

$\limit_{x\rightarrow3+}\dfrac{x^3 + 4x^2 + x + 1}{x - 3} = +\infty$ \\

$\limit_{x\rightarrow3-}\dfrac{x^3 + 4x^2 + x + 1}{x - 3} = -\infty$ \\

VA: $x = 3$\\

$\begin{array}{r|cccc}
    x    &   & 3 &   & \\
    \hline
    f(x) & + & | & - & \\
\end{array}$ \\

HA: \\

$\limit_{x\rightarrow\infty}\dfrac{x^3 + 4x^2 + x + 1}{x - 3} = \limit_{x\rightarrow\infty}\dfrac{x^3}{x} = \limit_{x\rightarrow\infty} x^2 = +\infty \Rightarrow$ geen HA \\

SA: \\

$\limit_{x\rightarrow\infty}\dfrac{\dfrac{x^3 + 4x^2 + x + 1}{x - 3}}{x} = \limit_{x\rightarrow\infty}\dfrac{x^3 + 4x^2 + x + 1}{x(x - 3)}$ \\

$= \limit_{x\rightarrow\infty}\dfrac{x^3 + 4x^2 + x + 1}{x^2 - 3x} = \limit_{x\rightarrow\infty}\dfrac{x^3}{x^2} = \limit_{x\rightarrow\infty} x = \infty\Rightarrow$ geen SA \\
