$ \displaystyle V=\pi\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{4}}\cos^2(y)\,dy $\\

$ =\displaystyle \pi\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{4}}\dfrac{1+\cos(2y)}{2}\,dy $\\

$ =\displaystyle \dfrac{\pi}{2}\int\limits_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{4}}(1+\cos(2y))\,dy $\\

$ =\displaystyle \dfrac{\pi}{2}\left[y+\dfrac{\sin(2y)}{2}\right]_0^{\tfrac{\pi}{4}} $\\

$ =\displaystyle \dfrac{\pi}{2}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}+\dfrac{\sin(\pi/2)}{2}\right) $\\

$ =\displaystyle \dfrac{\pi}{2}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}+\dfrac12\right) $\\

$ =\displaystyle \dfrac{\pi^2}{8}+\dfrac{\pi}{4} $
