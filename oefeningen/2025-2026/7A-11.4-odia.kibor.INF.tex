$f:\Omega \subseteq \mathbb{R}^3 \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^3 : (x,y,z) \rightarrow \left((xy)^z, x^{yz},x^{y^z}\right)$\\

$(a^b = e^{b\ln a})$\\

$\Rightarrow$ Voor $x$:\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_1}{\partial x} =  e^{z\ln(xy)} \cdot z \cdot \dfrac{1}{x} = \dfrac{z(xy)^z}{x}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_2}{\partial x} = e^{yz \ln x} \cdot yz \cdot \dfrac{1}{x} = \dfrac{x^{yz}yz}{x}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_3}{\partial x} = e^{y^z\ln x} \cdot y^z \cdot \dfrac{1}{x} = \dfrac{x^{y^z}y^z}{x}$\\

$\Rightarrow$ Voor $y$:\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_1}{\partial y} = e^{z\ln(xy)} \cdot z \cdot \dfrac{1}{y} = \dfrac{z(xy)^z}{y}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_2}{\partial y} = e^{yz \ln x} \cdot z\ln x = x^{yz}z\ln x$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_3}{\partial y} = e^{y^z\ln x} \cdot zy^{z-1} \ln x = x^{y^z} zy^{z-1} \ln x$\\

$\Rightarrow$ Voor $z$:\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_1}{\partial z} = e^{z\ln(xy)} \cdot \ln (xy) = (xy)^z \ln (xy)$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_2}{\partial z} = e^{yz \ln x} \cdot y\ln x = x^{yz}y\ln x$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_3}{\partial z} = e^{y^z\ln x} \cdot y^z \ln y \ln x = x^{y^z} \cdot y^z \ln y \ln x$\\


$Df(x,y,z)= \left(\begin{array}{ccc}
\dfrac{z(xy)^z}{x} & \dfrac{z(xy)^z}{y} & (xy)^z \ln (xy)\\
\dfrac{x^{yz}yz}{x} & x^{yz}z\ln x & x^{yz}y\ln x\\
\dfrac{x^{y^z}y^z}{x} & x^{y^z} zy^{z-1} \ln x & x^{y^z}y^z \ln y \ln x\\
\end{array} \right)$\\
