$S_1 = \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} (9\sin 2\theta)^2 d\theta$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} 81\sin^2 2\theta d\theta$\\

$= \dfrac{81}{2} \displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \sin^2 2\theta d\theta$\\

$= \dfrac{81}{2} \displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{1 - \cos 4\theta}{2} d\theta$\\

$= \dfrac{81}{4} \displaystyle\int_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} (1 - \cos 4\theta) d\theta$\\

$= \dfrac{81}{4} \left[ \theta - \dfrac{1}{4}\sin 4\theta \right]_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}}$\\

$= \dfrac{81}{4} \left( \dfrac{\pi}{2} - 0 \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{81\pi}{8}$\\

$S = 4 \cdot S_1$\\

$= 4 \cdot \dfrac{81\pi}{8}$\\

$= \dfrac{81\pi}{2}$\\
