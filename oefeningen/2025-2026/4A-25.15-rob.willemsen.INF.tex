$\dint \dfrac{2x^2-x+1}{2x+1} dx$ \\

$t = 2x + 1 \Rightarrow dt = 2 dx \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{2}dt = dx$ \\

$x = \dfrac{t-1}{2}$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{2\left(\dfrac{t-1}{2}\right)^2 - \dfrac{t-1}{2} + 1}{t} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} dt$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{\dfrac{(t-1)^2}{2} - \dfrac{t-1}{2} + 1}{t} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} dt$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{\dfrac{(t-1)^2-(t-1)+2}{2}}{t} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} dt$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{\dfrac{t^2-3t+4}{2}}{t} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} dt$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{(t^2-3t+4) \cdot \dfrac{1}{2}}{t} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} dt$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \dint \dfrac{t^2-3t+4}{t} dt$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \dint t - 3 + \dfrac{4}{t} dt$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \left( \dint t dt - 3 \dint 1 dt + 4 \dint \dfrac{1}{t} dt \right)$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{4} \left( \dfrac{1}{2}t^2 - 3t + 4 \ln |t| \right) + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}t^2 - \dfrac{3}{4}t + \ln |t| + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}(2x+1)^2 - \dfrac{3}{4}(2x+1) + \ln |2x+1| + C$ \\
