$\log_{3}2\log_{\sqrt{2}} x + \log_{3}(3-x) = \log_{\sqrt{3}}2$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_{3}(2\log_{\sqrt{2}} x (3-x)) = \log_{3}4$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_{\sqrt{2}}x(3-x) = 2$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow (3-x) \log_{2} x=1$\\

$\Leftrightarrow (3-2)\log_{2}2 = 1$\\

$ \Leftrightarrow x = 2$\\
