$\dint \dfrac{x^2-2x+4}{x^2-2x+1} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{(x-1)^2+3}{(x-1)^2} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{(x-1)^2}{(x-1)^2}dx + \dint \dfrac{3}{(x-1)^2} dx$ \\

$= \dint 1 dx + 3 \dint \dfrac{1}{(x-1)^2} dx$ \\

$t = x - 1 \Rightarrow dt = dx$ \\

$= \dint 1 dx + 3 \dint \dfrac{1}{t^2} dt$ \\

$= \dint 1 dx + 3 \dint t^{-2} dt$ \\

$= x - 3t^{-1} + C$ \\

$= x - \dfrac{3}{x-1} + C$ \\
