$s_7 = \dfrac{x_1 + x_7}{2} \cdot 7 = -42 $\\

$= \dfrac{x_1 + (x_1 + 6v)}{2} \cdot 7 = -42 $\\

$= \dfrac{2x_1 + 6v}{2} \cdot 7 = -42 $\\

$= (x_1 + 3v)\cdot 7 = -42 $\\

$x_1 + 3v = -6$\\

$s_{10} = \dfrac{x_1 + x_{10}}{2} \cdot 10 = -120 $\\

$= \dfrac{x_1 + (x_1 + 9v)}{2} \cdot 10 = -120 $\\

$= \dfrac{2x_1 + 9v}{2} \cdot 10 = -120 $\\

$= \left(x_1 + \dfrac{9}{2}v\right)\cdot 10 = -120 $\\

$ x_1 + \dfrac{9}{2}v = -12$\\

$ \Rightarrow (x_1 + 3v) - \left(x_1 + \dfrac{9}{2}v\right) = -6 - (-12)$\\

$- \dfrac{3}{2}v = 6$\\

$v = -4$\\

$\Rightarrow x_1 + 3(-4) = -6$\\

$x_1 - 12 = -6$\\
