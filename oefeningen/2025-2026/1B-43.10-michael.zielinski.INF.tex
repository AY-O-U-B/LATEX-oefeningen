$9z^2 + 3(2i - 5)z + (9 - 7i) = 0$ \\
  
$a = 9,\quad b = 6i - 15,\quad c = 9 - 7i$ \\
  
$b^2 = (6i - 15)^2 = 36i^2 - 180i + 225 = -36 - 180i + 225 = 189 - 180i$ \\
  
$4ac = 4 \cdot 9 \cdot (9 - 7i) = 36(9 - 7i) = 324 - 252i$ \\
  
$\Delta = b^2 - 4ac = (189 - 180i) - (324 - 252i) = -135 + 72i$ \\
  
$(x + yi)^2 = -135 + 72i \quad\Rightarrow\quad x^2 - y^2 = -135,\quad 2xy = 72$ \\
  
$xy = 36,\quad y = \dfrac{36}{x}$ \\
  
$x^2 - \left(\dfrac{36}{x}\right)^2 = -135 \quad\Rightarrow\quad x^4 + 135x^2 - 1296 = 0$ \\
  
$u = x^2$: $u^2 + 135u - 1296 = 0$, $u = 9$ of $-144$ \\  
  
$x^2 = 9 \quad\Rightarrow\quad x = 3,\quad y = \dfrac{36}{3} = 12$ \\
  
$\sqrt{\Delta} = \pm (3 + 12i)$ \\
  
$z = \dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{\Delta}}{2a} = \dfrac{15 - 6i \pm (3 + 12i)}{18}$ \\
  
$z_1 = \dfrac{18 + 6i}{18} = 1 + \dfrac{1}{3}i$ \\
  
$z_2 = \dfrac{12 - 18i}{18} = \dfrac{2}{3} - i$ \\
  
$\boxed{z_1 = 1 + \dfrac{1}{3}i \quad,\quad z_2 = \dfrac{2}{3} - i}$ \\
