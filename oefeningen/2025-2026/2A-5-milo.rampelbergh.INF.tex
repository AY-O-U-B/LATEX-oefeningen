$ f: \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}: x \mapsto x^2 $ \\

Stelling : $ f $ is continu in $ x = 0 $ \\

Bewijs: \\

Pak $ \varepsilon > 0 $ \\

Stel $ \delta = \sqrt{\varepsilon} $ \\
\begin{eqnarray*}
\forall \varepsilon > 0, \exists \delta > 0, \forall x \in \mathbb{R}: |x-0| < \delta & \Rightarrow & |f(x) - f(0)| \\
& = & |x^2| < \delta^2 =  \varepsilon
\end{eqnarray*}
