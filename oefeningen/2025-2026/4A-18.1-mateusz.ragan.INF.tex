$\displaystyle\int x \sin x dx$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u = x\\
dv = \sin x dx
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left
\{\begin{array}{l}
du = dx\\
v = -\cos x 
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$= -x\cos x + \displaystyle\int \cos xdx = -x\cos x + \sin x + C$
