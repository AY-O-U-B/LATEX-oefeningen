    ye^{xy}                                           & xe^{xy} \\
    \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{x}{y}}D\left(\dfrac{x}{y}\right) & \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{x}{y}}D\left(\dfrac{x}{y}\right)
\end{array}\right)$ \\

$= \left(\begin{array}{cc}
    ye^{xy}                           & xe^{xy} \\
    \dfrac{y}{x}\cdot\dfrac{1}{y}D(x) & \dfrac{y}{x}xD\left(\dfrac{1}{y}\right)
\end{array}\right)$ \\

$= \left(\begin{array}{cc}
    ye^{xy}      & xe^{xy} \\
    \dfrac{1}{x} & -\dfrac{1}{y}
\end{array}\right)$ \\
