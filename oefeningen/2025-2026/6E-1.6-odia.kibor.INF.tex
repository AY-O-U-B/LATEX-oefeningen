$f(x) = \sin x$ \\

afgeleiden berekenen\\

$f'(x) = \cos x$ \\

$f''(x) = -\sin x$ \\

$f^{(3)}(x) = -\cos x$ \\

$f^{(4)}(x) = \sin x$ \\

afgeleiden in $x = \dfrac{\pi}{2}$\\

$f\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = 1$ \\

$f'\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = 0$ \\

$f''\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = -1$ \\

$f^{(3)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = 0$ \\

$f^{(4)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) = 1$ \\

Taylorreeks van graad 4 rond $x = \dfrac{\pi}{2}$\\

$T_4\left(f,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)(x) = f\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) + f'\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) + \dfrac{f''\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{2!}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^2 + \dfrac{f^{(3)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{3!}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^3 + \dfrac{f^{(4)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{4!}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^4$ \\

$T_4\left(f,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)(x) = 1 + 0\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right) - \dfrac{1}{2}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^2 + 0\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^3 + \dfrac{1}{24}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^4$ \\

$T_4\left(f,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)(x) = 1 - \dfrac{\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^2}{2} + \dfrac{\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^4}{24}$ \\
