$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^4-12x^2+1}{4x^5-4x^4+x^3}dx$\\

$\Rightarrow 4x^5-4x^4+x^3= x^3\left(4x^2-4x+1\right)=x^3\left(2x-1\right)^2$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{x^4-12x^2+1}{x^3\left(2x-1\right)^2} = \dfrac{A}{x}+\dfrac{B}{x^2}+\dfrac{C}{x^3}+\dfrac{D}{2x-1}+\dfrac{E}{\left(2x-1\right)^2}$\\


$C = \left. \dfrac{x^4-12x^2+1}{\left(2x-1\right)^2}\right|_{x=0}=\dfrac{1}{1}=1$\\

$B = \left. \dfrac{\dfrac{x^4-12x^2+1}{\left(2x-1\right)^2}-1}{x}\right|_{x=0} = \left. \dfrac{x^4-12x^2+1-4x^2+4x-1}{x\left(2x-1\right)^2}\right|_{x=0}$\\

$=\left.\dfrac{x^3-16x+4}{\left(2x-1\right)^2}\right|_{x=0}=4$\\


$A = \left. \dfrac{\dfrac{x^3-16x+4}{\left(2x-1\right)^2}-4}{x}\right|_{x=0}= \left. \dfrac{x^3-16x+4-4\left(2x-1\right)^2}{x\left(2x-1\right)^2}\right|_{x=0}$\\

$= \left. \dfrac{x^3-16x+4-16x^2+16x-4}{x\left(2x-1\right)^2}\right|_{x=0}$\\

$= \left. \dfrac{x^3-16x^2}{x\left(2x-1\right)^2}\right|_{x=0}= \left. \dfrac{x^2-16x}{\left(2x-1\right)^2}\right|_{x=0}=0$\\

$E = \left. \dfrac{x^4-12x^2+1}{x^3} \right|_{x=\dfrac{1}{2}}=\dfrac{\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^4-12\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^2+1}{\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^3}=\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{16}-3+1}{\dfrac{1}{8}}$\\

$= -\dfrac{31}{2}$\\

$D =  \left. \dfrac{\dfrac{x^4-12x^2+1}{x^3}+\dfrac{31}{2}}{2x-1} \right|_{x=\tfrac{1}{2}}= \left. \dfrac{\dfrac{2x^4-24x^2+2}{2x^3}+\dfrac{31x^3}{2x^3}}{2x-1} \right|_{x=\tfrac{1}{2}}$\\

$= \left. \dfrac{2x^4+31x^3-24x^2+2}{2x^3(2x-1)} \right|_{x=\tfrac{1}{2}}$\\

$= \left. \dfrac{(2x-1)(x^3+16x^2-4x-2)}{2x^3(2x-1)} \right|_{x=\tfrac{1}{2}}$\\

$= \left. \dfrac{x^3+16x^2-4x-2}{2x^3} \right|_{x=\tfrac{1}{2}}= \dfrac{1}{2}$\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{4}{x^2}+\dfrac{1}{x^3}+\dfrac{1}{4x-2}+\dfrac{-31}{2\left(2x-1\right)^2}\right)dx$\\


$\boxed{= \dfrac{-4}{x}-\dfrac{1}{2x^2}+\dfrac{1}{4}\ln{\left|4x-2\right|+\dfrac{31}{4\left(2x-1\right)}+k}}$\\
