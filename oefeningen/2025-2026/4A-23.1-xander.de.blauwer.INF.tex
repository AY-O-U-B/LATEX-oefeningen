$\displaystyle\int \operatorname{Bgtan}(x)dx$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u = \operatorname{Bgtan}(x)  \\
dv = dx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du = \dfrac{1}{1+x^2}dx \\
v = x
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$ = x\operatorname{Bgtan}(x) - \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x}{1+x^2}dx$ \\

Stel $t = 1 + x^2 \Rightarrow dt = 2xdx $ \\

$ = x\operatorname{Bgtan}(x) - \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t}$ \\

$ = x\operatorname{Bgtan}(x) - \dfrac{1}{2}\ln|1+x^2| + c $
