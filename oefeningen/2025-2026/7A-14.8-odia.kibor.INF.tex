$f(x,y) = e^{-x^2-y^2}$ in het punt $a(0,0)$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = e^{-x^2-y^2}\cdot 2x$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(a) = 0$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = e^{-x^2-y^2}\cdot 2y$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(a) = 0$\\

$f(a) = 1$\\

$z = f(a) + \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(a)(x-a) + \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(a)(y-b)$\\

$= 1 + 0(x - 0) + 0(y - 0) = 1$\\
