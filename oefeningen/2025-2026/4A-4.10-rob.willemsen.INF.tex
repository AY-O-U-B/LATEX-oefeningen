$\dint \dfrac{x^3 \sqrt[5]{x^3}}{\sqrt[3]{x}}$ \\

$=\dint \dfrac{x^3 \cdot x^{\tfrac{3}{5}}}{x^{\tfrac{1}{3}}} dx = \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{18}{5}}}{x^{\tfrac{1}{3}}} dx = \dint x^{\tfrac{18}{5} - \tfrac{1}{3}} dx = \dint x^{\tfrac{49}{15}} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{15}{64} x^{\tfrac{64}{15}} + C = \dfrac{15}{64} \cdot x^{\tfrac{60}{15}} x^{\tfrac{4}{15}} + C = \dfrac{15}{64} x^4 \sqrt[15]{x^4} + C$ \\
