Bereken $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{3x^2+2}}$\\

Substitutie: $t = \sqrt{3x^2+2}$, $t^2 = 3x^2+2$, $2t dt = 6x dx$, $dx = \dfrac{t dt}{3x}$\\

Uit $t^2 = 3x^2 + 2$ volgt $x = \dfrac{\sqrt{t^2-2}}{\sqrt{3}}$, dus $dx = \dfrac{t dt}{\sqrt{3}\sqrt{t^2-2}}$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{3x^2+2}} &=& \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{t} \cdot \dfrac{t dt}{\sqrt{3}\sqrt{t^2-2}}\\
&=& \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{\sqrt{t^2-2}}\\
&=& \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \ln\left|t + \sqrt{t^2-2}\right| + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}

Terugsubstitueren: $t = \sqrt{3x^2+2}$ en $\sqrt{t^2-2} = \sqrt{3x^2} = x\sqrt{3}$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
&=& \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \ln\left|\sqrt{3x^2+2} + x\sqrt{3}\right| + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
