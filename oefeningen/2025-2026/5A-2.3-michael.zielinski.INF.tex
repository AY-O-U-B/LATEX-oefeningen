$ \displaystyle\int^2_{-1} x \lfloor x \rfloor dx $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int^0_{-1} x \lfloor x \rfloor dx + \displaystyle\int^1_0 x \lfloor x \rfloor dx + \displaystyle\int^2_1 x \lfloor x \rfloor dx $ \\

$ = \displaystyle\int^0_{-1} x \cdot (-1) dx + \displaystyle\int^1_0 x \cdot 0 dx + \displaystyle\int^2_1 x \cdot 1 dx $ \\

$ = -\displaystyle\int^0_{-1} x dx + 0 + \displaystyle\int^2_1 x dx $ \\

$ = -\left[ \dfrac{x^2}{2} \right]^0_{-1} + \left[ \dfrac{x^2}{2} \right]^2_1 $ \\

$ = -\left( 0 - \dfrac{1}{2} \right) + \left( 2 - \dfrac{1}{2} \right) $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} + \dfrac{3}{2} $ \\

$ = 2 $ \\
