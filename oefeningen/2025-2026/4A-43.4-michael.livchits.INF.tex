$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{2x+1}{(x^2-1)^2}dx$\\

$(x^2-1)^2 = (x-1)^2(x+1)^2$\\

$\dfrac{2x+1}{(x-1)^2(x+1)^2} = \dfrac{A}{(x-1)^2}+\dfrac{B}{(x+1)^2}+\dfrac{C}{x-1}+\dfrac{D}{x+1}$\\

$A = \dfrac{2x+1}{(x+1)^2}$ met $x = 1$\\

$A = \dfrac{3}{4}$\\

$B = \dfrac{2x+1}{(x-1)^2}$ met $x = -1$\\

$B = -\dfrac{1}{4}$\\

Vervolgens om $C$ te vinden\\

$\dfrac{2x+1}{(x-1)^2(x+1)^2} - \dfrac{A}{(x-1)^2}$\\

$= \dfrac{8x+4}{4(x-1)^2(x+1)^2} - \dfrac{3(x+1)^2}{4(x-1)^2(x+1)^2} = \dfrac{-3x^2+2x+1}{4(x-1)^2(x+1)^2}$\\

$= \dfrac{(x-1)(3x+1)}{4(x-1)^2(x+1)^2} = \dfrac{3x+1}{4(x-1)(x+1)^2}$\\

$B = \dfrac{3x+1}{4(x+1)^2}$ met $x=1$\\

$B = \dfrac{1}{4}$\\

Vervolgens om $D$ te vinden\\

$\dfrac{2x+1}{(x-1)^2(x+1)^2} - \dfrac{B}{(x+1)^2}$\\

$\dfrac{8x+4}{4(x-1)^2(x+1)^2} + \dfrac{(x-1)^2}{4(x-1)^2(x+1)^2} = \dfrac{x^2+6x+5}{4(x-1)^2(x+1)^2}$\\

$\dfrac{(x+1)(x+5)}{4(x-1)^2(x+1)^2} = \dfrac{x+5}{4(x-1)^2(x+1)}$\\

$D = \dfrac{x+5}{4(x-1)^2}$ met $x=-1$\\

$D = \dfrac{1}{4}$\\

Nu de integraal\\

$I = \dfrac{3}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{(x-1)^2}-\dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{(x+1)^2}+\dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{x-1}+\dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{x+1}$\\

Stel $u = x-1$ en stel $t = x+1$\\

$du = dx$ en $dt = dx$\\

$I = \dfrac{3}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{du}{u^2}-\dfrac{1}{4}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dt}{t^2}+\dfrac{1}{4}\ln|x-1|+\dfrac{1}{4}\ln|x+1| + C$\\

$= -\dfrac{3}{4(x-1)}+\dfrac{1}{4(x+1)}+\dfrac{1}{4}\ln|x-1|+\dfrac{1}{4}\ln|x+1| + C$\\

$= -\dfrac{x+2}{2(x^2+1)}+\dfrac{1}{4}\ln|x-1|+\dfrac{1}{4}\ln|x+1| + C$\\
