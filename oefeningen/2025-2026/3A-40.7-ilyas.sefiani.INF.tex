$\dfrac{df}{dx}\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{2x^3-2x+1}}{\sqrt[3]{x+\dfrac{1}{x}}}\right)$\\

$=\dfrac{(\sqrt{2x^3-2x+1})'\sqrt[3]{x+\dfrac{1}{x}}-\left(\sqrt[3]{x+\dfrac{1}{x}}\right)'(\sqrt{2x^3-2x+1})}{\sqrt[3]{\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^2}}$\\

$=\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt[3]{x+\dfrac{1}{x}}(2x^3-2x+1)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}(6x^2-2)-\left(\dfrac{1}{3}\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^{-\tfrac{2}{3}}\left(1-\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)(2x^3-2x+1)^{\tfrac{1}{2}}\right)}{\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^{\tfrac{2}{3}}}$\\

$=\dfrac{\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^{-\tfrac{2}{3}}(2x^3-2x+1)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\dfrac{1}{2}(6x^2-2)\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)-\left(\dfrac{1}{3}\left(1-\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)(2x^3-2x+1)\right)\right)}{\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^{\tfrac{2}{3}}}$\\

$=\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^{-\tfrac{4}{3}}(2x^3-2x+1)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}\left((3x^2-1)\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)-\dfrac{1}{3}\left(1-\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)(2x^3-2x+1)\right)$\\

$=\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^{-\tfrac{4}{3}}(2x^3-2x+1)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\left(3x^3+2x-\dfrac{1}{x}\right)-\dfrac{1}{3}\left(2x^3-4x+\dfrac{2}{x}-1-\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)\right)$\\

$=\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^{-\tfrac{4}{3}}(2x^3-2x+1)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\dfrac{9x^3}{3}+\dfrac{6x}{3}-\dfrac{3}{3x}-\dfrac{2x^3}{3}+\dfrac{4x}{3}-\dfrac{2}{3x}-\dfrac{1}{3}+\dfrac{1}{3x^2}\right)$\\

$=\left(x+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^{-\tfrac{4}{3}}(2x^3-2x+1)^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\dfrac{7x^3}{3}+\dfrac{10x}{3}-\dfrac{5}{3x}-\dfrac{1}{3}+\dfrac{1}{3x^2}\right)$\\
