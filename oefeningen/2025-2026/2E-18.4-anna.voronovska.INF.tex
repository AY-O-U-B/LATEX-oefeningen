$\log_x \sqrt{3} = -\dfrac{1}{4}$\\

$\Rightarrow x^{-\tfrac{1}{4}} = \sqrt{3}$\\

$\Rightarrow x^{\tfrac{1}{4}} = 3^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}$\\

$\Rightarrow x = 3^{-2}$\\

$\Rightarrow x = \dfrac{1}{9}$
