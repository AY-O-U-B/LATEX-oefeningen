$w = \dfrac{1}{u^2 + v^2}$ met $u = \cos 2t$ en $v = \sin 2t$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial u} = \dfrac{-2u}{(u^2 + v^2)^2}$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial v} = \dfrac{-2v}{(u^2 + v^2)^2}$ \\

$\dfrac{du}{dt} = -2\sin 2t$ \\

$\dfrac{dv}{dt} = 2\cos 2t$ \\

$\dfrac{dw}{dt} = \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial u} \dfrac{du}{dt} + \dfrac{\partial w}{\partial v} \dfrac{dv}{dt}$ \\

$\dfrac{\partial w}{\partial t} = \dfrac{-2u}{(u^2 + v^2)^2} \cdot (-2\sin 2t) + \dfrac{-2v}{(u^2 + v^2)^2} \cdot 2\cos 2t$ \\

$= \dfrac{-2u}{((\cos 2t)^2 + (\sin 2t)^2)^2} \cdot (-2\sin 2t) + \dfrac{-2v}{((\cos 2t)^2 + (\sin 2t)^2)^2} \cdot 2\cos 2t$ \\

$= \dfrac{-2u}{1} \cdot (-2\sin 2t) + \dfrac{-2v}{1} \cdot 2\cos 2t$ \\

$= 4\cos 2t\sin 2t - 4\cos 2t\sin 2t$ \\

$= 0$ \\
