$\integral\dfrac{x\Bgsin^3 x}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $t = \Bgsin x \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}dx$ \\

$\Rightarrow x = \sin t$ \\

$= \integral\sin t \cdot t^3dt$ \\

Partiële integratie: $\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    u = t^3 \\
    dv = \sin tdt \\
\end{array}\right.\Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    du = 3t^2dt \\
    v = -\cos t \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$= -t^3\cos t + 3\integral t^2\cos tdt$ \\ 

Partiële integratie: $\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    u = t^2 \\
    dv = \cos tdt \\
\end{array}\right. \Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    du = 2tdt \\
    v = \sin t \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$= -t^3\cos t + 3\left(t^2\sin t - 2\integral t\sin tdt\right)$ \\ 

$= -t^3\cos t + 3t^2\sin t - 6\integral t\sin tdt$ \\

Partiële integratie: $\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    u = t \\
    dv = \sin tdt \\
\end{array}\right.\Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{l}
    du = dt \\
    v = -\cos t \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$= -t^3\cos t + 3t^2\sin t - 6\left(-t\cos t + \integral \cos tdt\right)$ \\

$= -t^3\cos t + 3t^2\sin t + 6t\cos t - 6\integral \cos tdt$ \\

$= -t^3\cos t + 3t^2\sin t + 6t\cos t - 6\sin t + c$ \\

$= (-t^2 + 6)t\cos t + (3t^2 - 6)\sin t + c$ \\

$= (-\Bgsin^2x + 6)\Bgsin x\cdot\cos(\Bgsin x) + (3\Bgsin^2x - 6)x + c$ \\
