$\mathbf{f}(x,y) = (\sin(xy), \sinh(xy))$\\

$D\mathbf{f}(x,y) = \left(\begin{array}{c c} \dfrac{\partial}{\partial x}(\sin(xy)) & \dfrac{\partial}{\partial y}(\sin(xy)) \\[3ex] \dfrac{\partial}{\partial x}(\sinh(xy)) & \dfrac{\partial}{\partial y}(\sinh(xy)) \end{array}\right)$\\

$= \left(\begin{array}{c c} y\cos(xy) & x\cos(xy) \\[1.5ex] y\cosh(xy) & x\cosh(xy) \end{array}\right)$\\

$\det(D\mathbf{f}(x,y)) = (y\cos(xy))(x\cosh(xy)) - (x\cos(xy))(y\cosh(xy))$\\

$= xy\cos(xy)\cosh(xy) - xy\cos(xy)\cosh(xy)$\\

$= 0$\\

$\Rightarrow \det(Df(x,y)) = 0 \quad \forall (x,y) \in \mathbb{R}^2$\\

$\Rightarrow \not\exists \f^{-1}$\\
