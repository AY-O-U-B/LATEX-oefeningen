$s_n =\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{n}\left(2k-1\right)2k\left(2k+2\right)$\\

$=\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{n}\left(2k-1\right)\left(4k^2+4k\right)$\\

$= \displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{n}\left(8k^3+4k^2-4k\right)$\\

$= 8\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{n}k^3+4\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{n}k^2-4\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{n}k$\\

$= 8\cdot\dfrac{n^2\left(n+1\right)^2}{4}+4\cdot\dfrac{n\left(n+1\right)\left(2n+1\right)}{6}-4\cdot\dfrac{n\left(n+1\right)}{2}$\\


$= 2n^2\left(n+1\right)^2+\dfrac{2n\left(n+1\right)\left(2n+1\right)}{3} - 2n\left(n+1\right)$\\

$= 2n\left(n+1\right) \left(n(n+1)+\dfrac{2n+1}{3}-1\right)$\\


$= 2n\left(n+1\right) \left(\dfrac{3n\left(n+1\right)+2n+1-3}{3}\right)$\\


$ =\dfrac{2n\left(n+1\right)}{3}\left(3n^2+3n+2n+1-3\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{2n\left(n+1\right)}{3}\left(3n^2+5n-2\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{2n\left(n+1\right)}{3}\left(3n-1\right)\left(n+2\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{2n\left(n+1\right)\left(n+2\right)\left(3n-1\right)}{3}$\\

Omdat $n\left(n+1\right)\left(n+2\right)$ een product is van 3 opeenvolgende getallen, is één van die factoren deelbaar door 3.\\

$\Rightarrow s_n\in\mathbb{N}$\\
