$f(x,y,z,t) = \ln\left(\dfrac{xy}{zt}\right) = \ln|x| + \ln|y| - \ln|z| - \ln|t|$\\

Eerste partiële afgeleiden:\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \dfrac{1}{x}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \dfrac{1}{y}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial z} = -\dfrac{1}{z}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial t} = -\dfrac{1}{t}$\\

Tweede partiële afgeleiden:\\

$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} = -\dfrac{1}{x^2}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} = -\dfrac{1}{y^2}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial z^2} =\dfrac{1}{z^2}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial t^2} =\dfrac{1}{t^2}$\\

Alle gemengde afgeleiden zijn $0$.\\

$\Rightarrow D^2f(x,y,z,t) = \left(\begin{array}{cccc}
-\dfrac{1}{x^2}&    0          &0             &0 \\
0              &-\dfrac{1}{y^2}&0             &0 \\
0              &0              &\dfrac{1}{z^2}&0 \\
0              &0              &0             &\dfrac{1}{t^2}
\end{array}\right)$\\
