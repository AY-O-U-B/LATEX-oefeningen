$f(x) = 3x - x\ln x^3$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{d}{dx}(3x) - \dfrac{d}{dx}(x\ln x^3)$ \\

$= 3 - \left(\dfrac{d}{dx}(x)\cdot\ln x^3 + x\cdot\dfrac{d}{dx}(\ln x^3)\right)$ \\

$= 3 - \left(\ln x^3 + x\cdot\dfrac{1}{x^3}\cdot\dfrac{d}{dx}(x^3)\right)$ \\

$= 3 - \ln x^3 - \dfrac{3x^2}{x^2}$ \\

$= -\ln x^3$ \\
