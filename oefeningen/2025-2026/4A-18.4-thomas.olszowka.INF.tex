$I = \dint x^3 \cos x dx$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    u =& x^3 \\ 
    dv =& \cos x dx \\ 
\end{array}\right. \Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    du =& 3x^2 dx \\
    v =& \sin x \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$I = x^3 \sin x - \dint 3x^2 \sin x dx $ \\

$= x^3 \sin x - 3 \dint x^2 \sin x dx$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    u =& x^2 \\ 
    dv =& \sin x dx \\ 
\end{array}\right. \Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    du =& 2x dx \\
    v =& -\cos x \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$I = x^3 \sin x - 3 \left( -x^2 \cos x - \dint -2x \cos x dx\right)$ \\

$= x^3 \sin x + 3x^2 \cos x - 6\dint x \cos x dx$ \\

$\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    u =& x \\ 
    dv =& \cos x dx \\ 
\end{array}\right. \Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    du =& dx \\
    v =& \sin x \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$I = x^3 \sin x + 3x^2 \cos x - 6 \left ( x \sin x - \dint  \sin x dx\right )$ \\

$= x^3 \sin x + 3x^2 \cos x - 6x \sin x + 6 \dint \sin x dx$ \\

$= x^3 \sin x + 3x^2 \cos x - 6x \sin x - 6 \cos x + c $ \\

$= \left(x^3 -6x\right)\sin x + \left(3x^2 - 6\right) \cos x + c$
