$\displaystyle\int^{+\infty}_1\dfrac{\ln{x}}{\left(x+1\right)^2}dx$\\

$=\lim\limits_{a\to+\infty}\displaystyle\int^a_1\ln{x}\left(x+1\right)^{-2}dx$\\

We weten dat: $\displaystyle\int^c_dudv=\left[uv\right]^c_d-\displaystyle\int^c_dvdu$\\

Laat:\\

$u=\ln{x}$ , $dv=\left(x+1\right)^{-2}dx$\\

$du=\dfrac{1}{x}dx$ , $v=-\left(x+1\right)^{-1}$\\

Dus:\\

$\lim\limits_{a\to+\infty}\displaystyle\int^a_1\ln{x}\left(x+1\right)^{-2}dx = \lim\limits_{a\to+\infty} \left(-\left[\dfrac{\ln{x}}{x+1}\right]^a_1+\displaystyle\int^a_1\dfrac{1}{x\left(x+1\right)}dx\right)$\\

We weten dat:\\

$\dfrac{1}{x\left(x+1\right)} = \dfrac{A}{x}+\dfrac{B}{x+1}$\\

$\Rightarrow A=1$ , $B=-1$\\

$\lim\limits_{a\to+\infty}\displaystyle\int^a_1\ln{x}\left(x+1\right)^{-2}dx = \lim\limits_{a\to+\infty} \left(-\left[\dfrac{\ln{x}}{x+1}\right]^a_1+\displaystyle\int^a_1\left(\dfrac{1}{x}-\dfrac{1}{\left(x+1\right)}\right)dx\right)$\\

$= \lim\limits_{a\to+\infty} \left(-\left(\dfrac{\ln{a}}{a+1}-\dfrac{\ln{1}}{2}\right)+\left[\ln{x} - \ln{x+1} \right]^a_1\right)$\\

$= \lim\limits_{a\to+\infty} -\dfrac{\ln{a}}{a+1}+\ln{a} - \ln\left({a+1}\right) -\ln{1} + \ln\left({2}\right)$\\

$= \lim\limits_{a\to+\infty} -\dfrac{\ln{a}}{a+1}+\ln{a} - \ln\left({a+1}\right) +\ln{2}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{a\to+\infty} -\dfrac{\ln{a}}{a+1}+\ln\left({\dfrac{a}{a+1}}\right) + \ln2$\\

$= \lim\limits_{a\to+\infty} -\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{a}}{1} + \ln1 + \ln2$\\

$= \lim\limits_{a\to+\infty} -\dfrac{1}{a}$\\

$=\ln2$\\
