$\lim\limits_{x \to \infty} \dfrac{5x + |x|}{8x - 3|x|}$ \\
    
$\lim\limits_{x \to +\infty} \dfrac{5x + x}{8x - 3x} = \dfrac{6x}{5x} = \dfrac{6}{5}$ \\
    
$\lim\limits_{x \to -\infty} \dfrac{5x - x}{8x + 3x} = \dfrac{4x}{11x} = \dfrac{4}{11}$ \\
