 $z^3 - 5(1 + 2i)^2 + 24 - 10i = 0$ \\

Stel $t = z^2 \Rightarrow t^2 - (5 + 10i)t + 24 - 10i = 0$ \\

Omdat de discriminant weer een complex getal is moeten we daarvoor de vierkantswortels uitrekenen. \\

$\Rightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	x^2 - y^2 = -171 \\
	2xy = 140 \Rightarrow x^2(-y^2) = 4900
\end{array}
\right . $\\

$ \lambda^2 + 171\lambda -4900 = 0$\\

$D = 171^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot (-4900) = 48841 \Rightarrow \sqrt{100} = 221$\\

$\lambda_{1} = 25$ en $\lambda_{2} = -196$\\

$x = \sqrt{5}$ en $y = \sqrt{-196} $\\

$\Rightarrow$ vierkantswortel van eerste discriminant: $5 + 14i$ of $-5 - 14i$ \\

$t_{1,2} = \dfrac{5+10 \pm (5 + 14i)}{2}$\\

$t_{1} = 5 + 12i$ en $t_{2} = -2i$\\

$ t = z^2 \Leftrightarrow z = \sqrt{t}:$\\

	x^2 - y^2 = 5 \\

\right . $\\

$ \lambda^2 - 5\lambda - 36 = 0$\\

$D = 25 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot (-36) = 169 \Rightarrow \sqrt{169} = 13$\\

$\lambda_{1,2} = \dfrac{5 \pm 13}{2}$\\

$\lambda_{1} = 9$ en $\lambda_{2} = -4$\\

$ x = \pm 3$ en $y = \pm 2$\\

$\Rightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	x^2 - y^2 = 0 \\
	2xy = -2 \Rightarrow x^2(-y^2) = 1
\end{array}
\right . $\\

$\lambda^2 - 1 = 0$\\

$\lambda^2 = 1$\\

$\lambda_{1, 2} = \pm 1$\\

$x = \pm 1$ en $y = \pm 1$\\

$\Rightarrow z_{3} = -1 + i$ en $z_{4} = 1 - i$\\
