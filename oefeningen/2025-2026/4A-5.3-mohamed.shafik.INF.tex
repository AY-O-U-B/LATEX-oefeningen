Bereken $\displaystyle\int (x^2 + 4x) dx$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int (x^2 + 4x) dx &=& \displaystyle\int x^2 dx + \displaystyle\int 4x dx\\
&=& \dfrac{x^3}{3} + 4 \cdot \dfrac{x^2}{2} + c\\
&=& \dfrac{x^3}{3} + 2x^2 + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
