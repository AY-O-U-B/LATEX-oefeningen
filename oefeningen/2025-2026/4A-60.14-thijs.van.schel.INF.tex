$\dint \dfrac{6x+2-\sqrt{4x+1}}{2x+1+\sqrt{4x+1}} dx$\\

$u = \sqrt{4x+1} \Rightarrow x = \dfrac{u^2-1}{4} \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{u}{2} du$\\

$= \dint \dfrac{\dfrac{3u^2+1}{2} - u}{\dfrac{u^2+1}{2} + u} \cdot \dfrac{u}{2} du$\\

$= \dint \dfrac{\dfrac{3u^2-2u+1}{2}}{\dfrac{u^2+2u+1}{2}} \cdot \dfrac{u}{2} du$\\

$= \dint \dfrac{3u^2-2u+1}{u^2+2u+1} \cdot \dfrac{u}{2} du = \dfrac{1}{2} \dint \dfrac{3u^3-2u^2+u}{(u+1)^2} du$\\

$u = t+1 \Rightarrow du = dt$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \dint \dfrac{3(u-1)^3 - 2(u-1)^2 + (u-1)}{u^2} du$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \dint \dfrac{3(u^3-3u^2+3u-1) - 2(u^2-2u+1) + u - 1}{u^2} du$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \dint \dfrac{3u^3 - 11u^2 + 14u - 6}{u^2} du$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \dint \left( 3u - 11 + \dfrac{14}{u} - \dfrac{6}{u^2} \right) du$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \left(\dfrac{3u^2}{2} - 11u + 14 \ln |u| + \dfrac{6}{u} \right) + C$\\

$= \dfrac{3(t+1)^2}{4} - \dfrac{11(t+1)}{2} + 7 \ln |u+1| + \dfrac{3}{u+1} + C$\\

$= \dfrac{3(\sqrt{4x+1}+1)^2}{4} - \dfrac{11(\sqrt{4x+1}+1)}{2} + 7 \ln |\sqrt{4x+1}+1| + \dfrac{3}{\sqrt{4x+1}+1} + C$
