Gegeven is de macht $z = \left(3 \operatorname{cis} \dfrac{\pi}{6}\right)^3$. \\

$z = 3^3 \operatorname{cis} \left(3 \cdot \dfrac{\pi}{6}\right)$ \\

$z = 27 \operatorname{cis} \left(\dfrac{3\pi}{6}\right)$ \\

$z = 27 \operatorname{cis} \dfrac{\pi}{2}$ \\

$z = 27 \left(\cos \dfrac{\pi}{2} + i \sin \dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)$ \\

$z = 27 (0 + 1i)$ \\

$z = 27i$ \\

\textbf{Antwoord:} $z = 27i$ \\
