$\sqrt[3]{4} = 8^x$\\

$\Rightarrow 4^{\tfrac{1}{3}} = 8^x$\\

$\Rightarrow 2^{2^{\tfrac{1}{3}}} = 2^{3x}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{2}{3} = 3x$\\

$\Rightarrow x = \dfrac{2}{9}$\\
