\normalsize

$f(x,y)= \sin(xy^2)$\\
	
Eerste orde:\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \cos(xy^2)\cdot y^2$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \cos(xy^2)\cdot 2xy$\\

Tweede orde:\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} = -\sin(xy^2)\cdot y^4$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} = -\sin(xy^2)\cdot 4x^2y^2 + \cos(xy^2)\cdot 2x$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x\partial y} = -\sin(xy^2)\cdot 2xy^3 + \cos(xy^2)\cdot 2y$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y\partial x} = -\sin(xy^2)\cdot 2xy^3 + \cos(xy^2)\cdot 2y$\\
