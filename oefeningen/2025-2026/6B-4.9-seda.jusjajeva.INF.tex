$x_n = \dfrac{13}{4}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow -\dfrac{5}{12} + (n - 1)\dfrac{1}{3} = \dfrac{13}{4}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow (n - 1)\dfrac{1}{3} = \dfrac{13}{4} + \dfrac{5}{12}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow n - 1 = 3\cdot\dfrac{11}{3}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow n - 1 = 11$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow n = 12$ \\
