$\displaystyle\int_{0}^{2} \dfrac{2xdx}{x^2 + 1}  $\\

$u = x^2 + 1$\\

$du = 2x dx$\\

$=\displaystyle\int_{1}^{5} \dfrac{1}{u}du  $\\

$= \left[\ln |u|\right]_{1}^{5}$\\

$= \ln |5| - \ln |1|$\\

$ = \ln \left(\dfrac{5}{1}\right)$\\

$= \ln 5$
