$I = \displaystyle\dint_{1}^{+\infty} x^{-\tfrac{2}{3}}dx$\\

Eerst vinden we de onbepaalde integraal\\

$\displaystyle\int x^{-\tfrac{2}{3}}dx = 3\sqrt[3]{x} + C$\\

Neem de limiet\\

$I = \lim\limits_{b\rightarrow +\infty}\left[3\sqrt[3]{x}\right]_{1}^{b}$\\

$I = \lim\limits_{b\rightarrow +\infty}\left(3\sqrt[3]{b} - 3\right) = + \infty$

$\Rightarrow$ divergeert\\
