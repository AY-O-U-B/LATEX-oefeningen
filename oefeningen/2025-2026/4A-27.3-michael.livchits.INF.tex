$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{x^3}{x^2-1}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\left(x+\dfrac{x}{x^2-1}\right)dx$\\

stel $u = x^2-1$\\

$du = 2xdx$\\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2}+\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{du}{u}$\\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2}+\dfrac{1}{2}\ln |u| + C$\\

$= \dfrac{x^2}{2}+\dfrac{1}{2}\ln |x^2-1| + C$\\
