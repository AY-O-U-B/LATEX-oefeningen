$f(x)=\sin x$\\

$f'(x)=\cos x$\\

$f''(x)=-\sin x$\\

$f''(x)=0 \Leftrightarrow -\sin x=0 \Leftrightarrow x=n\pi, n\in \mathbb{Z}$\\

\end{array}$ \\
