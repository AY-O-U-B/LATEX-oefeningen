$\dint \dfrac{dx}{4x^2+12x+18}$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{dx}{(2x+3)^2+9}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{9} \dint \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{(2x+3)^2}{9}+1}dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{9} \dint \dfrac{dx}{\left(\dfrac{2x+3}{3}\right)^2+1}$ \\

$t = \dfrac{2x+3}{3} \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{2}{3} dx \Rightarrow \dfrac{3}{2} dt = dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{9} \cdot \dfrac{3}{2} \dint \dfrac{dt}{t^2+1}$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{6} \Bgtan(t) + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{6} \Bgtan\left(\dfrac{2x+3}{3}\right) + C$ \\
