$f(x,y) = \ln(x^2+y^2)$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \dfrac{2x}{x^2+y^2}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \dfrac{2y}{x^2+y^2}$\\

Gedefinieerd voor $(x,y) \neq (0,0)$.\\

$Df = \left(\dfrac{2x}{x^2+y^2} \hspace{1cm} \dfrac{2y}{x^2+y^2}\right)$\\
