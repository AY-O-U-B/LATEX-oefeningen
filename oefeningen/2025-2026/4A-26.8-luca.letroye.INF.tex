$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{-3dx}{(7x-1)^2} $\\

$ = -3\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{(7x-1)^2} $\\

$t = 7x-1$\\

$\dfrac{dt}{dx} = 7$\\

$ \Rightarrow \dfrac{-3}{7}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{t^2} dt  $\\

$ = \dfrac{-3}{7}\displaystyle\int t^{-2} dt  $\\

$ = \dfrac{3}{7t} +C  $\\

$ = \dfrac{3}{7(7x-1)} +C  $\\
