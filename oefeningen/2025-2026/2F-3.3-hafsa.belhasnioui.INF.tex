Toon aan dat: \\

$\tanh x = \dfrac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} \overset{\textcircled{1}}{=} \dfrac{1 - e^{-2x}}{1 + e^{-2x}} \overset{\textcircled{2}}{=} \dfrac{e^{2x} - 1}{e^{2x} + 1}$\\

Bewijs: \\

1) $\tanh x = \dfrac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} \cdot \dfrac{e^{-x}}{e^{-x}} = \dfrac{1 - e^{-2x}}{1 + e^{-2x}}$\\

2) $\tanh x = \dfrac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} \cdot \dfrac{e^x}{e^x} = \dfrac{e^{2x} - 1}{e^{2x} + 1}$\\
