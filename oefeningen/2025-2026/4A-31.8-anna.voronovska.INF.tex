$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x+2}{\sqrt{3x-1}}dx $\\

$t = 3x-1 \Rightarrow \dfrac{dt}{dx} = 3 \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{dt}{3}$\\

$x = \dfrac{t+1}{3} \Rightarrow x + 2 = \dfrac{t+1}{3} + 2 = \dfrac{t+7}{3}$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{\dfrac{t+7}{3}}{\sqrt{t}} \cdot \dfrac{dt}{3}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{9}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{t+7}{\sqrt{t}}dt$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{9}\displaystyle\int \left( t^{\tfrac{1}{2}} + 7t^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} \right)dt$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{9}\left( \dfrac{2t^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{3} + 14t^{\tfrac{1}{2}} \right) + c$\\

$= \dfrac{2t^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{27} + \dfrac{14t^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{9} + c$\\

$= \dfrac{2(3x-1)^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{27} + \dfrac{14(3x-1)^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{9} + c$\\

$= \dfrac{2(3x-1)\sqrt{3x-1}}{27} + \dfrac{14\sqrt{3x-1}}{9} + c$\\
