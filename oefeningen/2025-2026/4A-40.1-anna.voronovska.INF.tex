$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{x(4-x)}}= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{4x - x^2}} =\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{-(x^2 - 4x)}}$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{-(x^2 - 4x + 4 - 4)}} =\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{4 - (x-2)^2}} $\\

$t = x-2 \Rightarrow dt = dx$\\

$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{4-(x-2)^2}} = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{\sqrt{4-t^2}} = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{\sqrt{4\left(1-\dfrac{t^2}{4}\right)}} = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{2\sqrt{1-\left(\dfrac{t}{2}\right)^2}}$\\

$u = \dfrac{t}{2} \Rightarrow du = \dfrac{1}{2}dt \Rightarrow dt = 2du$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2du}{2\sqrt{1-u^2}} = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{du}{\sqrt{1-u^2}} = \operatorname{Bgsin}(u) + c$\\

Dus:\\

$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{x(4-x)}} = \operatorname{Bgsin}\left(\dfrac{x-2}{2}\right) + c$
