$I = \displaystyle\dint_{-2}^2 \left(-x^2+4\right)dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\dint_{-2}^{0} \left(-x^2+4\right)dx + \displaystyle\dint_{0}^{2} \left(-x^2+4\right)dx$\\

$= \left[-\dfrac{x^3}{3} + 4x\right]_{-2}^{0} + \left[-\dfrac{x^3}{3} + 4x\right]_{0}^{2}$\\

$= \dfrac{16}{3} + \dfrac{16}{3} = \dfrac{32}{3}$\\
