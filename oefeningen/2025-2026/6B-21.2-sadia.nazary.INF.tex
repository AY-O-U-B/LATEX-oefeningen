$\dfrac{1}{1 \cdot 4} + \dfrac{1}{4 \cdot 7} + \dfrac{1}{7 \cdot 10} + \ldots + \dfrac{1}{(3n-2)(3n+1)}$\\

$s_n = \dfrac{1}{3} \left(1 - \dfrac{1}{4} + \dfrac{1}{4} - \dfrac{1}{7} + \dfrac{1}{7} - \dfrac{1}{10} + \ldots + \dfrac{1}{3n-2} - \dfrac{1}{3n+1} \right)$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{3} \left(1 - \cancel{\dfrac{1}{4}} + \cancel{\dfrac{1}{4}} - \bcancel{\dfrac{1}{7}} + \bcancel{\dfrac{1}{7}} - \cancel{\dfrac{1}{10}} + \ldots + \bcancel{\dfrac{1}{3n-2}} - \dfrac{1}{3n+1} \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \left(1 - \dfrac{1}{3n+1} \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \left(\dfrac{3n+1}{3n+1} - \dfrac{1}{3n+1} \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \dfrac{3n}{3n+1}$\\

$= \dfrac{n}{3n+1}$\\
