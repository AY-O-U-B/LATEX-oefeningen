$\dfrac{df}{dx}(x^{\sin(x)})$\\

$= \sin(x)x^{\sin(x)-1}+x^{\sin(x)}\cos(x)\ln(x)$\\

$= \sin(x)\dfrac{x^{\sin(x)}}{x}+x^{\sin(x)}\cos(x)\ln(x)$\\  

$= x^{\sin(x)}\left(\dfrac{\sin(x)+x\cos(x)\ln(x)}{x}\right)$\\
