$5\left(5^{x-1}-1\right) = 4\left(0.2^x-5\cdot0.04^x\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow 5^x - 5 = 4\left(\left(\dfrac{1}{5}\right)^x-5\cdot\left(\dfrac{1}{5^2}\right)^x\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow 5^x - 5 = 4\left(\dfrac{1}{5^x}-\dfrac{5}{5^{2x}}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow 5^x - 5 = 4\left(\dfrac{5^x}{5^{2x}}-\dfrac{5}{5^{2x}}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow 5^x - 5 = 4\left(\dfrac{5^x - 5}{5^{2x}}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow 5^{3x} - 5^{2x+1} = 4\left(5^x - 5\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow 5^{3x} - 5^{2x+1} = 4 \cdot 5^x - 20$\\

Laat $X = 5^x$\\

$\Rightarrow X^{3} - 5 X^2 - 4X + 20 = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow X^2\left(X - 5 \right) - 4\left(X-5\right) = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow \left(X - 5 \right)\left(X^2 - 4\right) = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow X=5$, $2$, $-2$\\
