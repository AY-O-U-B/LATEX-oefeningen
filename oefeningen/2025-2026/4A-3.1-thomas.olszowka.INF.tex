$\dint \sqrt{2x} dx$ \\

$= \sqrt{2} \dint x^{\tfrac{1}{2}} dx$ \\

$= \sqrt{2} \cdot \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{3}{2}}}{\dfrac{3}{2}} + c$ \\

$= \sqrt{2} \cdot \dfrac{2\sqrt{x^3}}{3} + c$ \\

$=  \dfrac{2\sqrt{2}x\sqrt{x}}{3} + c$ \\
