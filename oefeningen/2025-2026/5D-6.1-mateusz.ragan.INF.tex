$y^2 = 6x$ en $x^2 = 6y$ \\

snijpunten\\

$\sqrt{6x} = \dfrac{x^2}{6} \Leftrightarrow 6x = \dfrac{x^4}{36} \Leftrightarrow 216x = x^4 \Leftrightarrow x(x^3 - 216) \Leftrightarrow x = 0 \vee x = 6$\\

$ \displaystyle\int_{0}^{6} \left(\sqrt{6x} - \dfrac{x^2}{6}\right) dx$\\

 $ = \displaystyle\int_{0}^{6} \left(\sqrt{6} \sqrt{x}\right)dx - \displaystyle\int_{0}^{6} \dfrac{x^2}{6} dx $\\

$\left[\dfrac{2}{3} \sqrt{6} x^{\tfrac{3}{2}}\right]_{0}^{6} - \left[\dfrac{x^3}{18}\right]_{0}^{6}$\\

$= (24 - 0) - (12 - 0) = 12$\\
