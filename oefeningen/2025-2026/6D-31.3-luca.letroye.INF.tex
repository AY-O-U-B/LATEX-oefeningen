$ \displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{\ln n}{n} $\\

$\rightarrow $ integraal criterium\\

$\lim\limits_{t\rightarrow +\infty}\dint_2^{t} \dfrac{\ln x}{x}dx$\\

$t = \ln x \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{1}{x}dx$\\

$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{p\rightarrow +\infty}\dint_2^{p} t dt = \lim\limits_{p\rightarrow +\infty}\left[\dfrac{t^2}{2}\right]_2^p$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{p\rightarrow +\infty}\left[\dfrac{\ln^2 x}{2}\right]_2^p = +\infty \Rightarrow $ divergent\\
