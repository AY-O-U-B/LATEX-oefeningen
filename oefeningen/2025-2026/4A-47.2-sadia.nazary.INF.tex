$\dint\dfrac{3x-1}{(x^2+4x+5)^3}dx$\\

$x^2+4x+5=(x+2)^2+1$\\

$3x-1=\dfrac32(2x+4)-7$\\

Dus:\\

$\dint\dfrac{3x-1}{(x^2+4x+5)^3}dx$\\

$=
\dfrac32\dint\dfrac{2x+4}{(x^2+4x+5)^3}dx
-
7\dint\dfrac{dx}{((x+2)^2+1)^3}$\\

Eerste integraal:\\

$u=x^2+4x+5$\\

$du=(2x+4)dx$\\

$\dfrac32\dint u^{-3}du
=
\dfrac32\left(\dfrac{u^{-2}}{-2}\right)$\\

$=
-\dfrac{3}{4u^2}$\\

$=
-\dfrac{3}{4(x^2+4x+5)^2}$\\

Tweede integraal:\\

$x+2=\tan t$\\

$dx=\sec^2t dt$\\

$((x+2)^2+1)^3=(\tan^2t+1)^3=(\sec^2t)^3=\sec^6t$\\

Dus:\\

$-7\dint\dfrac{\sec^2t}{\sec^6t}dt$\\

$=
-7\dint\cos^4t dt$\\

Gebruik:\\

$\cos^4t=
\left(\dfrac{1+\cos(2t)}2\right)^2$\\

$=
\dfrac14(1+2\cos(2t)+\cos^2(2t))$\\

$=
\dfrac14
\left(
1+2\cos(2t)+\dfrac{1+\cos(4t)}2
\right)$\\

$=
\dfrac38+\dfrac12\cos(2t)+\dfrac18\cos(4t)$\\

Dus:\\

$-7\dint\cos^4t dt$\\

$=
-7\dint
\left(
\dfrac38+\dfrac12\cos(2t)+\dfrac18\cos(4t)
\right)dt$\\

$=
-7
\left(
\dfrac38t+\dfrac14\sin(2t)+\dfrac1{32}\sin(4t)
\right)$\\

$=
-\dfrac{21}{8}t-\dfrac74\sin(2t)-\dfrac7{32}\sin(4t)$\\

Terug substitueren:\\

$t=\Bgtan(x+2)$\\

$\sin(2t)=\dfrac{2\tan t}{1+\tan^2t}$\\

$=
\dfrac{2(x+2)}{(x+2)^2+1}$\\

$=
\dfrac{2(x+2)}{x^2+4x+5}$\\

$\cos(2t)=\dfrac{1-\tan^2t}{1+\tan^2t}$\\

$=
\dfrac{1-(x+2)^2}{(x+2)^2+1}$\\

$\sin(4t)=2\sin(2t)\cos(2t)$\\

$=
\dfrac{4(x+2)(1-(x+2)^2)}
{((x+2)^2+1)^2}$\\

$=
\dfrac{4(x+2)(1-(x+2)^2)}
{(x^2+4x+5)^2}$\\

Dus:\\

$\dint\dfrac{3x-1}{(x^2+4x+5)^3}dx$\\

$=
-\dfrac{3}{4(x^2+4x+5)^2}
-\dfrac{21}{8}\Bgtan(x+2)$\\

$-\dfrac{7(x+2)}{2(x^2+4x+5)}
-\dfrac{7(x+2)(1-(x+2)^2)}
{8(x^2+4x+5)^2}
+C$\\

Vereenvoudigen geeft:\\

$\boxed{
-\dfrac{7x+17}{4(x^2+4x+5)^2}
-\dfrac{21(x+2)}{8(x^2+4x+5)}
-\dfrac{21}{8}\Bgtan(x+2)
+C
}$\\
