$\displaystyle\int (4x^2+2x+1)(2x-1)^{-3}dx$\\

$t=2x-1 \left(\Rightarrow dt=2dx \Rightarrow dx=\dfrac{dt}{2} \Rightarrow x=\dfrac{t+1}{2} \right)$\\

$\displaystyle\int \left(4\left(\dfrac{t+1}{2}\right)^2+2\left(\dfrac{t+1}{2}\right)+1\right)t^{-3}\dfrac{dt}{2}$\\

$=\displaystyle\int \left(\dfrac{4(t+1)^2}{4}+\dfrac{2(t+1)}{2}+1\right)t^{-3}\dfrac{dt}{2}$\\

$=\displaystyle\int \left((t+1)^2+(t+1)+1\right)t^{-3}\dfrac{dt}{2}$\\

$=\displaystyle\int \left(t^2+2t+1+t+1+1\right)t^{-3}\dfrac{dt}{2}$\\

$=\displaystyle\int \left(t^2+3t+3\right)t^{-3}\dfrac{dt}{2}$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int \left(t^2+3t+3\right)t^{-3}dt$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int \left(t^{-1}+3t^{-2}+3t^{-3}\right)dt$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2}\left(\ln|t|-3t^{-1}-\dfrac{3}{2}t^{-2}\right)+C$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2}\ln|t|-\dfrac{3}{2}t^{-1}-\dfrac{3}{4}t^{-2}+C$\\

$=\dfrac{1}{2}\ln|2x-1|-\dfrac{3}{2(2x-1)}-\dfrac{3}{4(2x-1)^2}+C$\\
