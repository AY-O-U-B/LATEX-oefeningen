$0.01 \cdot 10^x - 1 = 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow 0.01 \cdot 10^x = 1$ \\

$\Rightarrow 10^x = \dfrac{1}{0.01}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 10^x = 100$ \\

$\Rightarrow 10^x = 10^2$ \\

$\Rightarrow x = 2$
