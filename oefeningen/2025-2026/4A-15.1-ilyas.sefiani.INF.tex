$\displaystyle\int x^2e^xdx$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u=x^2 \\
dv=e^xdx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du=2xdx \\
v=e^x
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$=x^2e^x - 2\displaystyle\int xe^xdx$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u=x \\
dv=e^xdx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du=dx \\
v=e^x
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$=x^2e^x - 2\left(xe^x-\displaystyle\int e^xdx\right)$\\

$=x^2e^x - 2xe^x+2e^x+C$\\
