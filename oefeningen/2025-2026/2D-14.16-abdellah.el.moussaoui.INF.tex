$=\lim\limits_{x\to0} \dfrac{2\sin^2 2x}{\tan^2 3x}$\\

$=2 \cdot \lim\limits_{x\to0} \dfrac{\sin^2 2x}{\left(2x\right)^2} \cdot \dfrac{\left(3x\right)^2}{\tan^2 3x} \cdot \dfrac{\left(2x\right)^2}{\left(3x\right)^2}$\\

$= \dfrac{8}{9}$
