$f(x,y) = 
\left\{ 
\begin{array}{ll} 
\dfrac{x^3-y^3}{x^2+y^2} & \text{als } (x,y) \neq (0,0)\\ 
0 & \text{als } (x,y) = (0,0) 
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

Continuïteit:\\

Stel $x = r\cos\theta$ en $y = r \sin \theta$\\

$\dlim_{(x,y)\rightarrow (0,0)} f(x,y) = \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{r^3 \cos^3 \theta - r^3 \sin^3 \theta}{r^2 \cos^2 \theta + r^2 \sin^2 \theta}$\\

$= \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{r^3 (\cos^3 \theta - \sin^3 \theta)}{r^2} = \dlim_{r\rightarrow 0} r (\cos^3 \theta - \sin^3 \theta) = 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow f$ is continu in $(0,0)$ \\

Partiële afgeleiden:\\

$\left\{ 
\begin{array}{l} 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(0,0) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(\lambda,0) - f(0,0)}{\lambda} = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{\lambda^3}{\lambda^2}}{\lambda} = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\lambda}{\lambda} = 1 \\ 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(0,0) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(0,\lambda) - f(0,0)}{\lambda} = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{-\lambda^3}{\lambda^2}}{\lambda} = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\lambda}{\lambda} = -1 
\end{array} 
\right.$ \\

$\Rightarrow f$ is partieel afleidbaar in $(0,0)$\\

Afleidbaarheid:\\

Stel $\overline{h} = (h_1,h_2)$\\

$Df(\overline{0}, \overline{h}) = \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(\lambda h_1, \lambda h_2) - f(0,0)}{\lambda}$\\

$= \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\dfrac{\lambda^3 h_1^3 - \lambda^3 h_2^3}{\lambda^2 h_1^2 + \lambda^2 h_2^2}}{\lambda}$\\

$= \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\lambda^3 (h_1^3 - h_2^3)}{\lambda (\lambda^2 h_1^2 + \lambda^2 h_2^2)}$\\

$= \dlim_{\lambda \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\lambda^3 (h_1^3 - h_2^3)}{\lambda^3 (h_1^2 + h_2^2)} = \dfrac{h_1^3 - h_2^3}{h_1^2 + h_2^2}$\\

$\Rightarrow f$ is afleidbaar in $(0,0)$\\

Differentieerbaarheid:\\

Voor differentieerbaarheid in $(0,0)$ moet gelden: $Df(\overline{0}, \overline{h}) = \left(\begin{array}{c c} \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(0,0) & \dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(0,0) \end{array}\right) \left(\begin{array}{c} h_1 \\ h_2 \end{array}\right)$\\

$\left(\begin{array}{c c} 1 & -1 \end{array}\right) \left(\begin{array}{c} h_1 \\ h_2 \end{array}\right) = h_1 - h_2$\\

$Df(\overline{0}, \overline{h}) = \dfrac{h_1^3 - h_2^3}{h_1^2 + h_2^2} \neq h_1 - h_2$\\

$\Rightarrow f$ is niet differentieerbaar in $(0,0)$\\
