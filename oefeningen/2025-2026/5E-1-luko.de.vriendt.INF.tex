$ y(t) = y(-t) $ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 4t-t^3 = -4t+t^3$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 8t-2t^3=0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow 2t(4-t^2) =0$ \\

$t = 0 \text{ of } t = \pm2$ \\

$S = \displaystyle \int_{-2}^{2}\left(|4t-t^3|2t\right)dt$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int_{-2}^{2}\left(8t^2-2t^4\right)dt$ \\

$= \left[\dfrac{8t^3}{3} -\dfrac{2t^5}{5}\right]_{-2}^2$ \\

$=\left(\dfrac{64}{3} -\dfrac{64}{5}\right) - \left(\dfrac{-64}{3} +\dfrac{64}{5}\right)$ \\

$= 128\left(\dfrac{5}{15} - \dfrac{3}{15}\right)$ \\

$= \dfrac{256}{15}$ \\
