\normalsize

$f(x,y)= e^{(x-2y^2)^2}$\\
	
Eerste orde:\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot(2x-4y^2)$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot(-8xy+16y^3)$\\

Tweede orde:\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} = e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot(2x-4y^2)^2+2e^{(x-2y^2)^2}$\\

$= 2e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot\left(2(x-2y^2)^2+1\right)$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} = e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot(-8xy+16y^3)^2+e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot(-8xy+48y^2)$\\

$= 8e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot\left(8(-xy+2y^3)^2-x+6y^2\right)$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x\partial y} = e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot(2x+4y^2)^2+e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot(-8y)$\\

$= 4e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot\left((x-2y^2)^2-2y\right)$\\
	
$\dfrac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y\partial x} = e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot(-8xy+16y^3)^2 + e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot(-8y)$\\

$= 8e^{(x-2y^2)^2}\cdot\left(8(-xy-2y^3)^2-y\right)$\\
