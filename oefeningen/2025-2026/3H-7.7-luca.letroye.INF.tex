$r = \dfrac{-1}{\cos\theta + 4\sin\theta}$\\

$\Rightarrow r(\cos\theta +4\sin\theta) = -1$\\

$\Rightarrow r\cos\theta +4r\sin\theta = -1$\\

$\Rightarrow x+4y=-1$\\

$\Rightarrow y =\dfrac{-x}{4} - \dfrac{1}{4}$\\
