$\integral\dfrac{5}{9(x)^2 + 1}dx$ \\

$= 5\integral \dfrac{1}{(3x)^2 + 1}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $t = 3x \Rightarrow dt = 3dx$ \\

$= 5\integral\dfrac{1}{t^2 + 1}\cdot\dfrac{1}{3}dt$ \\

$= \dfrac{5}{3} \operatorname{Bgtan}{t} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{5}{3} \operatorname{Bgtan}{(3x)} + c$ \\
