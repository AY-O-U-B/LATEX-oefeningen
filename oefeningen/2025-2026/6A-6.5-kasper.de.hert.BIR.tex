$y' + \dfrac{y}{x} = 3x$\\

bepalen integrerende factor:\\

$\mu = e^{\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x}dx}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \mu = e^{\ln|x|}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \mu = x$\\

vermeningvuldiging van de vergelijking met de integrerende factor:\\

$xy' + y = 3x^2 \Rightarrow (xy)' = 3x^2$\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int (xy)' = \displaystyle\int 3x^2 dx$\\

$\Leftrightarrow xy = \dfrac{3x^3}{3} + C$\\

$\Leftrightarrow xy = x^3 + C$\\

$\Leftrightarrow y = x^2 + \dfrac{C}{x}$\\
