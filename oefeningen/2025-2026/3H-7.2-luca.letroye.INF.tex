$r = -3\tan \theta \sec \theta$\\

$\Rightarrow r = -3\dfrac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta} \cdot \dfrac{1}{\cos\theta}$\\

$\Rightarrow r = -3\dfrac{\sin\theta}{\cos^2 \theta}$\\

$\Rightarrow r\cos^2\theta = -3\sin\theta$\\

$\Rightarrow r^2\cos^2\theta = -3r\sin\theta$\\

$\Rightarrow x^2 = -3y$\\

$\Rightarrow y = \dfrac{-x^2}{3}$\\
