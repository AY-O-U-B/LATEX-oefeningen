Te bewijzen: $\coth^2 x - \text{csch}^2 x = 1$\\

Bewijs:\\

$\coth^2 x - \text{csch}^2 x = \left(\dfrac{\cosh x}{\sinh x}\right)^2 - \left(\dfrac{1}{\sinh x}\right)^2$\\

$= \dfrac{\cosh^2 x}{\sinh^2 x} - \dfrac{1}{\sinh^2 x}$\\

$= \dfrac{\cosh^2 x - 1}{\sinh^2 x}$\\

$\cosh^2 x - 1 = \sinh^2 x$\\

$\dfrac{\sinh^2 x}{\sinh^2 x} = 1$
