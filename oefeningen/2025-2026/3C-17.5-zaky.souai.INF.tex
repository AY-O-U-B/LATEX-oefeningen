$\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{x - \sin x}{x^3} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\
 
$\overset{(H)}{=} \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{1 - \cos x}{3x^2} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\overset{(H)}{=} \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{\sin x}{6x}$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{\sin x}{x} \cdot \dfrac{1}{6} = \dfrac{1}{6}$ \\

want $\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{\sin x}{x} = 1$
