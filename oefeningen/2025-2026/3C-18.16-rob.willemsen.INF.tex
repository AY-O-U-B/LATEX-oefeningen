$\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{1 - \cos x - \ln \sqrt{1+x^2}}{x^4} $ \\

$= \dfrac{1 - 1 - \ln \sqrt{1+0^2}}{0^4} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\overset{H}{=}\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\sin x - \left(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1+x^2}} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{1+x^2}} \cdot 2x\right)}{4x^3} $ \\

$=\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\sin x - \dfrac{x}{1+x^2}}{4x^3} $ \\

$= \dfrac{0 - \dfrac{0}{1+0}}{0} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\overset{H}{=}\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\cos x - \dfrac{(1+x^2) - x(2x)}{(1+x^2)^2}}{12x^2} $ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\cos x - \dfrac{1-x^2}{(1+x^2)^2}}{12x^2} $ \\

$= \dfrac{1 - \dfrac{1-0}{(1+0)^2}}{0} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\overset{H}{=}\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\sin x - \left(\dfrac{-2x(1+x^2)^2 - (1-x^2) \cdot 4x(1+x^2)}{(1+x^2)^4}\right)}{24x} $ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\sin x - \left(\dfrac{(1+x^2) \cdot \left[-2x(1+x^2) - 4x(1-x^2)\right]}{(1+x^2)^4}\right)}{24x} $ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\sin x - \left(\dfrac{-2x(1+x^2) - 4x(1-x^2)}{(1+x^2)^3}\right)}{24x} $ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\sin x - \left(\dfrac{2x^3 - 6x}{(1+x^2)^3}\right)}{24x} $ \\

$= \dfrac{- 0 - \dfrac{0 - 0}{1}}{0} = \dfrac{0}{0} $\\

$\overset{H}{=}\limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\cos x - \left(\dfrac{(6x^2 - 6)(1+x^2)^3 - (2x^3 - 6x) \cdot 3(1+x^2)^2 \cdot 2x}{(1+x^2)^6}\right)}{24} $ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\cos x - \left(\dfrac{(6x^2 - 6)(1+x^2) - (2x^3 - 6x) \cdot 6x}{(1+x^2)^4}\right)}{24} $ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\cos x - \left(\dfrac{6x^2 - 6 + 6x^4 - 6x^2 - 12x^4 + 36x^2}{(1+x^2)^4}\right)}{24} $ \\

$= \limit_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\cos x - \left(\dfrac{-6x^4 + 36x^2 - 6}{(1+x^2)^4}\right)}{24} $ \\

$= \dfrac{- 1 + 6}{24} = \dfrac{5}{24} $ \\
