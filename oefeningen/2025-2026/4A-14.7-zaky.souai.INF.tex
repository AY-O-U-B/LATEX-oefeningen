$\left\{
	\begin{array}{l}
		 \ln x = u \\
		 \dfrac{dx}{x^3} = dv\\
	\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
	\begin{array}{l}
		\dfrac{dx}{x} = du\\
	    -\dfrac{1}{2x^2} = v\\
	\end{array}
\right.$

$= -\dfrac{\ln(x)}{2x^2} - \displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{-2x^3}$\\

$= -\dfrac{\ln(x)}{2x^2} + \dfrac{1}{4x^2}$ + C\\
