$T_4(f,0)(x)$ voor $f(x) = \dfrac{1}{1+x}$ \\

$T_n(f,a)(x) = \displaystyle\sum_{k = 0}^{n} \dfrac{f^{(k)}(a)}{k!}(x - a)^k$ \\


$f'(x)=\left(\dfrac{1}{1+x}\right)' = \dfrac{1}{(1+x)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(0)=-1$\\

$f''(x)=\left(\dfrac{1}{1+x}\right)'' = \dfrac{-2}{(1+x)^3}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f''(0)=2$\\

$f'''(x)=\left(\dfrac{1}{1+x}\right)''' = \dfrac{6}{(1+x)^4}$\\

$\Rightarrow f'''(0)=-6$\\

$f^{iv}(x)=\left(\dfrac{1}{1+x}\right)''' = \dfrac{-24}{(1+x)^5}$\\

$\Rightarrow f^{iv}(0)=24$\\


$T_4(f,0)(x) = 1 -x + \dfrac{2x^2}{2!} - \dfrac{6x^3}{3!} + 
\dfrac{24x^4}{4!}$ \\

$T_4(f,0)(x) = 1 -x + x^2 - x^3 + x^4$ \\
