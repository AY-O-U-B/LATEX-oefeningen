$0,2^{\tfrac{1}{x-1}} = 0,04^{\tfrac{1}{x+1}}$\\

$\Rightarrow 0,2^{\tfrac{1}{x-1}} = 0,2^{\tfrac{2}{x+1}}$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{x-1} = \dfrac{2}{x+1}$\\

$\Rightarrow 2x-2 = x+1$\\

$\Rightarrow x = 3$\\
