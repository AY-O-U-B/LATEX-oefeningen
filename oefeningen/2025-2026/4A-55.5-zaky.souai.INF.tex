$\displaystyle \int \tan^{-3} x dx$\\

$= \displaystyle \int \cot^3 x dx$\\

$= \displaystyle \int \cot x \cdot \cot^2 x dx$\\

$= \displaystyle \int \cot x \left( \dfrac{1}{\sin^2 x} - 1 \right) dx$\\

$= \displaystyle \int \cot x \dfrac{1}{\sin^2 x} dx - \displaystyle \int \cot x dx$\\

$u = \cot x\Rightarrow du = -\dfrac{1}{\sin^2 x}dx$\\

$= - \displaystyle \int u du - \ln |\sin x|$\\

$= - \dfrac{u^2}{2} - \ln |\sin x| + C$\\

$= - \dfrac{\cot^2 x}{2} - \ln |\sin x| + C$
