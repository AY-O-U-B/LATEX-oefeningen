$ \displaystyle\int\limits_0^1 \dfrac{xe^x}{(x+1)^2}dx $\\

$ \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
u=xe^x\\
dv=\dfrac{dx}{(x+1)^2}
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
du=(x+1)e^xdx\\
v=-\dfrac{1}{x+1}
\end{array}
\right. $\\

$ \displaystyle\int\limits_0^1 \dfrac{xe^x}{(x+1)^2}dx = \left[-\dfrac{xe^x}{x+1}\right]_0^1 - \displaystyle\int\limits_0^1 \left(-\dfrac{1}{x+1}\right)(x+1)e^xdx $\\

$ =\left[-\dfrac{xe^x}{x+1}\right]_0^1+\displaystyle\int\limits_0^1 e^xdx $\\

$ =\left(-\dfrac{e}{2}-0\right)+\left[e^x\right]_0^1 $\\

$ =-\dfrac{e}{2}+(e-1) $\\

$ =\dfrac{e}{2}-1 $\\
