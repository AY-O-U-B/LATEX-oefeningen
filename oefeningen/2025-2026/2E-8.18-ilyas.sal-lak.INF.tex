$2\left(3^x + 2^{x-1}\right) = 3\left(2^x - 3^{x-1}\right)$ \\

$\Rightarrow 2 \cdot 3^x + 2 \cdot 2^{x-1} = 3 \cdot 2^x - 3 \cdot 3^{x-1}$ \\

$\Rightarrow 2 \cdot 3^x + 2^x = 3 \cdot 2^x - 3^x$ \\

$\Rightarrow 2 \cdot 3^x + 3^x = 3 \cdot 2^x - 2^x$ \\

$\Rightarrow 3 \cdot 3^x = 2 \cdot 2^x$ \\

$\Rightarrow 3^{x+1} = 2^{x+1}$ \\

$\Rightarrow x + 1 = 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow x = -1$
