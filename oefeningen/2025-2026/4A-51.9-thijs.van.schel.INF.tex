$\dint \cos^5xdx = \dint \cos^4x\cos xdx$\\

$\dint (\cos^2x)^2\cos xdx = \dint (1-\sin^2x)^2\cos xdx$\\

$t = \sin x \Rightarrow dt = \cos x dx$\\

$\dint (1-t^2)^2dt = \dint (t^4-2t^2+1)dt$\\

$= \dint t^4dt -2 \dint t^2dt+\dint t^0dt$\\

$= \dfrac{t^5}{5}-\dfrac{2t^3}{3}+t + C$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{5}\sin^5 x - \dfrac{2}{3}\sin^3x+\sin x + C$
