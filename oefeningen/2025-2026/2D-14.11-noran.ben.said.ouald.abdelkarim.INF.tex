$ \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{\sin^2 2x}{x^2}$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \left( \dfrac{\sin 2x}{x} \right)^2$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \left( \dfrac{\sin 2x}{2x} \cdot 2 \right)^2$\\

$ = (1 \cdot 2)^2 = 4$
