$I = \displaystyle\dint_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \cos^3xdx$\\

Eerst vinden we de onbepaalde integraal\\

$\displaystyle\int \cos x\cos^2xdx = \displaystyle\int \cos x(1-\sin^2x)dx$\\

Stel $u = \sin x$\\

$du = \cos xdx$\\

$\displaystyle\int (1-u^2)du$\\

$= u\left(1-\dfrac{u^2}{3}\right) + C$\\

Pas grenzen aan en vul in\\

$I = \left[u\left(1-\dfrac{u^2}{3}\right)\right]_{0}^{1}$\\

$I = \dfrac{2}{3}$
