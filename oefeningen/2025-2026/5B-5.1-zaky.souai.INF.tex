$ \displaystyle\int \limits_{0}^{1} x^{-\tfrac{2}{3}} dx $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{a \to 0^+} \displaystyle \int \limits_{a}^{1} x^{-\tfrac{2}{3}} dx $\\

$ = \lim\limits_{a \to 0^+}\left[3x^{\tfrac{1}{3}}\right]_{a}^{1} $\\

$ =\lim\limits_{a \to 0^+}\left(3 - 3a^{\tfrac{1}{3}}\right) $\\

$ = 3 - 3 \cdot 0 = 3$\\
