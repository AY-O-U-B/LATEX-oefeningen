$\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{1}{\ln n}$\\

criterum 5:\\

$\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{1}{\ln n}\gtrsim\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{1}{n}$\\

$\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{1}{n} \rightarrow$ divergent\\

dus$\displaystyle\sum\limits_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{1}{\ln n}$ is divergent\\
