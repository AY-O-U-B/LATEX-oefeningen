$f:\Omega \subseteq \mathbb{R}^3 \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^3 : (x,y,z) \rightarrow \left(x + y + z, xy + yz + zx, x^2y + y^2z + z^2x\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow$ Voor $x$:\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_1}{\partial x} = 1$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_2}{\partial x} = y + z$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_3}{\partial x} = 2xy + z^2$\\

$\Rightarrow$ Voor $y$:\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_1}{\partial y} = 1$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_2}{\partial y} = x + z$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_3}{\partial y} = x^2 + 2yz$\\

$\Rightarrow$ Voor $z$:\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_1}{\partial z} = 1$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_2}{\partial z} = y + x$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f_3}{\partial z} = y^2 + 2zx$\\

$Df(x,y,z)= \left(\begin{array}{ccc}
1 & 1 & 1\\
y + z & x + z & y + x\\
2xy + z^2 & x^2 + 2yz & y^2 + 2zx\\
\end{array} \right)$\\
