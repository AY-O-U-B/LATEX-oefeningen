$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{5x-2}{x^2-4}\,dx$\\

$\displaystyle x^2-4=(x-2)(x+2)$\\

$\displaystyle \dfrac{5x-2}{x^2-4}=\dfrac{5x-2}{(x-2)(x+2)}=\dfrac{A}{x-2}+\dfrac{B}{x+2}$\\

$\displaystyle A=\left.\dfrac{5x-2}{x+2}\right|_{x=2}=\dfrac{8}{4}=2$\\

$\displaystyle B=\left.\dfrac{5x-2}{x-2}\right|_{x=-2}=\dfrac{-12}{-4}=3$\\

$\displaystyle \dfrac{5x-2}{x^2-4}=\dfrac{2}{x-2}+\dfrac{3}{x+2}$\\

$\displaystyle \int \dfrac{5x-2}{x^2-4}\,dx=2\int\dfrac{dx}{x-2}+3\int\dfrac{dx}{x+2}$\\

$\displaystyle =2\ln|x-2|+3\ln|x+2|+c$
