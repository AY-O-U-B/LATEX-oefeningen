$\displaystyle \int \Bgsin x dx$ \\

Na partiele integratie:

$= x \Bgsin x - \displaystyle \int \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} dx$ \\

Na substitutie:

$= x \Bgsin x + \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle \int \dfrac{du}{\sqrt{u}}$ \\

$= x \Bgsin x + \sqrt{1 - x^2} + C$ \\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle \int_0^{1} \Bgsin x  dx = \left[x \Bgsin x + \sqrt{1 - x^2} \right]_0^1$ \\

$= \dfrac{\pi}{2} - 1$ \\
