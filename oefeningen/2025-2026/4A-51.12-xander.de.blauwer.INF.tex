$\displaystyle\int \sin^3(x) \cos^2(x)dx = - \displaystyle\int \cos^2(x)(1-\cos^2(x))d(\cos(x)) $ \\

$ = -\displaystyle\int (\cos^2(x) - \cos^4(x))d(\cos(x)) $ \\

Stel $t = \cos(x)$ \\

$ = -\displaystyle\int (t^2 - t^4)dt$ \\

$ = -\dfrac{\cos^3(x)}{3} + \dfrac{\cos^5(x)}{5} + c $ \\
