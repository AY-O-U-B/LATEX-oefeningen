Los op in $\mathbb{C}$:\\

$z^4 + 4 = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow z^4 + 4z^2 + 4 - 4z^2 = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow (z^2+2)^2 - (2z)^2 = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow (z^2 - 2z + 2)(z^2 + 2z + 2) = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow z^2 - 2z + 2 = 0 \quad \mathrm{of} \quad z^2 + 2z + 2 = 0$ \\

$z^2 - 2z + 2 = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow z = \dfrac{2 \pm \sqrt{-4}}{2}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow z = \dfrac{2 \pm 2i}{2}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow z = 1 \pm i$ \\

$z^2 + 2z + 2 = 0$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow z = \dfrac{-2 \pm \sqrt{-4}}{2}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow z = \dfrac{-2 \pm 2i}{2}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow z = -1 \pm i$ \\
