$ \sinh(x-y)=\sinh x \cosh y-\cosh x \sinh y $\\

$ =\left(\dfrac{e^x-e^{-x}}{2}\right)\left(\dfrac{e^y+e^{-y}}{2}\right)-\left(\dfrac{e^x+e^{-x}}{2}\right)\left(\dfrac{e^y-e^{-y}}{2}\right) $\\

$ =\dfrac{(e^x-e^{-x})(e^y+e^{-y})}{4}-\dfrac{(e^x+e^{-x})(e^y-e^{-y})}{4} $\\

$ =\dfrac{(e^{x+y}+e^{x-y}-e^{-x+y}-e^{-x-y})-(e^{x+y}-e^{x-y}+e^{-x+y}-e^{-x-y})}{4} $\\

$ =\dfrac{2e^{x-y}-2e^{-x+y}}{4} $\\

$ =\dfrac{e^{x-y}-e^{-x+y}}{2} $\\

$ =\dfrac{e^{x-y}-e^{-(x-y)}}{2} $\\

$ =\sinh(x-y) $\\
