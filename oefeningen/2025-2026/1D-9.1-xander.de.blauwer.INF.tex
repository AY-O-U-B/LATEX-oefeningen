Gegeven: $ z = 1+\sqrt{2}$ \\

$z^2 = (1+\sqrt{2})^2 = 3 + 2\sqrt{2}$ \\

$z^3 = z^2 \cdot z = (3 + 2\sqrt{2})(1+\sqrt{2}) = 7 + 5\sqrt{2}$ \\

$z^4 = (z^2)^2 = (3 + 2\sqrt{2})^2 = 17 + 12\sqrt{2}$ \\


\begin{eqnarray*}
z^4 - z^3 - 5z^2 + 4z + 2
&=& (17 + 12\sqrt{2}) - (7 + 5\sqrt{2}) - 5(3 + 2\sqrt{2}) + 4(1+\sqrt{2}) + 2 \\
&=& (17 - 7 - 15 + 4 + 2) + (12 - 5 - 10 + 4)\sqrt{2} \\
&=& 1 + \sqrt{2} \\
\end{eqnarray*}
