$f(x) = \dfrac{x^2-1}{x}$\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{2x^2-(x^2-1)}{x^2} = \dfrac{x^2+1}{x^2}$\\

$f''(x) = \dfrac{2x^3-2x(x^2+1)}{x^4} = \dfrac{-2x}{x^4} = \dfrac{-2}{x^3}$\\

De teller is constant, dus $f''(x) = 0$ heeft geen oplossingen\\


Verticale asymptoot: $x^3 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 0$\\
