$\dint \dfrac{\fifthroot{x^3} + \sixthroot{x^5}}{\fourthroot{x}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{\fifthroot{x^3}}{\fourthroot{x}} dx + \dint \dfrac{\sixthroot{x^5}}{\fourthroot{x}} dx$ \\

$= \dint x^{\tfrac{3}{5}} x^{\tfrac{-1}{4}} dx +\dint x^{\tfrac{5}{6}} x^{\tfrac{-1}{4}} dx$ \\

$= \dint x^{\tfrac{12}{20}} x^{\tfrac{-5}{20}} dx +\dint x^{\tfrac{10}{12}} x^{\tfrac{-3}{12}} dx$ \\

$= \dint x^{\tfrac{7}{20}} dx + \dint x^{\tfrac{7}{12}} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{27}{20}}}{\dfrac{27}{20}} + \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{19}{12}}}{\dfrac{19}{12}} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{20 \twentiethroot{x^{27}}}{27} + \dfrac{12 \twelfthroot{x^{19}}}{19} +c$ \\

$= \dfrac{20x \twentiethroot{x^{7}}}{27} + \dfrac{12x \twelfthroot{x^{7}}}{19} +c$ \\
