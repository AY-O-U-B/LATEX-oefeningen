$f(x,y) = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$ \\

$a(1,0)$ en $h\left(\dfrac{2\sqrt{5}}{5}, \dfrac{\sqrt{5}}{5}\right)$. \\

$
\left\{ 
\begin{array}{l} 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x,y) = \dfrac{2x}{2\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}} = \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}} \\ 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(x,y) = \dfrac{2y}{2\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}} = \dfrac{y}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}} 
\end{array} 
\right.
\Rightarrow
\left\{ 
\begin{array}{l} 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}(1,0) = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1^2 + 0^2}} = 1 \\ 
\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}(1,0) = \dfrac{0}{\sqrt{1^2 + 0^2}} = 0 
\end{array} 
\right.
$ \\

$\Rightarrow Df(a,h) = \left(\begin{array}{c c} 1 & 0 \end{array}\right) \left(\begin{array}{c} \dfrac{2\sqrt{5}}{5} \\ \dfrac{\sqrt{5}}{5} \end{array}\right) = \dfrac{2\sqrt{5}}{5}$
