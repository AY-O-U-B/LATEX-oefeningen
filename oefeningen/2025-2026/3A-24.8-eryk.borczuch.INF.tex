$f(x) = \dfrac{1}{3} \cos^3 x -\cos x$\\

$ f'(x) = \cos ^2 x \cdot - \cos x + \sin x$\\

$= (1- \sin ^2 x)- \sin x + \sin x$\\

$= - \sin x + \sin ^3 x+\sin x$\\

$= \sin ^3 x$\\
