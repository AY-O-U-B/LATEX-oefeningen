$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{x\left( 4-x \right) }}$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{- x^2 + 4x}} $\\

$\Rightarrow - x^2 + 4x = - \left( x^2 -4x +2^2 \right) + 4 = 4 - \left(x-2 \right)^2 $\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\sqrt{4 - \left(x -2 \right)^2}}$\\

Stel $t = x-2 \Rightarrow dt = dx  $\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{\sqrt{4 - t^2}} = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{\sqrt{2^2 - t^2}} $\\

$= \Bgsin \dfrac{t}{2} + c = \Bgsin \dfrac{x-2}{2} + c $\\
