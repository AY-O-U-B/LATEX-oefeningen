Opgave: Los op in $\mathbb{C}$:\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
   \dfrac{1}{\cos x + i \sin x} &=& \dfrac{\cos x - i\sin x} {(\cos x + i \sin x)(\cos x - i\sin x)}\\
    &=& \dfrac{\cos x - i\sin x}{(\cos x)^2 + (\sin x)^2}\\
    &=& \cos x - i\sin x\\
\end{eqnarray*}
