$r=4+3\cos\theta \Rightarrow S = \dfrac{1}{2}\ldint(4+3\cos\theta)^2$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\ldint16d\theta + \dfrac{1}{2}\ldint24\cos\theta d\theta + \dfrac{1}{2}\ldint9\cos^2\theta d\theta$\\

$=16\pi + 12\left[\sin\theta\right]_0^{2\pi} + \dfrac{9}{2}\ldint \dfrac{1+\cos(2\theta)}{2}d\theta$\\

$=16\pi + 0 + \dfrac{9}{4}\ldint d\theta + \dfrac{9}{4}\ldint\cos(2\theta) d\theta$\\

$=16\pi + \dfrac{18\pi}{4}+\dfrac{9}{4}\left[\sin(2\theta)\right]_0^{2\pi}$\\

$=16\pi+\dfrac{18\pi}{4} = \dfrac{64+18}{4}\pi = \dfrac{41}{2}\pi$\\

Tekening:\\

$\theta = 0 \Rightarrow r = 7$\\

$\theta = \dfrac{\pi}{2} \Rightarrow r = 4$\\

$\theta = \pi \Rightarrow r = 1$\\

$\theta = \dfrac{3\pi}{2} \Rightarrow r = 4$\\
