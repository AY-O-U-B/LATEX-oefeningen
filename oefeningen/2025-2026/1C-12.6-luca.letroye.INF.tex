$z_1 = -1$\\

$r_1 = \sqrt{(-1)^2 + 0^2} = 1$\\

$ \theta_1 = \pi$\\

$z_2 = 2+2i$\\

$r_2 = \sqrt{2^2 + 2^2} = \sqrt{8} = 2\sqrt{2}$\\

$ \theta_2 = \dfrac{\pi}{4}$\\

$\Rightarrow r = \dfrac{r_1}{r_2} = \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{2}} = \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4}$\\

$\Rightarrow \theta = \theta_1 - \theta_2 = \pi - \dfrac{\pi}{4} = \dfrac{3\pi}{4}$\\

$\Rightarrow z = \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{4}\left(\cos\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4}\right)+ i\sin\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4}\right)\right)$
