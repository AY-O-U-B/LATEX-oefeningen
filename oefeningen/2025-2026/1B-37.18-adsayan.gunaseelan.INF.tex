Opgave: Vierkantswortel ($\mathbb{C}$) van $20i +21$ nemen.\

$20i +21 = 21 +20i$\\

$\Rightarrow 21 +20i = (x+yi)^2 = x^2 - y^2 + 2xyi$\\

$ \Rightarrow\left \{
\begin{array}{l} 
x^2 - y^2=21 \\
2xy=20 \\
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$ \Leftrightarrow\left \{
\begin{array}{l} 
x^2 - y^2=21 \\
x^2(-y)^2=-100\\
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$ \Rightarrow\lambda^2 - 21\lambda - 100 = 0 $\\

$ \Rightarrow D = 441-4\cdot1\cdot(-100) = 841 \Rightarrow \sqrt{D}=29 $ \\

$ \Rightarrow\left \{
\begin{array}{l} 
\lambda_1 = \dfrac{21 + 29}{2}=25\\
\lambda_2 = \dfrac{21 - 29}{2}=-4\\
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$ \Rightarrow\left \{
\begin{array}{l} 
x^2 = 25 \overset{b>0}\Leftrightarrow x= 5\\
y^2 = 4 \Leftrightarrow y = 2\\
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$ \Rightarrow\left \{
\begin{array}{l} 
w_1 = 5 + 2i \\
w_2 = -5 - 2i\\
\end{array}
\right .$\\
