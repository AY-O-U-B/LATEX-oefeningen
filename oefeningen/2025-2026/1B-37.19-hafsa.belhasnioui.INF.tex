$-60i + 11 = (x + yi)^2$\\

$-60i + 11 = x^2 + 2xyi - y^2$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
x^2 - y^2 = 11 \\
2xy = -60 \\ % xy < 0
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
x^2 - y^2 = 11 \\
x^2y^2 = (-30)^2 \\
\end{array}
\right.
\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
x^2 - y^2 = 11 \\
x^2(-y^2) = -900 \\
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$\lambda^2 - 11\lambda - 900 = 0$ \\

$D = (-11)^2 - 4 \cdot (-900) = 3721$ \\

$\lambda_{1} = \dfrac{11 + \sqrt{3721}}{2} = 36 = x^2 \Rightarrow x = \pm 6$\\
  
$\lambda_{1} = \dfrac{11 - \sqrt{3721}}{2} = -25 = -y^2 \Rightarrow y = \pm 5$\\

$x + yi \in \{6 - 5i, -6+5i \}$ \\
