$ \lim\limits_{x\rightarrow \tfrac{1}{4}} \dfrac{4x^3+13x^2+\dfrac{5}{2}x-\dfrac{3}{2}}{3x^2+\dfrac{13}{4}x-1}$\\

$ = \dfrac{0}{0}$\\

Teller ontbinden in factoren\\

\begin{tabular}{c|cccccc}
	& $4$ 	& $13$ 	& $\dfrac{5}{2}$ 	& $\dfrac{-3}{2}$ 	 	\\
$\dfrac{1}{4}$ 	& 		& $1$ 	& $\dfrac{14}{4}$ 	& $\dfrac{3}{2}$  			\\ \hline
	& $4$   & $14$ 	& $\dfrac{24}{4}$ 	& \multicolumn{1}{|c}{$0$}  \\
\end{tabular}\\

Teller = $(4x-1)\left(x^2+\dfrac{14}{4}+\dfrac{3}{2}\right)$\\

Noemer ontbinden in factoren\\

\begin{tabular}{c|ccccc}
	& $3$ 	& $\dfrac{13}{4}$ 	& $-1$ 	\\
$\dfrac{1}{4}$ 	& 		& $\dfrac{3}{4}$ 	& $1$ \\ \hline
	& $3$   & $4$ 	& \multicolumn{1}{|c}{$0$}  \\
\end{tabular}\\

Noemer = $(4x-1)\left(\dfrac{3}{4}x+1\right)$\\

$ \lim\limits_{x\rightarrow \tfrac{1}{4}} \dfrac{(4x-1)\left(x^2+\dfrac{14}{4}+\dfrac{3}{2}\right)}{(4x-1)\left(\dfrac{3}{4}x+1\right)}$\\

$= \dfrac{39}{19}$
