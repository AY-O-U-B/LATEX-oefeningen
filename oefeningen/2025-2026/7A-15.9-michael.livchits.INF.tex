$f(x,y) = 3x^2+12x+4y^3-6y^2+5$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 6x+12$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 12y(y-1)$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} = 0 \Rightarrow x = -2$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y} = 0\Rightarrow y = 0$ en $y = 1$\\

In de punten $(-2,0)$ en $(-2,1)$ is er een horizontaal raakvlak.\\
