Volgens de reststelling van Horner: \\

$A(2) = 5$ \\

$A(3) = 7$ \\

$R(z) = az + b$ \\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
2a + b = 5 \\
3a + b = 7
\end{array}
\right.$ \\

$(3a + b) - (2a + b) = 7 - 5$ \\

$a = 2$ \\

$2(2) + b = 5 \Rightarrow b = 1$ \\

\textbf{Antwoord:} $R(z) = 2z + 1$ \\
