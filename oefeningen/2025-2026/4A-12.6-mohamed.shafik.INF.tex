Bereken $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{4}{9^x} dx$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int \dfrac{4}{9^x} dx &=& 4 \displaystyle\int 9^{-x} dx\\
\end{eqnarray*}

Substitueer $t = -x$, dan is $dt = -dx$\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
4 \displaystyle\int 9^{-x} dx &=& -4 \displaystyle\int 9^t dt\\
&=& -4 \cdot \dfrac{9^t}{\ln 9} + c\\
&=& \dfrac{-4 \cdot 9^{-x}}{\ln 9} + c\\
&=& \dfrac{-4}{9^x \cdot \ln 9} + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
