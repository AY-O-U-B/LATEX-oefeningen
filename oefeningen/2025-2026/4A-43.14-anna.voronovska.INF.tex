$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{8x^2}{x^4+2x^3-2x-1}dx$\\

Noemer ontbinden in factoren:\\

$x=1$: $1+2-2-1=0$ en $x=-1$: $1-2+2-1=0$\\

$x^4+2x^3-2x-1 = (x-1)(x^3+3x^2+3x+1) = (x-1)(x+1)^3$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{8x^2}{(x-1)(x+1)^3} = \dfrac{A}{x-1}+\dfrac{B}{x+1}+\dfrac{C}{(x+1)^2}+\dfrac{D}{(x+1)^3}$\\

$A = \left.\dfrac{8x^2}{(x+1)^3}\right|_{x=1} = \dfrac{8}{8} = 1$\\

$D = \left.\dfrac{8x^2}{x-1}\right|_{x=-1} = \dfrac{8}{-2} = -4$\\

Coëfficiënten voor $B$ en $C$:\\

$\dfrac{8x^2}{(x-1)(x+1)^3} - \dfrac{1}{x-1} 
= \dfrac{8x^2-(x+1)^3}{(x-1)(x+1)^3} 
= \dfrac{-x^3+5x^2-3x-1}{(x-1)(x+1)^3} 
= \dfrac{-x^2+4x+1}{(x+1)^3}$\\

$\dfrac{-x^2+4x+1}{(x+1)^3} - \dfrac{-4}{(x+1)^3} 
= \dfrac{-x^2+4x+5}{(x+1)^3} 
= \dfrac{-x+5}{(x+1)^2}$\\

$C = \left.\dfrac{-x+5}{(x+1)^2}\cdot(x+1)^2\right|_{x=-1} = \left.(-x+5)\right|_{x=-1} = 6$\\

$\dfrac{-x+5}{(x+1)^2} - \dfrac{6}{(x+1)^2} 
= \dfrac{-x-1}{(x+1)^2} 
= \dfrac{-1}{x+1}$\\

$B = -1$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x-1}dx - \displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{x+1}dx + 6\displaystyle\int(x+1)^{-2}dx - 4\displaystyle\int(x+1)^{-3}dx$\\

$= \ln|x-1| - \ln|x+1| - \dfrac{6}{x+1} + \dfrac{2}{(x+1)^2} + c$\\
