$f(x,y)=2xye^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x}=2ye^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}+2xy\left(\dfrac{-8x}{8}\right)e^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}=2ye^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}-2x^2ye^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}=2ye^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}(1-x^2)$\\

$\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}=2xe^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}+2xy\left(\dfrac{-2y}{8}\right)e^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}=2xe^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}-\dfrac{1}{2}xy^2e^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}=2xe^{\dfrac{-4x^2+y^2}{8}}\left(1-\dfrac{y^2}{4}\right)$\\

$\Rightarrow y(1-x^2)=0 \rightarrow y=0, x=\pm1$\\

$\Rightarrow x\left(1-\dfrac{y^2}{4}\right)=0$\\

Als $ y=0 : x\left(1-\dfrac{0^2}{4}\right)=0$\\

$\Rightarrow x=0$\\

Als $ x=\pm1 : 1\left(1-\dfrac{y^2}{4}\right)=0$\\

$\Rightarrow 1-\dfrac{y^2}{4}=0$\\

$\Rightarrow -\dfrac{y^2}{4}=-1$\\

$\Rightarrow y^2=4$\\

$\Rightarrow y=\pm2$\\

$\Rightarrow (0,0),(1,2),(-1,2),(1,-2),(-1,-2)$\\
