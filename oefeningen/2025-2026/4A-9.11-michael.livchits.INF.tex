$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{1+\cos x}$\\

Maak gebruik van halvering van de hoek formule (tabel 7 in de formularium):\\

$\cos^2\alpha = \dfrac{1+\cos2\alpha}{2}$\\

$2\cos^2\alpha - 1 = \cos2\alpha$ met $2\alpha = x$\\

$2\cos^2\dfrac{x}{2} - 1 = \cos x$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{1+2\cos^2\dfrac{x}{2} - 1} = \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{\cos^2\dfrac{x}{2}}$\\

$\left\{
\begin{array}{l}
	u = \dfrac{x}{2}\\
	2du =  dx
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{du}{\cos^2u}$\\

Via de stamintegraal weten wij dan:\\

$= \tan u + C = \tan\dfrac{x}{2} + C$\\
