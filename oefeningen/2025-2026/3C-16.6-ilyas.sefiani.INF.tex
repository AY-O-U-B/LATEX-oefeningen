$\llim_{x \rightarrow -4} \dfrac{\sqrt{x+4}}{2x^2+5x-12} = \dfrac{0}{0}$\\

$\overset{H}{=}\llim_{x \rightarrow -4} \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x+4}}}{4x+5}$\\

$= \llim_{x \rightarrow -4} \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x+4}(4x+5)} = \dfrac{1}{0} = \infty$\\
