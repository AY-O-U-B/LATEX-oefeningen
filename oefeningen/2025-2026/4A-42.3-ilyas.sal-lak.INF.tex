$\dint \dfrac{dx}{x^2 + 3x + 2}$ \\

$x^2 + 3x + 2 = (x+1)(x+2)$ \\

$\dfrac{1}{(x+1)(x+2)} = \dfrac{A}{x+1} + \dfrac{B}{x+2}$ \\

$A = \left.\dfrac{1}{x+2}\right|_{x=-1} = 1$ \quad $B = \left.\dfrac{1}{x+1}\right|_{x=-2} = -1$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dint \dfrac{dx}{x^2+3x+2} = \ln|x+1| - \ln|x+2| + c = \ln\left|\dfrac{x+1}{x+2}\right| + c$
