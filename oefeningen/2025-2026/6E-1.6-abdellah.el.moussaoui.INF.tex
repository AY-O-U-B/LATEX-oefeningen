$T_4\left(f,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)(x)$ voor $f(x) = \sin{x}$ \\

$T_n(f,a)(x) = \displaystyle\sum_{k = 0}^{n} \dfrac{f^{(k)}(a)}{k!}(x - a)^k$ \\

$T_4\left(f,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)(x) = \displaystyle\sum_{k = 0}^{4} \dfrac{f^{(k)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{k!}\left(x - \dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^k$ \\

$f^{(1)}(x)= \cos x \Rightarrow f^{(1)}(\dfrac{\pi}{2})=0$\\

$f^{(2)}(x)= -\sin x \Rightarrow f^{(2)}(\dfrac{\pi}{2})=-1$\\

$f^{(3)}(x)= -\cos x \Rightarrow f^{(3)}(\dfrac{\pi}{2})=0$\\

$f^{(4)}(x)= \sin x \Rightarrow f^{(4)}(\dfrac{\pi}{2})=1$\\


$T_6\left(f,\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)(x) = \dfrac{f^0\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{0!}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^0 + \dfrac{f^{(1)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{1!}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^1  + \dfrac{f^{(2)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{2!}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^2 + \dfrac{f^{(3)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{3!}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^3  + \dfrac{f^{(4)}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)}{4!}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^4$ \\

$= 1 - \dfrac{1}{2}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^2+\dfrac{1}{24}\left(x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right)^4 $ \\
