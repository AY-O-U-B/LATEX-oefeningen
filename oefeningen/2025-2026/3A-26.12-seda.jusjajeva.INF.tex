$f(x) = \sqrt{1 - x^2}\operatorname{Bgsin}(x)$ \\

$f'(x) = \sqrt{1 - x^2}\cdot\dfrac{d}{dx}(\operatorname{Bgsin}(x)) + \operatorname{Bgsin}(x)\cdot\dfrac{d}{dx}(\sqrt{1 - x^2})$ \\

$= \sqrt{1 - x^2}\cdot\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} + \operatorname{Bgsin}(x)\cdot\dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\cdot\dfrac{d}{dx}(-x^2)$ \\

$= 1 - \operatorname{Bgsin}(x)\cdot\dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\cdot2x$ \\

$= 1 - \dfrac{x\operatorname{Bgsin}(x){\sqrt{1 - x^2}}$ \\
