$\limit_{x \rightarrow -\tfrac{\pi}{3}} \dfrac{2\sin x + \sqrt{3}}{9x^2 - \pi^2}$ \\  

$= \dfrac{2\sin(-\tfrac{\pi}{3}) + \sqrt{3}}{9(-\tfrac{\pi}{3})^2 - \pi^2}$ \\ 

$= \dfrac{-\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{3}}{\pi^2 - \pi^2} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\

$\overset{H}{=}\limit_{x \rightarrow -\tfrac{\pi}{3}} \dfrac{2\cos x}{18x}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2\cos(-\tfrac{\pi}{3})}{18(-\tfrac{\pi}{3})}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2\cdot \tfrac{1}{2}}{-6\pi}$ \\

$= -\dfrac{1}{6\pi}$ \\
