$f(x)=\tan x$\\

We berekenen de afgeleiden in $x=0$.\\

$f'(x)=\sec^2x$\\

$f'(0)=1$\\

$f''(x)=2\sec^2x\tan x$\\

$f''(0)=0$\\

$f^{(3)}(x)=2\sec^2x(\sec^2x+2\tan^2x)$\\

$f^{(3)}(0)=2$\\

$f^{(4)}(0)=0$\\

$f^{(5)}(0)=16$\\

$f^{(6)}(0)=0$\\

$f^{(7)}(0)=272$\\

Taylorformule:\\

$T_7(f,0)(x)=f(0)+\dfrac{f'(0)}{1!}x+\dfrac{f''(0)}{2!}x^2+\dfrac{f^{(3)}(0)}{3!}x^3+\dfrac{f^{(4)}(0)}{4!}x^4+\dfrac{f^{(5)}(0)}{5!}x^5+\dfrac{f^{(6)}(0)}{6!}x^6+\dfrac{f^{(7)}(0)}{7!}x^7$\\\\

$=0+\dfrac11x+\dfrac02x^2+\dfrac26x^3+\dfrac0{24}x^4+\dfrac{16}{120}x^5+\dfrac0{720}x^6+\dfrac{272}{5040}x^7$\\

$=x+\dfrac13x^3+\dfrac2{15}x^5+\dfrac{17}{315}x^7$
