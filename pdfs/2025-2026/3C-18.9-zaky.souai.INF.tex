$\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{e^x - 1}{\ln 1-x} = \dfrac{0}{0}$ \\
 
$\overset{(H)}{=} \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{e^x}{\dfrac{-1}{1-x}}$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{e^x \cdot (1-x)}{-1} = -1$ \\
