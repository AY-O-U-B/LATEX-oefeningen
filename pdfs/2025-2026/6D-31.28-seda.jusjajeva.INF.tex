$\displaystyle\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\dfrac{n + 1}{n(n + 2)}$ is een positieve reeks. \\

$\displaystyle\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n}$ is divergent. \\

Verhoudingscriterium: \\

$\limit_{n \to +\infty} \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{n}}{\dfrac{n + 1}{n(n + 2)}}$ \\ 

$= \limit_{n \to +\infty} \dfrac{n(n + 2)}{n(n + 1)}$ \\

$= \limit_{n \to +\infty} \dfrac{n + 2}{n + 1} \Rightarrow$ onbepaald \\

$\overset{H} = \lim\limits_{n \to +\infty} \dfrac{1}{1} = 1$ \\

$0 < 1 \leq 1 < +\infty \Rightarrow  \displaystyle\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\dfrac{n + 1}{n(n + 2)}$ is ook divergent. \\
