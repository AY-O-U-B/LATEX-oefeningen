$I = \displaystyle\dint_{0}^{\tfrac{\pi}{2}} \sin 2xdx$\\

Stel $u = 2x$\\

$du = 2dx$\\

Eerst vinden we de onbepaalde integraal\\

$\dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int \sin udu$\\

$= -\dfrac{1}{2}\cos u + C$\\

Pas grenzen aan en vul in\\

$I = \left[-\dfrac{1}{2}\cos u\right]_{0}^\pi$\\

$I = -\dfrac{\cos \pi}{2}+\dfrac{\cos 0}{2} = 1$\\
