Los op in $\mathbb{C}$:\\

$z^3 - (3+2i)z^2 + (1+4i)z + 1 - 2i = 0$\\

Probeer $z = 1$:\\

$1 - (3+2i) + (1+4i) + 1 - 2i$\\

$= 1 -3 -2i +1 +4i +1 -2i$\\

$= 0$\\

Dus $(z-1)$ is een factor.\\

Deel door $(z-1)$:\\

$z^3 - (3+2i)z^2 + (1+4i)z + 1 - 2i$\\

$= (z-1)(z^2 -(2+2i)z -(1-2i))$\\

Los op:\\

$z^2 -(2+2i)z -(1-2i)=0$\\

$\Delta = (2+2i)^2 +4(1-2i)$\\

$= 4 +8i +4i^2 +4 -8i$\\

$= 4 +8i -4 +4 -8i$\\

$= 4$\\

$z = \dfrac{2+2i \pm 2}{2}$\\

$z = \dfrac{4+2i}{2}$\\

$z = 2+i$\\

of\\

$z = \dfrac{2i}{2}$\\

$z = i$\\


Oplossingen:\\

$z\in\{ 1, 2+i, i\}$
