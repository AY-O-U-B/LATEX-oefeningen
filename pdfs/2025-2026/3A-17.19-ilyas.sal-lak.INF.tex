$f(x) = \dfrac{x}{x + 4}$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{(x + 4) \cdot 1 - x \cdot 1}{(x + 4)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{x + 4 - x}{(x + 4)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{4}{(x + 4)^2}$
