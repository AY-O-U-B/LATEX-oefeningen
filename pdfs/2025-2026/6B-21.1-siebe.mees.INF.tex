$\dfrac{1}{1 \cdot 3} + \dfrac{1}{3 \cdot 5} + \dfrac{1}{5 \cdot 7} + \ldots + \dfrac{1}{(2n-1)(2n+1)}$\\

$s_n = \dfrac{1}{2} \left(1 - \dfrac{1}{3} + \dfrac{1}{3} - \dfrac{1}{5} + \dfrac{1}{5} - \dfrac{1}{7} + \ldots + \dfrac{1}{2n-1} - \dfrac{1}{2n+1} \right)$\\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2} \left(1 - \cancel{\dfrac{1}{3}} + \cancel{\dfrac{1}{3}} - \bcancel{\dfrac{1}{5}} + \bcancel{\dfrac{1}{5}} - \cancel{\dfrac{1}{7}} + \ldots + \bcancel{\dfrac{1}{2n-1}} - \dfrac{1}{2n+1} \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \left(1 - \dfrac{1}{2n+1} \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \left(\dfrac{2n+1}{2n+1} - \dfrac{1}{2n+1} \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{2n}{2n+1}$\\

$= \dfrac{n}{2n+1}$\\
