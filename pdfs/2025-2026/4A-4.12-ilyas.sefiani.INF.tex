$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sqrt[4]{x}\sqrt[6]{x^7}}{\sqrt{x^3\sqrt[12]{x^{11}}}}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{1}{4}}x^{\tfrac{7}{6}}}{\sqrt{x^3x^{\tfrac{11}{12}}}}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{17}{12}}}{\sqrt{x^{\tfrac{47}{12}}}}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{17}{12}}}{x^{\tfrac{47}{24}}}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int x^{-\tfrac{13}{24}}dx = \dfrac{24}{11}x^{\tfrac{11}{24}} + C$\\
