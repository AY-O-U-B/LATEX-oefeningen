$\dint \dfrac{x\sqrt{x^2 + 1} - 5}{\sqrt{x^2 + 1}}$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x\sqrt{x^2 + 1}}{\sqrt{x^2 + 1}} dx - \dint \dfrac{5}{\sqrt{x^2 + 1}} dx = \dint x dx - 5 \dint \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+1}} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}x^2 - 5 \ln|x + \sqrt{x^2 + 1}| + C$ \\
