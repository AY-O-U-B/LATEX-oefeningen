$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{4x+3\sqrt{x^2-1}}{x\sqrt{x^2-1}}dx$\\

$ = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{4x}{x\sqrt{x^2-1}}dx + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{3\sqrt{x^2-1}}{x\sqrt{x^2-1}}dx$\\

$ = 4\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x^2-1}}dx + 3\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x}dx$\\

$ = 4\ln|x+\sqrt{x^2-1}| + 3\ln|x|+C$\\
