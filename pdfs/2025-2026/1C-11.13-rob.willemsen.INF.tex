\noindent Gegeven is het complex getal $z = -1 + i$. We zoeken de goniometrische vorm $z = r \operatorname{cis} \alpha$. \\

$r = \sqrt{(-1)^2 + 1^2} = \sqrt{1 + 1} = \sqrt{2}$ \\

$\tan \alpha = \dfrac{1}{-1} = -1$ \\

$\alpha = \pi - \dfrac{\pi}{4} = \dfrac{3\pi}{4}$ \\

\textbf{Antwoord:} $z = \sqrt{2} \operatorname{cis} \dfrac{3\pi}{4}$ \\
