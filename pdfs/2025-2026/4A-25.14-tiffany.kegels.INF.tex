$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{5x + 2}{x-3} dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{5x -15 + 17}{x-3} dx = \displaystyle\int \dfrac{5(x-3)}{x-3} dx + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{17}{x-3}dx$\\

$= 5 \displaystyle\int \dfrac{x-3}{x-3} dx +17 \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x-3} dx$\\

$z = x-3 , dz = 1dx$\\

$= 5\displaystyle\int 1dx + 17 \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dz}{z} = 5x + 17 \ln |z| + c$\\

$= 5x + 17 \ln |x-3| + c$\\
