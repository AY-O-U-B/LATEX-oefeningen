$\dint \dfrac{5dx}{x^2+5}$ \\

$= 5 \dint \dfrac{dx}{x^2+5}$ \\

$= 5 \dint \dfrac{dx}{5\left(\dfrac{x}{5}^2+1\right)}$ \\

$= 5 \cdot \dfrac{1}{5} \dint \dfrac{dx}{\dfrac{x^2}{5}+1}$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{dx}{\left(\dfrac{x}{\sqrt{5}}\right)^2+1}$ \\

$t = \dfrac{x}{\sqrt{5}} \Rightarrow dt = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{5}} dx \Rightarrow \sqrt{5}dt = dx$ \\

$= \sqrt{5} \dint \dfrac{dt}{t^2+1}$ \\

$= \sqrt{5} \Bgtan(t) + C$ \\

$= \sqrt{5} \Bgtan\left(\dfrac{x}{\sqrt{5}}\right) + C$ \\
