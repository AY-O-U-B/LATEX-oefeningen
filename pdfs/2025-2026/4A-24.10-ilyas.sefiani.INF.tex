$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\tan(x)}{\ln(\cos(x))}dx$\\

Stel $t = \ln(\cos(x)) \Rightarrow dt = -\dfrac{\sin(x)}{\cos(x)}dx $\\

$=-\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sin(x)\cos(x)}{t\cos(x)\sin(x)}dt$\\

$=-\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t}$\\

$=-\ln\lvert t \rvert+C$\\

$=-\ln\lvert\ln(\cos(x))\rvert+C$\\
