$\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^\infty \dfrac{7^n}{8^n} = \displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^\infty \left(\dfrac{7}{8}\right)^n$\\

Uit het Klein Criterium van Cauchy 8(+) volgt\\

$\limsup\limits_{n\rightarrow +\infty} \sqrt[n]{\left(\dfrac{7}{8}\right)^n} = \limsup\limits_{n\rightarrow +\infty} \left(\dfrac{7}{8
}\right)^{\dfrac{n}{n}} = \limsup\limits_{n\rightarrow +\infty} \dfrac{7}{8} = \dfrac{7}{8}\Rightarrow$ \textbf{Convergent}
