$7(1+i)z=(1-i)(12-z^2)$\\

$\Rightarrow 7\dfrac{1+i}{1-i}z=12-z^2$\\

$\Rightarrow 7iz=12-z^2$\\

$\Rightarrow z^2+7iz-12=0$\\

$\Delta=(7i)^2-4(1)(-12)=-49+48=-1$\\

$\Rightarrow z_1=\dfrac{-7i+i}{2}=-3i$ , $z_2=\dfrac{-7i-i}{2}=-4i$\\

$\Rightarrow z_1=-3i,\ z_2=-4i$\\
