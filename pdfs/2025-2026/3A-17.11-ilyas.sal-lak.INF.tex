$f(x) = \dfrac{x^n + 1}{x^n - 1}$ met $n \in \mathbb{N}_0$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{\left(x^n - 1\right) \cdot nx^{n-1} - \left(x^n + 1\right) \cdot nx^{n-1}}{\left(x^n - 1\right)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{nx^{n-1}\left(x^n - 1 - x^n - 1\right)}{\left(x^n - 1\right)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{nx^{n-1} \cdot (-2)}{\left(x^n - 1\right)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{-2nx^{n-1}}{\left(x^n - 1\right)^2}$
