$1^2 \cdot 4 + 2^2 \cdot 5 + 3^2 \cdot 6 + \ldots$\\

$s_n = \displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{n} i^2(i+3)$\\

$= \displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{n} i^3 + 3 \displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{n} i^2$\\

$= \left(\dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}\right)^2 + 3 \cdot \dfrac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}$\\

$= \dfrac{n^2(n+1)^2}{4} + \dfrac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{2}$\\

$= \dfrac{n(n+1)}{2} \left(\dfrac{n(n+1)}{2} + (2n+1)\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{n(n+1)}{2} \left(\dfrac{n(n+1) + 2(2n+1)}{2}\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{n(n+1)}{2} \left(\dfrac{n^2 + n + 4n + 2}{2}\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{n(n+1)}{2} \left(\dfrac{n^2 + 5n + 2}{2}\right)$\\

$= \dfrac{n(n+1)(n^2 + 5n + 2)}{4}$\\


$n(n+1)$ is deelbaar door $2$: Triviaal\\

$(n^2 + 5n + 2)$ is deelbaar door $2$: \\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
n \text{ even: } n^2 \text{ en } 5n \text{ even} \Rightarrow n^2 + 5n + 2 \text{ even} \\

n \text{ oneven: } n^2 + 5n \text{ oneven} + \text{ oneven} = \text{ even}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow $ deelbaar door 2\\
