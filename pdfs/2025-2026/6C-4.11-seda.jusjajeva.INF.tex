$\limit_{n \to +\infty} \dfrac{7n^3 + 4}{12n^3 + n^2 + n + 12}$ \\

$= \limit_{n \to +\infty} \dfrac{7n^3}{12n^3} = \dfrac{7}{12}$ \\

$\operatorname*{adh}\limits_{n \to +\infty} x_n = \left\{\dfrac{7}{12}\right\}$ \\

$\liminf\limits_{n \to +\infty} x_n = \limsup\limits_{n \to +\infty} x_n = \dfrac{7}{12}$ \\
