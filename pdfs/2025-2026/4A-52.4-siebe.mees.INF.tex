$\displaystyle\int \sin^4 x \cos^2 x dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \left( \dfrac{1-\cos 2x}{2} \right)^2 \left( \dfrac{1+\cos 2x}{2} \right) dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}\displaystyle\int \left( 1-2\cos 2x+\cos^2 2x \right)\left( 1+\cos 2x \right) dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}\displaystyle\int \left( 1-\cos 2x-\cos^2 2x+\cos^3 2x \right) dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}\displaystyle\int \left( 1-\cos 2x-\dfrac{1+\cos 4x}{2}+\cos 2x \dfrac{1+\cos 4x}{2} \right) dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}\displaystyle\int \left( 1-\cos 2x-\dfrac{1}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 4x+\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 2x+\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 2x \cos 4x \right) dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}\displaystyle\int \left( \dfrac{1}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 2x-\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 4x+\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 2x \cos 4x \right) dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}\displaystyle\int \left( \dfrac{1}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 2x-\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 4x+\dfrac{1}{4}\left( \cos 6x+\cos 2x \right) \right) dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{8}\displaystyle\int \left( \dfrac{1}{2}-\dfrac{1}{4}\cos 2x-\dfrac{1}{2}\cos 4x+\dfrac{1}{4}\cos 6x \right) dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int \left( \dfrac{1}{16}-\dfrac{1}{32}\cos 2x-\dfrac{1}{16}\cos 4x+\dfrac{1}{32}\cos 6x \right) dx$\\

$= \dfrac{x}{16}-\dfrac{1}{32}\dfrac{\sin 2x}{2}-\dfrac{1}{16}\dfrac{\sin 4x}{4}+\dfrac{1}{32}\dfrac{\sin 6x}{6}+C$\\

$= \dfrac{x}{16}-\dfrac{\sin 2x}{64}-\dfrac{\sin 4x}{64}+\dfrac{\sin 6x}{192}+C$\\
