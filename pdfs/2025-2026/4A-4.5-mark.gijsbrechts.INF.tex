$\dint \dfrac{\sqrt{3x}}{5x} dx $

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{5} \dint \dfrac{\sqrt{x}}{x} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{5} \dint x^{-\tfrac{1}{2}} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{5} \dfrac{x^{\tfrac{1}{2}}}{\tfrac{1}{2}} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{2\sqrt{3x}}{5} + c$ \\
