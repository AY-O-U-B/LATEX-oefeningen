$\dint \dfrac{2^{x+2} + 5^{x-2}}{4^{x-1}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{2^{x+2}}{4^{x-1}} dx + \dint \dfrac{5^{x-2}}{4^{x-1}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{2^{x+2}}{2^{2x-2}} dx + \dint \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{25} \cdot 5^x}{\dfrac{1}{4} \cdot 4^x} dx$ \\

$= \dint 2^{4-x} dx + \dint \dfrac{4}{25} \cdot \left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x dx$ \\

stel $t = 4-x \Rightarrow dx = -dt$ \\

$= \dint 2^t \cdot (-1) dt + \dint \dfrac{4}{25} \cdot \left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x dx$ \\

$= -\dint 2^t dt + \dint \dfrac{4}{25} \cdot \left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x dx$ \\

$= -\dfrac{2^t}{\ln 2} + \dfrac{4}{25} \cdot \dfrac{\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x}{\ln\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)}$ \\

$= -\dfrac{2^{4-x}}{\ln 2} + \dfrac{4}{25} \cdot \dfrac{\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)^x}{\ln\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\right)} + C$ \\
