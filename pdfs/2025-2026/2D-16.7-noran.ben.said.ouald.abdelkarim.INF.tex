$ \lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi}{2}} \cos 5x \cot 4x$\\

Stel $ u = x - \dfrac{\pi}{2}$\\

$ \cos 5x = \cos 5 \left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} + u \right)
= \cos \left(\dfrac{5\pi}{2} + 5u \right)
= -\sin 5u$\\

$ \cot 4x = \cot 4 \left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} + u \right)
= \cot \left(2\pi + 4u \right)
= \cot 4u
= \dfrac{\cos 4u}{\sin 4u}$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{u \to 0} \left( -\sin 5u \cdot \dfrac{\cos 4u}{\sin 4u} \right)$\\

$ = \lim\limits_{u \to 0} \left(-\dfrac{\sin 5u}{5u} 
\cdot \dfrac{4u}{\sin 4u} \cdot \cos 4u \cdot \dfrac{5u}{4u} \right)$\\

$ = -1 \cdot 1 \cdot 1 \cdot \dfrac{5}{4}$\\

$ = -\dfrac{5}{4}$\\

$ \Rightarrow \lim\limits_{x \to \tfrac{\pi}{2}}
\cos 5x \cot 4x = -\dfrac{5}{4}$\\
