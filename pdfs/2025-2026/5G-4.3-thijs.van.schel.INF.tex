$f(y) = \sqrt(1+y^3)$ op het interval $[1,2]$\\

$\pi\ldint(1+y^3)dy=\pi\ldint dy + \pi\ldint y^3dy$\\

$=\pi\left[y+\dfrac{y^4}{4}\right]_1^2$\\

$= \pi+4\pi-\dfrac{\pi}{4} = \dfrac{19\pi}{4}$\\
