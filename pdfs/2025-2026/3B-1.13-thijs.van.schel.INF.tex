$f(x) = \dfrac{1}{1+e^{2x}}$\\

$f'(x) = -\dfrac{2e^{2x}}{(1+e^{2x})^2}$\\

$f''(x) = -\dfrac{4e^{2x}}{(1+e^{2x})^2} + \dfrac{8e^{4x}}{(1+e^{2x})^3}$\\

$= \dfrac{4(e^{2x}-1)e^{2x}}{(e^{2x}+1)^3}$
