34.16) Bereken de afgeleide van de fucntie: $f(x) = \ln(x^3 + \sqrt{x^6+1})$\\

$f'(x) =  \dfrac{1}{x^3+\sqrt{x^6+1}}\cdot(3x^2+\dfrac{1}{2}(x^6+1)^\tfrac{-1}{2}\cdot6x^5)$\\

$f'(x) =  \dfrac{1}{x^3+\sqrt{x^6+1}}\cdot(3x^2+\dfrac{6x^5}{2\sqrt{x^6+1}})$\\

$f'(x) =  \dfrac{1}{x^3+\sqrt{x^6+1}}\cdot\left(\dfrac{3x^2\sqrt{x^6+1}+3x^5}{\sqrt{x^6+1}}\right)$\\

$f'(x) =  \dfrac{3x^2(\sqrt{x^6+1}+x^3)}{(x^3+\sqrt{x^6+1})(\sqrt{x^6+1})}$\\

$f'(x) =  \dfrac{3x^2}{\sqrt{x^6+1}}$\\
