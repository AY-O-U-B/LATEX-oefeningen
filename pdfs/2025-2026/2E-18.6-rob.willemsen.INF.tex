$4 \cdot \log_x\left(\sqrt{3}\right) + \log_x\left(3^{\tfrac{1}{3}}\right) + \log_x\left(\dfrac{1}{9}\right) + \dfrac{1}{3} = 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow 4 \cdot \log_x\left(3^{\tfrac{1}{2}}\right) + \log_x\left(3^{\tfrac{1}{3}}\right) + \log_x\left(3^{-2}\right) + \dfrac{1}{3} = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow 2 \cdot \log_x(3) + \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \log_x(3) - 2 \cdot \log_x(3) + \dfrac{1}{3} = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \log_x(3) + \dfrac{1}{3} = 0$\\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \log_x(3) = -\dfrac{1}{3}$\\

$\Rightarrow \log_x(3) = -\dfrac{1}{3} \cdot \dfrac{3}{1}$\\

$\Rightarrow \log_x(3) = -1$\\

$\Rightarrow x^{-1} = 3$\\

$\Rightarrow x = \dfrac{1}{3}$\\
