$\displaystyle \int x^2 \sqrt{x-1}dx$ \\

Stel $u = x- 1 \Rightarrow du = dx$ en $
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
     x = u + 1 \\
     x^2 = (u + 1)^2
\end{array}
\right .$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int (u+1) ^ 2 \sqrt{u}du$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int \left( u^2 + 2u + 1\right)u^{\tfrac{1}{2}}du$ \\

$= \displaystyle \int u^{\tfrac{5}{2}} + 2u^{\tfrac{3}{2}} + u^{\tfrac{1}{2}}du$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{7} (x-1)^{\tfrac{7}{2}} + \dfrac{4}{5} (x-1)^{\tfrac{5}{2}} + \dfrac{2}{3} (x-1)^{\tfrac{3}{2}} + c$ \\
