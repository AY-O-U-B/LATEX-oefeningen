$\dint 4x \thirdroot{3x} dx$ \\

$= 4 \thirdroot{3} \dint x \thirdroot{x} dx$ \\

$=4 \thirdroot{3} \dint x^{\tfrac{4}{3}} dx$ \\

$= 4 \thirdroot{3} \cdot \dfrac{\thirdroot{x^7}}{\dfrac{7}{3}} + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{12 \thirdroot{3} x^2 \thirdroot{x}}{7} + c$ \\
