Bereken in $\mathbb{C}$ de vierkantswortel van het volgende complexe getal: $ -1 - 2\sqrt{2}i $.\\

$ -1 - 2\sqrt{2}i = (x + yi)^2 = x^2 - y^2 + 2xyi $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = -1 \\
    2xy = -2\sqrt{2}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = -1 \\
    xy = -\sqrt{2}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 - y^2 = -1 \\
    x^2y^2 = 2
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 +1 = y^2 \\
    x^2(x^2+1) = 2
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
    x^2 +1 = y^2 \\
    x^4 + x^2 - 2 = 0
\end{array}
\right .$\\

Laat $x^2=\lambda$\\

$ \lambda^2 + \lambda - 2 = 0 $\\

$ (\lambda +2)(\lambda-1)=0 $\\

$ \lambda_1 = -2 $ en $ \lambda_2 = 1 $\\

$ x = 1 $ of $x=-1$ en $ y = \sqrt{2} $ of $y=-\sqrt{2}$\\

Aangezien $xy=-\sqrt{2}$, dan hebben $x$ en $y$ tegengesteld teken\\

Oplossing: $ 1 - \sqrt{2}i $ of $ -1 + \sqrt{2}i $
