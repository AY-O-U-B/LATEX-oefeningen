\textbf{Oefening:} $ \operatorname{Bgsin}(x) + \operatorname{Bgcos}(x) $\\

Stel $ x = \sin(\alpha) $ met $ \alpha \in \left[0, \dfrac{\pi}{2}\right] $\\

Dan geldt $x = \sin\left(\alpha\right) = \cos\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} - \alpha\right)$\\

Dus $\operatorname{Bgcos}(x) = \dfrac{\pi}{2} - \alpha$\\

$ \Rightarrow \operatorname{Bgsin}(x) + \operatorname{Bgcos}(x) = \alpha + \left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} - \alpha\right) = \dfrac{\pi}{2} $ \\

\textbf{Conclusie:} \\
$$ \forall x \ge 0,\ x \le 1 : \operatorname{Bgsin}(x) + \operatorname{Bgcos}(x) = \dfrac{\pi}{2} $$\\
