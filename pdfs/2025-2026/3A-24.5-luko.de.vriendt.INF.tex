$f(x) = x^2\sin(x) + 2x\cos(x) - 2\sin(x)$ \\

$f'(x) = 2x\sin(x) + x^2\cos(x) + 2\cos(x) + 2x\cdot\sin\left(-x\right) - 2\cos(x)$ \\

$= 2x\sin(x) + x^2\cos(x) + 2\cos(x) - 2x\cdot\sin(x) - 2\cos(x)$ \\

$= x^2\cos(x)$ \\
