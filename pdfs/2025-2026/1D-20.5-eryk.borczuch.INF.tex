$z^4 +4$\\

stel: $z^2 = t$\\

dan: $z^4+4 = t^2+4$\\

$t^2 = -4$\\

$t_{1,2} = \pm 2i$\\

$t_1 = 2i \Rightarrow z_{1,2}^2 = 2i$\\

$z_1 = 1+i$\\

$z_2 = -1-i$\\

$t_2 = -2i \Rightarrow z_{3,4}^2 = -2i$\\

$z_3 = 1+i$\\

$z_4 = -1+i$\\

dus: $z^4 +4 = (z-(1+i))(z-(1-i))(z-(-1-i))(z-(-1+i))$\\

$z^4 + 4 = (z^2-2z+2)(z^2+2z+2)$
