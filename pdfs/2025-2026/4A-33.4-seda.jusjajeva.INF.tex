$\integral\dfrac{e^{2x}}{1 + e^{4x}}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $ t = e^{2x} \Rightarrow dt = 2e^{2x} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\integral\dfrac{1}{1 + t^2}dt$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\operatorname{Bgtan}t + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{2}\operatorname{Bgtan} \left(e^{2x}\right) + c$ \\
