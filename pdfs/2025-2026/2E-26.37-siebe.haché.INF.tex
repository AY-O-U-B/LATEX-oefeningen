$\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x\right) = \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(2 - \dfrac{1}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x\right) + 1 + \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} 2 $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x\right) = \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}}\left(2 - \dfrac{1}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x\right) + 1 - 1$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x = 2 - \dfrac{1}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x $\\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x + \dfrac{1}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x = 2 $ \\

$ \Leftrightarrow \dfrac{4}{3}\log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x = 2$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow \log_{\tfrac{1}{2}} x = \dfrac{3}{2}$\\

$\Leftrightarrow x = \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^{\tfrac{3}{2}}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x = \dfrac{1}{2^{\tfrac{3}{2}}} $ \\
