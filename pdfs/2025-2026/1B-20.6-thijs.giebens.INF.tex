$\dfrac{1}{(1+i)^3} + \dfrac{1}{(1-i)^3}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{1+3i-3-i} + \dfrac{1}{1-3i-3+i}$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{-2+2i} + \dfrac{1}{-2-2i}$\\

$= \dfrac{-2-2i}{8} + \dfrac{-2+2i}{8}$\\

$= \dfrac{-1}{2}$\\
