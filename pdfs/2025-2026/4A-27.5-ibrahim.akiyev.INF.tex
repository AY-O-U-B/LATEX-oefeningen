$\displaystyle \int\dfrac{x}{x^2 + 2}dx$\\

$u = x^2 + 2 \Rightarrow \dfrac{du}{dx} = 2x \Rightarrow du = 2xdu \Rightarrow xdx = \dfrac{1}{2}du$\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle \int \dfrac{x}{x+2}dx = \int \dfrac{1}{u} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} du \Rightarrow \dfrac {1}{2} \int \dfrac{1}{u}du $\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle \int \dfrac{1}{u}du = \ln\left|u\right| \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{2}\ln\left|u\right| + C$\\

$\Rightarrow u = x^2 +2 \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{2}\ln(x^2+2)+C$\\

$\Rightarrow \displaystyle \int \dfrac{x}{x^2+2}dx = \dfrac{1}{2}\ln(x^2+2)+C$\\
