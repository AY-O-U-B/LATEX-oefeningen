$\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \left(\dfrac{1+3x}{1-x}\right)^{\tfrac{3}{x}} = \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \left(\dfrac{1+4x-x}{1-x}\right)^{\tfrac{3}{x}}$ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \left(\dfrac{1-x}{1-x} + \dfrac{4x}{1-x}\right)^{\tfrac{3}{x}} = \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \left(1 + \dfrac{4x}{1-x}\right)^{\tfrac{3}{x} \cdot \tfrac{1-x}{4x} \cdot \tfrac{4x}{1-x}} $ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \left(\left(1 + \dfrac{4x}{1-x}\right)^{\tfrac{1-x}{4x}}\right)^{\tfrac{12x}{x(1-x)}} = \lim\limits_{x \to 0} e^{\tfrac{12x}{x-x^2}} $ \\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 0} e^{\tfrac{12}{1-x}} = e^{\tfrac{12}{1-0}} = e^{12}$
