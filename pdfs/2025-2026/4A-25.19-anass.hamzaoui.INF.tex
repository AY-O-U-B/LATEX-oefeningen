$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{2x + 5}{3x - 7} dx$ \\

$= \displaystyle\int \dfrac{2}{3} dx + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{29}{3(3x - 7)} dx$ \\

$t = 3x - 7, dt = 3 dx, dx = \dfrac{dt}{3}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{3}x + \dfrac{29}{9} \displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{t} dt$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{3}x + \dfrac{29}{9} \ln |t| + c$ \\

$= \dfrac{2}{3}x + \dfrac{29}{9} \ln |3x - 7| + c$ \\
