$\dint \dfrac{11x-7}{3x^2-5x+2}dx$\\

$D = 1 \Rightarrow x_{1,2} = \left\{1,\dfrac{2}{3}\right\}$\\

$\dint \dfrac{11x-7}{(x-1)(3x-2)}dx$\\

$\dfrac{11x-7}{(x-1)(3x-2)} = \dfrac{A}{(x-1)}+\dfrac{B}{(3x-2)}$\\

$A = \left . \dfrac{11x-7}{3x-2}\right |_{x=1}=4$\\

$B = \left . \dfrac{11x-7}{x-1}\right |_{x=\tfrac{2}{3}}=-1$\\

$\Rightarrow 4\dint \dfrac{1}{x-1}dx-\dint \dfrac{1}{3x-2}dx$\\

$= 4\ln |x-1| - \dfrac{1}{3}\ln|3x-2| + C$
