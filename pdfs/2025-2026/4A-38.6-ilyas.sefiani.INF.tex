$\displaystyle\int \dfrac{5x-7}{x^2+3x+3}dx$\\

$= \dfrac{5}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{2x+3}{x^2+3x+3}dx-\dfrac{29}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x^2+3x+3}$\\

Deel 1:\\

$\dfrac{5}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{2x+3}{x^2+3x+3}dx$\\

$$t=x^2+3x+3$$ $$dt=(2x+3)dx$$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t}$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t}$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{2} \ln(t)+C$\\

$=\dfrac{5}{2} \ln(x^2+3x+3)+C$\\

Deel 2:\\

$$\left(x+\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^2+\dfrac{3}{4}=x^2+3x+3$$\\

$-\dfrac{29}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x^2+3x+3}$\\

$= -\dfrac{29}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{\left(x+\dfrac{3}{2}\right)^2+\dfrac{3}{4}}$\\

$$u=x+\dfrac{3}{2}$$ $$du=dx$$\\

$= -\dfrac{29}{2}\displaystyle\int \dfrac{du}{u^2+\dfrac{3}{4}}$\\

$= -\dfrac{29}{\sqrt{3}}\operatorname{Bgtan}\left(\dfrac{2u}{\sqrt{3}}\right)+C$\\

$= -\dfrac{29}{\sqrt{3}}\operatorname{Bgtan}\left(\dfrac{2x+3}{\sqrt{3}}\right)+C$\\

Deel 1 + Deel 2:\\

$=\dfrac{5}{2} \ln(x^2+3x+3)-\dfrac{29}{\sqrt{3}}\operatorname{Bgtan}\left(\dfrac{2x+3}{\sqrt{3}}\right)+C$\\
