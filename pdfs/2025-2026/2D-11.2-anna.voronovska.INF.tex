$\lim\limits_{x \to 3} \dfrac{\sqrt{2x+3}-3}{x-3} = \lim\limits_{x \to 3} \dfrac{(\sqrt{2x+3}-3)(\sqrt{2x+3}+3)}{(x-3)(\sqrt{2x+3}+3)}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 3} \dfrac{(\sqrt{2x+3})^2 - 3^2}{(x-3)(\sqrt{2x+3}+3)} = \lim\limits_{x \to 3}\dfrac{2x+3-9}{(x-3)(\sqrt{2x+3}+3)} = \lim\limits_{x \to 3}\dfrac{2(x-3)}{(x-3)(\sqrt{2x+3}+3)}$\\

$= \lim\limits_{x \to 3} \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{2x+3}+3} = \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{2 \cdot 3+3}+3} = \dfrac{2}{6} = \dfrac{1}{3}$\\
