$\dint \dfrac{5x+2}{x-3} dx $ \\

$5\dint \dfrac{x+\tfrac{2}{5}}{x-3} dx $ \\

$5\dint \dfrac{x-3}{x-3} dx + \dint \dfrac{\tfrac{17}{5}}{x-3} dx $ \\

$5\dint dx + \dfrac{17}{5}\dint \dfrac{dx}{x-3} $ \\

Stel $ t = x - 3 \Rightarrow dt = dx $ \\

$= 5\dint dx + \dfrac{17}{5} \dint \dfrac{dt}{t} $ \\

$= 5x + \dfrac{17}{5}\ln|t| + c $ \\

$= 5x + \dfrac{17}{5}\ln|x-3| + c $ \\
