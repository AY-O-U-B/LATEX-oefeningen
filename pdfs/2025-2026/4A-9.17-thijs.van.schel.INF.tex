$\dint\tan^2(4x)dx$\\

$t=4x \Rightarrow dt = 4dx$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{4}\dint\tan^2(t)dt = \dfrac{1}{4}\left(\dint\sec^2(t)dt - \dint dt \right)$\\

$= \dfrac{1}{4}\tan t - \dfrac{1}{4}t + C$\\

$= \dfrac{\tan(4x)}{4} - \dfrac{4}{4}x + C = \dfrac{\tan(4x)}{4} - x + C$
