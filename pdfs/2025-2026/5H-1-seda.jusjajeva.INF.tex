$V = \pi\integral_{0}^{2}(4t - t^3)^2(t^2)'dt$ \\

$= \pi\integral_{0}^{2} \left(16t^2 - 8t^4 + t^6\right)2tdt$ \\

$= \pi\displaystyle\int_{0}^{2} \left(32t^3 - 16t^5 + 2t^7 \right)dt $ \\

$= \pi\left[8t^4 - \dfrac{8t^6}{3} + \dfrac{t^8}{4}\right]_{0}^{2}$ \\

$= \pi\left(8\cdot2^4 - \dfrac{8\cdot2^6}{3} + \dfrac{2^8}{4}\right) - \pi\cdot0$ \\

$= \dfrac{64\pi}{3}$ \\
