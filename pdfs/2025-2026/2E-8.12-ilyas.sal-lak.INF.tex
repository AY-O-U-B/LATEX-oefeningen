$25 \cdot 2^x = 4 \cdot 5^x$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{25}{4} = \dfrac{5^x}{2^x}$ \\

$\Rightarrow \dfrac{25}{4} = \left(\dfrac{5}{2}\right)^x$ \\

$\Rightarrow \left(\dfrac{5}{2}\right)^2 = \left(\dfrac{5}{2}\right)^x$ \\

$\Rightarrow x = 2$
