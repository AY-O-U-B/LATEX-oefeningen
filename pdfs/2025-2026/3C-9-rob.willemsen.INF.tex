Gegeven: $f'(x) = \gamma_0 + \gamma_1 x + \gamma_2 x^2 + $ ... $+ \gamma_n x^n$ \\

$f(x) = \gamma_0 x + \dfrac{\gamma_1}{2} x^2 + \dfrac{\gamma_2}{3} x^3 + $ ... $+ \dfrac{\gamma_n}{n+1} x^{n+1}$ \\

$f(0) = \gamma_0(0) + \dfrac{\gamma_1}{2}(0)^2 + \dfrac{\gamma_2}{3}(0)^3 + $ ... $+ \dfrac{\gamma_n}{n+1}(0)^{n+1} $ \\

$= 0$ \\

$f(1) = \gamma_0(1) + \dfrac{\gamma_1}{2}(1)^2 + \dfrac{\gamma_2}{3}(1)^3 + $ ... $+ \dfrac{\gamma_n}{n+1}(1)^{n+1} $ \\

$= \gamma_0 + \dfrac{\gamma_1}{2} + \dfrac{\gamma_2}{3} + $ ... $+ \dfrac{\gamma_n}{n+1} $ \\

$= \displaystyle\sum\limits_{i=0}^{n} \dfrac{\gamma_i}{i+1} \rightarrow$ gegeven $f(1) = 0$ dus $f(0) = f(1) = 0$ \\

$\Rightarrow$ Stelling van Rolle: $f'(c) = 0$ dit geldt. \\

dus: $\gamma_0 + \gamma_1 c + \gamma_2 c^2 + $ ... $+ \gamma_n c^n = 0$ \\

$\blacksquare$ \\
