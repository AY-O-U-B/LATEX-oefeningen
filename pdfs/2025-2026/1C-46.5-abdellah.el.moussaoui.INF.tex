Trek de vierdemachtswortels in $\mathbb{C}$:\\

$z^4=-4$\\

We weten dat: \\

$-4=4(-1)=4(\cos(\pi)+i\sin(\pi))=4\operatorname{cis}(\pi)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_k=\sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi+2k\pi}{4}\right),k=0,1,2,3$\\

$\Rightarrow z_k=\sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}+\dfrac{k\pi}{2}\right),k=0,1,2,3$\\

$\Rightarrow z_0=\sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)=1+i$\\


$\Rightarrow z_1=\sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4}\right)=-1+i$\\

$\Rightarrow z_2=\sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{5\pi}{4}\right)=-1-i$\\

$\Rightarrow z_3=\sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{7\pi}{4}\right)=1-i$\\

$\Rightarrow \boxed{z_0=1+i,z_1=-1+i,z_2=-1-i,z_3=1-i}$\\
