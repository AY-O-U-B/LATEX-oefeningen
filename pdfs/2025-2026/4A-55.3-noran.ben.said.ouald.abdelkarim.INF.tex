$ \displaystyle\int \tan^5 x dx $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{4} \tan^4 x - \displaystyle\int \tan^3xdx$

$ = \dfrac{1}{4} \tan^4 x - \left(\dfrac{1}{2} \tan^2x -\displaystyle\int \tan xdx \right) $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{4} \tan^4 x - \left(\dfrac{1}{2} \tan^2x -\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\sin x}{\cos x} dx \right) $ \\

Stel $\cos x = t \Rightarrow{dt = - \sin x dx} \Rightarrow{dx = \dfrac{dt}{-\sin x}}$\\


$ \Rightarrow{}\dfrac{1}{4} \tan^4 x - \dfrac{1}{2} \tan^2x + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{- t} =\dfrac{1}{4} \tan^4 x - \dfrac{1}{2} \tan^2x - \displaystyle\int \dfrac{dt}{t} $ \\

$ =\dfrac{1}{4} \tan^4 x - \dfrac{1}{2} \tan^2x - \ln|t| + c$ \\

$ =\dfrac{1}{4} \tan^4 x - \dfrac{1}{2} \tan^2x - \ln|\cos x| + c$ \\
