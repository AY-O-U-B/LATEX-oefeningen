$f(x) = \dfrac{3}{x - 2} + \dfrac{x}{x - 1}$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{(x - 2) \cdot 0 - 3 \cdot 1}{(x - 2)^2} + \dfrac{(x - 1) \cdot 1 - x \cdot 1}{(x - 1)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{-3}{(x - 2)^2} + \dfrac{x - 1 - x}{(x - 1)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{-3}{(x - 2)^2} + \dfrac{-1}{(x - 1)^2}$ \\

$\Rightarrow f'(x) = \dfrac{-3}{(x - 2)^2} - \dfrac{1}{(x - 1)^2}$
