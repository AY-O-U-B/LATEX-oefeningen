$V(t) = 5000 - 5000\left(1 - \dfrac{t}{40}\right)^{2}$ \\

$V'(t) = -5000\cdot2\cdot\left(1 - \dfrac{t}{40}\right)\cdot\left(\dfrac{-1}{40}\right)$ \\

$= -10000 \cdot \left(\dfrac{-1}{40} + \dfrac{t}{1600}\right)$ \\

$= 250-\dfrac{25t}{4}$ \\

4.1: \\

$V'(5) = 250 - \dfrac{25\cdot5}{4} = \dfrac{1000 - 125}{4} = \dfrac{875}{4}$ \\

De snelheid is $\dfrac{875}{4}$ l/min. \\

4.2: \\

$V'(10) = 250 - \dfrac{25\cdot10}{4} = \dfrac{1000 - 250}{4} = \dfrac{375}{2}$ \\

De snelheid is $\dfrac{375}{2}$ l/min. \\
