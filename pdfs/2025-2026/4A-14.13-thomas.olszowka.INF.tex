$I = \dint \dfrac{x \ln x \cos x - \sin x}{x(\ln x)^2} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{x \ln x \cos x }{x \ln x \ln x} dx - \dint \dfrac{\sin x }{x \ln x \ln x} dx$ \\

$= \dint \cos x \dfrac{1}{\ln x} dx - \dint \dfrac{\sin x}{x \ln x \ln x} dx$ \\

$= \dint \cos x \dfrac{1}{\ln x} dx - \dint \sin x \dfrac{1}{x \ln x \ln x} dx$ \\

Partiële integratie van de 1ste integraal:
$\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    u =& \dfrac{1}{\ln x} \\ 
    dv =& \cos x dx \\ 
\end{array}\right. \Rightarrow\left\{\begin{array}{cc}
    du =& \dfrac{-1}{x \ln x \ln x} dx \\
    v =& \sin x \\
\end{array}\right.$ \\

$I = \dfrac{\sin x}{\ln x} - \dint \sin x \dfrac{-1}{x \ln x \ln x} dx - \dint \sin x \dfrac{1}{x \ln x \ln x} dx $ \\

$= \dfrac{\sin x}{\ln x} + \cancel{\dint \sin x \dfrac{1}{x \ln x \ln x} dx} - \cancel{\dint \sin x \dfrac{1}{x \ln x \ln x} dx} $ \\

$= \dfrac{\sin x}{\ln x} + c$ \\
