Opgave: Los de volgende vierkantwortel op in $\mathbb{C}$:\\

$ 1 + 2\sqrt{6} = Z^2$\\

Resolvente vergelijking opstellen:\\

$\left\{ \begin{array}{l} 
	x^2 - y^2 = 1\\ 
	2xy = 2\sqrt{6} \Rightarrow x^2(-y^2) = -6 
\end{array} \right.$

$\lambda^2 - \lambda - 6 = 0 $

\begin{eqnarray*}
D_{\lambda} &=&1^2 - 4(1)(-6) \\
		&=& 1 + 24 = 25
\end{eqnarray*}

$\lambda_1 = \dfrac{1 + 5}{2}$ en $\lambda_2 = \dfrac{1 - 5}{2}\Rightarrow \lambda_1 = 3$ en $\lambda_2 = -2 $ \\

Dus $x^2 = 3$  en $y^2 = 2$  \\

$xy = \sqrt{6} > 0$, dus:\\

$z_1 = \sqrt{3} + \sqrt{2}i$ en $z_2 = -\sqrt{3} -\sqrt{2}i$\\
