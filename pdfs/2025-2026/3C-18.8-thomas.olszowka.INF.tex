$\llim_{x\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{x \cos x - \sin x}{e^x - e^{-x} -2x} = \dfrac{0}{0}$\\

$\overset{H}{=}\llim_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\cos x + x(-\sin x) - \cos x}{e^x + e^{-x} -2}$\\

$= \llim_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-x\sin x}{e^x + e^{-x} -2} = \dfrac{0}{0}$\\  

$\overset{H}{=}\llim_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-\sin x -x\cos x}{e^x - e^{-x}}$\\

$= \llim_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-(\sin x + x\cos x)}{e^x - e^{-x}} = \dfrac{0}{0}$\\

$\overset{H}{=}\llim_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-(\cos x + \cos x + x(-\sin x))}{e^x + e^{-x}}$\\

$= \llim_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{-(2\cos x - x\sin x)}{e^x + e^{-x}}$\\

$= \dfrac{-(2 \cdot 1 -0 \cdot 0)}{1 + 1} =\dfrac{-2}{2} = -1$
