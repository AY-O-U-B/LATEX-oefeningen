$z^2 + 26 = 2z$ \\

$z^2 - 2z + 26 = 0$ \\

$\Delta = (-2)^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 26 = 4 - 104 = -100$ \\

$\sqrt{\Delta} = 10i$ \\

$z_{1,2} = \dfrac{2 \pm 10i}{2} = 1 \pm 5i$ \\

\textbf{Antwoord:} $z \in \{1+5i, 1-5i\}$
