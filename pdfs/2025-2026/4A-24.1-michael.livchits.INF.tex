$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{x\ln x}$\\

$u = \ln x$\\

$du = \dfrac{dx}{x}$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{du}{u}$\\

$= \ln |u|$\\

$= \ln \left|\ln x\right| + C$\\
