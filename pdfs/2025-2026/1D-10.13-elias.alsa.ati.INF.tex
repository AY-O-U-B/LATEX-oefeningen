$$\left(iz^2 + 2z + 5\right) \div \left(z- 1 + i\right)$$

stel $z - 1 + i = 0$, dan is $z = 1 - i$\\

\textbf{Horner:}\\

$$\begin{array}{r|cc|c}
      & i & 2     & 5       \\  
1 - i & | & 1 + i & 4 - 2i  \\  \cline{1-4}
      & i & 3 + i & 9 - 2i
\end{array}$$

\textbf{Resultaat:}\\

\begin{align*}
    A(z) &= B(z) \cdot Q(z) + R(z)                                                      \\
    A(z) &= \left(z- 1 + i\right) \cdot \left(iz + 3 + i\right) + \left(9 - 2i\right)   \\
\end{align*}
