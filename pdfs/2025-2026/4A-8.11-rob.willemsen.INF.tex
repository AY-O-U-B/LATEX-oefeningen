$\dint \dfrac{dx}{\tan(3x+5)}$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{\cos(3x+5)}{\sin(3x+5)} dx$ \\

stel $t = \sin(3x+5)$, dan is $dt = 3\cos(3x+5) dx \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{3}dt = \cos(3x+5) dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{3}}{t}dt = \dint \dfrac{1}{t} \cdot \dfrac{1}{3} dt = \dfrac{1}{3} \ln|t| + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{3} \ln|\sin(3x+5)| + C$ \\
