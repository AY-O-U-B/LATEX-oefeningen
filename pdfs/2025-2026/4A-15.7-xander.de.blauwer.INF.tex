$\displaystyle\int x^3e^{5x}dx$\\

$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u_1 = x^3 \\
dv_1 = e^{5x}dx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du_1 = 3x^2dx \\
v_1 = \dfrac{e^{5x}}{5}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$=\dfrac{x^3 \cdot e^{5x}}{5} - \displaystyle\int \dfrac{3}{5}x^2e^{5x}dx$\\




$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u_2 = \dfrac{3}{5}x^2 \\
dv_2 = e^{5x}dx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du_2 = \dfrac{6}{5}xdx \\
v_2 = \dfrac{e^{5x}}{5}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$=\dfrac{x^3 \cdot e^{5x}}{5} -\dfrac{3x^2e^5x}{25} + \displaystyle\int \dfrac{6}{25}xe^{5x}dx$\\


$\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
u_3 = \dfrac{6}{25}x \\
dv_3 = e^{5x}dx
\end{array}
\right .
\Rightarrow
\left \{
\begin{array}{l}
du_3 = \dfrac{6}{25}dx \\
v_3 = \dfrac{e^{5x}}{5}
\end{array}
\right .$\\
