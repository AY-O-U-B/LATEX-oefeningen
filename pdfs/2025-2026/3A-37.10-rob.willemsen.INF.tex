$f(x) = \ln\left(\sqrt{\sin x}\right)$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{\sin x}} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{\sin x}} \cdot \cos x$ \\

$= \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{\sin x}} \cdot \dfrac{\cos x}{2\sqrt{\sin x}}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\cos x}{2(\sqrt{\sin x})^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{\cos x}{2\sin x}$ \\
