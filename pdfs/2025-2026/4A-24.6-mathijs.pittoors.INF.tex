$\intt \dfrac{1}{x(1+\ln^2x)}dx$\\

Neem $t = \ln x$ dan $dx = xdt$\\

$\Rightarrow \intt \dfrac{1}{1+t^2}dt = \Bgtan t + C = \Bgtan (\ln x) + C$ met $C \in \mathbb{R}$\\
