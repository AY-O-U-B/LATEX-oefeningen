$f(x) = \dfrac{x^2 - x - 2}{x^2 + 5x + 6}$ \\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{(2x - 1)(x^2 + 5x + 6) - (x^2 - x - 2)(2x + 5)}{(x^2 + 5x + 6)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{(2x^3 + 9x^2 + 7x - 6) - (2x^3 + 3x^2 - 9x - 10)}{(x^2 + 5x + 6)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{2x^3 + 9x^2 + 7x - 6 - 2x^3 - 3x^2 + 9x + 10}{(x^2 + 5x + 6)^2}$ \\

$= \dfrac{6x^2 + 16x + 4}{(x^2 + 5x + 6)^2}$ \\
