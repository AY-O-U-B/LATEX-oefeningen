$z^3=-1$\\

$-1=\operatorname{cis}(\pi)$\\

$\Rightarrow z_k=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi+2k\pi}{3}\right),\ k=0,1,2$\\

$\Rightarrow z_0=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3}\right)=\dfrac12+\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}i$\\

$\Rightarrow z_1=\operatorname{cis}(\pi)=-1$\\

$\Rightarrow z_2=\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{5\pi}{3}\right)=\dfrac12-\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}i$\\

$\Rightarrow \boxed{z_0=\dfrac12+\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}i,\ z_1=-1,\ z_2=\dfrac12-\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}i}$\\
