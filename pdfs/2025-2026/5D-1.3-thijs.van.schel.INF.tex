$f(x) = \sqrt[3]{x} \Rightarrow S = \dint\limits_0^8\left|x^{\tfrac{1}{3}}\right|$\\

$= \left[\dfrac{3}{4}x^{\tfrac{4}{3}}\right]_0^8$\\

$=\left(\dfrac{3}{4}\cdot8^{\tfrac{4}{3}}\right)-\left(\dfrac{3}{4}\cdot0\right)$\\

$=\dfrac{3}{4}\cdot2^4-0 = 12$\\

Tekening: \\

$x=0 \Rightarrow y=0$\\

$x=1 \Rightarrow y=1$\\

$x=8 \Rightarrow y=2$\\
