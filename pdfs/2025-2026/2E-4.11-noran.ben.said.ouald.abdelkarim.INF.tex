$ \lim\limits_{x \to\infty}\left(\dfrac{x}{x-1}\right)^{x-1}$\\

$ \dfrac{x}{x-1} \Rightarrow \dfrac{x+1-1}{x-1} = 1 + \dfrac{1}{x-1}   $\\

$\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{x \to\infty}\left(1+\dfrac{1}{x-1}\right)^{x-1} = e$\\
