\normalsize

$ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{6x+2-\sqrt{4x+1}}{2x+1+\sqrt{4x+1}}dx $ \\

Neem: \\

$ t = \sqrt{4x+1} $ \\

$ t^2 = 4x+1 $ \\

$ x = \dfrac{t^2-1}{4} $ \\

$ dx = \dfrac{t}{2}dt $ \\

Dus: \\

$ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{6x+2-\sqrt{4x+1}}{2x+1+\sqrt{4x+1}}dx $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int
\dfrac{t\left(\dfrac{3(t^2-1)}{2}-t+2\right)}
{\dfrac{t^2-1}{2}+t+1}dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int
\dfrac{3t^3-2t^2+t}{t^2+2t+1}dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int
\left(3t-8+\dfrac{14t+8}{(t+1)^2}\right)dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int (3t-8)dt
+ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{7t+4}{(t+1)^2}dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{3}{4}t^2-4t
+ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{7t+4}{(t+1)^2}dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{3}{4}t^2-4t
+ \displaystyle\int\dfrac{7(t+1)-3}{(t+1)^2}dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{3}{4}t^2-4t
+ 7\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{t+1}dt
- 3\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1}{(t+1)^2}dt $ \\

$ = \dfrac{3}{4}t^2-4t
+ 7\ln|t+1| + \dfrac{3}{t+1} + c $ \\

Terug substitueren: \\

$ = \dfrac{3}{4}(4x+1)-4\sqrt{4x+1}
+ 7\ln|\sqrt{4x+1}+1|
+ \dfrac{3}{\sqrt{4x+1}+1} + c $ \\

$ = 3x -4\sqrt{4x+1}
+ 7\ln|\sqrt{4x+1}+1|
+ \dfrac{3}{\sqrt{4x+1}+1} + c $ \\
