$bc - ad = 104 - 54$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow (x_1 + v)\cdot (x_1 + 2v) - x_1\cdot (x_1 + 3v) = 50$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x_1^2 + 3x_1v + 2v^2 - x_1^2 - 3x_1v = 50$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow v^2 = \dfrac{50}{2}$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow v = -5 \vee v = 5$ \\

$v = -5$: \\

$x_1\cdot (x_1 + 3(-5)) = 54$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x_1^2 - 15x_1 - 54 = 0$ \\

$\Delta = (-15)^2-4\cdot(-54) = 441$ \\

$\Rightarrow x_1 = \dfrac{15 - 21}{2} = -3 \vee x_1 = \dfrac{15 + 21}{2} = 18$ \\

$x_1 = -3$: \\

$x_2 = -3 + (-5) = -8$ \\

$x_3 = -3 + (-10) = -13$ \\

$x_4 = -3 + (-15) = -18$ \\

$\Rightarrow -3 , -8, -13, -18$ \\

$x_1 = 18$: \\

$x_2 = 18 + (-5) = 13$ \\

$x_3 = 18 + (-10) = 8$ \\

$x_4 = 18 + (-15) = 3$ \\

$\Rightarrow 18, 13, 8, 3$ \\

$v = 5$: \\

$x_1\cdot (x_1 + 3\cdot5) = 54$ \\

$\Leftrightarrow x_1^2 + 15x_1 - 54 = 0$ \\

$\Delta = 15^2-4\cdot(-54) = 441$ \\

$\Rightarrow x_1 = \dfrac{-15 - 21}{2} = -18 \vee x_1 = \dfrac{-15 + 21}{2} = 3$ \\

$x_1 = -18$: \\

$x_2 = -18 + 5 = -13$ \\

$x_3 = -18 + 10 = -8$ \\

$x_4 = -18 + 15 = -3$ \\

$\Rightarrow -18 , -13, -8, -3$ \\

$x_1 = 3$: \\

$x_2 = 3 + 5 = 8$ \\

$x_3 = 3 + 10 = 13$ \\

$x_4 = 3 + 15 = 18$ \\

$\Rightarrow 3, 8, 13, 18$ \\
