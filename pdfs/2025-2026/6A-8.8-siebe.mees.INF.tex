$x_6 = ?? $ voor $\ \left( \dfrac{3}{4}, \dfrac{1}{4}, \dfrac{1}{12}, \ldots \right)$\\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
\dfrac{1}{4} - \dfrac{3}{4} = -\dfrac{1}{2}\\
\dfrac{1}{12} - \dfrac{1}{4} = -\dfrac{1}{6}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow $ geen rekenkundige rij \\

$ \left \{
\begin{array}{l}
r = \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{4}}{\dfrac{3}{4}} = \dfrac{1}{3}\\
r = \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{12}}{\dfrac{1}{4}} = \dfrac{1}{3}
\end{array}
\right .$\\

$\Rightarrow $ meetkundige rij \\

$x_n = x_1 \cdot r^{n-1}$\\

$x_n = \dfrac{3}{4} \cdot \left(\dfrac{1}{3}\right)^{n-1}$\\

$x_6 = \dfrac{3}{4} \cdot \left(\dfrac{1}{3}\right)^5$\\

$x_6 = \dfrac{1}{324}$\\
