$25 \cdot 0.008^x = 0.2^{2x-1}$\\

$\Rightarrow 25 \cdot \left(\dfrac{1}{125}\right)^x = \left(\dfrac{1}{5}\right)^{2x-1}$\\

$\Rightarrow 5^2 \cdot (5^{-3})^x = (5^{-1})^{2x-1}$\\

$\Rightarrow 5^2 \cdot 5^{-3x} = 5^{-2x+1}$\\

$\Rightarrow 5^{2-3x} = 5^{-2x+1}$\\

$\Rightarrow 2 - 3x = -2x + 1$\\

$\Rightarrow -3x + 2x = 1 - 2$\\

$\Rightarrow -x = -1$\\

$\Rightarrow x = 1$\\
