$\dint \dfrac{\sqrt[5]{x^3} + \sqrt[6]{x^5}}{\sqrt[4]{x}} dx$ \\

$= \dint \dfrac{\sqrt[5]{x^3}}{\sqrt[4]{x}} dx + \dint \dfrac{\sqrt[6]{x^5}}{\sqrt[4]{x}} dx$ \\

$= \dint x^{\tfrac{3}{5} - \tfrac{1}{4}} dx + \dint x^{\tfrac{5}{6} - \tfrac{1}{4}} dx$ \\

$= \dint x^{\tfrac{7}{20}} dx + \dint x^{\tfrac{7}{12}} dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{20}{27} x^{\tfrac{27}{20}} + \dfrac{12}{19} x^{\tfrac{19}{12}} + C$ \\

$= \dfrac{20}{27} \sqrt[20]{x^{7}} + \dfrac{12}{19} \sqrt[12]{x^{9}} + C$ \\
