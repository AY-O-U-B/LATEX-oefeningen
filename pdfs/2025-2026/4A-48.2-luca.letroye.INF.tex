$\dint \ln|x^2+2|dx$\\

$ u = \ln|x^2+2| \Rightarrow Du = \dfrac{2x}{x^2+2}$\\

$ Dv = dx \Rightarrow v = xdx$\\

$\Rightarrow x\ln|x^2+2| - \dint\dfrac{2x^2}{x^2+2}dx$\\

$= x\ln|x^2+2| - \dint\dfrac{2(x^2+2)-4}{x^2+2}dx$\\

$= x\ln|x^2+2| - 2x+2\sqrt{2}\bgtan\left(\dfrac{x}{\sqrt{2}}\right)+C$\\
