$\displaystyle\int\dfrac{1-\sin^32x}{\sin^22x}dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\int\dfrac{dx}{\sin^22x} - \displaystyle\int\sin2xdx$\\

Stel $u = 2x\Rightarrow \dfrac{du}{2} =  dx$\\

$= \displaystyle\dfrac{1}{2}\int\dfrac{du}{\sin^2u} - \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle\int\sin udu$\\
