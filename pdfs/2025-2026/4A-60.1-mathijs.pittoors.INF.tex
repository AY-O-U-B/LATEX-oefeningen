$\intt \sqrt{1-\sqrt{x}}dx$\\

Neem $t = \sqrt{x}$ dan $dx = 2t dt$\\

$\Rightarrow \intt \sqrt{1-t}2tdt = 2 \intt t \sqrt{1-t}dt$\\

Neem $s = \sqrt{1-t}$ dan $dt = -2s^2ds$\\

$\Rightarrow 2 \intt (1-s^2)(-2)s^2 ds$ \\

$= -4 \intt (s^2 - 4^2)ds$\\

$=-4\left(\dfrac{s^3}{3}-\dfrac{s^5}{5}\right) + k$\\

$=-4\left(\dfrac{\sqrt{(1-\sqrt{x})^3}}{3}-\dfrac{\sqrt{(1-\sqrt{x})^5}}{5}\right) + k$\\

$=-\dfrac{4}{3}\sqrt{(1-\sqrt{x})^3} + \dfrac{4}{5} \sqrt{(1-\sqrt{x})^5} + k$\\

Waar $k$ steeds een element van $\mathbb{R}$
