$V = \pi \displaystyle \int_{-\pi}^\pi \cos^2\left(\dfrac{x}{2}\right)dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{\pi}{2}\displaystyle \int_{-\pi}^\pi 1+\cos(x)dx$ \\

$= \dfrac{\pi}{2}\left[x + \sin(x)\right]_{-\pi}^\pi$ \\

$= \dfrac{\pi}{2}((\pi+0)-(-\pi+0))$ \\

$= \pi^2$ \\
