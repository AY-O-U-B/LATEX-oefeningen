$ I =  \intgrl \dfrac{4dx}{2x^2 +1} dx$\\

Stel: $t = \sqrt{2}x \adR dt = \sqrt{2} dx \asa  dx = \dfrac{dt}{\sqrt{2}} $\\

$= \intgrl \dfrac{4}{t^2 +1} \dfrac{dt}{\sqrt{2}} = 4 \operatorname{Bgtan} \Lh t \Rh +c$\\

$= \dfrac{4}{\sqrt{2}} \operatorname{Bgtan} \Lh \sqrt{2}x \Rh +c = 2\sqrt{2} \operatorname{Bgtan} \Lh \sqrt{2}x \Rh +c$\\
