$\integral \coth x dx$ \\

$= \integral \dfrac{\cosh x}{\sinh x}dx$ \\

Substitutie: $t = \sinh x \Rightarrow dt = \cosh xdx$ \\

$= \integral \dfrac{1}{t}dt$ \\

$= \ln|t| + c$ \\

$= \ln|\sinh(x)| + c$ \\
