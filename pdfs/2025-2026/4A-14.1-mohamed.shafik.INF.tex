Bereken $\displaystyle\int \ln x  dx$ door parti\"ele integratie\\

\begin{tabular}{ll}
$u = \ln x$ & $dv = dx$\\
$du = \dfrac{dx}{x}$ & $v = x$\\
\end{tabular}\\

\begin{eqnarray*}
\displaystyle\int \ln x  dx &=& x \cdot \ln x - \displaystyle\int x \cdot \dfrac{dx}{x}\\
&=& x \ln x - \displaystyle\int dx\\
&=& x \ln x - x + c\\
\end{eqnarray*}
