\normalsize

$\dfrac{-1 + i}{\sqrt{2}}$ \\

Teller: \\

modulus: \\

$r_t = \sqrt{(-1)^2 + 1^2} = \sqrt{2}$ \\

argument: \\

$\alpha_t = \tan^{-1}\left(\dfrac{1}{-1}\right) = \tan^{-1}(-1) = \dfrac{3 \pi}{4}$ \\

goniometrische vorm: \\

$z_t = \sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}\dfrac{3\pi}{4}$ \\

Noemer: \\

modulus: \\

$r_n = \sqrt{\sqrt{2}^2 + 0^2} = \sqrt{2}$\\

argument: \\

$\alpha_n = \tan^{-1}\left(\dfrac{0}{\sqrt{2}}\right) = 0$ \\

goniometrische vorm: \\

$z_n = \sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}0$ \\

$\Rightarrow\dfrac{\sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}\dfrac{3\pi}{4}}{\sqrt{2}\operatorname{cis}0} = \dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{2}}\operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4}-0\right) = \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4}\right)$ \\
