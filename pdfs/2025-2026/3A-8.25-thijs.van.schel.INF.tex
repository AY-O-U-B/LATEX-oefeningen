$f(x) = \dfrac{2x^2-4x+1}{\sqrt{x}}$\\

$f'(x) = \dfrac{g\cdot f' - f \cdot g'}{g^2} = \dfrac{\sqrt{x}(4x-4) - (2x^2-4x+1)\dfrac{1}{2}x^{-\tfrac{1}{2}}}{\sqrt{x}}$\\

$= \dfrac{\dfrac{2x(4x-4) - (2x^2-4x+1)}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x}$\\

$= \dfrac{8x^2-8x-2x^2+4x-1}{2x\sqrt{x}}$\\

$= \dfrac{6x^2-4x-1}{2x\sqrt{x}}$\\
