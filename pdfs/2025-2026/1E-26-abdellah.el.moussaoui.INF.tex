$A(z)=z^4-3z^3+az^2+bz-8$\\

$A(z)=(z-2)^2(z^2+pz+q)$\\

$\Rightarrow A(z)=(z^2-4z+4)(z^2+pz+q)$\\

$\Rightarrow A(z)=z^4+(p-4)z^3+(q-4p+4)z^2+(-4q+4p)z+4q$\\

$\Rightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
p-4=-3\\
q-4p+4=a\\
-4q+4p=b\\
4q=-8
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
p-4=-3\\
q=-2
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow \left\{
\begin{array}{l}
p=1\\
q=-2
\end{array}
\right.$\\

$\Rightarrow a=q-4p+4=-2-4+4=-2$\\

$\boxed{a = -2}$\\

$\Rightarrow b=-4q+4p=8+4=12$\\

$\boxed{b = 12}$\\
