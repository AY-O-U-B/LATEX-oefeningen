$ \displaystyle\int (x-4)^{\tfrac{2}{3}}dx $ \\

Stel $ t = x - 4 \Rightarrow dt = dx$ \\

$ \displaystyle\int t^{\tfrac{2}{3}}dt = \dfrac{3}{5}t^{\tfrac{5}{3}} + c $ \\

$ = \dfrac{3}{5}(x-4)^{\tfrac{5}{3}} + c $ \\
