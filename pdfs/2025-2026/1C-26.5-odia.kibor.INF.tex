$ \dfrac{10\operatorname{cis} \left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4}\right)}{5\operatorname{cis} \left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)}$\\

$ = 2 \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{3\pi}{4} - \dfrac{\pi}{4}\right )$\\

$ = 2 \operatorname{cis}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2} \right)$ \\

$ = 2 \left( \cos \dfrac{\pi}{2} + i \sin \dfrac{\pi}{2} \right) $ \\

$ = 2 (0 + i)$ \\

$ = 2i$ \\
